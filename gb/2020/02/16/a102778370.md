<p>目前，持续了2个多月的武汉肺炎疫情日益严重，而在中共<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/封城.md#1">封城</a>封区封楼封户封口封网封死讯的严密封锁下，一幕幕家破人亡的惨剧，正在中国各地频繁上演。</p>
<p>在此之前，网上流传一则来自豆瓣网友<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/小杭.md#1">小杭</a>（武汉）的日记。</p>
<figure id="attachment_102773733" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-102773733" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/asia-1177099_960_720.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-102773733" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/asia-1177099_960_720-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-102773733" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/武汉女孩.md#1">武汉女孩</a>日记：妈妈走了，爸爸去找妈妈了，我也感染了。示意图（pixabay）</figcaption></figure>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/小杭.md#1">小杭</a>是父母的独生宝贝，自武汉1月23日<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/封城.md#1">封城</a>后，她每天写日记，记录她父母感染“新冠肺炎”之后，她的无助、恐惧和痛苦&#8230;&#8230;眼睁睁看着父母受尽折磨，先后撒手人寰，而她却无能为力。</p>
<p>在绝望中痛不欲生的小杭，在送走妈妈后，经过几天的沉默后，她在2月3日贴出想念母亲的文字，同时也轻唤妈妈不要害怕，因为大舅也走了，可以在另一个世界互相依靠，但她希望母亲能保佑爸爸平安无事，千万不要带走他。</p>
<p>2月5日，小杭突然表示“救救我”，原来她从4日开始，她也开始发烧，没敢说出去，因为爸爸已经病危了，她只能跪下来求这个世界救救爸爸。</p>
<p>小杭的愿望终究没实现，她写道：“爸爸，我把你也弄丢了，你去找妈妈，然后等我，我们一起回家”，并透露“我好害怕，我也感染了”。2月9日，她日记中表示“我要活下去”，因为自己需要“安排各种后事”。</p>

<p>1月21日，仅两天后，小杭尚在抢购<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/口罩.md#1">口罩</a>的同时，她的母亲就出现了发烧症状。</p>

<p>1月23日，武汉封城的当天，小杭发出了那条求救信息，与此同时她母亲的症状变得越发严重。在此期间，小杭也是想尽办法为她的母亲进行治疗。</p>

<p>1月24日，小杭的妈妈被送到医院，小杭家人要抢在24点前看医生，因为“只有看上医生，才能被确诊，才可能被收治入院”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_102773696" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-102773696" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/7ba66a5ee5eed826ff5ddfa6fd23c006.jpg"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-medium wp-image-102773696" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/7ba66a5ee5eed826ff5ddfa6fd23c006-600x263.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="263" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-102773696" class="wp-caption-text">（网络图片）</figcaption></figure>
<p>而当天小杭的妈妈就开始尝试办理住院，从小杭的描述看，当地医院的医护力量严重不足，而父亲和自己因为与母亲的近距离接触，存在着被传染的极高可能性。</p>
<figure id="attachment_102773697" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-102773697" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/5461a3fa72a23938e8eed13e200d2513.jpg"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-medium wp-image-102773697" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/5461a3fa72a23938e8eed13e200d2513-600x379.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="379" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-102773697" class="wp-caption-text">（网络图片）</figcaption></figure>
<p>1月25日，小杭的朋友告诉她，自己的外婆在家“中风”，因没有足够的医疗救助而去世。当天小杭母亲也没有及时消息回复，令小杭和小杭父亲无比担心。</p>
<figure id="attachment_102773698" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-102773698" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/6e870cbd20480488be60253e3aad69ce.jpg"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-medium wp-image-102773698" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/6e870cbd20480488be60253e3aad69ce-600x371.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="371" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-102773698" class="wp-caption-text">（网络图片）</figcaption></figure>
<p>1月26日，小杭母亲的情况已变得更加复杂，医院原本想将其转移至金银潭医院，结果“一问三不知”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_102773701" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-102773701" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/ef88a46135e8e871b160156c67577409.jpg"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-medium wp-image-102773701" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/ef88a46135e8e871b160156c67577409-600x569.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="569" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-102773701" class="wp-caption-text">（网络图片）</figcaption></figure>
<p>1月27日，小杭父亲做了CT检查，已确认感染。</p>
<figure id="attachment_102773702" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-102773702" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/4c39790bc52908bcb1619bd5e9079706.jpg"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-medium wp-image-102773702" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/4c39790bc52908bcb1619bd5e9079706-600x115.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="115" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-102773702" class="wp-caption-text">（网络图片）</figcaption></figure>
<p>1月28日，小杭的母亲去世，离出现“发烧症状”仅一周的时间。</p>
<figure id="attachment_102773703" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-102773703" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/c228ec1f22d1b6e51d928bed02514b1e.jpg"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-medium wp-image-102773703" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/c228ec1f22d1b6e51d928bed02514b1e-600x222.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="222" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-102773703" class="wp-caption-text">（网络图片）</figcaption></figure>
<p>1月29，还来不及悲痛，小杭又带着父亲四处寻医，希望“保护爸爸到最后”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_102773704" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-102773704" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/859d1b9ce66f946b27ce2d53446af090.jpg"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-medium wp-image-102773704" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/859d1b9ce66f946b27ce2d53446af090-600x323.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="323" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-102773704" class="wp-caption-text">（网络图片）</figcaption></figure>
<p>两日的医院体验，让小杭觉得“医院才是最危险的地方”：太容易交叉感染并且医护力量严重不足，自己太担心“妈妈的悲剧重演”，太后悔带着“最需要休息的父亲四处奔波”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_102773705" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-102773705" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/709cbb04ab4c94f798171c857b75de8b.jpg"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-medium wp-image-102773705" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/709cbb04ab4c94f798171c857b75de8b-600x334.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="334" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-102773705" class="wp-caption-text">（网络图片）</figcaption></figure>
<p>1月31日，距离“知道妈妈生病的第十天”，小杭仍在努力救父亲，明明身处“地狱”，但那两天武汉的天气给人一种“岁月静好盛世太平”的错觉。小杭无比怀念曾经平凡的生活。</p>
<figure id="attachment_102773706" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-102773706" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/811b8a12356dd4757246e092e1276aec.jpg"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-medium wp-image-102773706" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/811b8a12356dd4757246e092e1276aec-600x483.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="483" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-102773706" class="wp-caption-text">（网络图片）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2月2日，小杭父亲的情况变差，最终还是住进了医院。父亲在病房里“拚命赶我走”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_102773707" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-102773707" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/40e5c2badfdb5c46e723ff9c1f5ad90a.jpg"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-medium wp-image-102773707" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/40e5c2badfdb5c46e723ff9c1f5ad90a-600x241.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="241" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-102773707" class="wp-caption-text">（网络图片）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2月3日，小杭写下了一段纪念母亲的文字并写道“只是我要求求你，保佑爸爸。现在，还不能带走他。我每天都在拼了命的救他，并后悔没有如此的保护你。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_102773708" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-102773708" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/ca9ad73c145249f6a0bf15e987e1f19c.jpg"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-medium wp-image-102773708" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/ca9ad73c145249f6a0bf15e987e1f19c-600x467.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="467" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-102773708" class="wp-caption-text">（网络图片）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2月5日，小杭出现了发烧症状，担心自己也受到了感染，与此同时医院传来父亲病危的消息要求她赶去签字。</p>
<figure id="attachment_102773709" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-102773709" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/c4647e9ec6c63671c29846e26cca60ec.jpg"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-medium wp-image-102773709" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/c4647e9ec6c63671c29846e26cca60ec-600x188.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="188" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-102773709" class="wp-caption-text">（网络图片）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2月6日，小杭陆续也收到了许多来自各地热情的帮助，但这种救助对小杭时间的大量挤占也让她感觉很累更绝望。</p>
<figure id="attachment_102773710" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-102773710" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/477d9c8121ae1b90b7cb9694c6b98c31.jpg"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-medium wp-image-102773710" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/477d9c8121ae1b90b7cb9694c6b98c31-600x540.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="540" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-102773710" class="wp-caption-text">（网络图片）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2月7日，在好心人士和同事的帮助下，小杭父亲得到了转院治疗的机会，但父亲的情况已变得很差。小杭已经非常不忍看到呼吸衰弱的父亲所承受的苦痛。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/7c307ba6d1f775ace5c99a8763977e9f.jpg"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-medium wp-image-102773711" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/7c307ba6d1f775ace5c99a8763977e9f-600x243.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="243" /></a></p>
<p><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/43e8c1c53d5e19ae5cd2f2fcf33af92e.jpg"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-102773712" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/43e8c1c53d5e19ae5cd2f2fcf33af92e-600x128.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="128" /></a></p>
<figure id="attachment_102773713" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-102773713" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/bc54664ecab06b02099da35dcb782675.jpg"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-medium wp-image-102773713" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/bc54664ecab06b02099da35dcb782675-600x169.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="169" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-102773713" class="wp-caption-text">（网络图片）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2月8日，小杭同时得到了两条噩耗：父亲的去世以及自己的感染。</p>
<figure id="attachment_102773714" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-102773714" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/de627afae8228bb56ce25c4a39f61d13.jpg"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-medium wp-image-102773714" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/de627afae8228bb56ce25c4a39f61d13-600x220.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="220" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-102773714" class="wp-caption-text">（网络图片）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2月9日，在恐惧和绝望中的小杭，因还需要“安排各种后事”，她表示“我要活下去”！</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/7b2c89ef9e9c56117428991b26cabc76.jpg"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-102773715" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/7b2c89ef9e9c56117428991b26cabc76-600x340.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="340" /></a></p>
<figure id="attachment_102773716" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-102773716" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/aa4c0a291cfdb6bd96a426c5b7011885.jpg"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-medium wp-image-102773716" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/aa4c0a291cfdb6bd96a426c5b7011885-600x206.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="206" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-102773716" class="wp-caption-text">（网络图片）</figcaption></figure>

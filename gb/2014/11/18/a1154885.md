<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【禁闻】胡德平藉批周永康 向中共提意见？</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【新唐人2014年11月18日讯】已故<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/中共.md#1">中共</a>总书记胡耀邦的长子<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/胡德平.md#1">胡德平</a>，近来多次公开批评前<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/中共.md#1">中共</a>政治局常委<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/周永康.md#1">周永康</a>，以及周永康极力支持的重庆“唱红打黑”恐怖。尽管力挺要法治的胡德平身份特殊，还被认为与中共总书记习近平友好，但评论认为，他的主张未必能实现。至于胡德平对周永康的表态，外界又是怎么看呢？一起来了解。</p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/胡德平.md#1">胡德平</a>11月7号在参加“首届大梅沙中国创新论坛”时说，党内一些巨腐大贪，随意践踏法治，严重破坏社会秩序，极大地影响了人们安全感上的心理底线，有段时间让人特别之恐怖。他说自己也是其中之一，在一段时间里，觉得社会怎么成这个样了。</p>
<p>胡德平并没有点名“巨腐大贪”是谁，不过他紧接着说，自己在2010年10月曾给<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/周永康.md#1">周永康</a>写过一封信，一是帮一名老将军反映贪腐问题，同时也说明，自己对政治走势、社会秩序、治安极为焦虑。胡德平说，周永康后来最支持重庆的“唱红打黑”，每次讲话都给人们一个很恐怖的感觉，不知中国的政法法治要怎么发展。</p>
<p>另外，对国内的各种突发性矛盾，胡德平认为不会在短时期消失，也不能都简单的提高到所谓的“阶级斗争”。他表示，中共建政以后搞的很多阶级斗争，说实话是无谓的，没有什么意义，没解决什么问题。真正的问题完全可以用另外一种方法来解决，也就是“法治”。</p>
<p>旅美中国社会问题研究人士张健：“他讲这番话呢，最重要的问题是他不单是针对周永康这样的行为，他也对中共政权，提出了一个自己的看法。这个看法就是中共从建政到现在这么多年，靠血腥的镇压，对党内异己的剪除。”</p>
<p>胡德平13号还在《新京报》的采访中，又一次提到“唱红打黑”和“法治”。</p>
<p>他说，薄熙来在重庆“唱红打黑”期间，时任前重庆市公安局局长的王立军公开说：“只要把政治问题变成法律问题来查，我们就有绝对的发言权”。胡德平当时除了写信给中共当局，表示这种说法恐怖，后来还公开发表意见，认为主要是司法工作出了问题。</p>
<p>因此他认为，政治体制改革、司法体制改革越来越重要。依法治国，对权力进行制约，违宪审查，共产党要带头守法，都属于政治领域的改革。</p>
<p>不过，这次中共十八届四中全会，虽然强调了“依法治国”，但得出的结论还是“必须把党的领导贯彻到依法治国全过程”。因此，尽管胡德平把“对权力进行制约”、“共产党要带头守法”也考虑进政改范围，但实现的可能性，评论人士并不乐观。</p>
<p>张健：“在中国来看，只要有中共党政的那一天，依法治国就是中国人民无法实现的一个梦。党如果凌驾于法律之上的话，那么党就一定是独裁，并且邪恶的。因为它没有任何人去约束它。”</p>
<p>胡德平在《新京报》的采访中，还特别提到了“特殊利益集团”，说他们侵占人民、国家的财产，虽然现在还很有势力，但应该依法处理，一切非法所得，都应该归还人民。</p>
<p>著名政论家、《北京之春》主编胡平认为，归还人民是应当的，但现阶段不可能实现。</p>
<p>《北京之春》主编胡平：“因为要归还给人民，那其实本身就意味着，对共产党整个革命和改革的基本否定，而且也是对共产党一党专制的否定。共产党它就是两次掠夺：先是以革命的名义，把所有人的财产都给抢走了。然后以改革的名义，把所谓人民的财产变成权贵自己的财产。它整个统治就是建立在这个基础之上的。”</p>
<p>在四中全会结束后，被认为与习近平关系密切的胡德平，多次在各种场合表示对“依法治国”的支持，批评薄熙来、周永康等。胡德平上个月底还接任了大陆敢言杂志《炎黄春秋》的社长，“自由亚洲电台”的报导说，动用胡德平是为了对付中宣部，用“红二代”制约“红二代”。</p>
<p>采访/易如 编辑/尚燕 后制/葛雷</p>
<p>Hu Deping Criticizes Zhou Yongkang.</p>
<p>Hu Deping, eldest son of the late Chinese Communist Party <br />(CCP) General Secretary Hu Yaobang, has recently publicly <br />criticized Zhou Yongkang and his support of &#8220;sing the red, <br />hit the gang" campaign in Chongqing.<br />Hu Deping, with his special status and a supporter of the <br />rule of law, is known to be friendly with Xi Jinping. <br />But, commentators don&#8217;t believe his ideas will be realized. </p>
<p>How is his attitude towards Zhou Yongkang <br />being interpreted? Let&#8217;s take a look.</p>
<p>In a recent innovation forum held in Shenzheng, Hu Deping <br />indicated, some corrupt officials have trampled the rule of <br />law and seriously disturbed social order. It has greatly <br />violated the psychological sense of security, creating terror. <br />He said as one of those affected, he also questioned what <br />happened to the society.</p>
<p>Hu Deping did not name the corrupt officials. </p>
<p>He also said that he had written a letter to Zhou Yongkang <br />in October 2010, addressing the corrupt issues reflected by <br />an old general, and his own concerns over the political <br />movements, social order and security. <br />Hu Deping said, during the time when Zhou Yongkang was <br />highly supportive of &#8220;sing the red, hit the gang" campaign <br />in Chongqing, his talks would create terror in people <br />about the development of politics and law in China.</p>
<p>Hu Deping doesn&#8217;t believe the various unexpected social <br />conflicts, will disappear quickly or become class struggle. <br />He said that the CCP has engaged in many class struggles<br />in since the regime was established.<br />Most have been quite unnecessary and meaningless. </p>
<p>They don&#8217;t solve any problems. The real problems could <br />have been solved by different means, the rule of law.</p>
<p>US-based China social issue researcher Zhang Jian: &#8220;His talks <br />are not just about Zhou Yongkang but also towards the CCP. <br />He expressed his views about the many years of CCP <br />rule relying on the bloody crackdown on dissidents and <br />elimination of opponents within the party."</p>
<p>In an interview with Beijing News on Nov. 13, Hu Deping <br />again mentioned the Chongqing campaign and rule of law.</p>
<p>He said, during the time when Bo Xilai was engaging in the <br />&#8220;sing the red, hit the gang" campaign in Chongqing, former <br />director of the Chongqing Municipal Public Security Bureau <br />Wang Lijun had openly talked. <br />Wang said, &#8220;As long as the political issue is investigated as a <br />legal issue, we have the absolute right to speak." <br />Hu Deping asked the Central about the horrifying argument; <br />openly expressed that the judicial system is questionable.</p>
<p>Therefore, he believes that political reform and judicial <br />reform are becoming more important.<br />The rule of law will restrict the authority and put the <br />authority under constitutional review. <br />The Communist Party must take the lead in being <br />law-abiding. This is in the scope of political reform.</p>
<p>However, even though rule of law was emphasized during <br />the Fourth Plenary Session, the conclusion remained.<br />&#8220;The party&#8217;s leadership guides the implementation <br />of the rule of law." <br />Although Hu Deping considered to &#8220;constrain the exercise <br />of power", &#8220;the CCP must take the lead in law-abiding," <br />in the scope of political reform, but the commentators <br />are not optimistic about the achievement.</p>
<p>Zhang Jian: &#8220;In China, as long as the CCP rules, the rule of <br />law will only remain as a dream. <br />If the party is above the law, then the party must be the <br />dictator, and an evil one. Because there is no restraint."</p>
<p>Hu Deping also mentioned the special interest groups <br />in the Beijing News interview. <br />He indicated that the groups have occupied property <br />of the people and the state. <br />Although they still hold the power, they should be dealt <br />with according to the law; <br />Everything stolen should be returned to the people.</p>
<p>Famous political commentator, Beijing Spring editor Hu Ping <br />believes that giving back to the people is a must. <br />But, it is impossible at this stage.</p>
<p>Hu Ping: &#8220;To return what has been stolen, means a complete <br />denial of the CCP revolution and reform.<br />It is also a denial of the Communist one-party dictatorship. </p>
<p>The CCP has plundered twice: The first one was done in the <br />name of revolution to deprive all individuals&#8217; property. <br />Then it was done in the name of reform to turn peoples’ <br />property into the property of the powerful elites. <br />That is what its ruling was built upon."</p>
<p>After the fourth plenary session, Xi Jinping&#8217;s close ally, Hu <br />Deping, has expressed his support of rule of law on many <br />occasions, and criticisms of Bo Xilai and Zhou Yongkang. </p>
<p>Hu Deping also took over the outspoken magazine <br />&#8220;Yanhuang Chunqiu" as the president last month. <br />Radio Free Asia reported that Hu Deping has been mobilized <br />to deal with the Central Propaganda Department; <br />It is to handle the red second generation with another Red II.</p>

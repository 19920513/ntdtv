<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【禁闻】中共“示弱” 美国防部长参观辽宁号</h2>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【新唐人2014年04月09日讯】<ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/美国国防部长">美国国防部长</a>查克?<ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/哈格尔">哈格尔</a>7号到达中国，开始为期4天的访华行程。但出乎常规的是，哈格尔一行第一天就在青岛参观了中国海军的航空母舰“<ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/辽宁号">辽宁号</a>”。中共为什么罕见的让美国人参观自己最先进的军事装备呢？请看报导。</p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/哈格尔">哈格尔</a>（Chuck Hagel）访问中国的首站并不是北京，而是去了青岛的中共海军基地，哈格尔一行花了大约两个小时，参观中共的首艘航母——“<ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/辽宁号">辽宁号</a>”。</p>
<p>美方官员说，哈格尔是第一名被允许上“辽宁号”的外国人。</p>
<p>一名美国高级国防官员表示，美国提出了参观航母的要求，中方表示同意。这名官员同时表示，这次参观具有重要意义，因为“辽宁号”是中共军队加强海军力量的像征。</p>
<p>“辽宁号”是前苏联制造的军舰，1988年11月下水。中共以旅游娱乐的名义，从乌克兰手中购买，几经波折才在2002年开回中国。在2012年9月完成了航母改装。</p>
<p>中共计划明年在“辽宁号”上部署30架飞机。</p>
<p>旅美政论家伍凡：“实际上那条船是一个摆设，不起多大作用的。但是美国人想看看究竟你航母达到什么程度，美国人要第一手资料，眼睛看到你达到什么程度。”</p>
<p>“台湾国立政治大学国际关系研究中心”主任严震生认为，中共让<ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/美国国防部长">美国国防部长</a>登上“辽宁号”，主要是想取信于美国。 </p>
<p>新加坡的区域安全专家伊恩?斯多雷（Ian Storey）也认为，哈格尔登“辽宁号”像征中方对美方的善意，同时美方也可了解这艘改造的航母实际作战能力如何。但因中美航母差距巨大，中方的安排其实是一种“示弱”，无非想表明中国航母不足以在地区称霸，更无法与美国抗衡。</p>
<p>在此之前，中共军方人士认为，中国航母至少落后美国30年。</p>
<p>一些外媒也分析，“辽宁号”的技术远不如美军。</p>
<p>哈格尔到中国之前，首先访问了日本，并重申了美国对日本的军事承诺。</p>
<p>哈格尔在日本也以罕见的强硬姿态对中共发出警告，并提醒中共，作为大国不要在南海以及东海主权争端问题上仗势欺人。</p>
<p>哈格尔表示，美国反对中共以任何形式单方面改变现状，并强调，世界各国无论大小均应该受尊重，决不能透过“暴力、胁迫和恐吓”来侵犯他国领土、尊严，与国家主权。</p>
<p>哈格尔还许诺额外向日本派遣两艘导弹防御舰，将驻防在日本的宙斯盾级驱逐舰的数量增加到七艘，同时还将在日本部署第二个早期预警雷达、P-8海上监视飞机，引入全球鹰无人机等。</p>
<p>台湾国立政治大学国际关系研究中心主任严震生“毕竟日本是美国的盟友，那菲律宾是美国的盟友。如果美国在立场上支持日本和菲律宾的话，那是不是会影响美国跟中国的关系，甚至过去的一些交流会不会受到冲击？”</p>
<p>严震生认为，美国一方面希望中共在国际事务上面，担负起一个大国的责任，包括在乌克兰、叙利亚，以及在北韩半岛问题上，特别是美国目前跟俄罗斯因为乌克兰问题交恶的情况下，美国也想与中共在一些议题上结盟。但另一方面，美国又要防止中共破坏东南亚的稳定局势。</p>
<p>美国国务院东亚事务助理国务卿罗素在3号说，国际社会对俄国吞并克里米亚做出的制裁措施，将会遏阻中共采取类似行动。罗素还表示，中共不应该怀疑美国对盟友兑现防务承诺的决心。</p>
<p>伍凡：“因为哈格尔到北京去就是要警告中共，你不要胡思乱想，不要跟蒲亭走一条路。”</p>
<p>旅美政论家伍凡认为，俄罗斯总统蒲亭夺了乌克兰的克里米亚，还在进一步向乌克兰东部扩张。在这种局势下，美国打消中共对台湾和钓鱼岛的想法，是很重要的。</p>
<p>采访/陈汉 编辑/宋风 后制/孙宁</p>
<p>U.S. Defense Secretary Visited the Liaoning Aircraft Carrier.<br />Why is the Chinese Communist Party Showing Weakness?</p>
<p>U.S. Defense Secretary Chuck Hagel arrived in China on<br />March 7 for a four-day visit.<br />However, it is unusual that Hagel visited China Qingdao naval<br />Liaoning aircraft carrier in the first day.<br />Why did the Chinese Communist Party (CCP) allow Americans<br />to visit its most advanced military equipment?</p>
<p>Let&#8217;s look at the reports.</p>
<p>The first stop for Chuck Hagel was not Beijing,<br />but the Qingdao naval base.<br />Hagel and his party took about two hours to visit the CCP&#8217;s<br />first aircraft carrier the Liaoning.</p>
<p>The official said Hagel was the first official visitor from<br />outside China to be allowed on board the Liaoning.<br />A U.S. Defense Force Senior Officer confirmed that China<br />allowed the U.S. to visit the aircraft carrier.</p>
<p>He said the visit is significant because Liaoning aircraft carrier<br />is seen as a symbol of China&#8217;s growing naval power as well<br />as ambition for greater global influence.<br />The Liaoning is a Soviet-era vessel bought from the Ukraine<br />in the name of tourist recreation.</p>
<p>It was sailed back to China in 2002. It was later modified as<br />an aircraft carrier in September 2012<br />The CCP plans to deploy 30 aircrafts on Liaoning aircraft<br />carrier in 2015.</p>
<p>U.S. political commentator Wu Fan: &#8220;The boat is actually a<br />decoration, not of too much use.<br />The Americans only wonder how far advanced your aircraft<br />carrier technique is with firsthand information."</p>
<p>Yan Zhensheng Director of the Institute of International<br />Relations, Taiwan National Chengchi University, believes<br />that the CCP allowed the U.S. Defense Secretary on board<br />the Liaoning carrier to gain their trust.</p>
<p>Singapore regional security expert Ian Storey also believes<br />Hagel&#8217;s boarding the Liaoning carrier symbolizes China&#8217;s<br />goodwill to the United States, who can also learn the actual<br />defense capability of the carrier.</p>
<p>Since there is a huge gap between China and U.S. aircraft<br />carriers, the CCP arrangement is actually to show weakness<br />that their aircraft carrier is not enough to dominate, certainly<br />not compete with the United States.</p>
<p>Before that, some Chinese military believe that the aircraft<br />carrier is at least 30 years behind the United States.<br />It is also believed by outsiders that the Liaoning carrier is far<br />less sophisticated than U.S. carriers.</p>
<p>Before China, Hagel visited Japan first and reaffirmed the<br />military commitment from the U.S.<br />Hagel indirectly delivered strong warnings to China not to<br />push on the disputed islands&#8217; sovereignty of South and East<br />Sea as a big country.</p>
<p>Hagel said that the U.S. is against China changing the status<br />quo in any form unilaterally and stressed that all nations and<br />all people deserve respect no matter how large or how small.<br />There shouldn&#8217;t be invasion of territory, dignity or national<br />sovereignty by violence, coercion and intimidation.</p>
<p>Hagel promised to deploy two additional ballistic missile<br />defense destroyers to Japan making a total of seven U.S.<br />ballistic missile defense warships.<br />Hagel&#8217;s announcement follows deployment of a second<br />early warning U.S. radar to Japan, P-8 maritime surveillance<br />aircraft and plans to send unmanned Global Hawk drones.</p>
<p>Yan Zhensheng: &#8220;Japan is an ally of the United States. The<br />Philippines is also a U.S. ally.<br />Will it affect the relationship between China and the U.S. if<br />the U.S. supports Japan and the Philippines?"</p>
<p>Yan Zhensheng believes that the U.S. hopes China takes<br />on responsibility over the Ukraine, Syria, and the Korean<br />Peninsula.<br />He would like to see an alliance with China on some issues<br />since the U.S. and Russia became enemies over the Ukraine.<br />The US needs to prevent China destabilizing the<br />situation in Southeast Asia.</p>
<p>President Barack Obama&#8217;s diplomatic point man for East Asia,<br />Daniel Russel, issued a statement on April 3.<br />He said that retaliatory sanctions imposed on Russia by<br />the U.S., the EU and others should have a &#8220;chilling effect<br />on anyone in China who might contemplate the Crimea<br />annexation as a model.<br />The CCP should not doubt the U.S. commitment<br />to defend its Asian allies.</p>
<p>Wu Fan: The purpose of Hagel&#8217;s visit is to warn the CCP not<br />to think too much and follow Putin.</p>
<p>Commentator Wu Fan believes that Russian President<br />Vladimir Putin took the Crimea from Ukraine and still<br />further expanded to the east.</p>
<p>So it&#8217;s very important to the U.S. to dispel China&#8217;s thoughts<br />on Taiwan and the Diaoyu Islands.</p>

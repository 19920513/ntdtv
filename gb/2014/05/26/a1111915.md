<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【禁闻】中共军机逼近日本战机 日指中恐吓</h2>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【新唐人2014年05月26日讯】<b><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/中共军机">中共军机</a>逼近<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/日本战机">日本战机</a> 日指中<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/恐吓">恐吓</a></b></p>
<p>5月24号，2架<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/中共军机">中共军机</a>在东中国海上空，以不到50米左右的超短距离逼近2架日本自卫队飞机，这是中共战斗机首次与自卫队飞机“异常接近”。</p>
<p>日本防卫相5月25号表示，中共超出常规的近距离接近日本自卫队飞机的行为，可能会导致偶发事故，日本已经向中共提出交涉。</p>
<p>日本防卫相小野寺表示，这是一种带有<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/恐吓">恐吓</a>味道的不正常行为。中方则指称日本阻碍中俄军事演习。</p>
<p><b>美国拟阻止中共黑客参加国际黑客会议</b></p>
<p>美国政府一位匿名高级官员透露，美国政府正在考虑以拒签签证的方式，阻止中共黑客参加今年8月在美国拉斯维加斯举行的黑客大会和黑帽技术大会，这两个大会是最重要的国际黑客聚会。英国《路透社》评论说，这个举动将是美国遏制中共网路间谍活动的一个组成部分。</p>
<p>不过，这两个国际黑客大会的组织者认为，限制中共黑客入境，对他们的网路攻击能力并不会产生实质的影响，因为大会的所有讨论都有录像，并会在会议后公开出售。</p>
<p>美国《华尔街日报》报导，美国官员说，如果中共不限制黑客商业入侵的话，美国政府将考虑其他可能的反制措施，包括公布更多有关中共黑客入侵的证据，对被起诉的中共黑客以及与他们有关联的人员或组织，实施其他商业或金融限制等。</p>
<p><b>海外民运呼吁各界参加六四纪念会</b></p>
<p>“中国民主运动海外联席会议”等民运组织，5月25号发出呼吁，希望海外各界人士，参加31号晚，全美“中国学生学者自治联合会”（学自联IFCSS）在中共驻华盛顿大使馆前举办的“六四25周年纪念会”。</p>
<p>据《美国之音》报导，纪念会上将有同时来自中国大陆、香港、台湾、藏人社区、日本、越南等亚洲各地的人权活动人士，以及其他支持中国民主自由的人士发表演讲。纪念会将通过互联网同步向全球，尤其是中国大陆传送实况。</p>
<p>报导说，这是海外民运组织今年计划展开的一系列“纪念六四”活动中，最大的活动之一。</p>
<p>编辑/周玉林</p>
<p><b> Chinese Military Aircraft Approached Japanese Fighter Planes</b></p>
<p>On May 24, two Chinese Communist Party (CCP) military <br />aircraft approached two Japanese fighter aircraft less than <br />50 meters off the East China Sea.</p>
<p>It was the first time the CCP planes came so unusually close <br />to the Japanese Self Defense Forces (SDF) planes. </p>
<p>On May 25, the Japanese Defense Minister said the CCP&#8217;s <br />action might cause an accident. <br />Japan has made representations to the CCP.</p>
<p>Japanese Defense Minister Onodera called the Chinese actions <br />outrageous.<br />The CCP alleged that Japan interfered with the Sino-Russian <br />military exercises.</p>
<p><b> U.S. May Keep Chinese Hackers Out of Defcon Hacker <br />Conference </b> </p>
<p>U.S. officials are considering using visa restrictions preventing<br />Chinese Communist Party (CCP) hackers attending two popular <br />hacker conferences.</p>
<p>These include Defcon hacker and Black Hat conference in <br />Las Vegas in August.<br />Reuters said it is part of a broad effort to curb <br />Chinese cyber espionage.</p>
<p>However, both conference organizers believe that restricting <br />CCP hackers will not make any difference because Congress <br />has videos about all discussions to sell openly afterwards.</p>
<p>The Wall Street Journal reported that U.S. officials said if <br />the CCP does not restrict commercial hacker invasion, the <br />U.S. government would consider other options.</p>
<p>These including releasing additional evidence showing how <br />the hackers conducted their alleged operations, and imposing <br />other business and financial restrictions on those indicted or <br />people or organizations associated with them. </p>
<p><b> Overseas Democracy Appealed to Participate in The June 4 <br />Commemoration<b></p>
<p>On May 25, Overseas Chinese Democracy Coalition and other <br />pro-democracy organizations appealed to overseas people to <br />participate in the 25th anniversary commemoration of June 4.</p>
<p>This will be held by the Chinese Students and Scholars <br />Association of Autonomy at the Chinese embassy on May 31.</p>
<p>According to Voice of America, human right activists from <br />China, Hong Kong, Taiwan, the Tibetan community, Japan, <br />Vietnam, other Asian countries and those supporting Chinese <br />democracy and freedom will give speeches. <br />The Memorial will be synchronized across the world, <br />especially to China live via the Internet.</p>
<p>The report says this year, there is a plan to start an overseas <br />pro-democracy organization of a series of June 4 campaigns, <br />one of the biggest events.</p>

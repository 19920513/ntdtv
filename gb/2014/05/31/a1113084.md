<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【禁闻】《环球》泄江泽民卖国秘密 再热传</h2>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【新唐人2014年05月31日讯】随着近期中共与越南双方关系急剧恶化，中共官媒《<ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/环球">环球</a>时报》此前的一篇评论，再次被外界翻出热传，因为这一篇文章不小心泄露了一段有关前中共党魁<ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/江泽民">江泽民</a>在任期内，将“白龙尾岛”割让给越南的“<ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/卖国">卖国</a>”秘闻。</p>
<p>5月下旬，中国社交网路平台“微博”出现了一则图帖，附上《<ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/环球">环球</a>时报》这篇去年3月的评论文章截图。 </p>
<p>《环时》文章说，中方一贯重视所谓的“中越友谊”，在北部湾海上划界时，将历史上本来属于中方的“白龙尾岛”让给越方，越方近年来非但不领情，反而变本加厉侵犯中方在南海的权益。 </p>
<p>发布图帖的网友（“蛋蛋的坏坏3”）在微博上留言：“这不是丢，是送，是让……”不少民众转发这条相关信息时表示“怒转”。</p>
<p>据中共官方网站信息，2000年12月25号，中共与越南双方签署《北部湾划界协定》，将浮水洲岛（就是越方所称的白龙尾岛）正式割让给越南。签署这一协定的正是中共前总书记<ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/江泽民">江泽民</a>。目前，这一网页已遭删除。</p>
<p>时事评论员邢天行：“ 白龙尾岛，是现在的热点，是《环球时报》爆出来的，是中共党媒自己来证实自己<ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/卖国">卖国</a>确凿无疑的证据，所以大家在热传它。”</p>
<p>旅美中国问题评论人士李善鉴：“ 江泽民在中共台上的时候，为了获得国际对他的支持，他有非常多不得人心的政策，他通过各种方式，对很多国家都出卖了中国的领土。 这些事情连续还会有人往出揭。大家越来越意识到它（中共）天天以爱国的名义来镇压它认为对它政权有威胁的民众。它本身才是中国最大的卖国贼。”</p>
<p>1999年12月9号，江泽民在北京与来访的俄罗斯总统叶利钦，签订了《关于中俄国界线东西两段的叙述议定书》。</p>
<p>根据这一《议定书》，江泽民将中国总和100多万平方公里的领土，出让给了俄罗斯，这相当于东北三省面积。此外，《议定书》中还将图门江出海口划给了俄国，从而封死了中国东北通往日本海的出海口。这桩有关领土的大事一直被江泽民隐瞒，直到几年后，相关条约才经由俄方公布。</p>
<p>2001年7月16号，江泽民又与俄罗斯总统蒲亭签署了《中俄睦邻友好合作条约》，承认海参崴及邻近远东地区近700平方公里的土地，“永远”不再为中国的领土。</p>
<p>时事评论员邢天行表示，包括中共历史上的卖国行径，多年以前外界都在传。大陆的很多网民虽然知道，但都半信半疑，总是怀疑是否海外有人在造谣。</p>
<p>邢天行：“他就是没有这个概念，就是历史上被沙皇也好，还有被不平等条约夺走的那些土地，按照国际法来讲，你这个是不合理、不被国际上承认的，完全可以在适当的机会自己拿回来的。但是，你一旦代表国家签订条约，那永远就是对方的。可是这个知识点，就是中国老百姓被洗脑后，他不被告知，他不知道。”</p>
<p>早在1950年，中共当时的首脑毛泽东、总理周恩来就曾在莫斯科与苏共首脑斯大林、外交部长维辛斯基秘密签署卖国条约，正式承认蒙古独立，让苏联保持在中国东北的特权，战时允许苏军在华据守，输送数千万劳工给苏联等等。毛泽东自己当年也称签定这一条约是“丧权辱国”。</p>
<p>此外，毛泽东还随便赠送国土和资源给周边的苏联、缅甸、印度等国家。</p>
<p>邢天行：“ 这个卖国的罪责，江泽民是继承者，前面是毛泽东、周恩来，最后这一届是江泽民。所以它综合起来，真正的是中共在卖国。为了他们所谓的共产这个东西，为了他们私人集团的利益，不顾中国这么多年的主权、民族利益这些东西，全部不顾了。”</p>
<p>一位名为“人民煮河蟹”的大陆网友在“微博”上愤怒的表示：从它们（中共）建立政权的那一天起，对它们所作所为，都要彻底清算，如有卖国割地，死的要鞭尸，活的送审判，没有尸首的，立碑让它遗臭万年！</p>
<p>采访/朱智善 编辑/王子琦 后制/肖颜</p>
<p>News Article Exposing Jiang Zemin’s Treason by Giving<br />Up Territory Becomes Hot Again.</p>
<p>As tensions between the Chinese Communist Party (CCP) <br />and Vietnam spiked again recently, an article previously <br />published by the CCP’s media Global Times became <br />popular again in China. <br />The article “unintentionally” exposed a story of secret <br />treason of Jiang Zemin, who gave Bach Long Vi Island to <br />Vietnam when he was the CCP leader.</p>
<p>Just recently, a comment was posted on Weibo, <br />the Chinese social network. <br />The author attached a photo of Global Time’s <br />commentary published last March. </p>
<p>The commentary said the CCP always valued the <br />“China-Vietnam friendship” very much. <br />When marking off the boundary in the Beibu Gulf (Gulf of <br />Tonkin) between the two states, the CCP gave Bach Long Vi <br />Island to Vietnam. </p>
<p>The Global Times commented that Vietnam was ungrateful <br />for the CCP’s “kindness” in recent years, and had even gone <br />further in violating China’s interests in South China Sea.</p>
<p>The author of the post, “DanDan’s Huaihuai 3”, wrote a <br />message saying “This is giving land as a present instead <br />of discarding it”. </p>
<p>Some netizens commented that they felt angry <br />when forwarding the news.</p>
<p>According to a CCP official report, the CCP and Vietnam <br />signed the Sino-Vietnamese Beibu Gulf Demarcation <br />Agreement on December 25, 2000. </p>
<p>In the treaty, the CCP formally ceded Fushuizhou Island <br />(Bach Long Vi Island in Vietnamese) to Vietnam. <br />It was Jiang Zemin, the former CCP secretary-general <br />who signed the treaty. <br />Currently the report has been deleted from the Internet.</p>
<p>Xing Tianxing, political commentator: “Bach Long Vi Island <br />is a popular topic now. <br />The story was first exposed by the Global Times. </p>
<p>It is the CCP’s own mouthpiece media that offered <br />irrefutable proof of the treason. <br />So many Chinese are talking about it.”</p>
<p>Li Shanjian, China affairs commentator:”When Jiang was <br />head of the CCP, he made a lot of bad decisions to get <br />support from the global society. </p>
<p>He sold China’s land to many countries in different ways, <br />and those facts will be continuously revealed in the future. <br />The Chinese begin to realize that the CCP keeps suppressing <br />ordinary people who are considered threats to its regime. <br />The CCP does that in the name of patriotism, but actually <br />the party itself is the biggest traitor of our country.”</p>
<p>On December 9, 1999, Jiang Zemin signed a &#8220;Treaty between <br />the People&#8217;s Republic of China and the Russian Federation on <br />the East and West boundary of China and Russia," with the<br />visiting Russian President Yeltsin in Beijing.</p>
<p>By signing this treaty, Jiang handed over a million square <br />kilometers of land to Russia, which is the same area as<br />Northeast China (Manchuria). </p>
<p>The treaty also allocated the estuary of the Tumen River <br />to Russia, which blocked China’s path to the Sea of Japan. <br />Such a big decision was concealed by Jiang <br />against the Chinese people. <br />It was Russia that first announced the treaty several years <br />after it was signed.</p>
<p>On July 16, 2011, Jiang Zemin signed the “Treaty of Good-<br />Neighborliness and Friendly Cooperation” with Russian <br />President Putin. </p>
<p>The treaty acknowledged that an area of about 700 square <br />kilometers around Haishenwai (Vladivostok) would be <br />permanently ceded to Russia by the CCP.</p>
<p>Commentator Xing Tianxing said, all CCP treason acts in<br />its history had been spreading among Chinese years ago. <br />Although many netizens heard about it, they don’t fully <br />believe the stories and always worry that those are rumors <br />created by foreign sources.</p>
<p>Xing Tianxing: “They just don’t know that according to<br />International Law, all our territories ceded to other <br />countries such as Russia by unequal treaties are not <br />acknowledged by the global society. <br />We can definitely get them back at the right time. </p>
<p>However, once someone represents our state and signs <br />treaties, he will permanently give the land to others. <br />The Chinese people have no knowledge about this from <br />the brainwashing propaganda by the CCP. <br />Nobody teaches them about this.”</p>
<p>As early as 1950, the CCP former leader Mao Zedong <br />and Premier Zhou Enlai had a meeting with Stalin <br />in Moscow and the Soviet Foreign Minister Vyshinsky. </p>
<p>They signed a secret “treason treaty” there, which formally <br />acknowledged independence of Mongolia. <br />It also gave the Soviet Union privileges in Northeast China, <br />permitting Soviet militaries to stay in China during wartime, <br />and promised to deliver tens of millions of Chinese workers <br />to the USSR. <br />Even Mao himself had called the treaty “humiliating”.</p>
<p>Mao had also easily given territories and resources as <br />presents to neighbors like the USSR, Myanmar and India.</p>
<p>Xing Tianxing: “Jiang Zemin succeeded Mao Zedong <br />and Zhou Enlai’s crimes of treason. <br />He was the last CCP leader to do that. So put altogether it is <br />the CCP that betrayed our people. <br />For their Communistic ideal, and interests of their own group, <br />the CCP completely ignored China’s sovereignty and national <br />interests for all these years.”</p>
<p>A netizen named “Renmin Zhu Hexie” made <br />the following angry comments on Weibo: <br />“All their (CCP) crimes since the first day they took power, <br />would have to be brought to justice; <br />Those who ceded our territory to others would be tried <br />if they are alive, or their bodies whipped if they are dead. <br />Or be given everlasting shame on monuments <br />if they don’t have any bodies left!”</p>

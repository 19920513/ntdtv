<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【禁闻】挑战洗脑班  江天勇揭中共末日疯狂</h2>
<a href="https://d3d2r098q79b5l.cloudfront.net/R1uSCNLBb"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/19920513/www/master/t/ntdtv/ntdtv600.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/19920513/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【新唐人2014年05月10日讯】中国4名维权律师因代理法轮功案件，被黑龙江建三江警方殴打致肋骨骨折事件过去了一段时间，被打断8根肋骨的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/江天勇">江天勇</a>律师获释后，仍一直遭到国保控制，5月9号，江天勇终于有机会发声，他披露了自己在被关押期间，被酷刑折磨的经过。江天勇表示，中共一直把法律当成工具，想打击谁就打击谁，用暴力制造恐惧，已完全疯狂。</p>
<p>代理建三江法轮功案件的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/江天勇">江天勇</a>、唐吉田、王成、张俊杰4位律师，3月20号陪同受害人及家属前往建三江“青龙山<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/洗脑班">洗脑班</a>”，要求释放被非法关押的法轮功学员石孟昌、韩淑娟、和蒋欣波等人。</p>
<p>3月21号早上，约20名警察把他们强行绑架和暴力殴打，他们四人还被构陷罪名刑事拘留。张俊杰被拘留5天后释放，检查出被打断3根肋骨。</p>
<p>4月6号，江天勇等三位律师才获释，回北京后江天勇一直受到北京国保的所谓“重点保护”，电话也被做了手脚。</p>
<p>江天勇回去多天后，仍然感到全身疼痛难忍，他到医院检查，结果“一切正常”；4月18号，江天勇再到天津做CT检查，22号结果出来，发现他的8根肋骨骨折。</p>
<p>江天勇说，他从3月21号被抓捕到获释，期间多次遭到警方的暴力殴打，被抓当天早上，他被塞到警车的后备箱带到警局。</p>
<p>大陆维权律师江天勇：“我们被带到建三江公安局大兴分局里面，白天开始对我们审问，在讯问室里面对我就进行殴打，那个时候就是坐在椅子上，对我前胸使劲用拳头打，用矿泉水瓶打脑袋、打脸。”</p>
<p>当晚7点，江天勇被警察反手铐上手铐，带上黑头套，架出询讯室，带到到另一间屋子。</p>
<p>江天勇：“把我弄到里面，从后面弄了个手铐穿着链子还是绳，拉着就给吊起来，吊起来之后，就对我拳打脚踢，踢我的腹部，踢我的胸部，非常的残酷，一脚下去就完全不能喘气，身子立即就缩在一块，就这样持续的踢，大概5、6个人一起动手。”</p>
<p>江天勇说，他们边打边问话，一句话不合他们的意，就往死里打，江天勇被打的全身冒冷汗，身上的保暖内衣和外面的衣服全部湿透了。22号凌晨他从吊铐中被放下来后，整个人都瘫了，被几个人架回了审讯室。</p>
<p>江天勇：“27日的时候，还是那个翟长坤带着他的助手，又来对我审讯，他找一张纸，用浆糊把那个摄像头糊上，然后就过来，抠我的两边肋骨，对我又有殴打。”</p>
<p>江天勇说，这期间他已经被折磨的整个人都模糊了。</p>
<p>江天勇：“4月1号的晚上，在七星拘留所的二楼最东边的这个房间，于文波和另外一个人又来对我进行审讯，照我的臀部猛踢，就那一脚踢下去，我立即就倒了。”</p>
<p>唐吉田和王成律师获释当天也讲述了他们受酷刑的经历。</p>
<p>唐吉田被殴打致10根肋骨骨折，还被警方威胁挖坑活埋、活摘肾脏、罚款十万元，以及送到<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/洗脑班">洗脑班</a>去强制转化。而王成被打折了3根肋骨，王成说，他遭抓捕的当天，就被建三江警方套上黑头套拳打脚踢，也被威胁要把他活埋、枪毙、活摘器官等。</p>
<p>大陆维权律师王成：“他把我带到了另外一个房间，首先用一个黑头套把头套起来，什么都看不到，然后用手铐把手反铐铐到后面，然后用绳子吊手铐把人吊起来，吊的双脚离地开始殴打，主要打的是左侧的胸部、肋骨还有背部。”</p>
<p>此外，因声援四位律师也被拘留的大陆公民张圣雨等人，同样遭到建三江当局的酷刑折磨。与4名律师一起被非法绑架的7人，其中3人被酷刑折磨到命危，送医抢救。</p>
<p>建三江警方的恶行遭到了全球声讨，中国民间以及多个国际人权机构和人权组织都发表声明谴责，同时要求对“建三江事件”施暴的责任人进行追责。</p>
<p>4月28号，“青龙山洗脑班”被迫关闭，但与4律师一起遭绑架的7人，被秘密转移它处关押。</p>
<p>日前，“追查迫害法轮功国际组织”再次发布公告，对“建三江事件”涉案相关责任单位和责任人立案追查，同时对中共各地仍在运行的“洗脑班、黑监狱”进行全面追查。</p>
<p>采访编辑/李韵 后制/李智远</p>
<p>Chinese Lawyer Jiang Tianyong Reveals Violence Experienced <br />While Detained By Police</p>
<p>Several weeks have passed since four Chinese human<br />rights lawyers were violently beaten by police in Jiansanjiang.<br />The lawyers were demanding the release of Falun Gong<br />practitioners, but were themselves detained.<br />Even after his release, one of the lawyers Jiang Tianyong<br />was still under strict surveillance by state security officers.<br />On May 9, Jiang was finally able to describe his experiences<br />of being persecuted when under detention in Jiansanjiang.<br />Jiang said that the Chinese Communist Party (CCP)<br />only regards law as a tool to suppress anyone at will.<br />The party has been foolish, recklessly<br />using violence to frighten Chinese people.</p>
<p>Jiang Tianyong, Tang Jitian, Wang Cheng and<br />Zhang Junjie are lawyers defending Falun Gong<br />practitioners currently imprisoned in Jiansanjiang.</p>
<p>On March 20, they visited Jiansanjiang&#8217;s Qinglongshan<br />Brainwashing center with the victims family members.<br />They called for the release of practitioners including<br />Shi Mengchang, Han Shujuan and Jiang Xinbo.</p>
<p>On the morning of March 21, approximately 20 police<br />officers kidnapped the lawyers and violently beat them.<br />Following this experience, the four lawyers were further<br />placed under criminal detention on false charges.<br />Zhang Junjie was released five<br />days later with three broken ribs.</p>
<p>The other three lawyers were not released until April 6.<br />After returning to Beijing, Jiang Tianyong was put under<br />24 hour &#8220;special protection" by state security staff.<br />His phone was also tapped.</p>
<p>The injuries Jiang sustained persisted, but was told &#8220;nothing<br />was wrong with his health" when he visited a hospital.<br />He had another CT inspection in Tianjin on April 18, and<br />he was told four days later that eight ribs were broken.</p>
<p>Jiang revealed that he had been beaten violently by the<br />police many times after he was kidnapped on March 21.<br />On the morning of that day, he was put into the trunk<br />of a police vehicle on the way to the local police station.</p>
<p>Jiang Tianyong: &#8220;We were taken to Daxing<br />branch of Jiansanjiang Public Security Bureau.<br />We were interrogated that day and beaten right there.<br />I was put on a chair. They hit my chest with fists<br />and used water bottles to strike my head and face."</p>
<p>At 7pm that day, Jiang Tianyong was<br />handcuffed and forced into black hoods.<br />Then, he was taken out of the<br />interrogation room to another place.</p>
<p>Jiang Tianyong: &#8220;They took me inside and suspended<br />me by connecting my handcuffs with a rope or a chain.<br />They then began to beat me, including<br />kicking my abdomen or chest.<br />I could not breathe at all after each<br />kick and my body shrank in pain.<br />They kept kicking me like that, with about<br />five or six doing that simultaneously."</p>
<p>Jiang said that the &#8220;police officers"<br />also asked questions while beating him.<br />If he said that there was anything &#8220;wrong"<br />then they beat him as much as they could.<br />Under such violence, Jiang&#8217;s whole body went into<br />cold sweats, and all his clothes were soaking wet.<br />After his handcuffs were removed early<br />on March 22, Jiang was completely paralyzed.<br />Several people then took him<br />back to the interrogation room.</p>
<p>Jiang Tianyong: &#8220;On March 27, the same officer named<br />Zhai Changkun interrogated me again with several assistants.<br />He first used a seal to cover the camera.<br />Then, he began to beat me again,<br />including scratching my ribs."</p>
<p>Jiang described his mental<br />confusion after days of persecution.</p>
<p>Jiang Tianyong: &#8220;In the evening of April 1,<br />I was brought to the east most room on the<br />second floor of Qixing Detention Center.<br />Yu Wenbo and another guy interrogated me.<br />They kicked my hip hard until I could<br />hardly stand. I fainted after one kick.”"</p>
<p>Tang Jitian and Wang Cheng also revealed how<br />they were persecuted on the day of their release.</p>
<p>Tang had 10 ribs broken due to violence he experienced.<br />He was also threatened with words including that he would<br />be buried alive, or that his kidney would be live harvested.<br />They said he would be fined by 100,000 Yuan ($ 16,000),<br />or that he would be imprisoned in a brainwashing center.<br />Wang Cheng, who had 3 ribs broken, said<br />he had a black hood placed over his head.<br />He was violently beaten on<br />the day of being kidnapped.<br />Wang was threatened with similar words.</p>
<p>Wang Cheng, Chinese human rights<br />lawyer: &#8220;They brought me to another room.<br />I was first put into a black hood and couldn&#8217;t see anything.<br />I was handcuffed behind my back, and<br />suspended with ropes through the handcuffs.<br />When my feet left the ground they began to beat<br />me, mostly on the left of my head, ribs and back."</p>
<p>Zhang Shengyu and other Chinese civilians<br />visited Jiansanjiang to support these lawyers.<br />They were also seriously persecuted by the local authority.<br />Among seven who were kidnapped<br />together with the four lawyers, three<br />had suffered life-threatening persecution.</p>
<p>They were sent to hospital<br />for emergency treatment.</p>
<p>The violence of Jiansanjiang has<br />been condemned internationally.<br />Many human rights organizations from China or<br />other countries, made announcements demanding<br />actions against the individuals responsible.</p>
<p>On April 28, Qinglongshan Brainwashing<br />center was closed following this pressure.<br />However, the seven civilians who were kidnapped<br />together with four lawyers were secretly moved<br />to other places and are yet to be released.</p>
<p>The World Organization to Investigate the<br />Persecution of Falun Gong (WOIPFG) announced<br />that it was launching a thorough investigation.<br />It will look into individuals and units involved<br />in this persecution case in Jiansanjiang.<br />WOIPFG also stated that it will call all<br />current CCP brainwashing centers and<br />illegal prisons to find their whereabouts.</p>

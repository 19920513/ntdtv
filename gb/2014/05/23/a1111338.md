<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【禁闻】中共网络间谍跨红线 美国不再容忍</h2>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【新唐人2014年05月23日讯】美国司法部日前起诉了5名<ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/中共">中共</a>军队黑客，并公布嫌疑人的名字和照片。美国安全人员表示，国与国之间互相刺探情报是正常的，但中共跨越了“红线”，针对美国民间组织、私人企业和商业界进行网络攻击，这是不道德的。</p>
<p>5月19号，美国司法部起诉了5名<ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/中共">中共</a>军队黑客，指控这些人侵入5家美国钢铁、太阳能、核电企业以及一家劳工组织的电脑，窃取商业机密和其他敏感商业信息。</p>
<p>美国安全专家表示，军方对军方之间、情报单位对情报单位之间的互相刺探，是正常的，大家都心照不宣，但是中共军队利用国家庞大资源攻击美国私人企业，窃取宝贵的知识产权后，再交给中国企业，其中很多是国有企业，发展山寨产品，借此来打击美国公司，这是非常不公平、也是不道德的。</p>
<p>美国前助理国务卿温斯顿?洛德大使表示，中共的商业盗窃行为，每年给美国公司造成数百亿美元的损失。</p>
<p>美国《芝加哥论坛报》社论指出，这个起诉的目地是向中共传递一个信息：积极盗取美国经济及商业机密的行为，将不再被容忍。</p>
<p>旅美中国社会问题研究人士张健：“美国这个方面起到一个非常好的警示作用，就是警告所有的共产主义国家，像他们黑客这种不法的行为，只要是触犯了美国的法律，一定会被绳之以法。我相信中共只能是巧立名目，更换它的战略手段，继续还会在美国和其他民主国家搞这种大量的网络攻击行为，因为这是它的本质决定。”</p>
<p>美国官员表示，除了遭到起诉的中共61398部队外，美国国家安全局及情报合作伙伴，目前还在调查20多个中共黑客组织，这些组织大多为中共军方单位，它们侵入了美国大量政府机构和公司。</p>
<p>据报导，中共当局对美国的指控做出了强烈反应，除召见美国大使以示抗议外，还中止中美网络工作组的活动。外交部发言人秦刚表示，中国是美方网络窃密和监控的严重受害者。他所指的是，前美国国家安全局承包商雇员斯诺登揭露的美国监听丑闻。</p>
<p>张健：“美国所监控的东西，它是起到一个维护世界安定的作用，在这个作用前提下，它无论是从事反恐、监控，它是有法可依的、有章可循的。”</p>
<p>北京时政观察人华颇：“国与国之间利用网络窃取它国的机密，这不是什么秘密，但是美国不会监控异议份子，如果你在美国网络上对美国政府的做法提出异议和反对，美国政府，上至总统，下至工作人员，不敢把你怎么样，不敢窃取你的机密和干扰你的系统，但中国不一样，中国的金盾（工程）实在是无孔不入，如果你对中国政府提出了一些异议，你就会受到监控，网络系统就会受到干扰。”</p>
<p>据了解，以往，美国基本上是通过外交或媒体等途径，来表达对中共窃取机密的不满，为何美国现在改变态度呢？北京时政观察人士华颇认为，有几方面原因。</p>
<p>北京时政观察人士华颇：“中国召开亚信会议，声称亚洲是亚洲人的亚洲，那意思就想把美国踢出亚洲，而且中、俄又举行联合军演，针对的就是美国在东海的盟国日本，而且中国这次跟俄罗斯签订了能源协议，这样一来，美国和欧洲国家制裁俄罗斯，中共却暗中支持俄罗斯，美国非常恼火，所以在这个时间点上提出了中国的5名军官。”</p>
<p>美国官员表示，他们将关注中共当局是否限制<ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/网络间谍">网络间谍</a>活动，以及将采取哪些步骤。</p>
<p>大陆时事评论员周晓辉在博文中指出，中共高层现在不会忽视美国的警告，毕竟中共有太多令人惊骇的秘密掌握在美国人手中，比如活摘法轮功学员器官，比如在美高官的直系亲属和存款。美国抛出任何一个，都将彻底要了中共的命。</p>
<p>采访/朱智善 编辑/陈洁 后制/钟元</p>
<p>U.S. Begins Showdown with the Chinese Regime</p>
<p>Recently, the U.S. Department of Justice charged five hackers<br />of the Chinese Communist Party (CCP) military, and released<br />their names.<br />U.S. security experts explained that intelligence activities<br />between countries are normal,<br />but the CCP has crossed the red line and launched cyber<br />attacks on U.S. NGOs, private enterprises and the business world.<br />This is immoral.</p>
<p>On May 19 U.S. Department of Justice charged<br />five hackers working for the CCP military,<br />They are accused of invading the computers of five U.S.<br />companies,<br />such as U.S. Steel, solar and nuclear power companies<br />and a labor organization, in order to steal business secrets and<br />other sensitive data.</p>
<p>U.S. security experts expressed that intelligence gathering<br />between militaries and intelligence units are common.<br />But the CCP military utilizes huge national resources to<br />attack private U.S. companies, to steal valuable knowledge and IP&#8217;s<br />and hand these details over to Chinese enterprises,<br />many of which are state-owned enterprises,<br />to develop and produce pirate products and undermine U.S. companies.<br />This is unfair, as well as immoral.</p>
<p>Former U.S. Assistant Secretary of State and<br />Ambassador to China Winston Lord said that,<br />the CCP&#8217;s commercial theft has caused tens of billions of dollars<br />in losses annually to U.S. companies.</p>
<p>Chicago Tribune editorial stated that the purpose of<br />this prosecution is to send a message to the CCP:<br />actively stealing U.S. economic and business secrets<br />will no longer be tolerated.</p>
<p>US-based China Issue analyst Zhang Jian:<br />&#8220;It was a very powerful warning. It warned communist countries<br />that illegal activities such as hacking would be<br />brought to justice as long they violate the U.S. law.<br />I believe the CCP has to adopt other strategies and<br />would continue to engage in large scale network attacks on<br />the U.S. and other democratic countries. This is its nature."</p>
<p>U.S. officials said in addition to suing the 61,398 troop,<br />FBI and its partners are investigating more than 20 CCP<br />hacker organizations, which mostly belong to the CCP military.<br />These organizations invaded a large number of<br />U.S. government agencies and companies.</p>
<p>CCP regime reacted strongly to the charge from the U.S.<br />In addition to summoning the U.S. ambassador to protest,<br />it also suspended the activities of the China and U.S. Network<br />Working Group.<br />Foreign Ministry spokesman Qin Gang said that China is a serious<br />victim of U.S. network theft and monitoring,<br />referring to the monitoring scandal revealed by<br />former FBI contractor Snowden.</p>
<p>Zhang Jian: &#8220;Activities monitored by the U.S. is to<br />play a role in maintaining world stability. Counter-terrorism<br />and surveillance is based on the law."</p>
<p>Beijing political affair observer Hua Po:<br />&#8220;Intelligence activities between countries is a secret.<br />But U.S. does not monitor dissidents or persecute anyone.<br />It does not steal your secretes or interfere with your system.<br />The CCP is different. CCP military attacks everywhere.<br />It monitors dissidents and damages their systems."</p>
<p>In the past, U.S. mainly expressed dissatisfactions over<br />CCP&#8217;s stealing intelligence through diplomatic or media channels.<br />why did the U.S. change?<br />Hua Po thinks there are several reasons.</p>
<p>Hua Po: &#8220;China held the CICA, claiming Asia is for Asians,<br />meaning kick out the U.S.<br />China and Russia held joint military exercises,<br />aiming at U.S.ally Japan.<br />China also signed the energy agreement with Russia,<br />to support Russia while U.S. and European countries<br />are carrying out sanctions against Russia.<br />The U.S. is very angry and revealed the CCP&#8217;s five suspects."</p>
<p>U.S. officials said they would pay attention to whether the CCP<br />authorities restrict cyber espionage and what steps would be taken .</p>
<p>China&#8217;s commentator Zhou Xiaohui pointed out in his blog that<br />CCP leadership will not ignore the U.S.warning.<br />After all, the U.S. is holding too many nasty secrets on the CCP.<br />such as organ harvesting from living Falun Gong practitioners,<br />deposits of immediate family members in U.S. banks, etc.<br />U.S. release of any of these would collapse the CCP.</p>

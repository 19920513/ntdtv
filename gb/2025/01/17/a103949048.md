<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>中国儿童和年轻人大量死亡 湖南妈妈失去两女儿</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id103949062-FotoJet-16-868x488.jpeg" width="868" height="488" />
<h6>近期，中国大陆病毒蔓延，儿童和年轻人大量死亡，一个湖南妈妈因为疫情失去了两个女儿。（网络截图）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【新唐人北京时间2025年01月18日讯】近期，中国大陆<ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/病毒">病毒</a>蔓延，儿童和年轻人大量死亡，一个湖南妈妈因为疫情失去了两个女儿，有医生博主的抖音评论区，出现大量儿童和年轻人死亡的事例。</p>
<p>在大陆小红书平台，一位湖南妈妈哭诉，两个女儿都被甲流急性坏死性脑病带走了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103949049" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103949049" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" fetchpriority="high" class="wp-image-103949049 size-medium" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id103949049-FotoJet-13-600x464.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="464" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103949049" class="wp-caption-text">（网络截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>该妈妈说，她女儿是在甲流高峰期发病的。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103949051" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103949051" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" class="wp-image-103949051 size-medium" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id103949051-FotoJet-14-600x414.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="414" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103949051" class="wp-caption-text">（网络截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>抖音网名“医路向前巍子”的北京大学第一医院密云医院急诊科的医生在一个视频中分享自己治疗的病例，说儿童感到小腿酸痛时要注意，在该视频的评论区，众多网友分享身边有人去世的事例。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103949060" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103949060" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" class="wp-image-103949060 size-medium" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id103949060-7268b913d416534c4f4039fdd608b1f9-600x333.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="333" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103949060" class="wp-caption-text">（网络视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>浙江网友“晴空”介绍，她儿子学校有两个孩子走了，就是因为孩子的父母当普通感冒在药店买药吃，一直没有太注意，“最后引发并发症走啦”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103949052" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103949052" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="wp-image-103949052" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id103949052-a4d8683f699d09fb4faee1c65cd5135a-600x313.jpeg" alt="" width="400" b="209" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103949052" class="wp-caption-text">（网络截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>湖北网友说，“我的小同事儿子13岁，以为感冒没当回事，在课堂上晕倒了，送到医院就没有再醒来。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_103949053" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103949053" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="wp-image-103949053" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id103949053-081e2d5d2856ad377fecf2b2fa064d69.jpeg" alt="" width="400" b="243" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103949053" class="wp-caption-text">（网络截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>有福建网友说，“我女儿是急性爆发<ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/心肌炎">心肌炎</a>没了，这种真的好快，快到连转上级医院都来不及”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103949055" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103949055" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="wp-image-103949055" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id103949055-47d7d30396cbae2410bb2fc4c341c180.jpeg" alt="" width="400" b="506" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103949055" class="wp-caption-text">（网络截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>江苏网友称，“我女儿暴发性<ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/心肌炎">心肌炎</a>，在幼儿园感染了诺如<ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/病毒">病毒</a>，证状:<ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/发烧">发烧</a>不到39度，呕吐，手脚冰凉，无力行走！四个小时抢救无效！走时差十六天六周岁”。</p>
<p>除了儿童，还有很多年轻人死亡的病例。</p>
<p>广东网友“小郑”，“我永远也不知道他是不是心肌炎走了，走的前几天低烧，还浑身酸痛”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103949057" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103949057" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="wp-image-103949057" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id103949057-79bb305c4bca0ef5f0df5306596261ec.jpeg" alt="" width="400" b="286" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103949057" class="wp-caption-text">（网络截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>江苏网友称，“楼下有个人说儿子18岁心肌炎走了”，内蒙古网友说，她同事还怀着孕，其丈夫感冒后心肌炎走了。</p>
<p>山东网友说，“女朋友前几天<ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/发烧">发烧</a>，第二天去医院了，心肌炎，当天就走了，90后，孩子还那么小”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103949058" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103949058" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="wp-image-103949058" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id103949058-95117d0b68d37446ad3afc2a6b870afa.jpeg" alt="" width="400" b="539" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103949058" class="wp-caption-text">（网络截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>1月12日，一位小红书健身博主的朋友发文称，“号主于昨天下午高烧昏迷住院，今晨因流感诱发心肌炎，经抢救无效，生命永远定格。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_103949059" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103949059" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="wp-image-103949059 size-medium" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2025/01/id103949059-FotoJet-15-600x414.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="414" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103949059" class="wp-caption-text">（网络截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>中国大陆多病毒蔓延，医院排起长队，殡仪馆人员称连轴转，但中共当局掩盖真实情况，不进行统计，让民众疏于防护。</p>
<p>（记者李郦综合报导／责任编辑：徐耕文）</p>

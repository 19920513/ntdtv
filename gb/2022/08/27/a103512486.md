<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>自称赵立坚侄女 疑官三代炫富“坑爷”</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/FbKCenoUcAExOCk.jpg" />
<h6>抖音账号“程晨sabrina”公布的赵立坚抓拍照。（网络照片）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【新唐人北京时间2022年08月28日讯】近来多有中共官员后代网上<ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/炫富">炫富</a>被挖出，遭嘲讽“坑爹”。日前网友又发现一名<ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/官三代">官三代</a>不但高调<ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/炫富">炫富</a>，还公开“官爷爷”的照片和“叔叔<ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/赵立坚">赵立坚</a>”的抓拍照。</p>
<p>近日大陆抖音平台上一个名为“程晨sabrina”的女性网民引发舆论关注。此人在网上晒出银行账户上千万元人民币的入账或者花销，以及高达8000万的账户余额，还经常炫耀各种奢侈品的照片。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103512490" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103512490" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/007VjjRHgy1h5lpl5c9r9j30ku112ndk.jpg"><img decoding="async" fetchpriority="high" class="wp-image-103512490" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/007VjjRHgy1h5lpl5c9r9j30ku112ndk-600x1067.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="711" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103512490" class="wp-caption-text">抖音账号“程晨sabrina”未被清空前的截图。</figcaption></figure>
<p><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/FbKCdmOVQAEZRNl.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="aligncenter wp-image-103512491" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/FbKCdmOVQAEZRNl.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="866" /></a></p>
<figure id="attachment_103512492" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103512492" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/FbKfxsLVQAQlCeY.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="wp-image-103512492" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/FbKfxsLVQAQlCeY.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="866" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103512492" class="wp-caption-text">抖音账号“程晨sabrina”公布的银行账户截图。</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_103512494" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103512494" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/FbKCeTcVQAAvQEZ.jpg"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="wp-image-103512494" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/FbKCeTcVQAAvQEZ.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="866" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103512494" class="wp-caption-text">抖音账号“程晨sabrina”晒出的奢侈品。</figcaption></figure>
<p>此外，该网民还经常公布一些北京大会堂会议和其它官方会议的非公开照片，疑似与会人员私下拍摄。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/FbKfuK_UIAANNwt.jpg"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="aligncenter wp-image-103512493" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/FbKfuK_UIAANNwt.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="866" /></a></p>
<p>照片还包括在白宫前拍摄的美国总统拜登夫妇。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/FbKfw-HVQAE2pon.jpg"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="aligncenter wp-image-103512495" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/FbKfw-HVQAE2pon.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="866" /></a></p>
<p>还有一张被该网民称为“同父异母兄弟”的年轻人照片。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/FbKfxPRaQAEDY0r.jpg"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="aligncenter wp-image-103512496" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/FbKfxPRaQAEDY0r.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="866" /></a></p>
<p>其中还包括疑似一名官员在北京开会时的个人留影。该网民在照片说明中暗示这是她的爷爷。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/FbKCd-HUsAItJHa.jpg"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="aligncenter wp-image-103512497" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/FbKCd-HUsAItJHa.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="866" /></a></p>
<p>其中最引人注目的是一张中共外交部发言人、新闻司副司长<ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/赵立坚">赵立坚</a>的照片。该照片在2021年1月发布，疑似赵立坚在一个高档场合被抓拍，明显不是官方发布的公开照。</p>
<p>该网民在照片说明中称呼赵立坚为“叔叔”。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/FbKCenoUcAExOCkgg.jpg"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="aligncenter wp-image-103512498" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2022/08/FbKCenoUcAExOCkgg.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="866" /></a></p>
<p>目前尚不清楚照片中的“官爷爷”到底是谁，以及是否真是该网民的爷爷。但她所发布的“赵立坚叔叔”的照片引发大量关注。</p>
<p>网友们纷纷留言，“还是第一次见到赵家人”、“原来赵立坚也是红后代”。</p>
<p>在引发大量关注后，“程晨sabrina”的抖音号全部内容已被清空。大陆各大平台上流传的相关截图也陆续被屏蔽。有网友指，此人的抖音内容本来并没有非常确凿的证据证明，她就是<ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/官三代">官三代</a>或赵立坚侄女，但是官方这一屏蔽就“洗不清了”。</p>
<p>不久前，江西官二代周劼在朋友圈炫富并曝光父亲职务和家族关系网，引爆网络舆论。周劼任职的国企江西国有资本运营控股集团有限公司被迫连发“调查通报”。</p>
<p>随后又有一名自称吉林省水利厅厅长韩沐恩（现任中共吉林省人大常委会秘书长）儿子的“周公子”被曝在网上炫富。不过这次官方没有承认，而是辟谣称此人是“冒充”。</p>
<p>（责任编辑：景中明）</p>

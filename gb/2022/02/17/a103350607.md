<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>网曝徐州又一名“精神病女” 牙齿同样快掉光</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/67078778baa9c6ceeae7b6c0628961b3.jpg" />
<h6>网传徐州又发现一名牙齿快掉光的“精神病女”。（网络截图）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【新唐人北京时间2022年02月18日讯】<ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/徐州">徐州</a>“八孩母”事件真相未明，网上又传出徐州另一名妇女的视频，同样是牙齿快掉光，并且看似已精神失常。</p>
<p>“八孩母”事件曝光后，有网友在快手视频平台发现一位播主发布的视频，其中显示有一名妇女和“八孩母”境遇相似。</p>
<p>拍视频的播主疑似在网上经营儿童用品商店，收养了一个名叫“大亮”的男童。视频是在到“大亮”亲生父母家中接他时所拍摄。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103350619" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103350619" style="width: 397px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/FLzeWeNWQAMywTL-1.jpg"><img decoding="async" fetchpriority="high" class="wp-image-103350619" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/FLzeWeNWQAMywTL-1-600x1333.jpg" alt="" width="397" b="882" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103350619" class="wp-caption-text">网页截图。</figcaption></figure>
<p>视频显示，“大亮”的母亲牙齿几乎掉光，据说生了两个儿子，“大亮”是老二，生完“大亮”后因为出血摘除了子宫。家人说她“有精神病”。“大亮”的父亲据说已年过七十。</p>
<p>网络热传这个母亲也被拴着铁链，但网传视频截图中未见铁链。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103350620" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103350620" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/FLzeWzsWQAARn6h-1.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="wp-image-103350620" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/FLzeWzsWQAARn6h-1-600x1299.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="866" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103350620" class="wp-caption-text">网页截图。</figcaption></figure>
<p>这个家庭生活条件非常艰苦，播主说去接“大亮”时，看到孩子饿得“让人心疼”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103350621" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103350621" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/826048b21a53d6c2d4e694bd3aac643f-1.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="wp-image-103350621" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/826048b21a53d6c2d4e694bd3aac643f-1-600x1195.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="797" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103350621" class="wp-caption-text">网页截图。</figcaption></figure>
<p>此事引发关注后，网友关心这个“精神病女”到底身在何方。有网友指事发河北保定，但也有网友说，河北保定是收养孩子的播主家住址，“精神病女”就是在<ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/徐州">徐州</a>。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103350622" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103350622" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/d2c96249fb293d2254111dc48d0cc6d5.jpg"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="wp-image-103350622" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/d2c96249fb293d2254111dc48d0cc6d5-600x220.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="147" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103350622" class="wp-caption-text">网页截图。</figcaption></figure>
<p>网传截图显示，有人询问了收养孩子的播主，确认“精神病女”确实是在徐州。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103350623" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103350623" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/a86ce9b98bceb5cbc5cf42b2954d181f.jpg"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="wp-image-103350623" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2022/02/a86ce9b98bceb5cbc5cf42b2954d181f-600x542.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="361" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103350623" class="wp-caption-text">网页截图。</figcaption></figure>

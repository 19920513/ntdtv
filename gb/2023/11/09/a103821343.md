<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>传兰州高校宿舍4女生深夜被掳走 校方封校消声</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id103821394-FotoJet-868x488.jpg" />
<h6>网传2023年10月，兰州现代职业学院四名女生深夜被人“偷走”。（网络图片合成）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【新唐人北京时间2023年11月10日讯】近日网传甘肃省<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/兰州现代职业学院.md#1">兰州现代职业学院</a>发生恐怖事件，四名女生深夜被人从宿舍掳走。此外还有女生在校外被奸杀。事发后官方压制消息，学校<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/封校.md#1">封校</a>一周。</p>
<p>日前，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/兰州现代职业学院.md#1">兰州现代职业学院</a>出事的消息在网上流传。根据网传学生群聊天截图，该校下令<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/封校.md#1">封校</a>一周，只进不出，学生有事出校门必须报学校批准。</p>
<p>关于封校原因，有人提到“现在四个姑娘还没找到”。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id103821344-001.jpg"><img decoding="async" fetchpriority="high" class="aligncenter wp-image-103821344" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id103821344-001.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="892" /></a></p>
<p><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id103821345-002.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="aligncenter wp-image-103821345" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id103821345-002.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="892" /></a></p>
<figure id="attachment_103821346" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103821346" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id103821346-003.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="wp-image-103821346" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id103821346-003.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="892" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103821346" class="wp-caption-text">（网页截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>还有群组消息显示，上月26日，校方通知学生晚上睡觉反锁宿舍门，半夜之后不要出门，有人敲门不要开门等。学生们看到通知非常害怕，有的说“晚上不敢上厕所了”。校方通知提到，该校卫生分院（指卫生健康学院）的2-1宿舍楼“有点事”。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id103821357-tongzhi.jpg"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="aligncenter wp-image-103821357" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id103821357-tongzhi.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="820" /></a></p>
<figure id="attachment_103821358" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103821358" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id103821358-tonzhi2.jpg"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="wp-image-103821358" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id103821358-tonzhi2.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="821" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103821358" class="wp-caption-text">（网页截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>根据网络消息，有两名男子趁2-1宿舍楼施工进入宿舍，藏在厕所中，深夜用药物迷晕四名女生，偷偷运走。</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id103821373-duandianjpg.jpg"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="aligncenter wp-image-103821373" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id103821373-duandianjpg.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="817" /></a></p>
<figure id="attachment_103821374" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103821374" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id103821374-duandian2.jpg"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="wp-image-103821374" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id103821374-duandian2.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="807" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103821374" class="wp-caption-text">（网页截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>此外，网上还有人传出照片并爆料，兰州现代职业学院还有一名女生在校外被奸杀。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id103821380-Untitled.jpg"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="aligncenter wp-image-103821380" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id103821380-Untitled-600x701.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="468" /></a></p>
<figure id="attachment_103821381" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103821381" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id103821381-Untitled-1.jpg"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="wp-image-103821381" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id103821381-Untitled-1-600x534.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="356" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103821381" class="wp-caption-text">（网页截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>还有多个爆料称，出事的不只是兰州现代职业学院，还有兰州石化职业技术大学。两个学校都在封校，压制消息。</p>

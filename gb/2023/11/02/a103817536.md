<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>招聘通知：新唐人电视台诚聘主播与主持</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id103817590-919Xu-ApIOL-868x488.jpg" />
<h6>（新唐人）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/新唐人.md#1">新唐人</a>北京时间2023年11月03日讯】<span style="font-weight: 400;"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/新唐人.md#1">新唐人</a>电视台是海外最大的中文电视新闻网，总部位于纽约，目前通过卫星、有线无线频道及各大网络平台，覆盖欧、美、澳、亚等地的上亿观众。</span></p>
<p><img decoding="async" fetchpriority="high" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-103817559" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id103817559-d39dde1f7a586bdc2c6745f8a2894c76.png" alt="" width="1208" b="552" /><img decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-103817560" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id103817560-feb73e2a716884fd33cda8ac9c474c55.png" alt="" width="1212" b="844" /><img decoding="async" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-103817561" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id103817561-ac72c3e5578facec0aa5f61fb8cf1a5f.png" alt="" width="1170" b="676" /><br />
<span style="font-weight: 400;">由于节目快速发展的需要，新唐人现诚招<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/主播.md#1">主播</a>及<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/主持.md#1">主持</a>人。</span></p>
<h2><span style="font-weight: 400;">一、新闻<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/主播.md#1">主播</a></span></h2>
<p><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-103817563" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id103817563-bf970c14b0623ab68b696e5b783804c1.png" alt="" width="1200" b="624" /></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">主播条件要求：</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">1、男女不限，女士年龄35岁以下，男士年龄40岁以下；条件优异可放宽；</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">1、新闻出镜<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/主持.md#1">主持</a>；</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">2、在播音时段以外，在记者、文编、剪辑工作中任择其一；</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">3、部门交办的其它工作；</span></p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/新唐人电视台.md#1">新唐人电视台</a>拥有多档大型新闻评论节目，如：<img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-103817565" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id103817565-ed5ba69aaabc7a5506593225d7995921.png" alt="" width="1212" b="634" /></span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-103817567" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id103817567-c8a7baf2a43c79f5b15de6e8c6da4880.png" alt="" width="1194" b="634" /></span></p>
<p><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-103817571" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id103817571-18f3a7eeca598a5468e059dcd8e90f73.png" alt="" width="1198" b="668" /></p>

<h3><span style="font-weight: 400;">节目主持人条件要求：</span></h3>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">1、男女不限，女士年龄40岁以下，男士年龄45岁以下；条件优异可放宽；</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">2、普通话标准、口齿清晰；</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">3、五官端正，形象大方；</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">4、抗压能力强，有团队合作精神；</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">5、有持续学习的意愿与能力；</span></p>
<h3><span style="font-weight: 400;">岗位职责：</span></h3>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">1、节目策划及出镜主持；</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">2、部门交办的其它工作；</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">申请工作请提交简历给：</span><ahref="mailto:jobs@ntdtv.com"><span style="font-weight: 400;">jobs@ntdtv.com</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;"> ；请提供个人简历及近照一份；如条件合适，我们将联系您进行面试。</span></p>

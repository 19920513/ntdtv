<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>全程直击：四川油罐车大爆炸监控画面曝光(组图)</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id103820919-1c6b098daa8eb7810736d03e5d2f67f1-868x488.png" />
<h6>2023年11月7日下午5点40分许，四川省雅安市一辆油罐车停放在休息区时发生大爆炸。（视频截图）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【新唐人北京时间2023年11月09日讯】当地时间11月7日晚间5点40分许，四川省雅安市一辆<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/油罐车.md#1">油罐车</a>停放在休息区时，发生气体外泄，随后发生大<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/爆炸.md#1">爆炸</a>，现场火光一片。已知此事故导致2人当场身亡，另有2人受伤送医。现场<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/监控画面.md#1">监控画面</a>看上去惊心动魄。</p>
<p>当前，互联网上已有两段关于四川雅案<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/油罐车.md#1">油罐车</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/爆炸.md#1">爆炸</a>事故现场的监控视频片段曝光。从其中一段监控视频画面中可看到，事发当时有2名男子在油罐车上工作，突然油罐车顶部四周缝隙处冒出大量白烟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103820908" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103820908" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" fetchpriority="high" class="size-full wp-image-103820908" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id103820908-ef783fea07aba3600ea9d077661503aa.png" alt="" width="600" b="380" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103820908" class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>两名男子刚开始时还试图堵住泄漏处，但白烟迅速扩张，大约1分多钟的时间，白色气体就把油罐车淹没。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103820910" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103820910" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-103820910" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id103820910-c31bfd9bf065dc7c3abeec2c36be6636.png" alt="" width="600" b="321" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103820910" class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>两名男子吓得赶快爬下油罐车，但随后油罐车就起火爆炸，瞬间将周围的树木都点燃。</p>
<p>另后一段监控视频是距事发地点有一段距离的位置上拍摄下的画面。显示在不远处的油罐车突然冒出大量白色烟雾后，有两个人影从烟雾中跑出。随后油罐车发生猛烈爆炸，瞬间火光冲天。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103820918" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103820918" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-103820918" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id103820918-134ad56213035ab6fa8f7298eb145d28.png" alt="" width="600" b="333" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103820918" class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_103820911" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103820911" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-full wp-image-103820911" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id103820911-74f5e459a0df8b85b8a1b3de0a64885d.png" alt="" width="600" b="373" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103820911" class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>刚刚逃离的两个人中，有一人在爆炸瞬间卧倒在地，另一人还在继续奔跑，但身上已被点燃。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103820915" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103820915" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-full wp-image-103820915" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id103820915-901fc1f1964cb72ace9721994731c96c.png" alt="" width="600" b="323" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103820915" class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>后者身上带着火苗跑出不远就倒在地上翻滚并扑灭身上的火焰。前一个卧倒在地的人稍后起身，跑向着火倒地的另一人，帮助他彻底熄灭火苗后将其扶起，两人走向远处更安全一些的地方。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103820917" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103820917" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-full wp-image-103820917" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id103820917-167e0b58b0f888ad8f554df09b7be863.png" alt="" width="600" b="354" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103820917" class="wp-caption-text">（视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>整个场面惊心动魄，尤其大爆炸发生的瞬间十分恐怖。</p>
<p>据陆媒报导，事后经查实，这次爆炸导致2人当场死亡，2人受伤送医，还有2辆车被波及损毁。至于导致气体泄漏并引发爆炸的详细原因，尚有待进一步的调查。</p>
<p>（记者竺颖综合报导/责任编辑：胡龙）</p>

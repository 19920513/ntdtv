<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>剥石榴有妙招 不费力不脏手 2分钟搞定</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id103828382-484f0971d05b87eafc644e3f-868x488.jpg" />
<h6>石榴酸甜可口全身都是宝，但是石榴好吃皮难剥。（作者提供）</h6>
<hr>
	<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/石榴.md#1">石榴</a>酸甜可口全身都是宝，但是石榴好吃皮难剥，很多人<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/剥石榴.md#1">剥石榴</a>，都是将外皮一点一点的掰掉，将里面的籽一点点的抠出来，这样剥<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/石榴.md#1">石榴</a>真是麻烦了，今天和大家分享剥石榴的小妙招，石榴籽轻松脱落，不费力不脏手，非常的实用。</p>
<p>1.首先把石榴外皮清洗干净，擦干水分，石榴放在案板上，石榴花瓣朝上，用小刀在上面画一个小正方形，不要切到果肉。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103828387" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103828387" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" fetchpriority="high" class="size-full wp-image-103828387" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id103828387-download-2.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="544" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103828387" class="wp-caption-text">石榴用清水洗净。（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_103828388" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103828388" style="width: 498px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-103828388" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id103828388-download-3.jpg" alt="" width="498" b="600" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103828388" class="wp-caption-text">在石榴顶部划一个正方形。（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2.将石榴上面的菊花掰掉，把这个小正方形打开，可以看到里面的结构，整个石榴分成几个区域，被一个一个筋膜隔开。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103828389" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103828389" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-103828389" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id103828389-download-4.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="656" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103828389" class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3.用小刀对着筋膜往下划，只把外皮割开就可以了，不要划到底，留两公分左右的位置。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103828390" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103828390" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-full wp-image-103828390" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id103828390-download-5.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="690" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103828390" class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4.轻轻的用手掰开，底部不要掰断，准备一个盘子，籽朝下，用汤匙，轻轻的拍打石榴外皮，果粒就会滑落到盘子里。只要2分钟，石榴的果粒就会全部脱落，而且不费力还不脏手。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103828391" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103828391" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-full wp-image-103828391" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id103828391-download-6.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103828391" class="wp-caption-text">石榴果肉。（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/石榴的功效.md#1">石榴的功效</a></h2>
<p>1.石榴汁富含单宁，具有抗动脉粥样硬化、抗衰老、强效抗氧化的特性。</p>
<p>2.石榴汁对心肌缺血和缺血再灌注损伤具有保护作用。</p>
<p>3.喝石榴汁可以减轻糖尿病患者的炎症。</p>
<p>4..石榴富含植物雌激素，有助于预防和调控女性绝经前后的围绝经期和更年期症状，比如预防骨质疏松。</p>
<p>5.石榴含有的化合物有助于清除导致口臭和蛀牙的微生物。</p>

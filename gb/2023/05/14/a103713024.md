<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>英超：曼城零封埃弗顿夺十一连胜 京多安传射</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id103713154-GettyImages-1490121783-800x450.jpg" />
<h6>2023年5月13日，2022-2023赛季英超联赛第36轮，卫冕冠军曼城队凭借德国球星京多安（左）的两射一传，客场3比0大胜埃弗顿队，联赛取得十一连胜，距联赛冠军仅咫尺之遥。(Clive Brunskill/Getty Images)</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【新唐人北京时间2023年05月14日讯】当地时间5月13日，<ahref=><a href="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/2022-2023赛季英超.md#1">2022-2023赛季英超</a>联赛第36轮展开争夺，卫冕冠军<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/曼城.md#1">曼城</a>队凭借德国球星<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/京多安.md#1">京多安</a>的两射一传，客场3比0大胜<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/埃弗顿.md#1">埃弗顿</a>队，联赛取得十一连胜，距离联赛冠军仅咫尺之遥。</p>
<p><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/2022/12/19/a103601784.md#1">2022-2023赛季英超积分榜及赛程赛果</a></p>
<p>本场比赛前，领头羊<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/曼城.md#1">曼城</a>队领先积分榜第二名的阿森纳队四分，争冠形势极为有利，而<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/埃弗顿.md#1">埃弗顿</a>队排在联赛第十七位，正在降级区边缘挣扎。</p>
<p>双方在本赛季的首场交锋中，“蓝月亮”主场被对手1比1逼平，让人有些意外，本轮两队再次交手，曼城队客场作战。</p>
<p>上半时第35分钟，曼城队后腰罗德里（Rodri）在禁区外一脚远射，对方门将侧扑将球得到。</p>
<p>第37分钟，曼城队边锋马赫雷斯（Riyad Mahrez）右路传中，后腰8号<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/京多安.md#1">京多安</a>（Ilkay Gündogan）背身大腿停球后，轻巧地用右脚背勾射，攻破了对方门将的十指关，打破场上僵局。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103713128" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id103713128-GettyImages-1490067005.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103713128" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id103713128-GettyImages-1490067005-600x391.jpg" alt="LIVERPOOL, ENGLAND - MAY 14: Ilkay Guendogan of Manchester City scores the team's first goal during the Premier League match between Everton FC and Manchester City at Goodison Park on May 14, 2023 in Liverpool, England. " width="600" b="391" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">第37分钟，曼城队后腰京多安接队友传中，轻巧地用右脚背勾射破门。(Clive Brunskill/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>第39分钟，曼城队左路快攻，京多安下底传中，中锋<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/哈兰德.md#1">哈兰德</a>（Erling Haaland）门前头球攻门得手，将比分扩大为2比0。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103713134" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id103713134-GettyImages-1254776377.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103713134" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id103713134-GettyImages-1254776377-600x400.jpg" alt="Manchester City's Norwegian striker Erling Haaland (R) heads the ball and scores his team's second goal during the English Premier League football match between Everton and Manchester City at Goodison Park in Liverpool, north west England on May 14, 2023. <p>上半时，曼城队两球领先进入休息室，双方下半时易边再战。</p>
<p>第51分钟，曼城队获得禁区前任意球机会，京多安主罚直接攻门，打入球门左上角，锁定胜局。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103713137" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id103713137-GettyImages-1490072169.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103713137" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id103713137-GettyImages-1490072169-600x400.jpg" alt="LIVERPOOL, ENGLAND - MAY 14: Jordan Pickford of Everton fails to save a free kick from Ilkay Guendogan of Manchester City (not pictured) which leads to their sides third goal during the Premier League match between Everton FC and Manchester City at Goodison Park on May 14, 2023 in Liverpool, England. " width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">第51分钟，曼城队后腰京多安主罚禁区前任意球直接攻门，打入球门左上角。(Clive Brunskill/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>第66分钟，埃弗顿队右侧开出角球，后卫塔尔科夫斯基（James Tarkowski）门前头球攻门，击中横梁弹出，险些破门。</p>
<p>第67分钟，埃弗顿依然是右侧开出角球，后卫耶里·米纳（Yerry Mina）抢到落点，顶出一记反弹球，高出横梁。</p>
<p>最终全场比赛结束，曼城队客场三球大捷，本场比赛独中两元并奉献助攻的京多安被球迷评为本场比赛最佳球员。</p>
<p>曼城首发（4-4-2）：</p>
<p>31-埃德森/ 25-阿坎吉，14-拉波尔特，3-鲁本·迪亚斯（90&#8217;↑21-塞尔吉奥·戈麦斯），2-沃克/47-福登，8-京多安（78&#8217;↑20-贝尔纳多·席尔瓦），16-罗德里（88&#8217;↑4-菲利普斯），26-马赫雷斯/9-哈兰德（78&#8217;↑10-格拉利什），19-阿尔瓦雷斯</p>
<figure id="attachment_103713152" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id103713152-4c32d868e37127bb5cd13751a24112a7.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103713152" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id103713152-4c32d868e37127bb5cd13751a24112a7-600x428.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="428" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">2023年5月14日，<ahref=><a href="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/2022-2023赛季英超.md#1">2022-2023赛季英超</a>联赛第36轮，曼城队客场3比0大胜埃弗顿队，全场比赛技术统计。（新唐人制图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>由于本轮阿森纳队失利，曼城队在少赛一场的情况下领先兵工厂四分，在剩下三轮联赛中只要再取两分就已基本锁定联赛冠军，以目前“蓝月亮”的状态犹如探囊取物一般容易。</p>

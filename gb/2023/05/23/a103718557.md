<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>夏天多喝三鲜豆腐汤 汤鲜味美又健康</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id103719904-IMG_2951-800x450.jpeg" />
<h6>三鲜白菜豆腐汤。（作者提供）</h6>
<hr>
	<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/豆腐.md#1">豆腐</a>含有多种营养物质，口感松软滑嫩，很多人天天吃都不腻，今日和大家分享养生健康又好喝<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/三鲜豆腐汤.md#1">三鲜豆腐汤</a>，做法简单，营养美味。一起来看看是怎么做的吧。</p>
<p>1.一块<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/豆腐.md#1">豆腐</a>改刀，切成小方块，放入盘中备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103719880" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103719880" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" fetchpriority="high" class="size-medium wp-image-103719880" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id103719880-IMG_2941-600x450.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103719880" class="wp-caption-text">豆腐切成块。（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2.准备适量的白菜撕成块装入碗中备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103719878" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103719878" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-103719878" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id103719878-IMG_2939-600x450.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103719878" class="wp-caption-text">白菜撕成块。（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3.把泡发好的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/木耳.md#1">木耳</a>切碎。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103719882" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103719882" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-103719882" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id103719882-IMG_2946-600x413.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="413" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103719882" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/木耳.md#1">木耳</a>切碎。（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3.生姜切成片，大葱切成片，放入盘中备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103719884" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103719884" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-medium wp-image-103719884" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id103719884-IMG_2944-600x450.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103719884" class="wp-caption-text">葱和姜（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5.碗中打入4个鸡蛋，不用搅散。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103719886" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103719886" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-medium wp-image-103719886" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id103719886-IMG_2932-600x450-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103719886" class="wp-caption-text">四个鸡蛋。（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6.起锅，加少许油，油热后倒入鸡蛋液，小火把鸡蛋煎至定型，煎至两面金黄，盛出来，改刀切成块，放入碗中备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103719896" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103719896" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-medium wp-image-103719896" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id103719896-IMG_2947-600x450.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103719896" class="wp-caption-text">鸡蛋不用打散，直接倒入锅中。（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>7.锅中加少许油，下入葱姜，炒香，下入鸡蛋，倒入多一些的开水，放入豆腐，煮10分钟左右，加入食盐、鸡精、胡椒粉和适量生抽，搅拌均匀，放入木耳、白菜煮熟，撒上葱花就可以装入大碗中了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103719903" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103719903" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-medium wp-image-103719903" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id103719903-IMG_2949-600x472.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="472" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103719903" class="wp-caption-text">三鲜白菜豆腐汤。（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这样一道美味的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/三鲜豆腐汤.md#1">三鲜豆腐汤</a>就做好了。</p>

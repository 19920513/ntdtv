<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>番茄和鸡蛋的新吃法 多汁嫩滑 太好吃了</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id103719095-IMG_2938-800x450.jpeg" />
<h6>番茄炸鸡蛋。（作者提供）</h6>
<hr>
	<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/番茄">番茄</a>炒蛋大家是不是都吃腻味了，今天和大家分享番茄炸<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/鸡蛋">鸡蛋</a>的新吃法，把鸡蛋倒入滚烫的油锅中，出锅变<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/美食">美食</a>，味道比番茄炒蛋还好吃。</p>
<p>1.准备适量的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/番茄">番茄</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/西红柿">西红柿</a>）和<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/鸡蛋">鸡蛋</a>。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103719090" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103719090" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" fetchpriority="high" class="size-medium wp-image-103719090" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id103719090-IMG_2930-600x450.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103719090" class="wp-caption-text">鸡蛋和蕃茄。（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2.鸡蛋打入碗中，加入一勺食盐，少许的胡椒粉去腥，搅拌均匀备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103719091" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103719091" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-103719091" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id103719091-IMG_2932-600x450.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103719091" class="wp-caption-text">鸡蛋打人碗中。（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3.番茄去皮，番茄底部插进一把叉子，放在火上烤一下，几秒钟番茄皮就会裂开，轻松取下番茄皮。然后，把番茄切成丁，放入盘中备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103719093" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103719093" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-103719093" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id103719093-IMG_2931-600x450.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103719093" class="wp-caption-text">番茄丁。（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4.生姜切成片，?小葱葱白切成段，葱叶切成葱花，放入盘中备用。</p>
<p>5.起锅点火，锅中加入多一些油，油温烧至放入筷子，快速出现小泡泡，鸡蛋液慢慢的倒入油锅中，不要一下全部倒入，转至中小火，慢慢的把鸡蛋液炸至定型，微黄后翻面，两面都炸至微黄，捞起来，控干油分放入盘中备用。</p>
<p>6.锅中留少许油，下入葱姜，炒出香味，把生姜夹出，倒入番茄丁，翻炒均匀，炒出汁水，这样香味更浓郁，加入适量的清水，加入一勺食盐，适量生抽，少许蚝油，少许胡椒粉，搅拌均匀，放入炸好的鸡蛋，小火烧开两分钟，鸡蛋充分吸收汤汁，撒上葱花出锅装盘。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103719094" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103719094" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-medium wp-image-103719094" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id103719094-IMG_2936-600x450.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103719094" class="wp-caption-text">番茄炸鸡蛋做好了。（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这样一道非常好吃的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/番茄炸蛋">番茄炸蛋</a>就做好了。</p>

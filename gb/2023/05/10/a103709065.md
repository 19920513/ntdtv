<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>欧冠：AC米兰0比2完败国米 遭遇黑色三分钟</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id103709276-GettyImages-1488931181-800x450.jpg" />
<h6>2023年5月10日，2022-2023赛季欧冠半决赛米兰德比战首回合，AC米兰队主场0比2完败国际米兰队，哲科和姆希塔良分别为蓝黑军团建功。(Alex Grimm/Getty Images)</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【新唐人北京时间2023年05月11日讯】当地时间5月10日，2022-2023赛季<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/欧冠半决赛.md#1">欧冠半决赛</a><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/米兰德比.md#1">米兰德比</a>战首回合，AC米兰队主场0比2完败<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/国际米兰.md#1">国际米兰</a>队，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/哲科.md#1">哲科</a>和<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/姆希塔良.md#1">姆希塔良</a>分别为蓝黑军团建功。</p>
<p>两队同为意甲劲旅，彼此非常熟悉。然而在欧冠赛场上两队交锋历史只有四次，红黑军团两胜两平，未尝败绩，占据心理优势。</p>
<p>然而几天前，红黑军团箭头人物莱奥（Rafael?Le?o）在意甲对阵拉齐奥的比赛中受伤，无缘本场比赛，对AC米兰队是致命打击，因此开赛前很多专家更看好国米。</p>
<p>上半时开场不久，蓝黑军团接连发起攻势。第8分钟，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/国际米兰.md#1">国际米兰</a>队中场核心恰尔汗奥卢（Hakan Calhanoglu）左路开出角球，中锋<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/哲科.md#1">哲科</a>（Edin Dzeko）门前先出一脚，打入球门右上角，先拔头筹。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103709173" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id103709173-GettyImages-1488928722.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103709173" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id103709173-GettyImages-1488928722-600x407.jpg" alt="MILAN, ITALY - MAY 10: Edin Dzeko of FC Internazionale scores the team's first goal whilst under pressure as Mike Maignan of AC Milan looks on during the UEFA Champions League semi-final first leg match between AC Milan and FC Internazionale at San Siro on May 10, 2023 in Milan, Italy. " width="600" b="407" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">第8分钟，国际米兰队中锋哲科门前先出一脚，打入球门右上角。(Alex Grimm/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>第11分钟，国际米兰队左前卫迪马尔科（Federico Dimarco）传中，中场球员<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/姆希塔良.md#1">姆希塔良</a>（Henrikh Mkhitaryan）高速插上接应，突入禁区至12码处，劲射破门。蓝黑军团三分钟内连入两球。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103709187" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id103709187-GettyImages-1488929181.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103709187" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id103709187-GettyImages-1488929181-600x400.jpg" alt="MILAN, ITALY - MAY 10: Henrikh Mkhitaryan of FC Internazionale scores the team's second goal whilst under pressure from Sandro Tonali of AC Milan during the UEFA Champions League semi-final first leg match between AC Milan and FC Internazionale at San Siro on May 10, 2023 in Milan, Italy. " width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">第11分钟，国际米兰队中场球员姆希塔良接队友传中，突入禁区至12码处，劲射破门。(Alex Grimm/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>第16分钟，国际米兰队依然是从左路传中，中场核心恰尔汗奥卢25米外大力远射，击中右侧门柱弹出。</p>
<p>随后蓝黑军团再从左路传中，中场球员尼科洛·巴雷拉（Nicolo Barella）远门柱扣球至中路，姆希塔良禁区内劲射，角度太正，被门将挡出。</p>
<p>第31分钟，国际米兰队前锋马丁内斯（Lautaro Martinez）在对方禁区内摔倒，主裁判判罚点球，但通过VAR确认后，又改判AC米兰队防守球员没有犯规。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103709215" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id103709215-GettyImages-1488933104.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103709215" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id103709215-GettyImages-1488933104-600x400.jpg" alt="MILAN, ITALY - MAY 10: Lautaro Martinez of FC Internazionale is tackled by Simon Kjaer of AC Milan, which results in a VAR Review for a potential Penalty incident, during the UEFA Champions League semi-final first leg match between AC Milan and FC Internazionale at San Siro on May 10, 2023 in Milan, Italy. " width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">第31分钟，国际米兰队前锋马丁内斯在对方禁区内摔倒，主裁判起初判罚点球，但看过VAR后又改判。(Marco Luzzani/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>上半时蓝黑军团攻势如潮，以两球领先进入中场休息，下半场双方易边再战。</p>
<p>第53分钟，国际米兰队后卫巴斯托尼（Alessandro Bastoni）禁区前直塞空档，队友哲科在中路低射，被出击门将挡出界外。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103709234" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id103709234-GettyImages-1488943180.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103709234" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id103709234-GettyImages-1488943180-600x400.jpg" alt="MILAN, ITALY - MAY 10: Edin Dzeko of FC Internazionale shoots whilst under pressure from Theo Hernandez of AC Milan as Mike Maignan of AC Milan attempts to make a save during the UEFA Champions League semi-final first leg match between AC Milan and FC Internazionale at San Siro on May 10, 2023 in Milan, Italy. " width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">第53分钟，国际米兰队前锋哲科在禁区中路低射，对方门将及时出击，将球封出界外。(Alex Grimm/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>第63分钟，AC米兰队左路传中，前锋吉鲁（Olivier Giroud）回敲，队友托纳利迎球怒射，击中左侧立柱弹出底线，蓝黑军团躲过一劫。</p>
<p>全场比赛结束，国际米兰队依靠闪电战，客场2比0完胜AC米兰队，打入第二球的中场悍将姆希塔良被欧足联技术观察小组评为本场比赛最佳球员。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103709257" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id103709257-GettyImages-1488956118.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103709257" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id103709257-GettyImages-1488956118-600x368.jpg" alt="MILAN, ITALY - MAY 10: Henrikh Mkhitaryan of FC Internazionale celebrates after scoring the team's second goal during the UEFA Champions League semi-final first leg match between AC Milan and FC Internazionale at San Siro on May 10, 2023 in Milan, Italy. " width="600" b="368" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">2023年5月10日，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/欧冠半决赛.md#1">欧冠半决赛</a>首回合，国际米兰队中场姆希塔良在对阵AC米兰队的比赛中攻入一球，被评为全场比赛最佳球员。(Clive Rose/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>他们认为，姆希塔良不仅打入一球，并且持续在中场表现强悍，他与恰尔汗奥卢在防守和进攻两端不断轮换。他的传球非常准确，阅读比赛的能力出色。</p>
<p>AC米兰队（4-2-3-1）：16-迈尼昂/19-特奥·埃尔南德斯，23-菲卡约·托莫里，24-克亚尔（59&#8217;↑28-马里克·蒂奥），2-卡拉布里亚（82&#8217;↑20-卡鲁鲁）/8-托纳利，33-克鲁尼奇/10-迪亚斯（82&#8217;↑32-波贝加），4-本纳塞尔（18&#8217;↑30-儒尼奥尔·梅西亚斯），56-萨勒马克尔斯（59&#8217;↑27-奥里吉）/9-吉鲁</p>
<p>国际米兰首发（3-5-2）：24-奥纳那/95-巴斯托尼，15-阿切尔比，36-达米安/32-迪马尔科（70&#8217;↑6-斯特凡·德弗里），23-尼科洛·巴雷拉，20-恰尔汗奥卢（78&#8217;↑5-罗伯特·加里亚尔蒂尼），22-姆希塔良（62&#8217;↑77-布罗佐维奇），2-邓弗里斯/9-哲科（70&#8217;↑90-卢卡库），10-劳塔罗·马丁内斯（78&#8217;↑11-华金·科雷亚）</p>
<figure id="attachment_103709266" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id103709266-649f6ffda14a501dccc2a3211b0278ff.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103709266" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id103709266-649f6ffda14a501dccc2a3211b0278ff-600x441.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="441" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">2023年5月10日，2022-2023年度欧冠半决赛首回合，AC米兰队主场0比2完败同城对手国际米兰队，全场比赛技术统计。（新唐人制图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>AC米兰队由于莱奥的缺阵，使得实力大打折扣，难以与国际米兰队抗衡。六天后的5月16日，双方将迎来欧冠半决赛第二回合的交锋，而国米带着两个净胜球坐镇主场迎战，晋级决赛极为有利。</p>

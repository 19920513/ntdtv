<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>欧冠半决赛：曼城4比0横扫卫冕冠军 B席独中两元</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id103715187-GettyImages-1255611062-800x450.jpg" />
<h6>当地时间5月17日，2022-2023赛季欧冠半决赛第二回合，曼城队主场4比0横扫卫冕冠军王家马德里队，从而以总比分5比1淘汰对手，第二次打入欧冠决赛，将与国际米兰队争夺冠军。(PAUL ELLIS/AFP via Getty Images)</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【新唐人北京时间2023年05月18日讯】当地时间5月17日，2022-2023赛季另一场<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/欧冠半决赛.md#1">欧冠半决赛</a>第二回合，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/曼城.md#1">曼城</a>队主场4比0横扫卫冕冠军王家马德里队，从而以总比分5比1淘汰对手，第二次打入欧冠决赛，将与国际米兰队争夺冠军。比赛中<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/b席.md#1">B席</a>独中两元，对方后卫<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/米利唐自摆乌龙.md#1">米利唐自摆乌龙</a>。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103715177" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id103715177-7fc65dd239edd45e74ec487aa214fe8f.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103715177" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id103715177-7fc65dd239edd45e74ec487aa214fe8f-600x347.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="347" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">2022-2023年度欧洲冠军杯淘汰赛赛程赛果。（新唐人制图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>上赛季<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/欧冠半决赛.md#1">欧冠半决赛</a>，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/曼城.md#1">曼城</a>队正是被王家马德里队挡在决赛之外，因此本场比赛，“蓝月亮”在主场憋足一口气要“复仇”对手，并从一开场就向对方禁区发动潮水般的攻势。</p>
<p>上半时第13分钟，曼城队左路传中，中锋哈兰德（Erling Haaland）高高跃起，头球攻门，打在门将身上弹出。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103715100" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id103715100-GettyImages-1255512640.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103715100" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id103715100-GettyImages-1255512640-600x390.jpg" alt="TOPSHOT - Real Madrid's Belgian goalkeeper Thibaut Courtois (L) saves a header from Manchester City's Norwegian striker Erling Haaland during the UEFA Champions League second leg semi-final football match between Manchester City and Real Madrid at the Etihad Stadium in Manchester, north west England, on May 17, 2023. (Photo by Paul ELLIS / AFP) " width="600" b="390" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">第13分钟，曼城队左路传中，中锋哈兰德高高跃起，头槌攻门，打在门将身上弹出。(PAUL ELLIS/AFP via Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>第21分钟，曼城队右路传中，后卫阿坎吉（Manuel Akanji）头球回摆，哈兰德头球攻门，被门将飞身用指尖扑出底线，险些破门。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103715110" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id103715110-GettyImages-1255512798.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103715110" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id103715110-GettyImages-1255512798-600x387.jpg" alt="TOPSHOT - Real Madrid's Belgian goalkeeper Thibaut Courtois saves a header from Manchester City's Norwegian striker Erling Haaland (R) during the UEFA Champions League second leg semi-final football match between Manchester City and Real Madrid at the Etihad Stadium in Manchester, north west England, on May 17, 2023. (Photo by Oli SCARFF / AFP) " width="600" b="387" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">第21分钟，曼城队中锋哈兰德头球攻门，被门将飞身用指尖扑出底线。(OLI SCARFF/AFP via Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>第23分钟，曼城队中场核心德布劳内（Kevin De Bruyne）右侧肋部直传禁区内的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/b席.md#1">B席</a>（Bernardo Silva），后者劲射近角破门得分。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103715158" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id103715158-GettyImages-1255512875.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103715158" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id103715158-GettyImages-1255512875-600x392.jpg" alt="Manchester City's Portuguese midfielder Bernardo Silva (C) scores the opening goal during the UEFA Champions League second leg semi-final football match between Manchester City and Real Madrid at the Etihad Stadium in Manchester, north west England, on May 17, 2023. (Photo by Paul ELLIS / AFP) " width="600" b="392" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">第23分钟，曼城队右边锋B席在禁区右路劲射近角破门得分。(PAUL ELLIS/AFP via Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>第35分钟，王家马德里队后腰克罗斯（Toni Kroos）禁区外25米处一脚远射击中横梁弹出底线，惊出曼城队一身冷汗。</p>
<p>第37分钟，曼城队左路短传渗透至禁区，京多安（Ilkay Gündogan）低射打在对方防守球员身上弹至点球点附近，B席头球补射再次攻破对方大门，扩大领先优势。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103715162" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id103715162-GettyImages-1255517998.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103715162" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id103715162-GettyImages-1255517998-600x335.jpg" alt="Manchester City's Portuguese midfielder Bernardo Silva (C) scores the team's second goal during the UEFA Champions League second leg semi-final football match between Manchester City and Real Madrid at the Etihad Stadium in Manchester, north west England, on May 17, 2023. (Photo by Oli SCARFF / AFP) " width="600" b="335" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">第37分钟，曼城队京多安低射被挡，20号B席在12码处头球补射破门。(OLI SCARFF/AFP via Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>上半时曼城队依靠B席的两粒进球2比0领先，下半时双方易边再战。</p>
<p>第51分钟， 王家马德里队后卫阿拉巴（David Alaba）主罚禁区前任意球，直接攻门被门将单掌托出横梁。</p>
<p>第73分钟，曼城队后腰京多安禁区内脚后跟妙传哈兰德，后者推射远角，打在门将腿上弹至横梁飞出界外，“银河战舰”逃过一劫。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103715165" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id103715165-GettyImages-1491070970.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103715165" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id103715165-GettyImages-1491070970-600x400.jpg" alt="MANCHESTER, ENGLAND - MAY 17: Erling Haaland of Manchester City hits the crossbar during the UEFA Champions League semi-final second leg match between Manchester City FC and Real Madrid at Etihad Stadium on May 17, 2023 in Manchester, England. " width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">第73分钟，曼城队哈兰德禁区左路推射远角，打在门将腿上弹至横梁飞出界外。(Clive Brunskill/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>第76分钟，曼城队主罚前场左路任意球45度角斜传禁区，对方后卫米利唐（éder Militao）误将球捅入自家大门。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103715166" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id103715166-GettyImages-1491073441.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103715166" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id103715166-GettyImages-1491073441-600x400.jpg" alt="MANCHESTER, ENGLAND - MAY 17: Eder Militao of Real Madrid scores an own goal, Manchester City's third goal during the UEFA Champions League semi-final second leg match between Manchester City FC and Real Madrid at Etihad Stadium on May 17, 2023 in Manchester, England. " width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">第76分钟，曼城队前场任意球45度角斜传禁区，对方后卫<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/米利唐自摆乌龙.md#1">米利唐自摆乌龙</a>。(Michael Regan/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>第83分钟，王家马德里队中锋本泽马（Karim Benzema）禁区中路的射门被门将飞身扑出。</p>
<p>伤停补时阶段，曼城队前场断球发动快攻，两位刚刚替补登场的球员完成配合，福登（Phil Foden）直塞禁区内的阿尔瓦雷斯（Julián álvarez），后者低射球门远角得分，将比分锁定为4比0。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103715168" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id103715168-GettyImages-1255600292.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103715168" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id103715168-GettyImages-1255600292-600x403.jpg" alt="TOPSHOT - Manchester City's Argentinian striker Julian Alvarez (R) scores the team's fourth goal past Real Madrid's Belgian goalkeeper Thibaut Courtois during the UEFA Champions League second leg semi-final football match between Manchester City and Real Madrid at the Etihad Stadium in Manchester, north west England, on May 17, 2023. (Photo by Oli SCARFF / AFP) " width="600" b="403" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">第91分钟，曼城队前锋阿尔瓦雷斯禁区中路低射破门。(OLI SCARFF/AFP via Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>最终全场比赛结束，曼城队横扫卫冕冠军晋级决赛，独中两元的B席被欧足联技术观察小组评为本场比赛最佳球员。他们认为，B席打入两粒重要的进球，表现出极大的创造力，并不知疲倦地在中场和边路向对手施压以夺回球权。</p>
<p>曼城首发（4-2-3-1）：31-埃德森/25-阿坎吉，3-迪亚斯，5-约翰·斯通斯，2-沃克/8-京多安（79&#8217;↑26-马赫雷斯），16-罗德里/10-格拉利什，17-德布劳内（84&#8217;↑47-福登），20-<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/贝尔纳多·席尔瓦.md#1">贝尔纳多·席尔瓦</a>/9-哈兰德（89&#8217;↑19-阿尔瓦雷斯）</p>
<p>王家马德里首发（4-3-3）：1-库尔图瓦/12-卡马文加（80&#8217;↑18-楚阿梅尼），4-阿拉巴，3-米利唐，2-卡瓦哈尔（80&#8217;↑卢卡斯·巴斯克斯）/10-摩德里奇（63&#8217;↑22-吕迪格），8-克罗斯（70&#8217;↑11-阿森西奥），15-巴尔韦德/20-维尼修斯，9-本泽马，21-罗德里戈（80&#8217;↑19-塞瓦略斯）</p>
<figure id="attachment_103715170" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id103715170-b1fc4190d22f1a2e4a6b2e0d0ab670b8.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103715170" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id103715170-b1fc4190d22f1a2e4a6b2e0d0ab670b8-600x441.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="441" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">2023年5月17日，2022-2023年度欧冠半决赛第二回合，曼城队主场4比0横扫王家马德里队，全场比赛技术统计。（新唐人制图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>本场比赛从起始到吹响终场哨，曼城队一直未曾间断地向对手施压，最终击溃卫冕冠军，取得一场伟大的胜利。目前距离球队的首个欧冠奖杯只有一步之遥。6月10日，“蓝月亮”将与国际米兰队决战伊斯坦布尔。</p>

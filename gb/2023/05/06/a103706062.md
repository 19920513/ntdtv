<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>英超：曼城2比1胜利兹夺十连胜 京多安双响</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id103706199-GettyImages-1487963462-800x450.jpg" />
<h6>2023年5月6日，2022-2023赛季英超第35轮，曼城主场2比1击败利兹联，中场球员京多安（右二）独中两元。(Gareth Copley/Getty Images)</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【新唐人北京时间2023年05月06日讯】当地时间5月6日，<ahref=><a href="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/2022-2023赛季英超.md#1">2022-2023赛季英超</a>联赛第35轮展开争夺，卫冕冠军<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/曼城.md#1">曼城</a>队凭借后腰<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/京多安.md#1">京多安</a>的两脚远射，主场2比1击败<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/利兹联.md#1">利兹联</a>队，继续稳固自己积分榜榜首的位置。</p>
<p><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/2022/12/19/a103601784.md#1">2022-2023赛季英超积分榜及赛程赛果</a></p>
<p>赛前，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/利兹联.md#1">利兹联</a>队为保级辞去了前主教练哈维·格拉西亚（Javi Gracia）的职务，并聘请素有“保级专家”之称的68岁老帅山姆·<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/阿勒代斯.md#1">阿勒代斯</a>（Sam Allardyce）“救火”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103706125" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id103706125-GettyImages-1487959448.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103706125" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id103706125-GettyImages-1487959448-600x400.jpg" alt="MANCHESTER, ENGLAND - MAY 06: Sam Allardyce, Manager of Leeds United, looks on during the Premier League match between Manchester City and Leeds United at Etihad Stadium on May 06, 2023 in Manchester, England. " width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">2023年5月3日，利兹联队官方宣布，山姆·<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/阿勒代斯.md#1">阿勒代斯</a>成为球队新主帅，助球队保级。(Shaun Botterill/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>山姆·阿勒代斯需要带队打四场联赛，分别是对阵<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/曼城.md#1">曼城</a>（客）、纽卡斯尔（主）、西汉姆联（客）和热刺（主），场场都是硬仗，而利兹联队目前排名第十七位，身处降级区边缘。本轮客场对阵曼城队的比赛是他执教的首秀。</p>
<p>而曼城队由于三天后将在欧冠比赛中客场挑战王家马德里队，因此主教练瓜迪奥拉（Josep Guardiola）对阵容进行了一些调整，雪藏了部分主力球员。</p>
<p>上半场第17分钟，曼城队边后卫里科·刘易斯（Rico Lewis）直塞禁区左路，中锋哈兰德（Erling Haaland）低射，被门将用身体挡出。</p>
<p>第19分钟，曼城队边锋马赫雷斯（Riyad Mahrez）右路横传，队友8号<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/京多安.md#1">京多安</a>（Ilkay Gündogan）在禁区中路低射右下角破门得分，将比分改写成1比0。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103706163" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id103706163-GettyImages-1487963180.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103706163" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id103706163-GettyImages-1487963180-600x407.jpg" alt="MANCHESTER, ENGLAND - MAY 06: Ilkay Guendogan of Manchester City celebrates after scoring their sides first goal during the Premier League match between Manchester City and Leeds United at Etihad Stadium on May 06, 2023 in Manchester, England. " width="600" b="407" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">第19分钟，曼城队后腰8号京多安在禁区中路低射右下角破门后庆祝进球。(Gareth Copley/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>第27分钟，曼城队故伎重演，马赫雷斯右路横传，京多安几乎是在刚才进球的相同位置，低射左下角得手，将比分扩大为2比0。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103706176" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id103706176-GettyImages-1487963248.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103706176" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id103706176-GettyImages-1487963248-600x369.jpg" alt="MANCHESTER, ENGLAND - MAY 06: Ilkay Guendogan of Manchester City scores their sides second goal during the Premier League match between Manchester City and Leeds United at Etihad Stadium on May 06, 2023 in Manchester, England. " width="600" b="369" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">第27分钟，曼城队后腰京多安在禁区弧顶处低射左下角破网。(Gareth Copley/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>第29分钟，利兹联队左路开出角球，中场球员韦斯特·麦肯尼（Weston McKennie）中路头球攻门，被门将双拳封出。</p>
<p>第32分钟，利兹联队门将摘高空球脱手，曼城队前锋阿尔瓦雷斯（Julián álvarez）得球后在禁区前沿轰门，被门将用身体拦截。</p>
<p>半场结束时，“蓝月亮”暂时以两球领先，下半场双方互换场地。</p>
<p>第62分钟，曼城队里科·刘易斯中路直塞给中锋哈兰德，后者在禁区弧顶处低射，击中右侧立柱弹出界外，利兹联队躲过一劫。</p>
<p>第83分钟，曼城队边锋福登从左侧肋部突入禁区，被对方后卫放倒，主裁判吹罚点球。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103706187" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id103706187-GettyImages-1252776282.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103706187" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id103706187-GettyImages-1252776282-600x400.jpg" alt="Manchester City's English midfielder Phil Foden (DOWN) fights for the ball with Leeds United's Dutch defender Pascal Struijk (C) during the English Premier League football match between Manchester City and Leeds United at the Etihad Stadium in Manchester, north west England, on May 6, 2023. <p>为了让京多安在本场比赛中上演帽子戏法，哈兰德让出罚球权，然而京多安的点球却击中右侧立柱弹出。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103706188" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id103706188-GettyImages-1487979249.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103706188" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id103706188-GettyImages-1487979249-600x390.jpg" alt="MANCHESTER, ENGLAND - MAY 06: Ilkay Guendogan of Manchester City misses their sides penalty during the Premier League match between Manchester City and Leeds United at Etihad Stadium on May 06, 2023 in Manchester, England. " width="600" b="390" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">第84分钟，曼城队获得点球机会，京多安的射门击中右侧立柱弹出。(Shaun Botterill/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>第85分钟，利兹联队替补登场的前锋罗德里戈（Rodrigo）在对方禁区前断球，低射破门，扳回一城。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103706196" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id103706196-GettyImages-1487979468.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103706196" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/05/id103706196-GettyImages-1487979468-600x410.jpg" alt="MANCHESTER, ENGLAND - MAY 06: Rodrigo Moreno of Leeds United scores their sides first goal during the Premier League match between Manchester City and Leeds United at Etihad Stadium on May 06, 2023 in Manchester, England. <p>全场比赛结束，最终曼城队以2比1赢得胜利，梅开二度的中场球员京多安被球迷评为本场比赛最佳球员。</p>
<p>曼城首发（4-4-2）：</p>
<p>31-埃德森/ 6-阿克（54&#8217;↑2-沃克），25-阿坎吉，14-拉波尔特，82-里科·刘易斯/47-福登，8-京多安（93&#8217;↑16-罗德里），17-德布劳内，26-马赫雷斯/9-哈兰德，19-阿尔瓦雷斯（87&#8217;↑20-贝尔纳多·席尔瓦）</p>

<p>“保级专家”山姆·阿勒代斯没能在曼城客场取得分数，利兹联队的保级之路愈发凶险。而曼城队此役获胜后已是联赛十连胜，巩固了自己在积分榜上的领先优势，继续排名榜首。</p>

<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>英超补赛：阿森纳四球大胜 马丁内利独中两元</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id103660271-GettyImages-1247591585-800x450.jpg" />
<h6>2023年3月1日，2022-2023赛季英超第7轮补赛，阿森纳主场4比0击败埃弗顿，萨卡传射，马丁内利（右二）梅开二度。(GLYN KIRK/AFP via Getty Images)</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【新唐人北京时间2023年03月02日讯】当地时间3月1日，2022-2023赛季英格兰足球超级联赛进行了第7轮的一场补赛，兵工厂<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/阿森纳队.md#1">阿森纳队</a>主场4比0大胜<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/埃弗顿队.md#1">埃弗顿队</a>，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/马丁内利.md#1">马丁内利</a>独中两元，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/萨卡.md#1">萨卡</a>传射。</p>
<p><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/2022/12/19/a103601784.md#1">2022-2023赛季英超积分榜及赛程赛果</a></p>
<p>此前两队在英超联赛中交手61次，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/阿森纳队.md#1">阿森纳队</a>35胜14平12负，占据上风。然而就在一个月前本赛季的首场交锋中，埃弗顿换帅如换刀，结果兵工厂一球败走古迪逊公园球场。此次二度交手，阿森纳坐镇主场希望一雪前耻。本场比赛是英超的第7轮补赛，由于英国女王去世而延期进行。</p>
<p>上半场第8分钟，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/埃弗顿队.md#1">埃弗顿队</a>后场断球发动快速反击，边锋麦克尼尔（Dwight McNeil）左路下底传中，中锋莫派（Neal Maupay）门前6码处脚后跟射门，没有打上力量，被对方门将没收。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103660259" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id103660259-GettyImages-1470578831.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103660259" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id103660259-GettyImages-1470578831-600x378.jpg" alt="LONDON, ENGLAND - MARCH 01: Neal Maupay of Everton has his saved by Aaron Ramsdale of Arsenal during the Premier League match between Arsenal FC and Everton FC at Emirates Stadium on March 01, 2023 in London, England. " width="600" b="378" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">第8分钟，埃弗顿队边锋麦克尼尔左路下底传中，中锋莫派门前6码处脚后跟射门，没有打上力量，被对方门将没收。(Julian Finney/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>第40分钟，阿森纳队右路直塞禁区肋部，边锋<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/萨卡.md#1">萨卡</a>（Bukayo Saka）顺势在小禁区边缘爆射球门右上角入网，为球队首开纪录。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103660260" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id103660260-GettyImages-1470581104.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103660260" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id103660260-GettyImages-1470581104-600x400.jpg" alt="LONDON, ENGLAND - MARCH 01: Bukayo Saka of Arsenal scores the team's first goal during the Premier League match between Arsenal FC and Everton FC at Emirates Stadium on March 01, 2023 in London, England. " width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">第40分钟，阿森纳队右路直塞禁区肋部，边锋萨卡顺势在小禁区边缘爆射球门右上角入网。(Clive Rose/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>上半场伤停补时，埃弗顿队后卫出球犹豫不决，被萨卡从背后抢断，随即主队前锋<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/马丁内利.md#1">马丁内利</a>（Gabriel Martinelli）带球进入禁区右路，单刀破门。阿森纳队2比0领先。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103660263" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id103660263-GettyImages-1470582449.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103660263" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id103660263-GettyImages-1470582449-600x388.jpg" alt="LONDON, ENGLAND - MARCH 01: Gabriel Martinelli of Arsenal reacts after scoring the team's second goal while waiting for a VAR decision during the Premier League match between Arsenal FC and Everton FC at Emirates Stadium on March 01, 2023 in London, England. " width="600" b="388" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">上半场补时第1分钟，前锋马丁内利利用对方后卫失误，断球后单刀破门。(Clive Rose/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>上半场最后阶段，兵工厂连入两球，下半场双方易边再战。</p>
<p>第57分钟，阿森纳队右路传中，前锋特罗萨德（Leandro Trossard）在远门柱凌空抽射踢空。</p>
<p>第62分钟，阿森纳队后场传球失误，埃弗顿边锋麦克尼尔带球长驱直入，在禁区外劲射远角，被门将倒地扑出。</p>
<p>第71分钟，阿森纳队发动快攻，特罗萨德左侧下底倒三角回传，中场大将厄德高（Martin ?degaard）包抄到位，轻松推射入网，阿森纳队将领先优势扩大到三球。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103660265" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id103660265-GettyImages-1470593040.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103660265" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id103660265-GettyImages-1470593040-600x400.jpg" alt="LONDON, ENGLAND - MARCH 01: Martin Odegaard of Arsenal acknowledges the fans following the team's victory in the Premier League match between Arsenal FC and Everton FC at Emirates Stadium on March 01, 2023 in London, England. " width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">第71分钟，阿森纳队左侧下底倒三角回传，厄德高中路包抄到位，轻松推射入网。(Julian Finney/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>第78分钟，阿森纳队中路过顶球传入禁区，刚刚替补登场的前锋恩凯蒂亚（Eddie Nketiah）反越位成功，但射门被及时出击的门将化解。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103660266" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id103660266-GettyImages-1470598072.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103660266" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id103660266-GettyImages-1470598072-600x350.jpg" alt="LONDON, ENGLAND - MARCH 01: Eddie Nketiah of Arsenal shoots as Jordan Pickford of Everton attempts to save during the Premier League match between Arsenal FC and Everton FC at Emirates Stadium on March 01, 2023 in London, England." width="600" b="350" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">第78分钟，阿森纳队中路过顶球传入禁区，前锋恩凯蒂亚反越位成功，但射门被及时出击的门将化解。(Julian Finney/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>第80分钟，阿森纳队从左路打穿对手防线，恩凯蒂亚下底回传，马丁内利抢前点捅射破门，阿森纳队锁定胜局。</p>

<p>第84分钟，阿森纳队右路传中，刚刚登场的中场球员法比奥·维埃拉（Fábio Vieira）在远门柱射门，被对方防守球员封堵，再吊射高出横梁。</p>
<p>伤停补时阶段，埃弗顿队右路反越位成功，替补登场的前锋格雷（Demarai Gray）右路小角度射门，被门将飞身扑出，中场球员汤姆·戴维斯（Tom Davies）跟上补射，再次被门将神勇化解。</p>

<p>最终全场比赛结束，阿森纳队4比0零封对手，本场比赛奉献一粒进球和一次助攻的边锋萨卡获得比赛最佳球员。</p>

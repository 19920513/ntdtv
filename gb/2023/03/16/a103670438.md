<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>欧冠：那不勒斯轻松晋级 历史首次进欧冠8强</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id103670615-GettyImages-1473779654-800x450.jpg" />
<h6>2023年3月15日，欧冠16强淘汰赛次回合，那不勒斯队主场3比0轻取法兰克福队，以两回合5比0的总比分历史性的首次打入欧冠8强。(Francesco Pecoraro/Getty Images)</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【新唐人北京时间2023年03月17日讯】当地时间3月15日，2022-2023年度欧冠16强赛次回合展开争夺，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/意甲.md#1">意甲</a>领头羊<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/那不勒斯队.md#1">那不勒斯队</a>3比0轻取德甲劲旅<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/法兰克福队.md#1">法兰克福队</a>，从而以两回合5比0的总比分晋级<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/欧冠8强.md#1">欧冠8强</a>，这也是球队历史上首次打入欧冠8强。</p>
<p>上半时刚开场，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/那不勒斯队.md#1">那不勒斯队</a>前锋波利塔诺（Politano）在禁区弧顶处突施冷箭，皮球直奔左下角，对方门将侧扑双掌将其击出。</p>
<p>第16分钟，那不勒斯队前锋奥西姆亨（Osimhen）在禁区左侧肋部低射远角，被门将得到。</p>
<p>第19分钟，那不勒斯队中场抢断，前锋克瓦拉茨克赫利亚（Kvaratskhelia）带球长驱直入至禁区右路底线，小角度射门被门将用身体挡出界外。</p>
<p>第43分钟，那不勒斯队中场球员齐林斯基（Piotr Zielinski）沿左路带球快速推进，直塞队友克瓦拉茨克赫利亚，后者在小禁区边缘的射门，被出击的门将挡出底线。</p>
<p>上半时伤停补时阶段，那不勒斯队右路下底传中，前锋奥西姆亨头球攻门，门将扑救不及，皮球飞入球门右上角。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103670596" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id103670596-GettyImages-1248352222.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103670596" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id103670596-GettyImages-1248352222-600x338.jpg" alt="TOPSHOT - Napoli's Nigerian forward Victor Osimhen (2ndL) shoots a header to open the scoring during the UEFA Champions League round of 16, second leg football match between SSC Napoli and Eintracht Frankfurt at the Diego-Maradona stadium in Naples on March 15, 2023. (Photo by Tiziana FABI / AFP) (Photo by TIZIANA FABI/AFP via Getty Images)" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">上半时伤停补时阶段，那不勒斯队右路下底传中，前锋奥西姆亨头球攻门，门将扑救不及，皮球飞入球门右上角。(TIZIANA FABI/AFP via Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>半场休息时，那不勒斯队1比0领先。下半场双方易边再战。</p>
<p>第53分钟，那不勒斯队右路横传禁区前，奥西姆亨门前铲射将球打进，帮助球队扩大领先优势。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103670603" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id103670603-GettyImages-1248352546.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103670603" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id103670603-GettyImages-1248352546-600x338.jpg" alt="Napoli's Nigerian forward Victor Osimhen (C) scores his side's second goal past Frankfurt's German goalkeeper Kevin Trapp (L) during the UEFA Champions League round of 16, second leg football match between SSC Napoli and Eintracht Frankfurt at the Diego-Maradona stadium in Naples on March 15, 2023. (Photo by Tiziana FABI / AFP) (Photo by TIZIANA FABI/AFP via Getty Images)" width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">第53分钟，那不勒斯队右路横传禁区前，奥西姆亨门前铲射将球打进。(TIZIANA FABI/AFP via Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>第61分钟，那不勒斯队左边锋克瓦拉茨克赫利亚在禁区内横向带球后兜射远角，被门将飞身扑出底线。</p>
<p>一分钟后，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/法兰克福队.md#1">法兰克福队</a>防守球员在禁区内将对方左前卫齐林斯基勾倒，主裁判判罚点球，齐林斯基亲自操刀主罚命中，锁定胜局。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103670606" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id103670606-GettyImages-1473778365.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103670606" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id103670606-GettyImages-1473778365-600x400.jpg" alt="NAPLES, ITALY - MARCH 15: Piotr Zielinski of SSC Napoli scores the team's third goal during the UEFA Champions League round of 16 leg two match between SSC Napoli and Eintracht Frankfurt at Stadio Diego Armando Maradona on March 15, 2023 in Naples, Italy. (Photo by Francesco Pecoraro/Getty Images)" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">第62分钟，法兰克福队防守球员在禁区内将对方中场球员齐林斯基勾倒，主裁判判罚点球，齐林斯基亲自操刀主罚命中。(Francesco Pecoraro/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>最终全场比赛结束，那不勒斯3比0轻取法兰克福，从而以两回合5比0的总比分晋级。独中两元的奥西姆亨被评为本场比赛最佳球员。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103670645" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id103670645-GettyImages-1473951408.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103670645" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id103670645-GettyImages-1473951408-600x400.jpg" alt="NAPLES, ITALY - MARCH 15: Victor Osimhen of SSC Napoli during the UEFA Champions League round of 16 leg two match between SSC Napoli and Eintracht Frankfurt at Stadio Diego Armando Maradona on March 15, 2023 in Naples, Italy. (Photo by Francesco Pecoraro/Getty Images)" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">2023年3月15日，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/欧冠16强淘汰赛.md#1">欧冠16强淘汰赛</a>第二回合，那不勒斯队的中锋奥西姆亨在对阵法兰克福队的比赛中梅开二度，被评为全场比赛最佳球员。(Francesco Pecoraro/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>那不勒斯首发（4-3-3）：1-梅里特/6-鲁伊，3-金玟哉（66&#8217;↑5-胡安），13-拉赫玛尼，22-迪洛伦佐/20-齐林斯基（74&#8217;↑91-恩东贝莱），68-罗伯特卡，99-扎姆博/77-克瓦拉茨克赫利亚（74&#8217;↑7-埃尔马斯），9-奥西姆亨（81&#8217;↑18-乔瓦尼·西蒙尼），21-波利塔诺（66&#8217;↑11-洛扎诺）</p>
<p>法兰克福首发（4-2-3-1）：1-特拉普/25-兰茨（67&#8217;↑32-菲利普·马克斯），2-恩迪卡，6-亚基奇，24-布塔/8-索乌，17-塞巴斯蒂安·罗德（74&#8217;↑6-克里斯蒂安·亚基奇）/15-镰田大地，27-格策，36-克瑙夫（62&#8217;↑11-法里德·阿里杜）/19-博雷</p>
<figure id="attachment_103670582" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id103670582-501acadf444a833b624b6406fcaefca9.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103670582" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id103670582-501acadf444a833b624b6406fcaefca9-600x434.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="434" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">2023年3月15日，2022-2023年度<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/欧冠16强淘汰赛.md#1">欧冠16强淘汰赛</a>第二回合，那不勒斯队主场3比0轻取法兰克福队，全场比赛技术统计。（新唐人制图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>本场比赛呈现一边倒，法兰克福队始终难以威胁到主队球门，在实力上确实与对手存在差距。因此最终被淘汰也是必然。</p>
<p>这是那不勒斯队历史上首次进军<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/欧冠8强.md#1">欧冠8强</a>，也是自2006年以来，3家意大利球队同时进入欧冠8强（另两支为米兰双雄）。</p>
<p>除三支<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/意甲.md#1">意甲</a>队伍外，英超占据两席（曼城和切尔西），德甲（拜仁）、葡超（本菲卡）和西甲（皇马）各占一席。而当地时间3月17日，欧足联将举行1/4决赛的抽签仪式。</p>

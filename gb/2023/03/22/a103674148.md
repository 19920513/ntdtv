<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>白菜烧豆腐 清香可口 这样做太好吃了</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id103674193-IMG_2556-800x450.jpeg" />
<h6>白菜烧豆腐，白菜烧豆腐 清香可口 这样做太好吃了。（作者提供）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>大鱼大肉吃多了，是不是吃<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/清淡.md#1">清淡</a>一点的食物呢，下面和大家分享一道清淡的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/美食.md#1">美食</a>——<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/白菜.md#1">白菜</a>烧<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/豆腐.md#1">豆腐</a>，清香可口太好吃了。</p>
<p>1.一块<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/豆腐.md#1">豆腐</a>改刀，切成片，再切成条，放人大碗中泡水去除豆腥味，10分钟后控水放人盆中备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103674188" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103674188" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id103674188-IMG_2550-600x450.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2.一棵大<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/白菜.md#1">白菜</a>根部切一下，掰开，用清水洗净。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103674189" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103674189" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id103674189-IMG_2549-600x450.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3.白菜横着切一刀，竖着切成条放人盆中备用。</p>
<p>4.胡萝卜切成条放人盆中备用。小辣椒切成段不喜欢吃辣的可以不放，放人盆中备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103674190" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103674190" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id103674190-IMG_2552-600x450.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5.准备少许的干辣椒，生姜切成丝备用。大葱切成段备用。</p>
<p>6.锅中加少许的食用油，撒少许的盐这样更入味，下入豆腐，小火慢煎至两面金黄，倒出控油。</p>
<p>7.锅中加少许油烧热，放入葱、姜和干辣椒炒香，炒香后倒入白菜和胡萝卜炒至断生。</p>
<p>8.开始调味，锅中加入盐，少许的鸡精，翻炒均匀，盖上盖子焖一会，下入豆腐，翻炒几下，让豆腐吸收汤汁。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103674192" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103674192" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/03/id103674192-IMG_2556-600x338.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这样<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/清淡.md#1">清淡</a>可口的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/白菜烧豆腐.md#1">白菜烧豆腐</a>做好了。营养丰富，鲜香入味，好吃下饭。</p>
<p>（责任编辑：嘉欣）</p>

<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>传商丘宁陵断网 14岁学生被曝哀嚎9小时活活疼死</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/12/id103837591-henan1-868x488.jpg" />
<h6>2023年12月27日和28日，河南商丘宁陵县大批民众聚集育华园学校，为惨死的学生讨公道。（视频截图）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【新唐人北京时间2024年01月01日讯】河南商丘宁陵县初中生被<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/虐杀">虐杀</a>，引发万人抗议。据传当地持续封城，网络也在很大程度上遭到屏蔽。网传更多细节，据称死者被多名老师折磨，哀嚎一夜惨死。</p>
<p>12月28日，网上传出大量视频显示，宁陵县育华园学校和县城爆发万人抗议，愤怒的民众与公安推撞，砸坏学校领导办公室，校领导被殴，一处官方机构遭冲击。抗议民众还游行到县政府，要求“杀人偿命”。</p>
<p>当天中共当局调拨大批特警赶赴宁陵县镇压抗议。视频显示，当晚特警和公安就已将涉事学校和县城封路戒严。据传当地调来的公安数以千计，被抓的抗议民众至少上百人。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103838915" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103838915" style="width: 598px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id103838915-GCsKWttbUAApYTl.jpg"><img decoding="async" fetchpriority="high" class=" wp-image-103838915" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id103838915-GCsKWttbUAApYTl-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="598" b="797" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103838915" class="wp-caption-text">网传中共当局调来大量公安镇压抗议民众。（网页截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>次日，封锁继续，据传村民再进城都需要登记身份证。</p>
<p>28日晚，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/商丘市">商丘市</a>当局就以“大雾”为由，封闭了全市的高速公路。当地民众透露，商丘的雾并不大，封路的真正原因，是商丘市及其它地区的民众正在开车赶赴宁陵，支援抗议。</p>
<p>直到31日，还有网民传出雾气中大批公安在宁陵县城街道上列队的照片，显示当地封锁仍在继续。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103838900" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103838900" style="width: 401px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id103838900-e9c9b808c5a2d561c5c110de416d96e7.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-103838900" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id103838900-e9c9b808c5a2d561c5c110de416d96e7.jpg" alt="" width="401" b="567" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103838900" class="wp-caption-text">中共公安在阴霾中的宁陵县街头列队。（网络图片）</figcaption></figure>
<p>自从29日后，网上少有当地视频流出。有当地网民爆料，“我们这里路封了，网停了”。有网民回复，“今天下午就刷不到几个（视频）了”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103838902" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103838902" style="width: 419px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id103838902-GCq-quqW8AAdqC9.jpg"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-103838902" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id103838902-GCq-quqW8AAdqC9-600x493.jpg" alt="" width="419" b="344" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103838902" class="wp-caption-text">网传宁陵县封网。（网络图片）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_103838903" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103838903" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id103838903-feng.jpg"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class=" wp-image-103838903" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id103838903-feng.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="616" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103838903" class="wp-caption-text">网民暗示宁陵县阴霾“阻断信号”。（网页截图）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>学生惨死黑幕</h2>
<p>12月24日，宁陵县育华园学校14岁杨姓学生的家长接到学校通知，赶到学校看到了孩子满身瘀伤的遗体，孩子新伤旧伤都有，手上有多个螺丝刀戳的洞，右脚脖子被扭断，露着骨头。家长不认可学校“跳楼自杀”的说法，开始连日维权。27日，当地官方强行定性“自杀”，引发民众大规模抗议。</p>
<p>日前网上传出更多来自涉事学校内部的爆料，其中透出的黑幕令人胆战心惊。</p>
<p>这些爆料的共同点是，死者是23日晚间在宿舍内被施虐，哀嚎多时才悲惨死去，同宿舍的其他学生被校方通知不准回宿舍，被迫在教室过夜。校方最后将死者从宿舍楼扔下，伪造“跳楼自杀”。</p>
<p>但在一些具体细节上，不同的爆料存在些许差异。</p>
<p>有学生称，死者是因为打架，被人在宿舍的厕所中打死，后来校长介入，开启宿舍楼上封闭的楼门，将死者从楼上扔下。家长见到死者时，遗体仍有余温，是因为死者一直包裹在被褥中。</p>
<p>但更多爆料指，死者是被几名教师<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/虐杀">虐杀</a>。该学生被打得全身多处致命伤，还被强迫留在宿舍。学校的学生们听到他哀嚎一夜，有人猜测他是活活疼死。校方第二天虽然将家长喊来，但一直不让见死者，直到死者咽气后，才让家长见遗体，所以家长见到的遗体还有余温。<ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id103838913-GCh3D6rW8AE5gMW.jpg"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="aligncenter wp-image-103838913" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id103838913-GCh3D6rW8AE5gMW.jpg" alt="" width="395" b="578" /></a></p>
<p><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id103838904-teng.jpg"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class=" wp-image-103838904 aligncenter" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id103838904-teng.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="513" /></a></p>
<p><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id103838907-c3646e1101124274b9c39982030e50df.jpg"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="aligncenter wp-image-103838907" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id103838907-c3646e1101124274b9c39982030e50df-600x867.jpg" alt="" width="398" b="575" /></a></p>
<figure id="attachment_103838905" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103838905" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id103838905-teng2.jpg"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="wp-image-103838905 " src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id103838905-teng2.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="472" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103838905" class="wp-caption-text">网传中学生惨死黑幕。（网页截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>还有未经证实的消息说，是因为该学生撞破两名教师的奸情，才被几名老师打死。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103838908" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103838908" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id103838908-qing.jpg"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class=" wp-image-103838908" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id103838908-qing.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="276" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103838908" class="wp-caption-text">网传案发是因为教师奸情被撞破。（网页截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>有网友爆料，事发后学校老师通知学生删除打人视频。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103838909" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103838909" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id103838909-bf85283034dcadad0d8f77ac3c08a6f9.jpg"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="wp-image-103838909" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id103838909-bf85283034dcadad0d8f77ac3c08a6f9-600x1003.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="669" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103838909" class="wp-caption-text">网传学习老师事后要求删除视频。（网页截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>有当地网民称，死者是个留守少年，父母在外打工，哥哥还是现役军人。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103838910" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103838910" style="width: 398px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id103838910-liushou.jpg"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="wp-image-103838910 " src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id103838910-liushou.jpg" alt="" width="398" b="389" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103838910" class="wp-caption-text">（网页截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>公开资料显示，涉事育华园学校是当地官方重点扶植的全封闭私立学校，此前就因为教师指使殴打学生激怒舆论，但也被官方包庇。有网友说，“能开私立学校的能是普通人吗”。</p>

<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>上海万圣节大火 民众变装讽时局 警方封路清场</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id103816362-Unknown-868x488.jpeg" />
<h6>上海青年人的万圣节造型引发外界关注和热议。（网络图/新唐人合成）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【新唐人北京时间2023年11月01日讯】连日来，中国“魔都”<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/上海.md#1">上海</a>的街头聚集了大量化妆游行的年轻人，有不少所扮形象疑讽刺时局，备受关注。31日，警方以“客流量过大”封路，宣布封路清场。</p>
<p>西方<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/万圣节.md#1">万圣节</a>前夕，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/上海.md#1">上海</a>迪士尼、巨鹿路、富民路、长乐路等地成了<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/变装.md#1">变装</a>（cosplay）的海洋。明星名人、蝙蝠侠、蔡英文、核酸检测者、打工人……各种角色纷纷登场。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103816310" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103816310" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" fetchpriority="high" class="size-medium wp-image-103816310" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id103816310-sh3-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103816310" class="wp-caption-text">（上海警方微博）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_103816308" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103816308" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-103816308" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id103816308-sh-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103816308" class="wp-caption-text">（上海警方微博）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_103816313" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103816313" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-103816313" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id103816313-15-600x385.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="385" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103816313" class="wp-caption-text">蝙蝠侠大热。（网络图 )</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_103816374" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103816374" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id103816374-F9tDP8EbUAACHzY.jpg"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="wp-image-103816374 size-medium" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id103816374-F9tDP8EbUAACHzY-600x959.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="959" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103816374" class="wp-caption-text">（网页截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>一些年轻人扮相影射了中国经济的现状，股市的一路下滑，找工的艰难，法律的不公。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103816320" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103816320" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-medium wp-image-103816320" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id103816320-ognguzo-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103816320" class="wp-caption-text">(新唐人合成)</figcaption></figure>
<p>过去3年痛苦的防疫经历，大白“做核酸”无疑成为<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/变装.md#1">变装</a>的一个主要对象，但有的很快被警察“光顾”。还有一人扮成“鼠鸭”给大家做核酸。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103816306" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103816306" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-medium wp-image-103816306" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id103816306-hesuan-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103816306" class="wp-caption-text">（新唐人合成）</figcaption></figure>
<p>人们通过举牌，变装进行各种讽刺：举着“虽远必诛”讽刺吴京；变身“摄像头”影射中国的“监控大国”；化身“程蝶衣”影射文革；“小熊”和“禁止倒流”则疑似影射<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/习近平.md#1">习近平</a>；还有人身贴多张白纸，影射“白纸运动”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103816337" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103816337" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-medium wp-image-103816337" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id103816337-fengci-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103816337" class="wp-caption-text">（新唐人合成）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_103816315" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103816315" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-medium wp-image-103816315" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id103816315-F9yAMEHXoAk_ck5-e1698776782961-600x380.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="380" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103816315" class="wp-caption-text">（网络图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>上海<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/万圣节.md#1">万圣节</a>最先引发关注是10月28日晚，有女子变装成丧礼奠祭花圈，旁边一男子则是化身成标语杆，上面还写着“我在上海，很想你死”。由于当时传出中共前总理<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/李克强.md#1">李克强</a>离奇去世，“很想你死”被认为有所影射。这两名变装者最后遭上海公安处罚，引起网友议论纷纷。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103816331" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103816331" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-medium wp-image-103816331" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id103816331-si-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103816331" class="wp-caption-text">（网络图/新唐人合成）</figcaption></figure>
<p>据大陆澎湃网31日晚报导，警方宣布“因客流量过大，上海市巨鹿路158号酒吧已不再接待客源，实施只出不进措施”。13号线淮海中路站临时封站。</p>
<p>视频显示，警方清场，包括“蜘蛛侠”扮演者在内的很多人被警方驱赶。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103816314" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103816314" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-medium wp-image-103816314" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/11/id103816314-banfu-600x378.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="378" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103816314" class="wp-caption-text">蝙蝠侠被清场。（网路图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>有人认为，上海的万圣节抓住了“变装”这一元素予以发挥，已和西方传统的万圣节相去甚远，被人戏称为“万梗节”。</p>
<p>相关话题在微博上引发很多热议：“自己的文化自己都不放开让年轻人创新发扬，一做点什么就辱这辱那…..逼着小盆友(朋友)崇洋媚外。”</p>
<p>“核酸检测”扮演者被警方盘问，引发不少网友嘲讽：“历史事实才过一年就不想承认了？自己都觉得不好意思了吧？”“过去三年祖国全力保护老百姓的工作怎么不能提了？”“怎么就成低俗内容了？过去三年岂不是全民集体低俗内容，造次，用词太不当了。”</p>
<p>有网友评论称：“上海万圣节，表面是一场狂欢，背后是人们心里的一块伤；只有在特定的日子里，才敢拿出来宣泄。”</p>
<p>（记者薛飞综合报导/责任编辑：云涛）</p>

<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>悼念李克强继续蔓延 传定远郑州均现花海（视频）</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id103814646-FotoJet-868x488.jpg" />
<h6>2023年10月28日，网传安徽定远李克强祖屋（左）和河南郑州千玺广场祭奠李克强的鲜花。（网络图片合成）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【新唐人北京时间2023年10月29日讯】中共前总理李克强猝死，引发诸多猜测。继李的合肥故居之后，网传他的老家安徽定远和工作过的河南郑州也出现悼念人潮。</p>
<p>10月28日，网上传出大量视频和照片显示，安徽省滁州市定远县九梓乡的李克强祖屋前摆满鲜花，前来送花拜祭的人依然络绎不绝。</p>
</p>
<p><a title="李克强安徽祖屋也出现悼念花海" src="https://www.ganjing.com/embed/1g8pec2jmj470Ata64XWvuiTn1i11c" width="640" b="360" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen"></a></p>
<p>
<p>目前没有消息显示地方当局阻止民众祭奠。但网络视频显示，茅屋的屋檐下疑似已安装了摄像头，被质疑意在监控和恐吓。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103814634" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103814634" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id103814634-974321c5b3efdac5f7d0285f6277842d.jpg"><img decoding="async" fetchpriority="high" class="wp-image-103814634" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id103814634-974321c5b3efdac5f7d0285f6277842d.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="607" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103814634" class="wp-caption-text">李克强定远祖屋前疑现监控镜头。（网络视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>28日晚，网上还传出照片显示，在河南省会郑州市的千玺广场，也出现祭奠人群，鲜花摆放地点和当地的地标建筑玉米楼隔水相望。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103814635" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103814635" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id103814635-F9hQJJCWIAAhhfF.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-103814635" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id103814635-F9hQJJCWIAAhhfF-600x453.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="453" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103814635" class="wp-caption-text">10月28日，网传郑州市千玺广场出现祭奠李克强的人流。（网络图片）</figcaption></figure>
<p>李克强被质疑在主政河南时，掩盖“血浆经济”导致的艾滋病大流行。有时评人士在海外社交媒体上表示，如今连郑州人都开始悼念李克强，可见民众对中共当局是多么不满。</p>
<p>许多自媒体人指出，李克强在任时只是说了几句实话，并没做什么实事，他实际上也做不了什么实事，现在民众悼念他，可能只是借机宣泄对中共当局的不满。还有人认为，也可能中共内部有人借机造势，试图推翻习近平。</p>
<p>网络消息指，李克强的合肥故居的悼念队伍已从红星路排到长江路主干道。当局派人监控，但尚未采取暴力行动。</p>
<p>另外，网传大量消息显示，许多学校和官方机构已通知，禁止参加悼念活动。外界猜测，当局害怕局面失控，出现类似胡耀邦去世引发的学潮。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103814637" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103814637" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id103814637-F9eQ6TLWsAAAhlf.jpg"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="wp-image-103814637" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id103814637-F9eQ6TLWsAAAhlf.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="260" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103814637" class="wp-caption-text">网传山东某高校通知。（网络图片）</figcaption></figure>
<p>与此同时，当局也在压制相关消息。在一些网络平台上，“给李克强故居送花”的信息也被通知“包含敏感词”，无法发送。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103814638" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103814638" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id103814638-F9gsL-OaYAAUkWj.jpg"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="wp-image-103814638" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id103814638-F9gsL-OaYAAUkWj-600x896.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="597" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103814638" class="wp-caption-text">（网页截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>不过，许多网民依然热烈讨论李克强猝死的种种疑点，质疑他可能是“被病死”。</p>
<p>在一则直播合肥民众悼念李克强的视频下，不少网民利用弹幕发表不满。据说出现敏感词后，直播被关闭。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id103814639-001jpg.jpg"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="aligncenter wp-image-103814639" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id103814639-001jpg-600x924.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="616" /></a></p>
<p><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id103814640-002.jpg"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="aligncenter wp-image-103814640" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id103814640-002-600x344.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="229" /></a></p>
<p><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id103814641-003.jpg"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="wp-image-103814641 aligncenter" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id103814641-003-600x309.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="206" /></a></p>
<figure id="attachment_103814642" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103814642" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id103814642-004.jpg"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="wp-image-103814642" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id103814642-004-600x328.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="219" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103814642" class="wp-caption-text">（网络视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>还有许多人涌入电视剧《非自然死亡》的评论区留言，暗讽李克强是“死于非命”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103814643" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103814643" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id103814643-F9fDXSMa8AAhvUa.jpg"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="wp-image-103814643" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id103814643-F9fDXSMa8AAhvUa-600x1124.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="749" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103814643" class="wp-caption-text">（网页截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>也有网友转发各种截图，隐晦地表达对“李克强死于心脏病”的质疑。</p>

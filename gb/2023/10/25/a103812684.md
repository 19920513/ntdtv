<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>葱油饼的做法 外酥里软 层层分明</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id103815704-IMG_3352-868x488.jpg" />
<h6>葱油饼的家常做法，简单美味，层层分明。（作者提供）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>今天和大家分享用烫面烙的葱油饼，做法简单，柔软层多、凉了也不硬。</p>
<p>1.首先准备500克普通面粉放入盆中，加入4克盐，增加筋性，加入8克白糖，糖可以使烙出来的饼颜色比较好看。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103815687" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103815687" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" fetchpriority="high" class="size-full wp-image-103815687" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id103815687-d64ba00ee3ff23117771941ae192d964.png" alt="" width="600" b="447" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103815687" class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2.盆中加入200克开水， 搅拌均匀，加入160克温水，搅拌均匀，揉成面团。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103815686" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103815686" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-103815686" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id103815686-a968831d39926a95b355e365aa596639.png" alt="" width="600" b="480" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103815686" class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>如果面团比较粘，可以手上适当沾点油，或醒几分钟再揉，就不会那么黏了，揉成柔软光滑的面团后，盖上盖子静置40-60分钟分钟。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103815698" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103815698" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-103815698" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id103815698-468738578012e6a9c5feb17d27cbc334.png" alt="" width="600" b="469" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103815698" class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3.做一个油酥，准备一个小碗，放入2汤匙面粉，两小匙油，搅拌均匀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103815695" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103815695" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-full wp-image-103815695" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id103815695-2a7a8235d4bc8a5c3d64127d5a5cb5c0.png" alt="" width="600" b="576" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103815695" class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4.一个小时后，放在案板上撒一点干粉，取出醒好的面团， 简单揉到光滑，搓成条状，分成6等分，整理成圆形。盖上保鲜膜。</p>
<p>5.准备葱花。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103815693" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103815693" style="width: 592px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-full wp-image-103815693" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id103815693-16df8bede3c0e6634233246b0590b2cb.png" alt="" width="592" b="522" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103815693" class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6.面饧好了，开始做饼。案板和擀面杖上都涂一点油。把面饼擀开，涂上油酥，撒上一把葱花，在面饼的中心处，向下开一个缝隙切开，然后从一头卷起，底部收口，把面团从外围压到中间就好，一个葱油饼坯就做好了。放到保鲜膜下面松弛15分钟。时间到，拿出来擀成薄饼。</p>
<p>7.起锅烧热，滴一点点油，放入饼，中火，饼面微微鼓起，刷上一些油，颜色变白，翻面，有一些焦黄点就可以出锅了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103815703" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103815703" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-full wp-image-103815703" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id103815703-aeb2f3efc9cb8bb6db2798a2fd8450f3.png" alt="" width="600" b="479" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103815703" class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这样简单葱香味十足的油饼就做好，外酥里软 层层分明。</p>

<h2>李鸿荣老师~葱香小排冬瓜球</h2>
<figure id="attachment_103803616" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103803616" style="width: 1200px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" fetchpriority="high" class="size-full wp-image-103803616" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id103803616-1.jpg" alt="" width="1200" b="675" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103803616" class="wp-caption-text">葱香小排冬瓜球的葱香酱也可以蒸鱼</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_103803617" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103803617" style="width: 1200px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-103803617" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id103803617-2.jpg" alt="" width="1200" b="675" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103803617" class="wp-caption-text">葱香小排冬瓜球 要蒸40分钟</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_103803618" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103803618" style="width: 1200px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-103803618" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id103803618-3.jpg" alt="" width="1200" b="675" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103803618" class="wp-caption-text">葱香小排冬瓜球 宴客大方</figcaption></figure>
<p>葱香酱：<br />
红葱头末210g、纯猪油90g、盐麹5g、酒酿15g、黑胡椒5g、酱油8g米酒适量、净水适量。<br />
主食材：<br />
猪小排900g、盐3g、白胡椒粉1g、肉桂粉1g、生抽10g、老抽3g、葱20g、姜10g。<br />
辅料：<br />
冬瓜球适量、鲜汤100g、生抽5g、老抽2g、米酒20g、沾粉300g。</p>
<p>做法 :<br />
1. 小排切段后不断冲水去腥味后沥干。<br />
2. 葱、姜加水或酒，拧净水分。再把所有材料放入小排内，再加入盐、甘糀、白胡椒粉、肉桂粉、酱油、老抽，腌渍半小时。<br />
3. 油温约150度，将红葱头末慢慢放入炸至金黄色，捞起沥油。<br />
4. 锅中加入猪油，放入炸过的红葱头末，加酱油、甘糀、酒酿、胡椒粉、水，煮开即成葱香酱。<br />
5. 小排沾地瓜粉，并让它反潮。<br />
6. 油温160度，放入已反潮的小排，炸上色即可捞起。<br />
7. 葱香酱放入老抽、生抽、米酒、鲜汤，再加入小排拌匀。<br />
8. 小排放另一碗里，加入冬瓜球，放入蒸笼蒸40分钟。<br />
9. 摆盘加些葱花，完成。</p>
<p>◎ Tips：<br />
“米麹甘酒”也称做为“甘糀”，是以米与米麹发酵，因为米麹的作用而产生由淀粉转化出的甘甜口感，不含酒精、无添加糖，又有维生素B、酵素，营养价值极高。</p>
<h2>黄经典老师~蜜糖杏仁</h2>
<figure id="attachment_103803619" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103803619" style="width: 1200px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-full wp-image-103803619" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id103803619-11.jpg" alt="" width="1200" b="675" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103803619" class="wp-caption-text">蜜糖杏仁 杏仁要先烤至金黄香脆</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_103803620" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103803620" style="width: 1200px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-full wp-image-103803620" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id103803620-22.jpg" alt="" width="1200" b="675" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103803620" class="wp-caption-text">蜜糖杏仁 以糖水不离火烹煮 杏仁才能均匀裹上糖水</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_103803621" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103803621" style="width: 1200px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-full wp-image-103803621" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id103803621-33.jpg" alt="" width="1200" b="675" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103803621" class="wp-caption-text">杏仁要边煮边拌至糖水焦糖化</figcaption></figure>
<p>材料：杏仁150g、水20g。<br />
调味料：细砂糖20g。</p>
<p>做法：<br />
1. 杏仁以180-190度烤约五分钟至金黄香脆备用。<br />
2. 锅内加入水、细砂糖，煮溶。<br />
3. 加入杏仁，边煮边拌至糖水焦糖化，并且均匀包裹在杏仁上。<br />
4. 离火。持续将杏仁拌开、放凉即完成。</p>
<p>※ Tips：<br />
1. 以糖水方式不离火烹煮，杏仁才能均匀裹上糖水。<br />
2. 焦糖化后必须拌至冷却，杏仁分开不沾黏在一起。</p>

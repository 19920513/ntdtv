<h2>第一道~洛神花蜜子排</h2>
<figure id="attachment_103797537" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103797537" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id103797537-1-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">洛神花蜜子排迸发好滋味</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_103797538" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103797538" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id103797538-2-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">洛神花蜜子排 风味独特</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_103797539" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103797539" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id103797539-3-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">洛神花蜜子排 酸甜滋味很够味</figcaption></figure>
<p>食材：排骨400克、鸡蛋1颗。</p>
<p>调味料：<br />
洛神花蜜饯30克、梅子醋100克、味醂30克、柴鱼酱油30克、玉米粉40克、卡士达粉20克。</p>
<p>做法：<br />
1. 猪肋排以清水不断冲洗血水，2支为一个单位横切，再放入鸡蛋、盐、玉米粉及卡士达粉抓腌约15分钟备用。<br />
2. 起一油锅，放入排骨将其炸至熟透，捞起沥油，再下去抢酥后捞起沥油备用。<br />
3. 熄火。锅中放入梅子醋、柴鱼酱油、味醂、洛神花蜜饯，开小火热煮。<br />
4. 加入排骨收汁，至汤汁浓稠收干即可起锅盛盘。上面可以再加些爆小米或爆玉米花、薏仁花。</p>
<h2>第二道~凉拌乌鱼子皮蛋</h2>
<figure id="attachment_103797540" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103797540" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id103797540-11-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">别有一番风味的凉拌乌鱼子皮蛋</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_103797541" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103797541" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id103797541-22-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">凉拌乌鱼子皮蛋 轻松上桌</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_103797542" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103797542" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id103797542-33-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">凉拌乌鱼子皮蛋 绝美滋味</figcaption></figure>
乌鱼子半副、皮蛋2颗、蒜苗半支、苹果1/4颗、蒜末1小匙、香菜少许、白芝麻少许、萝蔓叶适量。</p>
<p>调味料：香油少许、鸡粉少许。</p>
1. 先将乌鱼子炙烧或用高粱酒燃烧一下，让香气出来。切成细丁状。<br />
2. 皮蛋连壳下去先蒸熟再切丁。<br />
3. 大蒜先泡水再切成大蒜花，放入乌鱼子、皮蛋的碗内。<br />
4. 再加入切丁苹果、鸡粉、白芝麻、蒜末、香菜、香油搅拌均匀即可盛盘。<br />
5. 盘底可加生菜，再摆上凉拌乌鱼子皮蛋，最后撒上干燥辣椒丝。</p>
<h2>第三道~桂花炒秋蟹</h2>
<figure id="attachment_103797543" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103797543" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id103797543-111-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">桂花炒秋蟹 像桂花般的蛋花</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_103797544" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103797544" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id103797544-222-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">古早味台湾料理桂花炒秋蟹</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_103797545" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103797545" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id103797545-333-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">桂花炒秋蟹令人垂涎</figcaption></figure>
<p>食材：<br />
螃蟹2只约250克、咸蛋黄3颗、鸡蛋3颗、洋葱半颗、黄红椒各半颗、蒜末一小匙、葱1支。</p>
<p>调味料：盐5克、细砂糖30克、白醋40克、香油少许。</p>
<p>做法：<br />
1. 将活螃蟹先泡冰水，等它昏迷再处理成1开6份。再加入胡椒粉、盐、米酒、 玉米淀粉稍腌一下，炸时可再沾干粉炸熟。<br />
2. 生的咸蛋黄蒸熟磨成细沙，放入锅中炒到冒泡，加入黄红椒丝增色。<br />
3. 葱蒜爆香，加入糖、炸好的螃蟹、蛋液、白醋盛盘，上面再加些金沙（咸蛋黄）。<br />
4. 鸡蛋打好备用，洋葱、黄红椒切备用。<br />
5. 锅中放一匙油，放入炒至冒泡后放入，并依序加入盐、糖、醋以及鸡蛋拌炒，最后加入香油稍作翻炒即可出锅盛盘。</p>

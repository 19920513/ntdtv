<h2>◎赖学城~古早味煎猪肝</h2>
<figure id="attachment_103806647" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103806647" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" fetchpriority="high" class="size-full wp-image-103806647" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id103806647-1.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103806647" class="wp-caption-text">古早味煎猪肝 热锅冷油</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_103806648" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103806648" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-103806648" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id103806648-2.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103806648" class="wp-caption-text">经典台菜 古早味煎猪肝</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_103806650" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103806650" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-103806650" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id103806650-3.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103806650" class="wp-caption-text">古早味煎猪肝 手法简单</figcaption></figure>
猪肝300g、白芝麻少许、香菜数叶。<br />
调味料：<br />
酱油40g、糖20g、米酒5g、番茄酱10g、胡椒粉少许、太白粉少许、香油少许。</p>
1. 切好片的猪肝，加米酒及撒上胡椒粉、太白粉抓匀，再加些香油腌制。<br />
2. 热锅冷油将猪肝炸熟，捞起沥油备用。<br />
3. 原锅小火将调料酱油、糖、番茄酱煮滚收汁，加入猪肝拌匀。<br />
4. 撒上白芝麻、香菜点缀后完成。</p>
<h2>◎谢铭哲~菌菇无骨牛小排</h2>
<figure id="attachment_103806651" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103806651" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-full wp-image-103806651" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id103806651-11.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103806651" class="wp-caption-text">香醇风味 菌菇无骨牛小排</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_103806652" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103806652" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-full wp-image-103806652" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id103806652-22.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103806652" class="wp-caption-text">菌菇无骨牛小排 细嫩口感</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_103806653" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103806653" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-full wp-image-103806653" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/10/id103806653-33.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103806653" class="wp-caption-text">菌菇无骨牛小排鲜嫩多汁</figcaption></figure>
<p>食材：<br />
无骨牛小排180g、洋菇 (开1/4) 80g、鸿喜菇30g、精灵菇30g、白酒50g、奶油30g、义式综合香料2g、盐少许、黑胡椒粒少许。</p>
<p>做法：<br />
1. 将菇类处理好切片或切段。<br />
2. 将牛小排切块，撒上盐、黑胡椒粒。取平底不沾锅煎香备用 (表面上色)。<br />
3. 另一锅开小火加入油、奶油，让奶油溶化。<br />
4. 炒香洋菇、鸿喜菇、精灵菇，再加入洋葱继续炒香，再加入蒜头、意大利综合香料炒香。<br />
5. 加入牛小排及肉汁，火开大浓缩牛肉汁，倒入白酒提味浓缩。<br />
6. 加入盐巴、胡椒调味，再放入一块奶油增香及滑顺感。熬煮熟透即上盘装饰。<br />
7. 锅底再用白酒炝烧做成酱汁，离火，并陆续加入一块块的奶油，让它们慢慢乳化，再将酱汁放在牛小排上，旁边再加些小番茄、葱花。完成。</p>

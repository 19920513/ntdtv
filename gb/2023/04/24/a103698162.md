<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>解冻大虾不能用水泡 1妙招5分钟快速解冻</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103698764-545fd6f3ca03a8c4a351d9de6cfb9d53-800x450.jpg" />
<h6>大虾是补钙非常强的一种食物。（新唐人）</h6>
<hr>
	<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/大虾.md#1">大虾</a>不仅口感鲜美，而且虾中含有20%的蛋白质，是蛋白质含量很高的食品之一，大虾还是有效补钙的一种食物。一般家庭为了方便，冰箱中都会储存一些冷冻的大虾，今天分享快速解冻大虾的方法，解冻后的大虾几乎和新鲜的一样。</p>
<h2><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/大虾.md#1">大虾</a>快速解冻法</h2>
<p>从冰箱拿出来的冻虾，有一层厚厚的冰霜，不要直接加水，而是撒上一层薄薄的食用盐，?等待几分钟，时间过了3-4分钟，大虾的冰霜就不见了，大虾和新鲜的几乎一样。</p>
<p>冰冻的海鲜如果直接放到水中浸泡，解冻速度慢，时间久了还会导致营养流失，影响口感。</p>
<p>食盐不仅有杀菌消毒作用，还有去除腥味的功效。</p>
<h2>【罗厨寻味】<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/黄金酥大虾.md#1">黄金酥大虾</a></h2>
<p>下面和大家分享，北京名厨罗子昭“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/黄金酥大虾.md#1">黄金酥大虾</a>”的具体做法。</p>
<p>罗子昭大厨，2011年，在新唐人电视台主办的第四届全世界中国菜厨技大赛上，摘得粤菜金奖。</p>
<p>主料：明虾6个</p>
<p>配料：葱粒、香菜粒、炸面碎</p>
<p>调料：胡椒盐、生粉</p>
<p>做法：</p>
<p>1. 先将大明虾开背。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103698770" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-full wp-image-103698770" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103698770-1-16-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2. 去明虾沙肠后冲洗干净。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103698771" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-full wp-image-103698771" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103698771-2-18-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3. 在明虾的背面撒上一点椒盐做底味。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103698773" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-full wp-image-103698773" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103698773-3-13-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4. 再撒上一层比较厚的生粉。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103698769" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-full wp-image-103698769" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103698769-4-10-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5. 用约180度的油温把明虾炸定型。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103698775" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-full wp-image-103698775" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103698775-5-10-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6. 将明虾里的油份吸出来。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103698777" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-full wp-image-103698777" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103698777-6-8-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>7. 放入香菜粒、葱粒炒香。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103698778" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-full wp-image-103698778" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103698778-7-9-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>8. 再放炸面碎。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103698779" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-full wp-image-103698779" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103698779-6-8-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>9. 放入炸好的明虾。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103698780" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-full wp-image-103698780" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103698780-9-6-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>10. 将面碎拌明虾炒匀。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103698781" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-full wp-image-103698781" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103698781-10-6-450x276.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="276" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>11. 将明虾一个一个取出摆盘。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103698782" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-full wp-image-103698782" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103698782-11-50-450x253.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="253" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（大纪元）</figcaption></figure>

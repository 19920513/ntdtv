<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>英超：利物浦客场2比1逆转西汉姆 联赛三连胜</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103699941-GettyImages-1252207195-800x450.jpg" />
<h6>4月26日，2022-2023赛季英超联赛第33轮展开激战，“红军”利物浦队客场2比1逆转西汉姆联队，取得联赛三连胜后，升至积分榜第六位。(BEN STANSALL/AFP via Getty Images)</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【新唐人北京时间2023年04月27日讯】当地时间4月26日，<ahref=><a href="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/2022-2023赛季英超.md#1">2022-2023赛季英超</a>联赛第33轮展开激战，“红军”<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/利物浦队.md#1">利物浦队</a>客场2比1逆转<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/西汉姆联队.md#1">西汉姆联队</a>，前锋<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/加克波.md#1">加克波</a>破门。球队取得联赛<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/三连胜.md#1">三连胜</a>后，升至积分榜第六位。</p>
<p><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/2022/12/19/a103601784.md#1">2022-2023赛季英超积分榜及赛程赛果</a></p>
<p>此前双方在英超历史上对垒53次，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/利物浦队.md#1">利物浦队</a>33胜10平10负，占据绝对优势。本赛季第12轮的首场交锋中，“红军”主场1比0小胜对手，此番二度交手，客场作战。</p>
<p>上半时第12分钟，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/西汉姆联队.md#1">西汉姆联队</a>影锋卢卡斯·帕奎塔（Lucas Paquetá）和队友安东尼奥（Michail Antonio）连续在左路做撞墙式配合后，横向带球至禁区外中路，突施冷箭打入球门左上角，率先打破场上僵局。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103699888" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103699888-GettyImages-1485493757.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103699888" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103699888-GettyImages-1485493757-600x400.jpg" alt="LONDON, ENGLAND - APRIL 26: Lucas Paqueta of West Ham United scores the team's first goal during the Premier League match between West Ham United and Liverpool FC at London Stadium on April 26, 2023 in London, England. " width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">第12分钟，西汉姆联队影锋卢卡斯·帕奎塔在禁区外突施冷箭，打入球门左上角。(Julian Finney/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>第18分钟，利物浦队还以颜色。后卫阿诺德（Trent Alexander-Arnold）直传给锋线杀手<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/加克波.md#1">加克波</a>（Cody Gakpo），后者“弯弓搭箭”，距离球门约30码处劲射，皮球击中左侧立柱内侧弹入网内，帮助球队扳平比分。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103699898" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103699898-GettyImages-1485495303.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103699898" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103699898-GettyImages-1485495303-600x400.jpg" alt="LONDON, ENGLAND - APRIL 26: Cody Gakpo of Liverpool celebrates after scoring the team's first goal with teammate Curtis Jones during the Premier League match between West Ham United and Liverpool FC at London Stadium on April 26, 2023 in London, England. " width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">第18分钟，利物浦队前锋加克波（左）禁区外远射破门后，与队友庆祝进球。(Justin Setterfield/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>第27分钟，利物浦队在对方禁区内制造混乱，前锋若塔（Diogo Jota）乱军之中凌空扫射，稍稍高出横梁。</p>
<p>第38分钟，利物浦队右路传中，中场亨德森（Jordan Henderson）前点头球摆渡，若塔门前抢点甩头攻门，稍稍偏出左侧门柱。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103699915" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103699915-GettyImages-1485546926.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103699915" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103699915-GettyImages-1485546926-600x400.jpg" alt="LONDON, ENGLAND - APRIL 26: Diogo Jota of Liverpool misses a chance as Lukasz Fabianski of West Ham United looks on during the Premier League match between West Ham United and Liverpool FC at London Stadium on April 26, 2023 in London, England. " width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">第38分钟，利物浦队前锋若塔门前甩头攻门，稍稍偏出左侧门柱。(Justin Setterfield/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>上半场双方战成1比1平，下半时易边再战。</p>
<p>第55分钟，西汉姆联队右路肋部直塞撕开对手防线，边锋杰罗德·鲍文（Jarrod Bowen）曾打入一粒进球，但是经过VAR确认，稍稍越位一个脚掌，被判进球无效。</p>
<p>第67分钟，利物浦队左后卫罗伯逊（Andrew Robertson）开出左侧角球，中后卫马蒂普（Jo?l Matip）禁区内头槌破门，将比分反超为2比1。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103699925" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103699925-GettyImages-1485510237.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103699925" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103699925-GettyImages-1485510237-600x400.jpg" alt="LONDON, ENGLAND - APRIL 26: Joel Matip of Liverpool scores the team's second goal whilst under pressure from Declan Rice of West Ham United during the Premier League match between West Ham United and Liverpool FC at London Stadium on April 26, 2023 in London, England. " width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">第67分钟，利物浦队左侧开出角球，中后卫马蒂普禁区内头槌破门。(Julian Finney/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>最终裁判员吹响全场比赛结束哨音，利物浦队客场2比1逆转西汉姆联队，为球队首开纪录的前锋加克波，被球迷评为本场比赛最佳球员。</p>
<p>利物浦首发（4-3-3）：1-阿利松/26-罗伯逊，4-&#33539;戴克、32-马蒂普，66-阿诺德/17-柯蒂斯·琼斯（84&#8217;↑7-米尔纳），3-法比尼奥，14-亨德森（59&#8217;↑6-蒂亚戈）/20-若塔（59&#8217;↑23-路易斯·迪亚斯），18-加克波（78&#8217;↑27-努涅斯），11-萨拉赫</p>
<figure id="attachment_103699935" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103699935-999474f9ce71bd16450e9bde6cbd0516.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103699935" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103699935-999474f9ce71bd16450e9bde6cbd0516-600x441.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="441" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">2023年4月26日，<ahref=><a href="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/2022-2023赛季英超.md#1">2022-2023赛季英超</a>联赛第33轮，利物浦队客场2比1力克西汉姆队的全场比赛技术统计。（新唐人制图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>本场比赛获胜后，利物浦队32战积53分，以净胜球的优势压倒热刺队，升至积分榜第六位。但如若要获得下赛季欧冠席位（联赛前四名），球队则还需继续保持在联赛中的连胜势头，同时还要寄希望于排在积分榜第三和第四位的纽卡斯尔联和曼联两队不断犯错，恐怕很有难度，然而利物浦队极有希望获得下赛季欧联杯席位，征战欧洲赛场。</p>

<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>英超：切尔西主场0比2连败 兰帕德率队一胜难求</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103700268-GettyImages-1485515386-800x450.jpg" />
<h6>2023年4月26日，2022-2023赛季英超联赛第33轮，切尔西主场0比2不敌布伦特福德，主教练兰帕德（左）自上任以来，遭遇各项赛事五连败。(Clive Rose/Getty Images)</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【新唐人北京时间2023年04月27日讯】当地时间4月26日，<ahref=><a href="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/2022-2023赛季英超.md#1">2022-2023赛季英超</a>联赛第33轮展开争夺，“蓝军”<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/切尔西队.md#1">切尔西队</a>主场0比2不敌<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/布伦特福德队.md#1">布伦特福德队</a>，主教练<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/兰帕德.md#1">兰帕德</a>自上任以来各项赛事遭遇五连败，一胜难求。</p>
<p><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/2022/12/19/a103601784.md#1">2022-2023赛季英超积分榜及赛程赛果</a></p>
<p>此前两队在英超历史上只交手过三次，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/切尔西队.md#1">切尔西队</a>1胜1平1负，战绩旗鼓相当。本赛季双方的首场交锋中，“蓝军”在客场与对手互交白卷，本轮比赛坐镇主场迎战。</p>
<p>上半时第31分钟，切尔西队从右路攻入对方禁区，但中场球员坎特（N&#8217;Golo Kanté）没有起脚机会，于是回敲给队友恩佐·费尔南德斯（Enzo Fernández），后者在大禁区角上起脚远射，被门将托出横梁。</p>
<p>第37分钟，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/布伦特福德队.md#1">布伦特福德队</a>左路开出角球，中后卫约根森（Mathias Zanka Jorgensen）前点后蹭，皮球碰到对方后卫28号<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/阿斯皮利奎塔.md#1">阿斯皮利奎塔</a>（César Azpilicueta）的肩膀，弹入网内，主队自摆乌龙，客队幸运取得领先。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103700316" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103700316-GettyImages-1485499699.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103700316" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103700316-GettyImages-1485499699-600x400.jpg" alt="LONDON, ENGLAND - APRIL 26: Kepa Arrizabalaga looks on as Cesar Azpilicueta of Chelsea concedes an own goal, the first goal for Brentford, during the Premier League match between Chelsea FC and Brentford FC at Stamford Bridge on April 26, 2023 in London, England. " width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">第37分钟，布伦特福德队左路开出角球，中后卫约根森前点后蹭，皮球碰到对方后卫28号<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/阿斯皮利奎塔.md#1">阿斯皮利奎塔</a>的肩膀，弹入球网。(Clive Rose/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>半场结束时，切尔西队暂时以0比1落后，下半时双方易边再战。</p>
<p>第55分钟，切尔西队左路45度角斜传禁区，前锋斯特林（Raheem Sterling）后点头球回顶至门前，队友奥巴梅扬（Pierre-Emerick Aubameyang）抢在门将之前头球攻门，稍稍高出横梁。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103700343" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103700343-GettyImages-1485508415.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103700343" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103700343-GettyImages-1485508415-600x396.jpg" alt="LONDON, ENGLAND - APRIL 26: David Raya of Brentford is beaten to the ball as Pierre-Emerick Aubameyang of Chelsea has a headed shot which goes over the crossbar during the Premier League match between Chelsea FC and Brentford FC at Stamford Bridge on April 26, 2023 in London, England. " width="600" b="396" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">第55分钟，切尔西队前锋奥巴梅扬接到队友头球摆渡，抢在门将之前头球攻门，稍稍高出横梁。(Mike Hewitt/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>第57分钟，切尔西队获得前场左侧任意球，45度角斜传禁区，后卫蒂亚戈·席尔瓦前点甩头攻门，稍稍偏出远门柱。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103700358" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103700358-GettyImages-1485508514.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103700358" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103700358-GettyImages-1485508514-600x386.jpg" alt="LONDON, ENGLAND - APRIL 26: Thiago Silva of Chelsea contends for the aerial ball with Ivan Toney of Brentford during the Premier League match between Chelsea FC and Brentford FC at Stamford Bridge on April 26, 2023 in London, England. " width="600" b="386" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">第57分钟，切尔西队前场左路任意球传至禁区，蒂亚戈·席尔瓦前点头球攻门偏出右侧门柱。(Mike Hewitt/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>第78分钟，布伦特福德队两位替补登场的球员形成配合，前卫巴普蒂斯特（Shandon Baptiste）右路直塞，队友布莱扬·姆布莫（Bryan Mbeumo）带球突入禁区后射门，打在对方防守球员腿上折射入网，客队锁定胜局。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103700373" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103700373-GettyImages-1485512752.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103700373" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103700373-GettyImages-1485512752-600x380.jpg" alt="LONDON, ENGLAND - APRIL 26: Bryan Mbeumo of Brentford scores the team's second goal whilst under pressure from Enzo Fernandez of Chelsea during the Premier League match between Chelsea FC and Brentford FC at Stamford Bridge on April 26, 2023 in London, England. " width="600" b="380" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">第78分钟，布伦特福德队布莱扬·姆布莫接队友后场直塞球，从右路突入禁区，起脚射门，皮球碰到对方防守球员腿上折射入网。(Mike Hewitt/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>很多切尔西球迷看到对方打入第二粒进球后，纷纷离场，不忍再看。</p>
<p>第83分钟，布伦特福德队左路传中，巴普蒂斯特中路头球摆渡，队友伊森·皮诺克（Ethan Pinnock）后点推射，稍稍高出横梁。</p>
<p>最终全场比赛结束，切尔西队主场0比2不敌布伦特福德队。客队门将大卫·拉亚·马丁（David Raya Martin）被球迷评为本场比赛最佳球员。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103700381" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103700381-GettyImages-1485567984.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103700381" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103700381-GettyImages-1485567984-600x400.jpg" alt="LONDON, ENGLAND - APRIL 26: David Raya of Brentford in action during the Premier League match between Chelsea FC and Brentford FC at Stamford Bridge on April 26, 2023 in London, England. <p>切尔西首发（3-4-3）：1-凯帕·阿里萨瓦拉加/33-韦斯里·福法纳，6-蒂亚戈·席尔瓦，14-查洛巴/21-奇尔维尔，5-恩佐·费尔南德斯（79&#8217;↑11-菲利克斯），8-科瓦西奇，28-阿斯皮利奎塔（45&#8217;↑9-奥巴梅扬）/23-加拉格尔（45&#8217;↑15-穆德里克），17-斯特林，7-坎特</p>
<figure id="attachment_103700394" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103700394-c6d77f9b0a15cca27e20591efdbe856d.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103700394" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103700394-c6d77f9b0a15cca27e20591efdbe856d-600x441.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="441" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">2023年4月126日，2022-2023赛季英超联赛第33轮，切尔西队主场0比2不敌布伦特福德队的全场比赛技术统计。（新唐人制图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>自从<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/兰帕德.md#1">兰帕德</a>上任以来，英超联赛三连败，欧冠主客场被王家马德里队双杀，遭遇各项赛事五连败，一分未得。</p>
<p>尽管兰帕德与球员之间保持良好的沟通，并且尽可能地把球队捏合在一起，然而球员们似乎认识到他只是一个过渡人选，下赛季俱乐部会聘用新的主教练。那么未来哪些球员还能留在“蓝军”踢球，一切都未可知，因此球员们各自规划着自己的未来，这样一支分崩离析的球队，输球也就不足为奇。</p>

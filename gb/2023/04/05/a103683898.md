<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>年龄大了 多吃五种“高钙”食物 为你的父母保存吧</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103683922-2020-09-08_160417-800x450.jpg" />
<h6>预防骨质疏松，要从补钙着手。 (Shutterstock)</h6>
<hr>
	<p>随着年龄逐渐增大更要注意身体的健康，春天是中老年人“养骨”的黄金季节。身子骨硬朗，腿脚才能有劲，如果骨骼出现问题，可能出现腰背疼痛或周身骨骼疼痛，以及容易跌倒骨折等现象，严重影响健康和生活质量。下面推荐五种“高钙”食物，为你的父母保存吧。</p>
<p>1.乳制品，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/牛奶.md#1">牛奶</a>、奶酪、酸奶这些都是<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/补钙.md#1">补钙</a>上好食物，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/牛奶.md#1">牛奶</a>中的钙能被人体很好的吸收，每百毫升牛奶中钙元素的含量约为120毫克，钙的吸收率约40%。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103683903" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103683903" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103683903-shutterstock_1464216647-600x400-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">牛奶、奶酪、酸奶这些都是<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/补钙.md#1">补钙</a>上好食物。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p>2.<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/豆制品.md#1">豆制品</a>， 是一种含钙量最高的食材，含有丰富蛋白质，富含大量的钙质。豆制品的补钙冠军包括：豆腐、豆干、豆皮、腐竹等。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103683905" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103683905" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103683905-shutterstock_1922626076-600x400-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/豆制品.md#1">豆制品</a>， 是一种含钙量最高的食材。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p>3.黑芝麻，黑芝麻含钙量有非常高，每一百克就含有近八百毫克的钙质。它的钙含量确实相当于牛奶的七、八倍。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103683909" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103683909" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103683909-shutterstock_389835139-600x400-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">黑芝麻钙含量确实相当于牛奶的七、八倍。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p>4.蛋类，每100克鸡蛋含56毫克钙元素，每天吃一个鸡蛋是很好的选择。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103683913" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103683913" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103683913-pick-fresh-egg_710378212-600x400-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">每天吃一个鸡蛋是很好的选择。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p>5.<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/紫菜.md#1">紫菜</a>， 是一种高钙食材， 每100克紫菜中含钙量大于400毫克，是补钙的佳品。常吃能增强免疫力。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103683916" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103683916" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103683916-2020-09-01_151418-800x450-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">美味的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/紫菜.md#1">紫菜</a>。(successo images/shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>

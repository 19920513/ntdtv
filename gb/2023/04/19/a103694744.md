<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>欧冠：曼城总比分4:1淘汰拜仁 连续三届闯入四强</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103694923-GettyImages-1251993931-800x450.jpg" />
<h6>2023年4月19日，2022-2023赛季欧冠1/4淘汰赛第二回合，曼城客场1比1逼平拜仁，以两回合总比分4比1淘汰对手，晋级半决赛。哈兰德（中）在比赛中射失点球，但随后将功补过又打入一球。(ODD ANDERSEN/AFP via Getty Images)</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【新唐人北京时间2023年04月20日讯】当地时间4月19日，2022-2023赛季欧冠1/4淘汰赛第二回合比赛结束，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/曼城队.md#1">曼城队</a>作客安联球场，1比1逼平德甲豪门拜仁慕尼黑队，以两回合总比分4比1淘汰对手，晋级半决赛。这是曼城队连续三个赛季闯入<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/欧冠四强.md#1">欧冠四强</a>。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103695031" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103695031-a40496f94c42576a2042797bf6d2d83d.jpg"><img class="wp-image-103695031 size-medium" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103695031-a40496f94c42576a2042797bf6d2d83d-600x347.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="347" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">2022-2023年度欧洲冠军杯淘汰赛赛程赛果。（新唐人制图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>一周前在<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/曼城队.md#1">曼城队</a>的伊蒂哈德球场，“蓝月亮”三球大胜，从而为本场比赛奠定基调，拜仁唯有回到主场全力进攻，才可挽回首回合三球的劣势。</p>
<p>上半时第17分钟，拜仁后场抢断后发动快攻，影锋穆西亚拉（Jamal?Musiala）直塞球打穿对方防线，队友萨内（Leroy Sané）高速带球突入禁区，在面对门将时推射远角偏出，惊出对手一身冷汗。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103694776" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103694776-GettyImages-1483354369.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103694776" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103694776-GettyImages-1483354369-600x400.jpg" alt="MUNICH, GERMANY - APRIL 19: Leroy Sane of FC Bayern Munich shoots and misses during the UEFA Champions League quarterfinal second leg match between FC Bayern M眉nchen and Manchester City at Allianz Arena on April 19, 2023 in Munich, Germany. " width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">第17分钟，拜仁前锋萨内高速带球突入禁区左路，面对出击门将，推射远角偏出。(Matthias Hangst/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>第21分钟，拜仁获得前场右路任意球机会，萨内直接攻门，被门将飞身扑出底线。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103694782" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103694782-GettyImages-1251992513.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103694782" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103694782-GettyImages-1251992513-600x400.jpg" alt="Manchester City's Brazilian goalkeeper Ederson (R) saves a free kick during the UEFA Champions League quarter final, secong leg football match between Bayern Munich and Manchester City at the Allianz Arena in Munich, southern Germany on April 11, 2023. (Photo by Odd ANDERSEN / AFP) " width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">第21分钟，拜仁获得前场右路任意球机会，萨内直接攻门被门将飞身扑出底线。(ODD ANDERSEN/AFP via Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>第35分钟，曼城队后腰京多安（Ilkay Gündogan）在禁区外左路起脚远射，皮球碰到对方后卫乌帕梅卡诺（Dayot Upamecano）的手臂，主裁判认定禁区内手球犯规，判罚点球。</p>
<p>中锋<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/哈兰德.md#1">哈兰德</a>（Erling Braut Haaland）操刀主罚，但射向中路的皮球高出横梁，错失绝佳机会。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103694791" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103694791-GettyImages-1483359414.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103694791" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103694791-GettyImages-1483359414-600x400.jpg" alt="MUNICH, GERMANY - APRIL 19: Erling Haaland of Manchester City misses a penalty kick during the UEFA Champions League quarterfinal second leg match between FC Bayern M眉nchen and Manchester City at Allianz Arena on April 19, 2023 in Munich, Germany. " width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">第37分钟，曼城队获得点球机会，中锋<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/哈兰德.md#1">哈兰德</a>操刀主罚打高。(Matthias Hangst/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>第42分钟，拜仁中路分球至禁区左路，边锋科曼（Kingsley Coman）迎球劲射被门将双拳封出。</p>
<p>上半场双方均无建树，下半场易边再战。</p>
<p>第57分钟，曼城队发动快速反击，中场核心德布劳内（Kevin De Bruyne）分球至左路，对方后卫乌帕梅卡诺意外滑倒，前锋哈兰德轻松突破防守，劲射球门右上角入网， 客队率先打破场上僵局。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103694807" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103694807-GettyImages-1483366386.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103694807" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103694807-GettyImages-1483366386-600x400.jpg" alt="MUNICH, GERMANY - APRIL 19: Erling Haaland of Manchester City scores the team's first goal during the UEFA Champions League quarterfinal second leg match between FC Bayern M眉nchen and Manchester City at Allianz Arena on April 19, 2023 in Munich, Germany. " width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">第57分钟，曼城队前锋哈兰德在禁区左路劲射球门右上角入网。(Alexander Hassenstein/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>第80分钟，拜仁替补登场的前锋马内（Sadio Mané）在禁区左路底线回传时，皮球打在对方后卫阿坎吉（Manuel Akanji）的手臂上，主裁判经过VAR确认后，判罚点球。后腰基米希（Joshua Kimmich）操刀主罚，一蹴而就，将比分扳平。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103694814" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103694814-GettyImages-1483371133.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103694814" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103694814-GettyImages-1483371133-600x400.jpg" alt="MUNICH, GERMANY - APRIL 19: Joshua Kimmich of FC Bayern Munich scores the team's first goal from a penalty kick past Ederson of Manchester City during the UEFA Champions League quarterfinal second leg match between FC Bayern M眉nchen and Manchester City at Allianz Arena on April 19, 2023 in Munich, Germany. " width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">第83分钟，拜仁获得点球机会，后腰基米希操刀主罚命中。(Alexander Hassenstein/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>尽管拜仁在比赛最后阶段打入一球，但是为时已晚，首回合三球落后的局面已难以挽回。曼城队最终在客场1比1逼平拜仁，成功晋级半决赛。</p>
<p>客队中场核心德布劳内被欧足联的技术观察小组评为本场比赛最佳球员。他们认为，德布劳内的视野和意识是顶级的，他帮助球队主宰了比赛，今晚还奉献了一次关键助攻。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103695016" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103695016-GettyImages-1251997349.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103695016" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103695016-GettyImages-1251997349-600x400.jpg" alt="Bayern Munich's German midfielder Joshua Kimmich (L) and Manchester City's Belgian midfielder Kevin De Bruyne vie for the ball during the UEFA Champions League quarter-final, second leg football match between Bayern Munich and Manchester City in Munich, southern Germany on April 19, 2023. (Photo by CHRISTOF STACHE / AFP) " width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">2023年4月19日，2022-2023年度欧洲冠军杯1/4淘汰赛第二回合，曼城队中场核心德布劳内（右）在对阵拜仁的比赛中奉献一次助攻，被评为全场比赛最佳球员。(CHRISTOF STACHE/AFP via Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>拜仁慕尼黑队（4-2-3-1）：27-扬·索默/22-坎塞洛（63&#8217;↑19-戴维斯·阿方索），4-德里赫特，2-乌帕梅卡诺，5-帕瓦德（77&#8217;↑44-斯坦尼希奇）/6-基米希，8-格雷茨卡/10-萨内（63&#8217;↑17-马内），42-穆西亚拉（71&#8217;↑25-托马斯·穆勒），11-金斯利·科曼/13-楚波莫廷（71&#8217;↑39-迪尔）</p>
<p>曼城首发（4-2-3-1）：31-埃德森/ 6-阿克（66&#8217;↑14-拉波尔特），3-迪亚斯，5-约翰·斯通斯，25-阿坎吉/8-京多安，16-罗德里/10-格拉利什，17-德布劳内（88&#8217;↑2-沃克），20-贝尔纳多·席尔瓦/9-哈兰德（84&#8217;↑19-阿尔瓦雷斯）</p>
<figure id="attachment_103694909" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103694909-cd17202c8134e8fe0e23397a5173a8cb.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103694909" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103694909-cd17202c8134e8fe0e23397a5173a8cb-600x442.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="442" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">2023年4月19日，2022-2023年度欧洲冠军杯1/4淘汰赛第二回合，曼彻斯特城队客场1比1逼平拜仁慕尼黑队，图为全场比赛技术统计。（新唐人制图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>拜仁慕尼黑队整场比赛都在努力寻找破门机会，但是后卫乌帕梅卡诺的意外滑倒成就了哈兰德的进球，这彻底扼杀了主队晋级的一线生机。</p>
<p>曼城队连续三个赛季打入<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/欧冠四强.md#1">欧冠四强</a>，半决赛他们将对阵宿敌王家马德里队，上赛季欧冠半决赛正是被对手淘汰出局，此番必将是一场鏖战。5月9日和17日，两队将在各自主场举行两回合淘汰赛，以决出晋级决赛的球队。</p>

<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>欧冠八强战首轮：国米2比0胜本菲卡 卢卡库点射</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103689260-GettyImages-1251627461-800x450.jpg" />
<h6>2023年4月11日，2022-2023赛季欧冠1/4淘汰赛首轮，意甲国际米兰客场2比0力克葡超本菲卡队。卢卡库（中）点射建功。(CARLOS COSTA/AFP via Getty Images)</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【新唐人北京时间2023年04月12日讯】当地时间4月11日，2022-2023赛季欧冠1/4淘汰赛首轮展开激战，意甲国际米兰反客为主，2比0击败葡超领头羊<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/本菲卡队.md#1">本菲卡队</a>。<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/卢卡库.md#1">卢卡库</a>点射建功。</p>
<p>本赛季，两队在本国联赛所处的境遇不同。<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/本菲卡队.md#1">本菲卡队</a>在葡超联赛中遥遥领先，精力可以更专注于欧冠赛事；而<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/国际米兰队.md#1">国际米兰队</a>近期在意甲已连续四轮不胜，排名下滑至第五，要为下赛季欧冠席位而战，再加上中场核心恰尔汗奥卢（Hakan Calhanoglu）近期肌肉拉伤，实力受到一定影响，赛前普遍认为国际米兰客场将经历苦战。双方近年来在欧洲赛场上没有交手记录。</p>
<p>上半时第16分钟，本菲卡队边后卫格里马尔多（Grimaldo）左路传中，对方防守球员解围，将球踢至中场核心拉法·席尔瓦（Rafa Silva）脚下，后者在禁区左路的射门被门将用身体挡出。</p>
<p>第25分钟，国米后卫阿切尔比（Acerbi）在距离球门30米开外大力远射，高出横梁。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103689183" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103689183-GettyImages-1251618711.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103689183" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103689183-GettyImages-1251618711-600x400.jpg" alt="Benfica's Greek goalkeeper Odysseas Vlachodimos dives as the ball flies wide during the UEFA Champions League quarter final first leg football match between SL Benfica and Inter Milan at the Luz stadium in Lisbon on April 11, 2023. (Photo by CARLOS COSTA / AFP) " width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">第25分钟，国米后卫阿切尔比（Acerbi）在距离球门30米开外大力远射高出横梁。(CARLOS COSTA/AFP via Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>两队在上半场打得都很谨慎，未能创造出太多机会，下半场双方易边再战。</p>
<p>第51分钟，国米边后卫巴斯托尼（Alessandro?Bastoni）前场左路45度角斜传，禁区内多点包抄，中场球员尼科洛·巴雷拉（Nicolo?Barella）在后门柱头槌顶入球门远角，打破场上僵局。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103689203" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103689203-GettyImages-1251625078.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103689203" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103689203-GettyImages-1251625078-600x400.jpg" alt="Inter Milan's Italian midfielder Nicolo Barella (C) scores the opening goal during the UEFA Champions League quarter final first leg football match between SL Benfica and Inter Milan at the Luz stadium in Lisbon on April 11, 2023. (Photo by CARLOS COSTA / AFP) " width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">第51分钟，国米边后卫巴斯托尼前场左路45度角斜传，队友尼科洛·巴雷拉在后门柱头槌顶入球门远角。(CARLOS COSTA/AFP via Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>第56分钟，本菲卡队右路下底传中，拉法·席尔瓦的射门被对方右前卫2号邓弗里斯（Dumfries）封堵。随后双方在小禁区内争抢时，防守球员有踢倒主队前锋贡萨洛·拉莫斯（Goncalo Ramos）之嫌，但裁判没有判罚。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103689207" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103689207-GettyImages-1481460887.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103689207" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103689207-GettyImages-1481460887-600x418.jpg" alt="LISBON, PORTUGAL - APRIL 11: Goncalo Ramos of SL Benfica is challenged by Alessandro Bastoni of FC Internazionale during the UEFA Champions League quarterfinal first leg match between SL Benfica and FC Internazionale at Estadio do Sport Lisboa e Benfica on April 11, 2023 in Lisbon, Portugal. " width="600" b="418" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">第56分钟，本菲卡队前锋贡萨洛·拉莫斯在争抢时倒地，有被对方防守球员踢倒之嫌，主裁判没有判罚。(Octavio Passos/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>国米主教练英扎吉（Simone Inzaghi）在球队打入一粒进球后马上进行换人调整，祭出防守反击战术，并派上锋霸<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/卢卡库.md#1">卢卡库</a>（Romelu Lukaku）冲击对方防线。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103689227" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103689227-GettyImages-1481515998.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103689227" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103689227-GettyImages-1481515998-600x400.jpg" alt="LISBON, PORTUGAL - APRIL 11: Head coach, Simone Inzaghi of FC Internazionale looks on during the UEFA Champions League quarterfinal first leg match between SL Benfica and FC Internazionale at Estadio do Sport Lisboa e Benfica on April 11, 2023 in Lisbon, Portugal. " width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">2023年4月11日，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/国际米兰队.md#1">国际米兰队</a>主教练英扎吉在欧冠八强战对阵本菲卡队的比赛中指挥。(Octavio Passos/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>第66分钟，国米发动快攻，前锋华金·科雷亚（Joaquin Correa）禁区左路直塞，队友姆希塔良（Henrikh Mkhitaryan）快速插上，小角度打门，被门将没收。</p>
<p>第78分钟，国米仍然由巴斯托尼在前场左路45度角传向禁区，远端无人盯防的邓弗里斯头球攻门，被门将挡出，再跟进补射，又被后卫封堵，险些破门。</p>
<p>国米继续组织二次进攻，邓弗里斯右路底线传中，打在禁区内防守球员的手臂上，主裁判通过VAR确认后判罚<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/点球.md#1">点球</a>。卢卡库操刀主罚命中，锁定胜局。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103689225" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103689225-GettyImages-1481468474.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103689225" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103689225-GettyImages-1481468474-600x400.jpg" alt="LISBON, PORTUGAL - APRIL 11: Romelu Lukaku of FC Internazionale scores the team's second goal from a penalty kick as Odisseas Vlachodimos of SL Benfica ( Obscured ) fails to make a save during the UEFA Champions League quarterfinal first leg match between SL Benfica and FC Internazionale at Estadio do Sport Lisboa e Benfica on April 11, 2023 in Lisbon, Portugal. " width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">第82分钟，国际米兰队获得<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/点球.md#1">点球</a>机会，前锋卢卡库操刀主罚命中。(Octavio Passos/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>伤停补时阶段，本菲卡队前场抢断后直塞禁区左路，贡萨洛·拉莫斯低射远角，被门将横身扑出，失去最后的得分机会。</p>
<p>最终全场比赛结束，国米客场2比0力克本菲卡，左边后卫巴斯托尼被欧足联技术观察小组评为本场比赛最佳球员。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103689251" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103689251-GettyImages-1481483813.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103689251" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103689251-GettyImages-1481483813-600x400.jpg" alt="LISBON, PORTUGAL - APRIL 11: Alessandro Bastoni of FC Internazionale reacts during the UEFA Champions League quarterfinal first leg match between SL Benfica and FC Internazionale at Estadio do Sport Lisboa e Benfica on April 11, 2023 in Lisbon, Portugal. " width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">2023年4月11日，国际米兰队左后卫巴斯托尼在欧冠八强战对阵本菲卡队的比赛中奉献一次助攻，被评为本场比赛最佳球员。(Octavio Passos/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>他们认为，巴斯托尼在进攻、防守两端均表现出色，他的左路传球是球队有效的进攻手段，并且奉献一次助攻。</p>
<p>本菲卡首发（4-2-3-1）：99-弗拉乔迪莫斯/3-格里马尔多，91-莫拉托，66-安东尼奥·席尔瓦，2-吉尔伯托·儒尼奥尔/22-奇奎尼奥，61-弗洛伦蒂诺（64&#8217;↑7-内雷斯）/8-奥尔斯内斯，27-拉法·席尔瓦，20-若奥·马里奥/88-贡萨洛·拉莫斯</p>
<p>国际米兰首发（3-5-2）：24-奥纳那/95-巴斯托尼（91&#8217;↑6-德弗里伊），15-阿切尔比，36-达米安/32-迪马尔科（63&#8217;↑8-戈森斯），22-姆希塔良，77-布罗佐维奇，23-尼科洛·巴雷拉，2-邓弗里斯（86&#8217;↑33-丹布罗西奥）/9-哲科（63&#8217;↑90-卢卡库），10-劳塔罗·马丁内斯（63&#8217;↑11-华金·科雷亚）</p>
<figure id="attachment_103689270" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103689270-00bfbc0d14e4226dd3ac94487a168b7f.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103689270" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103689270-00bfbc0d14e4226dd3ac94487a168b7f-600x447.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="447" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">2023年4月11日，2022-2023年度欧冠八强战首轮，葡超本菲卡队主场0比2不敌意甲国际米兰队，全场比赛技术统计。（新唐人制图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这是本菲卡队本赛季欧冠比赛的首场失利，国米取得首个进球后打起防守反击，并利用对方大举压上留下的空档取得了第二个进球，战术非常得当。</p>
<p>国米客场拿到两球的领先优势，在两回合的比赛中占据了绝对主动。4月19日，本菲卡队要想在圣西罗球场翻盘，是件非常困难的事情。</p>

<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>英超：热刺换帅未能取胜 凯恩点射 卢卡斯染红</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103682521-GettyImages-1479521792-800x450.jpg" />
<h6>2023年4月3日，2022-2023赛季英超联赛第29轮，热刺队终场前丢球，客场1比1被埃弗顿队逼平，边锋小卢卡斯被红牌罚下。(Stu Forster/Getty Images)</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【新唐人北京时间2023年04月04日讯】当地时间4月3日，<ahref=><a href="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/2022-2023赛季英超.md#1">2022-2023赛季英超</a>第29轮最后一场比赛，“白百合”<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/热刺队.md#1">热刺队</a>再次晚节不保，终场前丢球，客场被保级的埃弗顿队1比1逼平，凯恩射入点球，小卢卡斯染红。</p>
<p><ahref=><a href="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/2022/12/19/a103601784.md#1">2022-2023赛季英超积分榜及赛程赛果</a></p>
<p>赛前“白百合”刚刚经历<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/换帅.md#1">换帅</a>风波，前主帅孔蒂（Antonio Conte）因球队上轮对阵南安普敦队的联赛中补时丢球，赛后向媒体炮轰队内球员只考虑自己，缺乏进取心和荣誉感。热刺俱乐部高层考虑到将帅失和，与孔蒂协商一致后，解除了其主教练的职务。这是<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/热刺队.md#1">热刺队</a>连续四个赛季中途炒掉主教练，前三位分别为波切蒂诺（Mauricio Pochettino）、穆里尼奥（José Mourinho）和努诺·桑托（Nuno Espírito Santo）。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103682508" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103682508-FotoJet.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103682508" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103682508-FotoJet-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">自2019年底，热刺队连续四个赛季中途<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/换帅.md#1">换帅</a>，四位被炒的主教练分别为波切蒂诺（左上），穆里尼奥（右上），努诺·桑托（左下）和孔蒂（右下）。（Getty images/新唐人合成）</figcaption></figure>
<p>接手帅位的是助教——意大利人克里斯蒂安·斯泰利尼（Cristian Stellini），今年48岁。作为球员期间他曾效力于意大利巴里队和热那亚队，司职后卫。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103682510" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103682510-GettyImages-1472154972.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103682510" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103682510-GettyImages-1472154972-600x400.jpg" alt="LONDON, ENGLAND - MARCH 08: Cristian Stellini, Assistant Manager of Tottenham Hotspur, looks on prior to the UEFA Champions League round of 16 leg two match between Tottenham Hotspur and AC Milan at Tottenham Hotspur Stadium on March 08, 2023 in London, England. (Photo by Clive Rose/Getty Images)" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">3月26日，热刺队官方宣布主教练<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/孔蒂下课.md#1">孔蒂下课</a>，助教克里斯蒂安·斯泰利尼将接手球队直至本赛季结束。(Clive Rose/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>斯泰利尼将率领热刺队打完本赛季剩余比赛，本场对阵埃弗顿队的联赛是他的首秀。此前两队英超联赛交手61次，热刺29胜23平9负，占据绝对优势。本赛季首场交锋中，“白百合”主场2比0完胜对手。此番二度交手，埃弗顿队坐镇主场。</p>
<p>上半场第10分钟，埃弗顿队前场右路任意球传向禁区，中后卫迈克尔·基恩（Michael Keane）在禁区中路侧身抽射，高出横梁。</p>
<p>第16分钟，热刺队左路传中，哈里·凯恩（Harry Kane）门前头球攻门偏出。</p>
<p>第38分钟，埃弗顿队中场球员奥纳纳（Amadou Onana）在禁区外距离球门30码处远射，被门将得到。</p>
<p>双方上半场踢得较为沉闷，都未能有所建树，下半时易边再战。</p>
<p>第52分钟，埃弗顿队右路传中，前锋德马莱·格雷（Demarai Gray）没有射门机会，回敲队友奥纳纳，后者在中路远射，但力量不大，对方守门员侧扑将球没收。</p>
<p>第58分钟，埃弗顿队遭受打击，中场球员杜库雷（Abdoulaye Doucouré）掌掴对方中锋凯恩，被主裁判直接<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/红牌.md#1">红牌</a>罚下。主队不得不以10人应战。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103682515" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103682515-GettyImages-1479505574.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103682515" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103682515-GettyImages-1479505574-600x400.jpg" alt="LIVERPOOL, ENGLAND - APRIL 03: Harry Kane of Tottenham Hotspur and Abdoulaye Doucoure of Everton clash during the Premier League match between Everton FC and Tottenham Hotspur at Goodison Park on April 03, 2023 in Liverpool, England. (Photo by Alex Livesey/Getty Images)" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">第58分钟，埃弗顿队中场球员杜库雷掌掴对方中锋凯恩，被主裁判直接<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/红牌.md#1">红牌</a>罚下。(Alex Livesey/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>第66分钟，埃弗顿队后卫迈克尔·基恩在禁区内抢球时，踢倒了对方球员，主裁判判以极刑。热刺当家中锋凯恩成功骗过守门员，将球打进死角，帮助客队打破僵局。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103682516" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103682516-GettyImages-1479517900.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103682516" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103682516-GettyImages-1479517900-600x400.jpg" alt="LIVERPOOL, ENGLAND - APRIL 03: Jordan Pickford of Everton fails to save as Harry Kane of Tottenham Hotspur scores the team's first goal from a penalty during the Premier League match between Everton FC and Tottenham Hotspur at Goodison Park on April 03, 2023 in Liverpool, England. (Photo by Stu Forster/Getty Images)" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">第68分钟，热刺队中锋凯恩成功骗过守门员，将点球罚进。(Stu Forster/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>第75分钟，埃弗顿队前场高位逼抢后断球，前锋格雷禁区外横敲队友，盖耶（Idrissa Gueye）在弧顶处远射，被对方门将飞身托出底线。</p>
<p>第88分钟，热刺队遭受打击，替补登场仅仅六分钟的边锋卢卡斯·莫拉（Lucas Moura），因在拼抢中踩踏对手，被主裁判同样红牌罚下。双方人数回归均衡，都是10人作战。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103682513" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103682513-GettyImages-1479509414.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103682513" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103682513-GettyImages-1479509414-600x400.jpg" alt="LIVERPOOL, ENGLAND - APRIL 03: Match Referee, David Coote shows a red card to Lucas of Tottenham Hotspur after a tackle on Michael Keane of Everton during the Premier League match between Everton FC and Tottenham Hotspur at Goodison Park on April 03, 2023 in Liverpool, England. <p>第90分钟，埃弗顿队中卫迈克尔·基恩将功补过，在禁区外距球门25米远突施冷箭，皮球飞入右上死角，对方守门员只能望球兴叹。主队终场前将比分扳平。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103682512" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103682512-GettyImages-1479516599.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103682512" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103682512-GettyImages-1479516599-600x313.jpg" alt="LIVERPOOL, ENGLAND - APRIL 03: Everton player Michael Keane shoots to score the Everton goal during the Premier League match between Everton FC and Tottenham Hotspur at Goodison Park on April 03, 2023 in Liverpool, England. <p>最终全场比赛结束，热刺队客场与埃弗顿队战成1比1平，打入点球的前锋10号凯恩被球迷评为本场比赛最佳球员。</p>

<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>手机充电线断了别扔  一招修复 比原来的还耐用</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103699622-IMG_2728-800x450.jpeg" />
<h6>手机充电线使用时间久了很容易断裂。（作者提供）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>现在日常生活离不开手机，尤其是打游戏、刷视频的，手机一天充几次电都是常事，时间久了，可能造成<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/手机充电线.md#1">手机充电线</a>的损伤，接口处最容易断裂，买新的还要花钱，今天和大家分享手机或<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/电脑充电线.md#1">电脑充电线</a>折断后的修复方法。</p>
<p>手机或<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/电脑充电线.md#1">电脑充电线</a>中间，或者是近端头折断了，而且还可以使用，可以采取如下方法修复。</p>
<p>准备材料：一卷双面胶、空的矿泉水瓶、吹风机、剪刀、细线绳</p>
<figure id="attachment_103699623" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103699623" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103699623-IMG_2740-600x450.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_103699624" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103699624" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103699624-IMG_2747-600x450.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_103699625" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="wp-image-103699625 size-medium" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103699625-IMG_2749-600x450.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>1.用剪刀将双面胶，剪成三小段。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103699629" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103699629" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103699629-IMG_2760-600x450.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2.将两块双面胶分别粘在充电线断裂的两边，粘好将胶纸撕掉。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103699640" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103699640" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103699640-IMG_2742-600x450.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3.将用过的矿泉水瓶上的塑料标签撕下来。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103699641" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103699641" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103699641-IMG_2741-600x450.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4.根据刚才两边粘贴的长度，剪下适量的塑料片，用这块塑料片包裹住断裂的地方。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103699642" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103699642" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103699642-IMG_2743-600x367.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="367" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>6.最后收口的地方，粘上一层双面胶粘好，这样就完全贴合好了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103699644" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103699644" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103699644-IMG_2746-600x381.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="381" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>7.把吹风机开到最热的档，吹一吹，塑料标签受热后就会收缩，完美的贴合在充电线上。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103699645" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103699645" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103699645-IMG_2748-600x378.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="378" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>8.最后我们还要加上一层防护，取出一根细线绳，将线的一端折叠起来，贴和在充电线上。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103699647" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103699647" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103699647-IMG_2750-600x377.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="377" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>再用线的另外一头将充电线缠绕起来，缠绕一层线圈。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103699648" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103699648" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103699648-IMG_2751-600x450.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_103699649" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103699649" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103699649-IMG_2753-600x450.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>收尾时，将线头穿过前面的线圈，拿起另外一端的线头，用力将线头收紧。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103699652" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103699652" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103699652-IMG_2754-600x407.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="407" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>用剪子剪掉多余的线头。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103699653" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103699653" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103699653-IMG_2756-600x382.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="382" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>最后用打火机将线头收紧，这样修复好的充电线就很耐用了。如果想要美观可以选用自己喜欢的细线绳。</p>
<p>（责任编辑：嘉欣）</p>

<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>欧冠：国米总分5比3晋级 时隔13年再进四强</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103695461-GettyImages-1483373931-800x450.jpg" />
<h6>2023年4月19日，2022-2023年度欧冠八强战第二回合，国际米兰主场3比3战平本菲卡，从而以总比分5比3淘汰对手，时隔13年再次闯入欧冠四强。(Marco Luzzani/Getty Images)</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【新唐人北京时间2023年04月20日讯】当地时间4月19日，2022-2023年度<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/欧冠八强战.md#1">欧冠八强战</a>第二回合比赛结束，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/国际米兰队.md#1">国际米兰队</a>主场3比3战平葡超劲旅<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/本菲卡队.md#1">本菲卡队</a>，以两回合总比分5比3淘汰对手，时隔13年再次闯入四强，而半决赛将上演<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/米兰德比.md#1">米兰德比</a>。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103695377" style="width: 1200px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103695377-83dc7387fa05780c5867ddeaa916afba.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-103695377" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103695377-83dc7387fa05780c5867ddeaa916afba-1200x693.jpg" alt="" width="1200" b="693" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">2022-2023年度欧洲冠军杯淘汰赛赛程赛果。（新唐人制图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>由于上周“蓝黑军团”在客场取得两个净胜球的领先优势，本场比赛回到主场可以等对手压上进攻，伺机反击，形势极为有利；而<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/本菲卡队.md#1">本菲卡队</a>则只有“华山一条路”，必须在客场追回两个净胜球，才有希望晋级。</p>
<p>上半时第14分钟，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/国际米兰队.md#1">国际米兰队</a>前场争下球权，前锋马丁内斯（Lautaro Martinez）与队友尼科洛·巴雷拉（Nicolo Barella）完成踢墙式配合，后者突入禁区右路，晃开角度劲射球门远角入网。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103695343" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103695343-GettyImages-1251992953.jpg"><img class="wp-image-103695343 size-medium" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103695343-GettyImages-1251992953-600x400.jpg" alt="Benfica's Greek goalkeeper Odysseas Vlachodimos (C) concedes the opening goal during the UEFA Champions League quarter-finals second leg football match between Inter Milan and Benfica Lisbon on April 19, 2023 at the Giuseppe-Meazza (San Siro) stadium in Milan. (Photo by GABRIEL BOUYS / AFP)" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">第14分钟，国际米兰队中场尼科洛·巴雷拉突入禁区右路，晃开角度劲射球门远角入网。(GABRIEL BOUYS/AFP via Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>第31分钟，本菲卡队前场中路任意球，后卫3号格里马尔多（Alex Grimaldo）直接攻门，被门将飞身封出。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103695338" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103695338-GettyImages-1251992548.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103695338" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103695338-GettyImages-1251992548-600x338.jpg" alt="Benfica's Spanish defender Alejandro Grimaldo (Front L) shoots a free kick during the UEFA Champions League quarter-finals second leg football match between Inter Milan and Benfica Lisbon on April 19, 2023 at the Giuseppe-Meazza (San Siro) stadium in Milan. (Photo by Vincenzo PINTO / AFP) " width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">第31分钟，本菲卡队后卫3号格里马尔多主罚前场中路任意球，直接攻门被门将飞身封出。(VINCENZO PINTO/AFP via Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>第38分钟，本菲卡队边锋拉法·席尔瓦（Rafa Silva）右路传中，队友奥尔斯内斯（Fredrik Aursnes）中路抢点头球破门，将比分扳平。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103695347" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103695347-GettyImages-1483359963.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103695347" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103695347-GettyImages-1483359963-600x400.jpg" alt="MILAN, ITALY - APRIL 19: Fredrik Aursnes of SL Benfica celebrates after scoring the team's first goal as Francesco Acerbi of FC Internazionale looks dejected during the UEFA Champions League quarterfinal second leg match between FC Internazionale and SL Benfica at San Siro Stadium on April 19, 2023 in Milan, Italy. " width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">第38分钟，本菲卡队边锋奥尔斯内斯（红）头球破门后，庆祝进球。(Marco Luzzani/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>上半场结束，双方战成1比1平。中场休息时，本菲卡队主教练施密特（Roger?Schmidt）用前锋内雷斯（David Neres）换下后卫，志在放手一搏。</p>
<p>第65分钟，国际米兰队从左路撕开对方防线，左前卫迪马尔科（Federico Dimarco）下底横传，前锋马丁内斯门前垫射破门，再次帮助球队将比分超出。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103695353" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103695353-GettyImages-1251995235.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103695353" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103695353-GettyImages-1251995235-600x400.jpg" alt="Inter Milan's Argentinian forward Lautaro Martinez celebrates after scoring his side's second goal during the UEFA Champions League quarter-finals second leg football match between Inter Milan and Benfica Lisbon on April 19, 2023 at the Giuseppe-Meazza (San Siro) stadium in Milan. (Photo by GABRIEL BOUYS / AFP) " width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">第65分钟，国际米兰队前锋马丁内斯门前垫射破门后，庆祝进球。(GABRIEL BOUYS/AFP via Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>第78分钟，国际米兰队依然是从左路发动攻势，迪马尔科肋部直传给禁区内的华金·科雷亚（Joaquin Correa），后者闪开角度劲射后角，撞柱入网，将比分改写为3比1。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103695357" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103695357-GettyImages-1251995899.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103695357" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103695357-GettyImages-1251995899-600x400.jpg" alt="Inter Milan's Argentinian forward Joaquin Correa celebrates after scoring his side's third goal during the UEFA Champions League quarter-finals second leg football match between Inter Milan and Benfica Lisbon on April 19, 2023 at the Giuseppe-Meazza (San Siro) stadium in Milan. (Photo by GABRIEL BOUYS / AFP) " width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">第78分钟，国际米兰队前锋华金·科雷亚闪开角度劲射后角破门得分并庆祝进球。(GABRIEL BOUYS/AFP via Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>国际米兰队在大比分领先后，球员有些松懈。第83分钟，布罗佐维奇（Marcelo Brozovic）后场出现传球失误，本菲卡队前锋内雷斯断球后在禁区中路低射，击中右侧立柱弹出。</p>
<p>第86分钟，本菲卡队前场右路任意球，格里马尔多45度角斜传禁区，后卫安东尼奥·席尔瓦（Antonio Silva）前点头球攻门，顶入球门远角，本菲卡队追回一球。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103695369" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103695369-GettyImages-1251995451.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103695369" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103695369-GettyImages-1251995451-600x400.jpg" alt="Benfica's Portuguese defender Antonio Silva (R) scores a header past Inter Milan's Cameroonian goalkeeper Andre Onana, and his side's second goal, during the UEFA Champions League quarter-finals second leg football match between Inter Milan and Benfica Lisbon on April 19, 2023 at the Giuseppe-Meazza (San Siro) stadium in Milan. (Photo by GABRIEL BOUYS / AFP) " width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">第86分钟，本菲卡队前场右路任意球45度角斜传禁区，后卫安东尼奥·席尔瓦前点头球攻门，顶入球门远角。(GABRIEL BOUYS/AFP via Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>伤停补时最后阶段，本菲卡队左路突破后传中，前锋佩塔·穆萨（Petar Musa）禁区内转身低射入网，将比分扳平。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103695371" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103695371-GettyImages-1251996259.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103695371" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103695371-GettyImages-1251996259-600x400.jpg" alt="Benfica's Croatian forward Petar Musa (C) shoots to score his side's third goal during the UEFA Champions League quarter-finals second leg football match between Inter Milan and Benfica Lisbon on April 19, 2023 at the Giuseppe-Meazza (San Siro) stadium in Milan." width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">第95分钟，本菲卡队左路突破后传中，前锋佩塔·穆萨禁区内转身低射破门。(GABRIEL BOUYS/AFP via Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>全场比赛结束，双方战成3比3平。“蓝黑军团”最终以总比分5比3晋级，时隔13年再次闯入<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/欧冠四强.md#1">欧冠四强</a>。</p>
<p>本场比赛奉献一粒进球和一次助攻的国米锋将劳塔罗·马丁内斯被欧足联的技术观察小组评为最佳球员。他们认为，马丁内斯展现了跑位能力，开场奉献助攻，此后又打入一粒进球，表现非常出色。</p>
<p>国际米兰首发（3-5-2）：24-奥纳那/95-巴斯托尼（80&#8217;↑33-丹布罗西奥），15-阿切尔比，36-达米安/32-迪马尔科（80&#8217;↑8-戈森斯），22-姆希塔良，77-布罗佐维奇，23-尼科洛·巴雷拉（76&#8217;↑20-恰尔汗奥卢），2-邓弗里斯/9-哲科（76&#8217;↑90-卢卡库），10-劳塔罗·马丁内斯（76&#8217;↑11-华金·科雷亚）</p>
<p>本菲卡首发（4-2-3-1）：99-弗拉乔迪莫斯/3-格里马尔多，30-奥塔门迪，66-安东尼奥·席尔瓦，2-吉尔伯托·儒尼奥尔（46&#8217;↑7-内雷斯）/22-奇奎尼奥（80&#8217;↑33-佩塔·穆萨），61-弗洛伦蒂诺/8-奥尔斯内斯，20-若奥·马里奥（89&#8217;↑21-施尔德鲁普），27-拉法·席尔瓦（80&#8217;↑87-若昂·内维斯）/88-贡萨洛·拉莫斯（74&#8217;↑15-贡萨洛·格德斯）</p>
<figure id="attachment_103695405" style="width: 1200px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103695405-bbe5c992965b42c01855ee74435707d5.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-103695405" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103695405-bbe5c992965b42c01855ee74435707d5-1200x883.jpg" alt="" width="1200" b="883" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">2023年4月19日，2022-2023年度<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/欧冠八强战.md#1">欧冠八强战</a>第二回合，意甲国际米兰队主场3比3战平葡超本菲卡队全场比赛技术统计。（新唐人制图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>尽管本菲卡队整场比赛一直未曾放弃，并在最后阶段连入两球，但是“蓝黑军团”进攻端的三个进球足以确保他们晋级四强。</p>
<p>国际米兰队四强对手是同城死敌AC米兰队，两队的半决赛将于5月10日和16日进行。上次双方在欧冠中相遇还要追溯至2005年的1/4决赛，当时“红黑军团”两回合5比0淘汰“蓝黑军团”，挺进四强并杀入决赛，但被利物浦队上演“伊斯坦布尔奇迹”逆转，屈居亚军。</p>

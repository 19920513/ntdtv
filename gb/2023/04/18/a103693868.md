<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>欧冠：王家马德里双杀切尔西 罗德里戈梅开二度</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103693925-GettyImages-1251959789-800x450.jpg" />
<h6>2023年4月18日，2022-2023赛季欧冠1/4决赛第二回合，卫冕冠军王家马德里队客场2比0力克切尔西队，罗德里戈（右二）独中两元。(GLYN KIRK/AFP via Getty Images)</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【新唐人北京时间2023年04月19日讯】当地时间4月18日，2022-2023赛季欧冠1/4决赛第二回合展开激战，卫冕冠军王家马德里队客场2比0力克<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/切尔西.md#1">切尔西</a>队，边锋<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/罗德里戈.md#1">罗德里戈</a>梅开二度，从而以总比分4比0零封对手晋级<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/半决赛.md#1">半决赛</a>。</p>
<p>一周前，“银河战舰”刚刚在主场2比0击败对手，因此本场比赛带着两个净胜球的优势来到<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/切尔西.md#1">切尔西</a>的斯坦福桥球场作战。</p>
<p>上半时第10分钟，切尔西队右路下底传中，后腰坎特（N&#8217;Golo Kanté）在禁区中路的射门，可惜没有打正部位，偏出界外。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103693935" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103693935-GettyImages-1483114799.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103693935" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103693935-GettyImages-1483114799-600x400.jpg" alt="LONDON, ENGLAND - APRIL 18: Ngolo Kante of Chelsea shoots during the UEFA Champions League quarterfinal second leg match between Chelsea FC and Real Madrid at Stamford Bridge on April 18, 2023 in London, England. " width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">第10分钟，切尔西队右路下底传中，后腰坎特在禁区中路的射门，可惜没有打正部位，偏出界外。(Michael Regan/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>第20分钟，王家马德里队右路横传，边锋<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/罗德里戈.md#1">罗德里戈</a>（Rodrygo）在禁区内晃开角度打门，击中右侧立柱弹出底线，险些破门得分。</p>
<p>第32分钟，王家马德里队中场核心莫德里奇（Luka Modric）右路突入禁区，小角度射门，被门将双掌封出界外。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103693939" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103693939-GettyImages-1251954949.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103693939" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103693939-GettyImages-1251954949-600x389.jpg" alt="Real Madrid's Croatian midfielder Luka Modric shoots but fails to score during the Champions League quarter-final second-leg football match between Chelsea and Real Madrid at Stamford Bridge in London on April 18, 2023. (Photo by Glyn KIRK / AFP) " width="600" b="389" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">第32分钟，王家马德里队中场核心莫德里奇右路突入禁区，小角度射门，被门将双掌封出界外。(GLYN KIRK/AFP via Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>上半时伤停补时阶段，切尔西队右路下底横传，无人防守的左前卫库库雷亚（Marc?Cucurella）在远门柱推射，被对方门将扑出，错失绝佳机会。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103693940" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103693940-GettyImages-1251954819.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103693940" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103693940-GettyImages-1251954819-600x400.jpg" alt="Real Madrid's Belgian goalkeeper Thibaut Courtois (R) savers a shot from Chelsea's Spanish defender Marc Cucurella during the Champions League quarter-final second-leg football match between Chelsea and Real Madrid at Stamford Bridge in London on April 18, 2023. <p>中场休息时，双方均无建树，下半场易边再战。</p>
<p>第52分钟，切尔西队右路传中，前锋加拉格尔（Conor Gallagher）头球摆渡，坎特在门前6码处的射门被对方后卫米利唐（éder Militao）封堵。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103693943" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103693943-GettyImages-1251958196.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103693943" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103693943-GettyImages-1251958196-600x400.jpg" alt="Real Madrid's Belgian goalkeeper Thibaut Courtois saves a shot from Chelsea's French midfielder N'Golo Kante during the Champions League quarter-final second-leg football match between Chelsea and Real Madrid at Stamford Bridge in London on April 18, 2023. <p>第58分钟，王家马德里队边锋罗德里戈右路突破防守，内切至禁区横传至远门柱的维尼修斯·儒尼奥尔（Vinicius Junior），后者没有射门角度，又回传门前，罗德里戈门前推射破门，打破场上僵局。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103693944" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103693944-GettyImages-1483125462.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103693944" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103693944-GettyImages-1483125462-600x400.jpg" alt="LONDON, ENGLAND - APRIL 18: Rodrygo of Real Madrid scores the team's first goal during the UEFA Champions League quarterfinal second leg match between Chelsea FC and Real Madrid at Stamford Bridge on April 18, 2023 in London, England. " width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">第58分钟，王家马德里队边锋罗德里戈接队友横传，门前6码处推射破门。(Clive Rose/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>这个进球极大地打击了切尔西队球员的士气，也宣告王家马德里队已锁定本赛季<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/欧冠四强.md#1">欧冠四强</a>门票。</p>
<p>第80分钟，王家马德里队左路下底倒三角回传，中场球员巴尔韦德（Federico Valverde）带球突入禁区，吸引门将出击后横传，罗德里戈轻松推射空门得手，将比分定格为2比0。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103693947" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103693947-GettyImages-1483127974.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103693947" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103693947-GettyImages-1483127974-600x424.jpg" alt="LONDON, ENGLAND - APRIL 18: Rodrygo of Real Madrid scores the team's second goal past Kepa Arrizabalaga of Chelsea during the UEFA Champions League quarterfinal second leg match between Chelsea FC and Real Madrid at Stamford Bridge on April 18, 2023 in London, England. " width="600" b="424" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">第80分钟，王家马德里队中场球员巴尔韦德带球突入禁区，吸引门将出击后横传，罗德里戈轻松推射空门得手。(Michael Regan/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>最终全场比赛结束，王家马德里队客场两球零封对手，奉献一次助攻的巴尔韦德被欧足联技术观察小组评为本场比赛最佳球员。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103693949" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103693949-GettyImages-1251954697.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103693949" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103693949-GettyImages-1251954697-600x455.jpg" alt="Chelsea's English midfielder Conor Gallagher (R) vies with Real Madrid's Uruguayan midfielder Federico Valverde during the Champions League quarter-final second-leg football match between Chelsea and Real Madrid at Stamford Bridge in London on April 18, 2023. (Photo by Glyn KIRK / AFP) " width="600" b="455" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">2023年4月18日，2022-2023年度<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/欧冠八强战.md#1">欧冠八强战</a>第二回合，王家马德里队的中场大将巴尔韦德（白）在对阵切尔西队的比赛中奉献一次助攻，被评为本场比赛最佳球员。(GLYN KIRK/AFP via Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>他们认为，巴尔韦德在中场承上启下，为球队起到了很好地衔接作用，在控球方面他沉着冷静，防守端也表现出色，并且为第二个入球奉献了助攻。</p>
<p>切尔西首发（3-5-2）：1-凯帕/33-韦斯里·福法纳，6-蒂亚戈·席尔瓦，14-查洛巴/32-库库雷亚（67&#8217;↑15-穆德里克），8-科瓦西奇，5-恩佐（67&#8217;↑17-斯特林），7-坎特，24-里斯·詹姆斯/29-哈弗茨（77&#8217;↑19-芒特），23-加拉格尔（67&#8217;↑11-菲利克斯）</p>
<p>王家马德里首发（4-3-3）：1-库尔图瓦/12-卡马文加（82&#8217;↑6-纳乔），4-阿拉巴（46&#8217;↑22-吕迪格），3-米利唐，2-卡瓦哈尔/10-摩德里奇，8-克罗斯（76&#8217;↑19-塞瓦略斯），15-巴尔韦德/20-维尼修斯，9-本泽马（71&#8217;↑18-楚阿梅尼），21-罗德里戈（82&#8217;↑11-阿森西奥）</p>
<figure id="attachment_103693951" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103693951-c59cb57b3b865fe717453c8c70aa3cd3.jpg"><img class="wp-image-103693951 size-medium" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103693951-c59cb57b3b865fe717453c8c70aa3cd3-600x441.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="441" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">2023年4月18日，2022-2023年度<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/欧冠八强战.md#1">欧冠八强战</a>第二回合，王家马德里队客场2比0力克切尔西队，全场比赛技术统计。（新唐人制图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>本场比赛，尽管切尔西队最终0比2落败被淘汰出局，但是虽败犹荣。球员们展现出的拚搏精神和不屈斗志让人们看到了这支球队的希望，不得不说这是主教练兰帕德的功劳。赛后斯坦福桥球场的球迷们也为自己的主队鼓掌，感谢他们的努力付出，奉献了一场精彩的比赛。</p>
<p>王家马德里队<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/半决赛.md#1">半决赛</a>的对手将是另一场1/4决赛——曼城和拜仁之间的胜者。</p>

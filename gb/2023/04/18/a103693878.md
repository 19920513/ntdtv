<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>欧冠：AC米兰淘汰那不勒斯 时隔16年再进四强</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103694167-GettyImages-1251962081-800x450.jpg" />
<h6>2023年4月18日，欧冠八强战第二回合，AC米兰客场1比1逼平那不勒斯，从而以总比分2比1淘汰对手，时隔16年再次挺进欧冠半决赛。(ALBERTO PIZZOLI/AFP via Getty Images)</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【新唐人北京时间2023年04月19日讯】当地时间4月18日，2022-2023赛季欧冠1/4决赛第二回合，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/ac米兰队.md#1">AC米兰队</a>客场1比1逼平那不勒斯队，从而以总比分2比1淘汰对手，时隔16年再次入围欧冠四强。比赛中两队中锋<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/奥西姆亨.md#1">奥西姆亨</a>和<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/吉鲁.md#1">吉鲁</a>为各自球队建功。</p>
<p>两队均征战意甲，彼此非常熟悉。从历史战绩上看，那不勒斯队12胜11平8负，稍占上风。一周前在欧冠的首回合比赛中，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/ac米兰队.md#1">AC米兰队</a>主场1比0小胜，本场比赛带着一球领先的优势来到客场作战。</p>
<p>上半时第21分钟，AC米兰队边锋<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/拉斐尔·莱奥.md#1">拉斐尔·莱奥</a>（Rafael Leao）在禁区中路分边时被对方铲倒，主裁判判罚点球。中锋<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/吉鲁.md#1">吉鲁</a>（Olivier Giroud）操刀主罚，但门将梅里特（Alex Meret）表现神勇，判断对方向将球扑出。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103694128" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103694128-GettyImages-1251954528.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103694128" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103694128-GettyImages-1251954528-600x400.jpg" alt="Napoli's Italian goalkeeper Alex Meret (R) defelects a penalty shot by AC Milan's French forward Olivier Giroud (L) during the UEFA Champions League quarter-finals second leg football match between SSC Napoli and AC Milan on April 18, 2023 at the Diego-Maradona stadium in Naples. (Photo by Andreas SOLARO / AFP) " width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">第22分钟，AC米兰队获得点球机会，中锋吉鲁射出的点球被对方门将神勇扑出。(ANDREAS SOLARO/AFP via Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>第28分钟，那不勒斯队门将梅里特在AC米兰队球员的逼抢下仓促出球，被对方抢断，但又将功补过化解了中锋吉鲁在禁区中路的扫射。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103694146" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103694146-GettyImages-1251955114.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103694146" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103694146-GettyImages-1251955114-600x400.jpg" alt="AC Milan's French forward Olivier Giroud (C) challenges Napoli's French midfielder Tanguy Ndombele (L) and Napoli's Kosovan defender Amir Rrahmani during the UEFA Champions League quarter-finals second leg football match between SSC Napoli and AC Milan on April 18, 2023 at the Diego-Maradona stadium in Naples. (Photo by Alberto PIZZOLI / AFP) " width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">第28分钟，AC米兰队前场抢断成功，中锋吉鲁接队友传球，在禁区内扫射被对方门将梅里特化解。(ALBERTO PIZZOLI/AFP via Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>第43分钟，AC米兰队边锋<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/拉斐尔·莱奥.md#1">拉斐尔·莱奥</a>后场断球，利用速度带球长途奔袭60米后横传，中锋吉鲁在门前轻松推射空门得分，将比分改写为1比0。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103694155" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103694155-GettyImages-1251954828.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103694155" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103694155-GettyImages-1251954828-600x400.jpg" alt="AC Milan's Portuguese forward Rafael Leao makes a decisive assist for AC Milan's French forward Olivier Giroud (not in picture) to open the scoring during the UEFA Champions League quarter-finals second leg football match between SSC Napoli and AC Milan on April 18, 2023 at the Diego-Maradona stadium in Naples. (Photo by Alberto PIZZOLI / AFP) " width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">第43分钟，AC米兰队边锋拉斐尔·莱奥后场断球，利用速度带球长途奔袭60米后助攻队友得分。(ALBERTO PIZZOLI/AFP via Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>中场休息时，AC米兰队暂时以一球领先，双方下半场易边再战。</p>
<p>第46分钟，那不勒斯队边锋克瓦拉茨克赫利亚（Khvicha Kvaratskhelia）左路带球突破对方防守，在小禁区边缘小角度射门，高出横梁。</p>
<p>第58分钟，那不勒斯队依然从左路发动进攻，克瓦拉茨克赫利亚突破两人夹防，但在禁区内的射门失去准星，再次高出横梁。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103694183" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103694183-GettyImages-1251958018.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103694183" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103694183-GettyImages-1251958018-600x400.jpg" alt="Napoli's Georgian forward Khvicha Kvaratskhelia (L) shoots on target past AC Milan's Danish defender Simon Kjaer during the UEFA Champions League quarter-finals second leg football match between SSC Napoli and AC Milan on April 18, 2023 at the Diego-Maradona stadium in Naples. " width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">第58分钟，那不勒斯队边锋克瓦拉茨克赫利亚左路突破两人的夹防，但禁区内的射门高出横梁。(ALBERTO PIZZOLI/AFP via Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>第80分钟，那不勒斯队边后卫迪洛伦佐（Giovanni Di Lorenzo）禁区右路底线传中，对方后卫菲卡约·托莫里（Fikayo Tomori）在封堵时手碰到球犯规，主裁判将手指向点球点。</p>
<p>主队边锋克瓦拉茨克赫利亚操刀主罚，但对方门将迈尼昂（Mike Maignan）同样表现神勇，判断准确将球扑出底线。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103694192" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103694192-GettyImages-1251959933.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103694192" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103694192-GettyImages-1251959933-600x337.jpg" alt="Napoli's Georgian forward Khvicha Kvaratskhelia reacts after missing a penalty during the UEFA Champions League quarter-finals second leg football match between SSC Napoli and AC Milan on April 18, 2023 at the Diego-Maradona stadium in Naples. (Photo by Alberto PIZZOLI / AFP) " width="600" b="337" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">第82分钟，那不勒斯队获得点球机会，但是边锋克瓦拉茨克赫利亚的射门被对方门将扑出，错失得分良机。(ALBERTO PIZZOLI/AFP via Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>本场比赛两队门将的表现都十分抢眼，各扑出对方一个点球。</p>
<p>伤停补时第3分钟，那不勒斯队的进攻终于得到回报。中场球员拉斯帕多里（Giacomo Raspadori）右路传中，中锋<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/奥西姆亨.md#1">奥西姆亨</a>（Victor Osimhen）中路抢点攻门，顶入球门右下角，将比分扳平。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103694198" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103694198-GettyImages-1251960496.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103694198" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103694198-GettyImages-1251960496-600x338.jpg" alt="Napoli's Nigerian forward Victor Osimhen (2ndL) scores a last minute header past AC Milan's French goalkeeper Mike Maignan during the UEFA Champions League quarter-finals second leg football match between SSC Napoli and AC Milan on April 18, 2023 at the Diego-Maradona stadium in Naples. (Photo by Andreas SOLARO / AFP) " width="600" b="338" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">伤停补时第3分钟，那不勒斯队右路传中，中锋奥西姆亨抢点攻门，顶入球门右下角。(ANDREAS SOLARO/AFP via Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>然而那不勒斯队的进球来得太迟了，最终全场比赛结束，AC米兰队客场逼平那不勒斯队，从而以2比1的总比分，晋级欧冠四强。</p>
<p>本场比赛为“红黑军团”奉献助攻的拉斐尔·莱奥被欧足联技术观察小组评为本场比赛最佳球员。他们认为，莱奥的进攻能力非常出色，本场比赛不仅创造了点球，还助攻队友得分。对方完全盯防不住，他的天赋帮助球队打入欧冠半决赛。</p>
<p>那不勒斯首发（4-3-3）：1-梅里特/6-鲁伊（34&#8217;↑17-奥利维拉），5-胡安·热苏斯，13-拉赫玛尼（74&#8217;↑55-奥斯蒂高），22-迪洛伦佐/20-齐林斯基（74&#8217;↑81-拉斯帕多里），68-罗伯特卡，91-恩东贝莱（63&#8217;↑7-埃尔马斯）/77-克瓦拉茨克赫利亚，9-奥西姆亨，21-波利塔诺（34&#8217;↑11-洛扎诺）</p>
<p>AC米兰队（4-2-3-1）：16-迈尼昂/19-特奥·埃尔南德斯，23-菲卡约·托莫里，24-克亚尔，2-卡拉布里亚/8-托纳利，33-克鲁尼奇/17-拉斐尔·莱奥（84&#8217;↑56-萨勒马克尔斯），4-本纳塞尔，10-迪亚斯（59&#8217;↑30-儒尼奥尔·梅西亚斯）/9-吉鲁（68&#8217;↑27-奥里吉）</p>
<figure id="attachment_103694200" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103694200-3a36c0c65bcef505a409d04f4d448ccc.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103694200" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/04/id103694200-3a36c0c65bcef505a409d04f4d448ccc-600x439.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="439" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">2023年4月18日，2022-2023年度<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/欧冠八强战.md#1">欧冠八强战</a>第二回合，AC米兰队客场1比1逼平那不勒斯队，全场比赛技术统计。（新唐人制图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>此役那不勒斯队可谓拼尽全力，然而尽管他们射门高达23次，但是实际威胁性并不大，反而对手的几次反击颇具威胁，因此七届欧冠得主AC米兰队凭借稳固的防守笑到最后。</p>
<p>“红黑军团”半决赛的对手将是另一场1/4决赛——国际米兰与本菲卡的胜者。</p>

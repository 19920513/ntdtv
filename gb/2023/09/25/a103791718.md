<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>茄子丁炒肉的家常做法 鲜香入味</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id103791759-137f742b7dee775e542683225bc2edae-800x450.png" />
<h6>茄子丁炒肉的家常做法。（作者提供）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>常言道，秋季的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/茄子.md#1">茄子</a>最好吃，茄子富含多种人体所需的营养元素，秋天吃茄子有助于缓解“秋燥”。今天和大家分享，茄子的好吃又简单的做法，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/茄子丁炒肉.md#1">茄子丁炒肉</a>，鲜香入味，营养丰富。</p>
<p>1.准备2根<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/茄子.md#1">茄子</a>洗净，去除外皮，切成丁。</p>
<p>2.准备一盆清水，加入一点食用醋，放人茄子丁，水没过茄子，浸泡15分钟后，控水捞出。这样茄子不会变黑。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103791739" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103791739" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id103791739-5ded1df36def96b7dc4b3bf794209ae1-600x481.png" alt="" width="600" b="481" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">茄子丁（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3.一小块猪肉，切成丁，放人盘中备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103791742" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103791742" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id103791742-fc08c5610614959c3b8a2316adcf6783-600x447.png" alt="" width="600" b="447" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4.小葱切成段，姜切成丝，大蒜切成片，一粒八角。放人盘中备用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103791754" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103791754" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id103791754-download-600x589.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="589" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>5.准备一个料汁：碗中加入一勺生抽，一勺耗油，适量盐，少许白糖提鲜。</p>
<p>6.起锅烧热后，加入适量的食用油，下入，葱、姜、蒜和大料炒香，放人肉片，加入一点甜面酱，翻炒出香味，再加入茄子丁，翻炒变色，倒入调制好的料汁，加入一打勺清水，大约炖10分钟，就可以出锅了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103791762" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103791762" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id103791762-c6561e6b1e718b07dc397afa085d81b0-600x506.png" alt="" width="600" b="506" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（作者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这样一道，健康美味的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/茄子丁炒肉.md#1">茄子丁炒肉</a>就做好了。</p>

<h2>第一道~左宗棠鸡</h2>
<figure id="attachment_103787243" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103787243" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" fetchpriority="high" class="size-medium wp-image-103787243" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id103787243-1-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103787243" class="wp-caption-text">左宗棠鸡 鸡腿肉条要过油泡熟</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_103787244" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103787244" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-103787244" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id103787244-2-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103787244" class="wp-caption-text">好吃的左宗棠鸡</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_103787245" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103787245" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-103787245" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id103787245-3-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103787245" class="wp-caption-text">左宗棠鸡一定要选肉鸡</figcaption></figure>
<p>材料：<br />
肉鸡鸡腿肉1支、葱段40克、蒜片30克、红辣椒片60克。</p>
酱油膏20克、酱油15克、番茄酱30克、糖20克、白醋15克、水150克。</p>
<p>做法：<br />
1. 鸡腿肉切成条，先用全蛋、太白粉、米酒、酱油腌10-15分钟，开大火约150-160度，将鸡腿肉条过油泡熟至金黄色，起锅备用。<br />
2. 原锅内不用加油，直接放蒜、姜、辣椒片爆香，再放入番茄酱、酱油膏，关最小火再放入酱油、酒、水，再开大火再加入糖、白醋、葱段。<br />
3. 勾薄芡收汁即可，(也可再加入少许红油)。</p>
<h2>第二道~鸡丝拉皮</h2>
<figure id="attachment_103787246" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103787246" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-medium wp-image-103787246" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id103787246-11-600x337.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103787246" class="wp-caption-text">鸡丝拉皮用鸡胸肉</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_103787247" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103787247" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-medium wp-image-103787247" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id103787247-22-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103787247" class="wp-caption-text">鸡丝拉皮爽口美味</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_103787248" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103787248" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-medium wp-image-103787248" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id103787248-33-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103787248" class="wp-caption-text">鸡丝拉皮用全蛋太白粉油腌后要先去冷冻</figcaption></figure>
鸡胸肉1片、豆粉皮5张、小黄瓜2条（切丝）。</p>
<p>调味料：<br />
芝麻酱60克、酱油膏30克、糖20克、乌醋15克、红油20克。</p>
1. 鸡胸肉拍平切片再切丝，用全蛋、太白粉、油腌后先去冷冻，要煮的时候再拿出来。<br />
2. 白色的绿豆粉皮切条，小黄瓜切丝。<br />
3. 绿豆粉皮川烫后泡冷开水备用。<br />
4. 鸡胸肉丝川烫后也冲开水备用。<br />
5. 绿豆粉皮加些红油，摆盘，盘边加小黄瓜丝。<br />
6. 碗内放入芝麻酱、酱油膏、糖、乌醋，加入30克的开水，再加些红油拌匀。<br />
7. 芝麻酱汁淋到绿豆粉皮上，再加上鸡胸肉即可。</p>
<h2>第三道~炸汤圆</h2>
<figure id="attachment_103787249" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103787249" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-medium wp-image-103787249" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id103787249-111-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103787249" class="wp-caption-text">炸汤圆 油温不可超过140度</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_103787250" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103787250" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-medium wp-image-103787250" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id103787250-222-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103787250" class="wp-caption-text">炸汤圆甜嘴让大家心甜甜</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_103787251" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103787251" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-medium wp-image-103787251" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id103787251-333-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103787251" class="wp-caption-text">炸汤圆 汤圆可沾面粉及水 补满外皮裂缝</figcaption></figure>
<p>材料：<br />
芝麻汤圆10颗、花生汤圆10颗、花生粉100g、葡萄干50g、酥炸粉100g。</p>
<p>做法：<br />
1. 汤圆沾些面粉及加一点点水让外皮湿润，可再裹上酥炸粉。<br />
2. 起油锅，中小火油温约130度-140度(油温不可超过140度会爆开）。炸至膨胀上色，即可捞起撒上花生粉、葡萄干即可装盘食用。</p>

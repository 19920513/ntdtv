<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>健康小常识 对每个人都有用</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id103784180-DSC_0193_PLS-800x450.jpg" />
<h6>多掌握一些健康小常识，对我们日常生活有很大的帮助。（童宇／大纪元）</h6>
<hr>
	<p><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/生活小常识.md#1">生活小常识</a>小处着眼，受益无穷。生活中的小事情往往能带给我们意想不到的效果。下面几个生活小常识，相信对每个人都能有用。</p>
<p>1：将<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/虾仁.md#1">虾仁</a>放入碗中，加一点精盐、食用碱粉，用手抓搓一会儿，再用清水浸泡洗净。这样炒出的新鲜虾仁晶亮透明，爽嫩可口。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103784185" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103784185" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id103784185-99bbd76db5ba960c73ead0c74dee56f7-600x338.png" alt="" width="600" b="338" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/虾仁.md#1">虾仁</a>（美食天堂）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2: 和饺子面，在一斤面粉里掺入六个蛋清，使面里蛋白质增加，包好的饺子下锅后，蛋白质很快凝固收缩，饺子起锅后收水快，不易粘连。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103784186" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103784186" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id103784186-wrappers1-600x400-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">和饺子面，加入蛋清，可增加蛋白质。（美食天堂）</figcaption></figure>
<p>3：<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/豆腐.md#1">豆腐</a>下锅前，先在开水中浸泡十分钟，可以除去卤水味道，这样做出的豆腐不但口感好，而且味美香甜。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103784188" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103784188" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id103784188-IMG_3466-600x649-600x649.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="649" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">大<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/豆腐.md#1">豆腐</a>。（李雪/新唐人）</figcaption></figure>
<p>4：瓜果的清洗：食用前，先将瓜果在盐水中浸泡20-30分钟，可去除瓜果表皮残留的农药或寄生虫卵，且盐水还有杀灭某些病菌的作用。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103784190" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103784190" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id103784190-shutterstock_529325761-600x400-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">怎样洗瓜果才能尽可能去除污染物和病毒呢？ (Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p>5：空腹不宜吃过多柿子。如果空腹吃过多柿子，由于胃里的游离酸含量高时，就会凝结成块，形成柿石，引起肚子疼，呕吐等。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103784191" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103784191" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/09/id103784191-shutterstock_778059049-600x400-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">空腹不宜吃过多柿子。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>

<h2>第一道~<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/柠檬鸡片.md#1">柠檬鸡片</a></h2>
<figure id="attachment_103762452" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103762452" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id103762452-1-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">柠檬鸡片腌肉料 盐酒蛋黄</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_103762453" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103762453" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id103762453-2-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">柠檬鸡片简单好做</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_103762454" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103762454" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id103762454-3-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">柠檬鸡片 内软外酥</figcaption></figure>
<p>材料：鸡胸肉300g。</p>
<p>腌肉料：盐5g，料酒5g，蛋黄2个。</p>
<p>粉料：太白粉100g，玉米粉100g。</p>
<p>综合调味料：新鲜柠檬汁20g、糖20g、水30g、盐3g、太白粉10g、香油少许。</p>
<p>做法：<br />
1.将鸡胸肉去皮取肉，平均片切2.5公分宽5公分长之薄片，在碗内先将腌鸡料调匀，放入鸡片轻轻拌匀，腌5分钟。<br />
2.将腌好之鸡片沾粉放入150度油锅炸约1分钟，肉浮起来即可捞起，再将油温加至180度回炸约10秒捞起，排入已放好生菜的盘中。<br />
3.另用一新锅加一匙油及水、糖，溶化后加入柠檬汁拌匀后勾芡。<br />
4.将汁淋到盘中鸡片上即可食用。</p>
<h2>第二道~<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/椒盐炒里肌.md#1">椒盐炒里肌</a></h2>
<figure id="attachment_103762455" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103762455" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id103762455-11-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">椒盐炒里肌 香味四溢</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_103762456" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103762456" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id103762456-22-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">椒盐炒里肌 小火爆香黑胡椒粒</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_103762457" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103762457" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id103762457-33-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">椒盐炒里肌 回炸抢酥涮嘴</figcaption></figure>
<p>材料：猪里肌(300g)。葱花，蒜末，红辣椒末各5g。</p>
<p>腌肉料：鸡蛋1个、玉米粉100g、盐5g。</p>
<p>调味料：黑胡椒粒5g、盐3g。</p>
<p>做法：<br />
1. 里肌肉去筋再逆纹切片后用刀背拍一下。<br />
2. 太白粉、玉米粉混匀后加入鸡蛋、盐，全部打匀后倒入刚拍打好的肉片里。<br />
3. 肉料调成糊状并浸渍十分钟。再逐条入150度的油?炸约1分钟捞出，再将油温加至180度，回炸抢酥约十秒钟，关火捞起。<br />
4. 起一新锅加少许油，小火爆香黑胡椒粒、葱花、蒜末、红辣椒末，待香气出来再放入炸好的里肌肉，加少许盐巴拌均匀即可盛盘。</p>
<h2>第三道~<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/黄金流沙丝瓜镶雪白.md#1">黄金流沙丝瓜镶雪白</a></h2>
<figure id="attachment_103762458" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103762458" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id103762458-111-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">黄金流沙丝瓜镶雪宴客大方</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_103762459" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103762459" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id103762459-222-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">黄金流沙丝瓜镶雪白 用汤匙刮红萝卜泥</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_103762460" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img class="size-medium wp-image-103762460" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2023/08/id103762460-333-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">黄金流沙丝瓜镶雪白 丝瓜用小汤匙挖一个洞底要留着</figcaption></figure>
<p>材料：澎湖丝瓜1根(去皮切段4公分)，雪白菇200g，咸蛋黄1粒(切碎)，洋葱50g(切碎)，蟹腿肉100g(切小粒)，红萝卜1根(刮红萝卜泥)80g，清水200g。</p>
<p>调味料：盐巴10g、白胡椒粉少许、香油少许。</p>
<p>做法：<br />
1.将丝瓜去皮头尾切掉由中间切一半，再切段2.5-3公分，用小汤匙挖一个洞 (底要留着)塞入4-5朵的雪白菇，蒸4分钟，备用。<br />
2.咸蛋黄切碎，洋葱切碎，蟹腿肉切粒，红萝卜去皮，用汤匙刮红萝卜泥备用。<br />
3.起油锅加入香油，爆香盐蛋黄、洋葱末，待香味出来放入蟹腿肉、红萝卜泥，加入水或高汤、盐、胡椒粉，煮约五分钟滚后芶芡备用。<br />
4.将流沙酱汁淋在盘中，再将蒸好的丝瓜镶雪白菇排放盘中，把剩余的流沙酱汁淋至丝瓜上，即可食用。</p>

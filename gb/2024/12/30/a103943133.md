<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【林书阳-精彩好棋专栏】第77篇：强大的中生代</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id103943134-6735c351e3d23-868x488.jpg" />
<h6>2024年11月14日，台湾围棋第20届“国手赛”挑战者决定战，林立祥九段（前左）执白中盘胜赖均辅九段（前右）。（海峰棋院提供）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【新唐人北京时间2024年12月31日讯】接近年末的时刻，各大棋赛的得奖者也纷纷出炉。每位棋手都会为自己的年度排名而竭尽全力！一年看下来，在顶尖群中较年轻的棋手有许皓鋐、<ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/赖均辅">赖均辅</a>、陈祈睿，以及更年轻的徐靖恩等人。但是中生代棋手群的力量也不容小觑，包括王元均、林君谚、<ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/林立祥">林立祥</a>等依旧实力健在。这三位棋手也经常有令人惊喜的表现！</p>
<p>本期要介绍近期的国手战，这是从单败淘汰决出挑战者的赛制，一路过关斩将拼到最后挑战者决定战的两位棋手分别是<ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/赖均辅">赖均辅</a>和<ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/林立祥">林立祥</a>。以下就来欣赏这盘棋的精华。</p>
<p>图一：这盘棋赖均辅执黑，一上来焦点在右边。黑1碰是相当厉害的一&#33879;，瞄准着下一手A扳住，同时也想加强自身，黑棋伺机对白右边拆二两子进行攻击。那么持白棋的林立祥会如何应对呢？</p>
<figure id="attachment_103943135" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103943135" style="width: 498px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id103943135-image00127.png"><img decoding="async" fetchpriority="high" class="size-full wp-image-103943135" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id103943135-image00127.png" alt="" width="498" b="498" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103943135" class="wp-caption-text">台湾围棋第20届“<ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/国手赛">国手赛</a>”挑战者决定战，赖均辅（黑）VS.林立祥（白），图一。<ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/海峰棋院">海峰棋院</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>图二：实战白1、3扳完黏，照应。林立祥的棋风本身就擅长治孤，也考验着黑棋如何进攻右边白两子。实战黑棋4、6点完长，白7贴出必然。接着黑棋下一手非常重要！该如何攻击白棋呢？</p>
<figure id="attachment_103943136" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103943136" style="width: 498px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id103943136-image00220.png"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-103943136" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id103943136-image00220.png" alt="" width="498" b="498" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103943136" class="wp-caption-text">台湾围棋第20届“<ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/国手赛">国手赛</a>”挑战者决定战，赖均辅（黑）VS.林立祥（白），图二。<ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/海峰棋院">海峰棋院</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>图三：此时黑棋应1尖二路，白2挡，黑3跳，再补强自身。如此黑棋一边攻击白棋一边将右上角实地化，才是较为顺畅的下法。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103943137" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103943137" style="width: 498px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id103943137-image00327.png"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-103943137" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id103943137-image00327.png" alt="" width="498" b="498" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103943137" class="wp-caption-text">台湾围棋第20届“国手赛”挑战者决定战，赖均辅（黑）VS.林立祥（白），图三。（海峰棋院提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>图四：实战赖均辅选择黑1、3冲断，有些操之过急，白4简单接上，黑5爬回虽然吃到白两子。但也因为白A长会是绝对先手，因此黑棋右上二间缔角相对很薄弱，对黑棋来说有些得不偿失。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103943138" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103943138" style="width: 498px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id103943138-image00420.png"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-full wp-image-103943138" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id103943138-image00420.png" alt="" width="498" b="498" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103943138" class="wp-caption-text">台湾围棋第20届“国手赛”挑战者决定战，赖均辅（黑）VS.林立祥（白），图四。（海峰棋院提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>图五：实战林立祥白1、3、5简明碰完顶完断，吃掉黑棋角上一子即可。如此可以看到黑棋虽然吃掉右边白两子，但此前中央黑棋碰退和白棋扳完黏（方块数子）交换，明显亏损，也使得左下黑棋“圆圈”数子厚势无法发挥太多作用。因此目前是白棋较好下的局面。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103943139" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103943139" style="width: 498px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id103943139-image00527.png"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-full wp-image-103943139" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id103943139-image00527.png" alt="" width="498" b="498" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103943139" class="wp-caption-text">台湾围棋第20届“国手赛”挑战者决定战，赖均辅（黑）VS.林立祥（白），图五。（海峰棋院提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>图六：黑1托完3连扳，是全盘最大的大场。白4单立，是常见的应对，白6打吃时，也许赖均辅意识到局面不利，并未A黏，而是黑7打吃，开起劫争。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103943140" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103943140" style="width: 498px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id103943140-image00620.png"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-full wp-image-103943140" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id103943140-image00620.png" alt="" width="498" b="498" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103943140" class="wp-caption-text">台湾围棋第20届“国手赛”挑战者决定战，赖均辅（黑）VS.林立祥（白），图六。（海峰棋院提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>图七：实战白1提以下形成劫争，就在白5冲时，黑6打吃，扩大劫争的下法非常严厉。此下法的前提是必须要有很多的劫材！黑棋巨大的一枚劫材在哪里呢？</p>
<figure id="attachment_103943141" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103943141" style="width: 498px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id103943141-image00728.png"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-full wp-image-103943141" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id103943141-image00728.png" alt="" width="498" b="523" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103943141" class="wp-caption-text">台湾围棋第20届“国手赛”挑战者决定战，赖均辅（黑）VS.林立祥（白），图七。（海峰棋院提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>图八：实战，黑2挖打，是绝好的劫材，接着黑棋肯定是万劫不应。白棋选择5、7断开黑棋两子，收获也不小！黑8挡回，必然。白9上面拆边，此手棋也可考虑左边A飞吃净两子。这时局面依然是白棋稍优。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103943142" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103943142" style="width: 498px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id103943142-image00819.png"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-full wp-image-103943142" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id103943142-image00819.png" alt="" width="498" b="523" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103943142" class="wp-caption-text">台湾围棋第20届“国手赛”挑战者决定战，赖均辅（黑）VS.林立祥（白），图八。（海峰棋院提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>图九：实战，黑1接完3拐，寻求做活，但白棋4打吃有些优势意识。此手可以考虑A扳，黑B挡，白C二二点入，强杀黑棋。如此黑棋难办。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103943144" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103943144" style="width: 498px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id103943144-image00924.png"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-full wp-image-103943144" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id103943144-image00924.png" alt="" width="498" b="498" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103943144" class="wp-caption-text">台湾围棋第20届“国手赛”挑战者决定战，赖均辅（黑）VS.林立祥（白），图九。（海峰棋院提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>图十：左下让黑棋做活后，局面回到了五五开。但此时黑1上面点入，略显急躁，右上角先A二二贴和白B先交换掉，才是当务之急！此先手没下到，对后面的战局影响甚大！</p>
<figure id="attachment_103943147" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103943147" style="width: 498px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id103943147-image01014.png"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-full wp-image-103943147" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id103943147-image01014.png" alt="" width="498" b="498" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103943147" class="wp-caption-text">台湾围棋第20届“国手赛”挑战者决定战，赖均辅（黑）VS.林立祥（白），图十。（海峰棋院提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>图十一：实战，白1毫不犹豫挡住，黑2尖手筋，以下至白8顶住，看似左上吃掉白棋三子收获不小。但被白9拐到后，上面黑六子显得非常的薄。之后不论白棋是A点进去，或是先B拖，黑C挡，白A再夹都很严厉。更何况白D扳还是先手，上面黑棋更加危险。次一手绝艺推荐黑棋在C尖补棋，但是对人类棋手来说，实在很难这么忍耐。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103943148" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103943148" style="width: 498px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id103943148-image01117.png"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-full wp-image-103943148" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id103943148-image01117.png" alt="" width="498" b="498" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103943148" class="wp-caption-text">台湾围棋第20届“国手赛”挑战者决定战，赖均辅（黑）VS.林立祥（白），图十一。（海峰棋院提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>图十二：经过下面一整串的战斗，白棋优势更加确立。此时白棋上面动手是最好的时机！白1扳先手，接着白3夹以下的下法，也相当严厉。白9渡过后，黑棋整块没活，周围也都是白棋的子力，几乎宣判了黑棋死刑。最终这盘棋就是由林立祥白棋中盘获胜。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103943149" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103943149" style="width: 498px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id103943149-image0128.png"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-full wp-image-103943149" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/12/id103943149-image0128.png" alt="" width="498" b="498" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103943149" class="wp-caption-text">台湾围棋第20届“国手赛”挑战者决定战，赖均辅（黑）VS.林立祥（白），图十二。（海峰棋院提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>全局棋谱：<ahref="https://www.higohigo.org/#/kifu/39400">林立祥 执白中盘胜 赖均辅</a></p>
<p>林立祥以一盘几乎完胜的内容一举获得“国手赛”的挑战权，相当不容易，也宣示着资深中生代棋手依旧相当有实力。期盼在资深棋手的带领下也能刺激年轻棋手们有好表现。</p>

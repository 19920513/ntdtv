<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>中共公务员网上举报成风 公检法纪委官员轮番上场</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id103903947-FotoJet-868x488.jpg" />
<h6>大量中共体制内人员上网举报。（网络视频截图合成）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【新唐人北京时间2024年08月08日讯】近来中共体制内官员密集上网举报，声称自己受到“不公”对待，其中包括大量公检法和纪委官员。网民嘲讽，中共体制已经“烂透了”。</p>
<p>近日网上流出大量实名举报视频，其中不乏中共体制内人员，甚至来自中共的“执法部门”，以及负责整肃官员的<ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/中共纪委">中共纪委</a>。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103903940" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103903940" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id103903940-GUcNrNeW8AA1Dli.jpg"><img decoding="async" fetchpriority="high" class="wp-image-103903940" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id103903940-GUcNrNeW8AA1Dli.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="828" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103903940" class="wp-caption-text">大陆Bilibili短视频平台充斥举报视频，其中包括许多中共体制内官员的举报。（网页截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>近日，黑龙江哈尔滨市道外区一名检察官实名举报，该区法院和政法委采用非法证据，包庇纵容罪犯，枉断案件。他要求法院从新处理此案，“还检察官一个清白”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103903941" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103903941" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id103903941-f44d50bef63005586362cb48d547d4d2.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="wp-image-103903941" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id103903941-f44d50bef63005586362cb48d547d4d2.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="415" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103903941" class="wp-caption-text">哈尔滨检察官举报法官。（网络视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>辽宁盘锦盘山县原法官举报辽宁省高级法院的官员贪赃枉法。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103903942" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103903942" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id103903942-GUXFAgNWkAA-Nyh.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="wp-image-103903942" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id103903942-GUXFAgNWkAA-Nyh-600x1125.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="563" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103903942" class="wp-caption-text">辽宁盘锦盘山县原法官举报辽宁省高级法院的官员.（网络视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>福建莆田市涵江区法院法官举报涵江区法院原院长、莆田市中级法院党组政治部主任买官卖官。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103903943" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103903943" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id103903943-cd8793a996ba15be1dfd9f6741644226.jpg"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="wp-image-103903943" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id103903943-cd8793a996ba15be1dfd9f6741644226.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="445" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103903943" class="wp-caption-text">福建莆田市涵江区法院法官举报上级官员。（网络视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>吉林省四平市一名原纪委官员举报法院拒绝他再次申诉，阻止他为自己伸冤。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103903944" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103903944" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id103903944-6ef97aba9b5f229b7c7cea0faf973e5e.jpg"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="wp-image-103903944" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id103903944-6ef97aba9b5f229b7c7cea0faf973e5e.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="476" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103903944" class="wp-caption-text">四平市原纪委官员举报法院法官。（网络视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>唐山烧烤店打人案出警公安陈志伟，在网上实名举报市级领导曾拍桌子威胁他“照着稿子念”，导致他在该案中因为“背黑锅”入狱。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103903945" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103903945" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id103903945-https_______mmbiz.qpic_.cn___sz_mmbiz_png___SszalaZp6RTMzib37pOIoe7RSnLW3X6aRwgnOLib9ic4WD23UbwyfQPthCT3WKz3rcMibiaiaTrdsRaPdEAdxQSQ1fvQ___640_wx_fmtpngfromappmsg.jpg"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="wp-image-103903945" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id103903945-https_______mmbiz.qpic_.cn___sz_mmbiz_png___SszalaZp6RTMzib37pOIoe7RSnLW3X6aRwgnOLib9ic4WD23UbwyfQPthCT3WKz3rcMibiaiaTrdsRaPdEAdxQSQ1fvQ___640_wx_fmtpngfromappmsg-600x981.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="490" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103903945" class="wp-caption-text">唐山烧烤店打人案出警公安举报上级官员让他“背黑锅”入狱。（网络视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>此外，今年5月，重庆市梁平区司法局一名官员实名举报梁平区组织部正副部长等人，在处理“干部被非法调度的申诉”时不公，滥用职权等。这段视频近日又在网上流传。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103903946" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103903946" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id103903946-476e987b96ec08dde2cc52d1c83c46d3-1.jpg"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="wp-image-103903946" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id103903946-476e987b96ec08dde2cc52d1c83c46d3-1.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="516" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103903946" class="wp-caption-text">重庆市梁平区司法局官员举报组织部官员。（网络视频截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在海外社交平台上，有网民留言说，中国大陆从平民百姓到官员，都只能上网举报，说明早已法治无存。还有网友称，中共官员们密集地上网举报，意味着“党已经对于秩序无能为力了，和王朝末年的朝纲废弛一样”。</p>

<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>蓝鲸财经主编朋友圈发“杀妻”讯 自称家庭不睦心态崩</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id103909542-FotoJet-2024-08-30T143712.095-868x488.jpg" />
<h6>8月30日，蓝鲸财经主编何天骄在微信朋友圈发出“终于把老婆杀了”的信息，其后，又接连发几条信息解释说，因为家庭矛盾致崩溃因而胡言乱语.（网络截图）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【新唐人北京时间2024年08月31日讯】8月30日，<ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/蓝鲸财经主编">蓝鲸财经主编</a><ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/何天骄">何天骄</a>在微信朋友圈发出“终于把老婆杀了”的信息，其后，又接连发几条信息解释说，因为家庭矛盾致崩溃因而胡言乱语，引发关注。</p>
<p>网络流传图片显示，名为“蓝鲸文娱财经<ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/何天骄">何天骄</a>”的发的消息说，“终于把老婆杀了”，解脱了，还说，“十八年后又是一条好汉”，并配了血腥的图片。引发网络关注。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103909529" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103909529" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" fetchpriority="high" class="wp-image-103909529" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id103909529-b750952bly1ht5xqialz5j20ky0dodgw.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="261" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103909529" class="wp-caption-text">（网络截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在大陆搜索平台，搜索词中有三条是关于何天骄的。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103909530" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103909530" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" class="wp-image-103909530" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id103909530-c42d8fa5d2e3f7b406580cd0dd153419.png" alt="" width="400" b="332" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103909530" class="wp-caption-text">（网络截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>随后，何天骄在朋友圈一连发了好几条信息，为自己解释，先是说这是玩笑话，给朋友道歉。</p>
<p>第二条信息称，生活压力大，家庭琐事多，一早被打了几十个耳光，脑袋晕不理智，气到发抖，一时不冷静，<ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/心态崩了">心态崩了</a>。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103909532" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103909532" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" class="wp-image-103909532" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id103909532-27edbeec447fb07bffa6fa31feeeb786.png" alt="" width="400" b="211" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103909532" class="wp-caption-text">（网络截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>第三条信息称，家庭不和睦，一大早吵一架，暑假带孩子有点累，<ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/心态崩了">心态崩了</a>。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103909533" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103909533" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="wp-image-103909533" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id103909533-362412557cf5659c356b21ecf5837df9.png" alt="" width="400" b="322" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103909533" class="wp-caption-text">（网络截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>第四条信息说，近期压力大没及时吃药，家庭一直不和睦心累易崩溃，还说正在考虑离婚，一直放不下孩子。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103909534" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103909534" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="wp-image-103909534" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id103909534-b67bb210af6eaaaa988cb1563922740e.png" alt="" width="400" b="441" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103909534" class="wp-caption-text">（网络截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>每一条信息都涉及到家庭不和睦，夫妻吵架，心态崩溃，还不断的道歉。</p>
<p>网友评论说，明明可以离婚的，却偏要搞出这样的事来。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103909535" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103909535" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="wp-image-103909535" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id103909535-63a44f0acebe0d7786ca5d0455c3559e.png" alt="" width="400" b="141" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103909535" class="wp-caption-text">（网络截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>还有网友说，“发着玩的吗， wechat朋友圈是法外之地吗”、“这个必须住院观察治疗”、“这个还不报警啊，万一真的呢” “ 危，这不得为了母幼安全发人身禁止令？到实现也就‘一念之差’ ”</p>
<p>网络公开资料显示，蓝鲸财经是界面·财联社旗下财经信息服务平台。界面·财联社是上海报业集团主管主办，旗下拥有界面、蓝鲸、财联社、摩尔金融等4个财经媒体产。何天骄为蓝鲸产经主编。</p>
<p>（记者李郦综合报导／责任编辑：徐耕文）</p>

<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>台湾围棋十段赛：许皓鋐3:1赖均辅 达成七连霸</h2>
<a href="https://dnuodzs8nln8v.cloudfront.net/Z7gEnZxSx"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/1992513/www/master/t/ntdtv/ntdtv600.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/1992513/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【新唐人北京时间2024年08月13日讯】8月13日，<ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/台湾围棋">台湾围棋</a>第14届“友士杯<ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/十段赛">十段赛</a>”五番棋决赛第4局在海峰棋院结束，两位台湾的世界冠军展开巅峰对决，经过七个多小时的鏖战，亚运会金牌<ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/许皓鋐">许皓鋐</a>九段执黑3.5目的优势击败刚刚在“国手山脉杯”上封王的<ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/赖均辅">赖均辅</a>九段，以总比分3比1成功卫冕“十段”头衔，达成赛事七连霸，写下新纪录。</p>
<p>“<ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/十段赛">十段赛</a>”挑战赛五番胜负进入第4局，以2比1领先的<ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/许皓鋐">许皓鋐</a>延续上一局的气势，前半盘迅速掌握优势，将<ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/赖均辅">赖均辅</a>逼入退无可退的局面。面对困局，赖均辅发挥决不服输的精神，奋力求战。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103905222" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103905222" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id103905222-455048982_901257515379274_1188017022444986708_n.jpg"><img decoding="async" fetchpriority="high" class="size-medium wp-image-103905222" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id103905222-455048982_901257515379274_1188017022444986708_n-600x423.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="423" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103905222" class="wp-caption-text">2024年8月13日，<ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/台湾围棋">台湾围棋</a>第14届“友士杯十段赛”五番棋决赛第4局在海峰棋院拉开战幕，两位台湾的世界冠军许皓鋐九段（前左）和赖均辅九段（前右）展开巅峰对决。（中华职业围棋协会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>棋局进入中盘阶段，由于执黑的许皓鋐棋步过于稳健，让赖均辅抓到反击机会。白棋凭借敏锐的胜负感，瞬间拉近局面。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103905140" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103905140" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id103905140-130-139.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-103905140" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id103905140-130-139-600x599.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="599" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103905140" class="wp-caption-text">台湾围棋第14届“友士杯十段赛”五番棋决赛第4局，许皓鋐（黑）VS.赖均辅（白），第130至139手。（中华职业围棋协会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>许皓鋐坦言，“中后盘整个就是各种看不清的状态”，当时进入读秒阶段，他一度感觉白棋不太行，但思考了很久仍没找到简明的下法，局面也因此变得混乱异常。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103905141" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103905141" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id103905141-207-216.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-103905141" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id103905141-207-216-600x601.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="601" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103905141" class="wp-caption-text">台湾围棋第14届“友士杯十段赛”五番棋决赛第4局，许皓鋐（黑）VS.赖均辅（白），第207至216手。（中华职业围棋协会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>棋局最后的致胜关键取决于右上角的攻防，许皓鋐在右上角成功出棋，迫使赖均辅找了几枚损劫，黑棋才再度占据优势。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103905142" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103905142" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id103905142-222-230.jpg"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-medium wp-image-103905142" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id103905142-222-230-600x599.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="599" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103905142" class="wp-caption-text">台湾围棋第14届“友士杯十段赛”五番棋决赛第4局，许皓鋐（黑）VS.赖均辅（白），第222至230手。（中华职业围棋协会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>许皓鋐赛后表示，他对于今日的棋局内容印象深刻，双方始终都在拉锯；对于个人成功达成七连霸，他表示心情很好，同时也非常感谢友士公司加藤董事长对职业围棋的支持。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103905223" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103905223" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id103905223-455183178_901494372022255_8665633684889819633_n.jpg"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-medium wp-image-103905223" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id103905223-455183178_901494372022255_8665633684889819633_n-600x840.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="840" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103905223" class="wp-caption-text">台湾围棋第14届“友士杯十段赛”赛程赛果。（中华围棋职业协会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>展望下半年，许皓鋐表示，会继续争取国内赛冠军，自认为今年世界赛表现不算太好，接下来的三星杯世界围棋大赛希望可以下出好的内容。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103905224" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103905224" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id103905224-455001827_901494488688910_8039428597179486477_n.jpg"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-medium wp-image-103905224" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id103905224-455001827_901494488688910_8039428597179486477_n-600x789.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="789" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103905224" class="wp-caption-text">第14届“友士杯十段赛”冠军许皓鋐九段。（中华职业围棋协会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>获得十段赛亚军的赖均辅也大方表示，许皓鋐棋风全面，非常强。回顾起这次挑战经历，赖均辅仅对第一盘的内容稍微满意，他说，今天回去后一定会好好检讨这盘棋，会继续努力，带给大家更好的内容。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103905225" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103905225" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id103905225-455055683_901494332022259_5367064804937335385_n.jpg"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-medium wp-image-103905225" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/08/id103905225-455055683_901494332022259_5367064804937335385_n-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103905225" class="wp-caption-text">第14届“友士杯十段赛”亚军赖均辅九段。（中华职业围棋协会提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“友士杯十段赛”由友士公司独家赞助，冠军奖金为70万元、亚军30万元、季军10万元。许皓鋐此次再度卫冕台湾“十段”，不但写下赛事七连霸的新纪录，也创下近十年来台湾头衔赛连霸最长的纪录。</p>
<p>（转自海峰棋院/责任编辑：叶诚允）</p>

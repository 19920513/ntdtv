<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【林书阳-精彩好棋专栏】第75篇：登顶国手山脉(中)</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id103922693-6708ea86f1d79-868x488.jpg" />
<h6>2024年8月4日，第10届“国手山脉杯”国际围棋大会半决赛，台湾棋手赖均辅（右）执黑9目半力克韩国世界冠军卞相壹（左）。（海峰棋院提供）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【新唐人北京时间2024年10月19日讯】第10届“国手山脉国际围棋大会”八强战中，<ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/赖均辅">赖均辅</a>临危不乱，漂亮逆转范廷钰九段，晋级到半决赛。半决赛的对手是韩国世界冠军<ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/卞相壹">卞相壹</a>九段，当然也是一盘硬仗。但从棋风上来说，双方均是力战派，赖均辅只要发挥出他擅长乱战的特点，势必能和对手掰掰手腕！以下就来欣赏这盘棋的精华片段。</p>
<p>图一：<ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/赖均辅">赖均辅</a>执黑棋，这盘棋一上来右下形成了大乱斗，延烧了半个棋盘，现在焦点移到了左上角，白棋最后一手做眼是局部稍缓的下法。此时黑棋较正确的下法是黑A、白B、黑C再拆二，先将上边安定，左边那块黑棋随时可以D做活，相当有弹性。但赖均辅并不以这样为满足，黑棋实战是怎么下呢？</p>
<figure id="attachment_103922699" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103922699" style="width: 498px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id103922699-image00125.png"><img decoding="async" fetchpriority="high" class="size-full wp-image-103922699" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id103922699-image00125.png" alt="" width="498" b="498" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103922699" class="wp-caption-text">图一：第10届“国手山脉国际围棋大会”半决赛，赖均辅（黑）VS.<ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/卞相壹">卞相壹</a>（白）。<ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/海峰棋院">海峰棋院</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>图二：实战赖均辅勇猛的黑1打入！置上方两块棋不顾，这是相当大胆的想法。下一手瞄准着A飞封。白棋会如何应对呢？</p>
<figure id="attachment_103922700" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103922700" style="width: 498px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id103922700-image00218.png"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-103922700" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id103922700-image00218.png" alt="" width="498" b="498" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103922700" class="wp-caption-text">图二：第10届“国手山脉国际围棋大会”半决赛，赖均辅（黑）VS.卞相壹（白）。<ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/海峰棋院">海峰棋院</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>图三：实战白1选择左边夹击，黑2飞封必然。白3冲断，以下激烈战斗不可避免。白9顶住后，黑接下来若A碰，白可B冲；黑若C飞，白可D尖。黑左下暂时不好动手，因此黑棋该怎么办呢？</p>
<figure id="attachment_103922701" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103922701" style="width: 498px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id103922701-image00325.png"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-103922701" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id103922701-image00325.png" alt="" width="498" b="498" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103922701" class="wp-caption-text">图三：第10届“国手山脉国际围棋大会”半决赛，赖均辅（黑）VS.卞相壹（白）。（海峰棋院提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>图四：实战赖均辅黑1点，先问应手，白2黏，黑3叫吃，因读秒而打将，紧接着黑5托，白棋此时若是直接A黏，黑棋较不好处理。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103922702" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103922702" style="width: 498px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id103922702-image00418.png"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-full wp-image-103922702" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id103922702-image00418.png" alt="" width="498" b="498" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103922702" class="wp-caption-text">图四：第10届“国手山脉国际围棋大会”半决赛，赖均辅（黑）VS.卞相壹（白）。（海峰棋院提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>图五：实战白棋选择1挡，让黑棋有2碰的好手！接着白3冲，以下是单行道，到白7打吃，可以发现，黑棋只要A退，先手就可B断，左边黑棋不容易死，而且变得很有弹性。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103922704" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103922704" style="width: 498px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id103922704-image00525.png"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-full wp-image-103922704" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id103922704-image00525.png" alt="" width="498" b="498" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103922704" class="wp-caption-text">图五：第10届“国手山脉国际围棋大会”半决赛，赖均辅（黑）VS.卞相壹（白）。（海峰棋院提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>图六：实战黑棋继续1、3滚打完，再5退回。白6虎，先试问黑棋态度，黑7继续点二路，非常凶悍，赖均辅不是只要做活左边，而是要杀白棋。白8不能A忍，也必须叫吃上去。一场大战不可避免！</p>
<figure id="attachment_103922705" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103922705" style="width: 498px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id103922705-image00618.png"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-full wp-image-103922705" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id103922705-image00618.png" alt="" width="498" b="523" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103922705" class="wp-caption-text">图六：第10届“国手山脉国际围棋大会”半决赛，赖均辅（黑）VS.卞相壹（白）。（海峰棋院提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>图七：实战黑棋1开劫，只此一手，接着黑3尖，找劫，此时考验着白棋的判断是否该A应劫？</p>
<figure id="attachment_103922706" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103922706" style="width: 498px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id103922706-image00726.png"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-full wp-image-103922706" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id103922706-image00726.png" alt="" width="498" b="498" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103922706" class="wp-caption-text">图七：第10届“国手山脉国际围棋大会”半决赛，赖均辅（黑）VS.卞相壹（白）。（海峰棋院提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>图八：实战白1竟然选择消劫，可以说是败着，黑2以下吃通白角后，使得黑棋原本的左右两块孤棋也不必担心了。因此对白棋来说损失相当惨重！白原本1若下6，应劫，形势还尚早。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103922708" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103922708" style="width: 498px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id103922708-image00817.png"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-full wp-image-103922708" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id103922708-image00817.png" alt="" width="498" b="498" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103922708" class="wp-caption-text">图八：第10届“国手山脉国际围棋大会”半决赛，赖均辅（黑）VS.卞相壹（白）。（海峰棋院提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>图九：实战进行至此图，黑1、3跑完扳，又是好手！黑棋将白棋左上角吃得一干二净，也奠定了胜势。最后赖均辅就以大胜收场。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103922709" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103922709" style="width: 498px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id103922709-image00922.png"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-full wp-image-103922709" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id103922709-image00922.png" alt="" width="498" b="498" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103922709" class="wp-caption-text">图九：第10届“国手山脉国际围棋大会”半决赛，赖均辅（黑）VS.卞相壹（白）。（海峰棋院提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>这盘棋赖均辅从中盘战斗发力，制造出关键劫争，对手判断失误急于消劫，种下败因，从而漂亮的拿下胜利，挺进本届“<ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/国手山脉杯">国手山脉杯</a>”决赛！</p>

<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【林书阳-精彩好棋专栏】第74篇：登顶国手山脉(上)</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id103921475-3119mD-kCITHMbZ8iv53NeV4gImlkxBP.jpg" />
<h6>2024年8月3日，第10届“国手山脉杯”国际围棋大会八强战，赖均辅（左）执黑半目逆转击败范廷钰（右）。（海峰棋院提供）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【新唐人北京时间2024年10月15日讯】继2023年许皓鋐夺取亚运金牌后，2024年夏天，台湾棋手又传捷报。在8月初举行的“<ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/国手山脉杯">国手山脉杯</a>”决赛，台湾的<ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/赖均辅">赖均辅</a>击败世界第一人申真谞，夺取了小型杯赛的世界冠军！如此精彩的表现可以说震惊了世界棋坛。</p>
<p>本次台湾参赛代表正是目前最厉害的两人——许皓鋐和<ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/赖均辅">赖均辅</a>。其中许皓鋐第一盘就抽到世界第一人申真谞，经过一番缠斗，遗憾败下阵来。而赖均辅则是面对韩、中两国强敌，一路过关斩将！</p>
<p>本文要来介绍赖均辅夺冠之路重要的三局棋中的第一局，即：八强战对阵曾经拿过“应氏杯”冠军的<ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/范廷钰">范廷钰</a>，赖均辅执黑先行。</p>
<p>图一：这盘棋经过上面的战役后已经是白棋明显优势的局面，白棋实空相对较多又无弱棋。现在问题是白棋该如何转化为胜势？尤其是面对劣势下擅长搅局的赖均辅，白棋得小心翼翼才行。黑棋最后一手贴起，白棋该怎么办呢？</p>
<figure id="attachment_103921455" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103921455" style="width: 498px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id103921455-image00124.png"><img decoding="async" fetchpriority="high" class="wp-image-103921455 size-full" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id103921455-image00124.png" alt="" width="498" b="498" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103921455" class="wp-caption-text">图一：第10届国手山脉国际围棋大会八强战，赖均辅（黑）VS.<ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/范廷钰">范廷钰</a>（白）。<ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/海峰棋院">海峰棋院</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>图二：此时白棋1、3虎完再虎，走厚自身，让黑棋围左边即可。紧接着白5再跳，之后白A跑出也是大棋。如此将是白棋很好下的局面。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103921456" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103921456" style="width: 498px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id103921456-image00217.png"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-103921456" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id103921456-image00217.png" alt="" width="498" b="498" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103921456" class="wp-caption-text">图二：第10届国手山脉国际围棋大会八强战，赖均辅（黑）VS.范廷钰（白）。<ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/海峰棋院">海峰棋院</a>提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>图三：然而实战白棋范廷钰选择白1、3先手扳完打吃后，再5打入左边，被赖均辅黑6一长，这是相当重要的一手。之后白棋上面气紧，黑A、白B，黑C、白D，整体的厚薄差异显而易见。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103921457" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103921457" style="width: 498px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id103921457-image00324.png"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-103921457" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id103921457-image00324.png" alt="" width="498" b="498" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103921457" class="wp-caption-text">图三：第10届国手山脉国际围棋大会八强战，赖均辅（黑）VS.范廷钰（白）。（海峰棋院提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>图四：接着实战白1跳出，黑2冲完4提是先手权利，黑6先渡过左边，白7必须跳回，否则黑A、白B，黑C有绞封中央的手段。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103921460" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103921460" style="width: 498px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id103921460-image00417.png"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-full wp-image-103921460" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id103921460-image00417.png" alt="" width="498" b="498" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103921460" class="wp-caption-text">图四：第10届国手山脉国际围棋大会八强战，赖均辅（黑）VS.范廷钰（白）。（海峰棋院提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>图五：实战接着黑1觑，白2贴完4黏。黑5并回，黑将左边连络后，中央白棋还未连好。白6跳补。黑7走在二路抢大场稍缓，应A碰更为积极。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103921461" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103921461" style="width: 498px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id103921461-image00524.png"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-full wp-image-103921461" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id103921461-image00524.png" alt="" width="498" b="498" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103921461" class="wp-caption-text">图五：第10届国手山脉国际围棋大会八强战，赖均辅（黑）VS.范廷钰（白）。（海峰棋院提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>图六：下面定型后也正式进入了官子战，此时白1飞是明显失着，此手应A贴补棋。此时的黑棋该如何抓住机会？</p>
<figure id="attachment_103921462" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103921462" style="width: 498px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id103921462-image00617.png"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-full wp-image-103921462" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id103921462-image00617.png" alt="" width="498" b="498" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103921462" class="wp-caption-text">图六：第10届国手山脉国际围棋大会八强战，赖均辅（黑）VS.范廷钰（白）。（海峰棋院提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>图七：实战黑1拐，此时动手时机非常恰当，白不能3挡下，否则黑A、白B、黑C、白D、黑F10、白E、黑F、白G、黑H为单行道，可以把白五子断开，白棋不行。因此实战白2只好接回，接着黑3渡过，收获颇丰。形势再度接近了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103921463" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103921463" style="width: 498px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id103921463-image00725.png"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-full wp-image-103921463" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id103921463-image00725.png" alt="" width="498" b="498" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103921463" class="wp-caption-text">图七：第10届国手山脉国际围棋大会八强战，赖均辅（黑）VS.范廷钰（白）。（海峰棋院提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>图八：经过一连串官子战，还是白棋小胜概率较高的局面，但赖均辅永不放弃。此时黑1本手应先C大飞收官，否则被白棋B尖逆收官子很大。但在那之前，赖均辅先在黑1点左下角，问白应手，这手棋的目的明显是想之后A扳黏便宜官子，或是利用整体白棋需要收气吃便宜，那么此时白棋该怎么办呢？</p>
<figure id="attachment_103921467" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103921467" style="width: 498px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id103921467-image00816.png"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-full wp-image-103921467" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id103921467-image00816.png" alt="" width="498" b="498" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103921467" class="wp-caption-text">图八：第10届国手山脉国际围棋大会八强战，赖均辅（黑）VS.范廷钰（白）。（海峰棋院提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>图九：实战白棋在读秒慌乱下选择白1夹退让！此手正确下法应脱先抢上边A逆收官子，或是2立下吃到最大，实战被黑棋先手2、4扳黏相当愉快，之后下面黑B、白C，以后白空里可能还得补棋。这几步下来黑棋便宜了两目棋左右，形势又接近了许多。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103921468" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103921468" style="width: 498px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id103921468-image00921.png"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-full wp-image-103921468" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id103921468-image00921.png" alt="" width="498" b="498" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103921468" class="wp-caption-text">图九：第10届国手山脉国际围棋大会八强战，赖均辅（黑）VS.范廷钰（白）。（海峰棋院提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>图十：进入小官子阶段，此时赖均辅黑1扳是失着，这手棋错过了上面A先手大飞。白2一路小飞是局部官子好手，此手棋可先手防止黑A大飞。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103921471" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103921471" style="width: 498px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id103921471-image01012.png"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-full wp-image-103921471" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id103921471-image01012.png" alt="" width="498" b="498" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103921471" class="wp-caption-text">图十：第10届国手山脉国际围棋大会八强战，赖均辅（黑）VS.范廷钰（白）。（海峰棋院提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>图十一：实战赖均辅黑1、3只好无奈跟着应，但白棋4黏是大失误，也几乎是最后的败着，此时A长的官子明显更大一些。这当然也给了赖均辅天大的机会。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103921472" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103921472" style="width: 498px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id103921472-image01115.png"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-full wp-image-103921472" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id103921472-image01115.png" alt="" width="498" b="498" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103921472" class="wp-caption-text">图十一：第10届国手山脉国际围棋大会八强战，赖均辅（黑）VS.范廷钰（白）。（海峰棋院提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>图十二：实战赖均辅抓住机会，下到黑7、9扳完挡的官子，中间瞬间多围了好几目棋，因此黑半目胜几乎已成定局。最后赖均辅逆转胜范廷钰，晋级四强赛！</p>
<figure id="attachment_103921473" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103921473" style="width: 498px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id103921473-image0126.png"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-full wp-image-103921473" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/10/id103921473-image0126.png" alt="" width="498" b="498" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103921473" class="wp-caption-text">图十二：第10届国手山脉国际围棋大会八强战，赖均辅（黑）VS.范廷钰（白）。（海峰棋院提供）</figcaption></figure>

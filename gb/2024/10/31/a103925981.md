<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组图：台风康芮袭台74万户停电 多地铁皮屋被吹翻</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id103925983-20241031PHO0079l-e1730406549176.jpg" />
<h6>31日台9线多处路树、路灯被强风吹倒，民宅铁皮屋顶也被吹落。（花莲县新城警分局提供）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【新唐人北京时间2024年11月01日讯】<ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/台风">台风</a><ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/康芮">康芮</a>袭台，带来强风暴雨，其中花莲在深夜11时累计将近1200毫米雨量。全台一度有747,770户停电，共1人死亡，200多人伤。</p>
<p>中华民国气象署指出，<ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/康芮">康芮</a>持续减弱，并加速向北移动，预计明天上午台湾本岛就会脱离暴风圈，马祖则要明天下午到傍晚才会脱离。</p>
<p>目前台湾本岛跟离岛都还在暴风圈的范围内，仍将有强风豪雨。预计今天深夜到明天清晨，东半部地区、中南部还是有短延时强降雨出现。</p>
<p>中央灾害应变中心统计，截至31日晚上8时，受伤增加132人，累计205伤、1死；灾情共3688件，桃园市最多，以路树倾倒、民生基础设施受损最多。</p>
<p>截至31日晚10时，全台曾停电户数747,770户，目前剩143,337户尚未复电，以新北市、台东县、花莲县、宜兰县、桃园市为主。</p>
<p>台湾电力公司表示，康芮强劲风势及雨势引起部分线路受损及树枝触压电线，导致停电灾情，为加速复电时效，<ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/台电">台电</a>已启动跨区支援机制、扩大抢修量能。</p>
<h2>多地栋<ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/铁皮屋顶">铁皮屋顶</a>被吹飞</h2>
<p>康芮狂扫，多地众多栋<ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/铁皮屋顶">铁皮屋顶</a>被吹走。新北市新庄区仁爱街31日晚间有大片铁皮屋顶遭强风吹落后，直立紧贴公寓墙面、画面惊人。警方说，所幸无人受伤，不过有压到1辆小货车。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103925984" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103925984" style="width: 603px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" fetchpriority="high" class=" wp-image-103925984" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id103925984-20241031PHO0293l-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="603" b="452" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103925984" class="wp-caption-text">新北市新庄区仁爱街31日晚间有大片铁皮屋顶遭强风吹落。（中央社）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_103925985" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103925985" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-103925985" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id103925985-20241031PHO0292l.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="533" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103925985" class="wp-caption-text">新北市新庄区仁爱街31日晚间有大片铁皮屋顶遭强风吹落。（中央社）</figcaption></figure>
<p>新北市瑞芳区九份消防分队的铁皮屋顶31日晚也被掀翻，幸亏数片铁皮掀起后被卡住，没有造成伤亡。部分遭掀起的屋顶镂空，只剩下钢骨结构。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103925986" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103925986" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-103925986" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id103925986-20241031PHO0296l-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103925986" class="wp-caption-text">新北市瑞芳区九份消防分队的铁皮屋顶31日晚也被掀翻。（中央社）</figcaption></figure>
<p>基隆市31日晚间8时许也有一座大型铁架掉落至西定高架道路，占据双向车道，所幸无人受伤。警方到场封闭道路，相关单位已派员前往处理。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103925987" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103925987" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-medium wp-image-103925987" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id103925987-20241031PHO0291l-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103925987" class="wp-caption-text">基隆市31日晚间8时许也有一座大型铁架掉落至西定高架道路。（中央社）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_103925982" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103925982" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-medium wp-image-103925982" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id103925982-20241031PHO0069l-600x338.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103925982" class="wp-caption-text">花莲县新城乡多处铁皮屋顶被吹掀，消防人员获报到场协助清理。（花莲县消防局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_103925983" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103925983" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-medium wp-image-103925983" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id103925983-20241031PHO0079l-600x278.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="278" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103925983" class="wp-caption-text">31日台9线多处路树、路灯被强风吹倒，民宅铁皮屋顶也被吹落。（花莲县新城警分局提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>彰化县员林市一处咖啡馆招牌31日被强风吹袭掉落，所幸未造成人员受伤。警方获报后，到场协助管制并通知业者，请厂商移除。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103925991" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103925991" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-medium wp-image-103925991" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id103925991-20241031PHO0277l-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103925991" class="wp-caption-text">彰化县员林市一处咖啡馆招牌31日被强风吹袭掉落。（中央社）</figcaption></figure>
<p>连结台北市关渡与新北淡水、五股、八里的关渡大桥，因附挂桥外的景观灯管线槽损坏松脱，为顾及用路人安全，关渡桥暂时封闭，请用路人配合管制改道。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103925988" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103925988" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-medium wp-image-103925988" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id103925988-20241101PHO0003l-600x450.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103925988" class="wp-caption-text">关渡大桥灯管线槽松脱抢修。（中央社）</figcaption></figure>

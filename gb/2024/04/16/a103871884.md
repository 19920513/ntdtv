<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>重庆燃气突换气表 燃气费随即暴涨数倍</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id103871895-FotoJet-868x488.jpg" />
<h6>2024年4月，重庆燃气费突然飙涨，引发舆论关注。（网页截图）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【新唐人北京时间2024年04月17日讯】重庆燃气更换气表后燃气费高涨，引发关注。许多居民抱怨气费翻倍，甚至上涨十几倍。有媒体人分析，中共当局财政紧张，将大幅提高水电等基础物价。</p>
<p>近日，大量重庆市民反映燃气费高涨，成为大陆舆论焦点。</p>
<p>一名当地小区业主爆料，自从小区“免费安装新<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/智能燃气表">智能燃气表</a>”之后，业主群中就开始抱怨燃气费成倍增加。该业主在燃气群中提出质疑，就被抄表员踢出群组。该业主又直接向燃气公司反映，要求当面测试新燃气表的准确度，燃气公司百般推脱。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103871886" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103871886" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id103871886-GLH4gqwWgAAzLus.jpg"><img decoding="async" fetchpriority="high" class="wp-image-103871886" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id103871886-GLH4gqwWgAAzLus-600x1020.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="680" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103871886" class="wp-caption-text">重庆业主爆料。（网页截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>还有许多当地网民控诉，更换“新<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/智能燃气表">智能燃气表</a>”之后，家中的燃气费翻倍，业主联系燃气公司客服，没有任何回应。有人嘲讽：“明明可以靠抢，为什么还要弄得这么复杂呢？”</p>
<figure id="attachment_103871888" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103871888" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id103871888-GLH4tvIX0AAAq6k.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="wp-image-103871888" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id103871888-GLH4tvIX0AAAq6k-600x1074.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="716" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103871888" class="wp-caption-text">重庆业主爆料。（网页截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>也有当地业主爆料，自己家的燃气费直接从上个月的463元（人民币，下同）上涨到这个月的6,170.75元。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103871889" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103871889" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id103871889-GLNox7DaIAA6XfX.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="wp-image-103871889" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id103871889-GLNox7DaIAA6XfX-600x1246.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="831" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103871889" class="wp-caption-text">重庆业主爆料。（网页截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>事实上，从全年下半年开始，就不断有重庆居民在重庆官方网站的所谓“问政平台”反映燃气费飙涨，但当局一直没有解决问题。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103871890" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103871890" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id103871890-e9bb315aafffe7f59dfe3880f8503bfb.webp"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="wp-image-103871890 size-medium" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id103871890-e9bb315aafffe7f59dfe3880f8503bfb-600x326.webp" alt="" width="600" b="326" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103871890" class="wp-caption-text">大量重庆业主在当局的官方网站上反映燃气费飙涨。（网页截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>当地网民表示，重庆居民<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/换表">换表</a>的时间大多集中在去年年底，也有部分是在今年2月份前后。</p>
<p>公开资料显示，“重庆燃气”2023年第四季度的归母净利润为2,192亿元，同比涨幅高达824.84%。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103871891" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103871891" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id103871891-GLLRbaDa4AAhy_G.jpg"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="wp-image-103871891" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id103871891-GLLRbaDa4AAhy_G.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="608" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103871891" class="wp-caption-text">“重庆燃气”去年第四季度的净利润暴涨824.84%。（网页截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>网民纷纷猜测燃气费高涨的原因。有人认为是新表被做了手脚，有人认为是燃气公司偷偷降低了燃气的浓度，还有人猜测是燃气公司调涨了燃气价格。</p>
<p>还有网民爆料，燃气费暴涨的不止重庆市。网传一张燃气费账单显示，四川省某地一个小区业主的燃气费高达17,290元。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103871893" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103871893" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id103871893-GLRLt_maQAAfPaX.jpg"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="wp-image-103871893" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id103871893-GLRLt_maQAAfPaX-600x1333.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="889" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103871893" class="wp-caption-text">四川某地小区业主的燃气费超过一万元。（网页截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>中国的水、电、燃气、网络都是由中共国企垄断。网民总结“五大小偷”，嘲讽中共当局压榨百姓。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103871894" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103871894" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id103871894-GLRLt7_akAAf20E.jpg"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="wp-image-103871894" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/04/id103871894-GLRLt7_akAAf20E-600x699.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="466" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103871894" class="wp-caption-text">网民嘲讽中共用见不得人的手段压榨百姓。（网页截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>许多大陆自媒体发布视频，直言指责国企“抢钱”。还有一名自媒体人表示，上海的自来水已经涨价20%到52%，下一步就是电价、通讯及网络费，包括火车以及地铁票价的上调，中国将迎来一个“基础物价通胀期”。</p>
<p>（记者历澄宇综合报导/责任编辑：林清）</p>

<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>四川宜宾稻田安水表 自然水源也收费（视频）</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id103884408-FotoJet-868x488.jpg" />
<h6>2024年5月，四川宜宾市高县庆岭镇稻田安装水表。（网络视频截图）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【新唐人北京时间2024年05月25日讯】中共财政紧张，抢钱越来越不择手段。近日<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/四川宜宾">四川宜宾</a>市水稻田安装水表，许多人感叹“盘古开天以来没有过”。实际上，中国许多地方早已实现“灌溉用水收费”。</p>
<p>5月20日，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/四川宜宾">四川宜宾</a>市高县庆岭镇网民分享照片和视频，显示当地所谓的“高标准稻虾田”都安装了水表。网友们纷纷感叹，使用自然水源灌溉农田也要收费，闻所未闻。</p>
<a title="农民浇地也收水费？四川宜宾稻田安水表" src="https://www.ganjingworld.com/embed/1gp93t3m4k06AAF7Nn2Cm1ESN18p1c" width="640" b="360" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen"></a><br />
<p>有网民询问收费标准。有人爆料“水费收一毛钱一方”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103884396" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103884396" style="width: 595px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id103884396-de60bcfe193fcef8ec135daf27554652.jpg"><img decoding="async" fetchpriority="high" class="wp-image-103884396 size-full" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id103884396-de60bcfe193fcef8ec135daf27554652.jpg" alt="" width="595" b="501" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103884396" class="wp-caption-text">微博爆料水费标准。（网页截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在大陆网络上，许多网友留言怒斥中共当局：“敲骨吸髓啊”“哪怕只剩下半滴油也要榨干榨净，一定不能浪费。”“估计以后呼吸空气也快装表计价了”“怎么办，他掐着我脖子，问我幸不幸福。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_103884397" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103884397" style="width: 358px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id103884397-d7922d7504d9c6982a1c496c077e6da5.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="wp-image-103884397 size-full" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id103884397-d7922d7504d9c6982a1c496c077e6da5.jpg" alt="" width="358" b="484" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103884397" class="wp-caption-text">抖音网民大骂中共当局。（网页截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_103884403" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103884403" style="width: 358px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id103884403-e76eb1f2dee2828499bb991624f6c0ba.jpg"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="wp-image-103884403" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id103884403-e76eb1f2dee2828499bb991624f6c0ba.jpg" alt="" width="358" b="425" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103884403" class="wp-caption-text">大陆网民嘲讽“打井安水表”。（网页截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>实际上，在中国大陆，农民使用自然水源浇地被收费，早已不是新闻。中共当局美其名曰“水资源费”。</p>
<p>2023年7月，河南郑州某地就曾传出视频，显示官方人员强制在用来浇地的机井上安装水表。视频中，两名穿制服的人员向农民解释，按照官方条文（文件），农民取用地下水资源必须计量，也就是要装水表。</p>
<br />
<a loading="lazy" title="取用地下水浇地也要收费 郑州强制机井安装水表" src="https://www.ganjingworld.com/embed/1gp945lrnvs1PptJcUvF82Ks319m1c" width="640" b="360" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen"></a><br />
<p>其中一名官方人员要农民“不要断章取义”，似乎有意否认“装水表是为了收费”。但网友们认为这是欲盖弥彰：不为收费，安水表干什么？</p>
<p>其实，早在2017年7月，北京市党媒就曾报导，全市1.7万眼灌溉机井安装了智能计量设施，从此京郊农业灌溉用水“都将精确计量、收费”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103884405" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103884405" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id103884405-e2a28f21fa2aef284093b5376088cf34.jpg"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="wp-image-103884405 size-medium" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id103884405-e2a28f21fa2aef284093b5376088cf34-600x191.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="191" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103884405" class="wp-caption-text">党媒《北京日报》报导京郊灌溉用水收费。（网页截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>更早前的2008年，就有安徽凤阳县的农民在知乎网站上发帖称，“我家的井是自己家打的，现在自来水厂要到我家里给我家的井装一个水表，而且水表钱2000元还要我家出，以后用水还要缴费！”</p>
<figure id="attachment_103884406" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103884406" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id103884406-10e4f917810a0408710273f434eefe4e.jpg"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="wp-image-103884406 size-medium" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id103884406-10e4f917810a0408710273f434eefe4e-600x351.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="351" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103884406" class="wp-caption-text">安徽凤阳网民抱怨，自家打的井也要叫水费。（网页截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>（记者周归航综合报导/责任编辑：林清）</p>

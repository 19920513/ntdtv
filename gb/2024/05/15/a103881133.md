<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>520就职典礼在即 空中全兵力预演登场</h2>
<a href="https://dgk92ykceobaf.cloudfront.net/L4kZvpL5y"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/19920513/www/master/t/ntdtv/ntdtv600.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/19920513/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【新唐人北京时间2024年05月15日讯】520中华民国总统、副总统就职典礼即将到来，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/国军">国军</a>今天清晨进行首次空中全兵力预演，陆军CH-47SD契努克运输直升机吊挂巨幅国旗飞越<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/总统府">总统府</a>，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/空军雷虎小组">空军雷虎小组</a>5架AT-3教练机施放蓝白红彩烟，场面壮观。</p>
<p>军方今天跟明天(17日)进行全兵力预演；19日合并大会预演，当天下午进行最后一次全兵力预演。20日当天<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/国军">国军</a>出动陆、海、空军各型军机飞越<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/总统府">总统府</a>上空向<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/三军统帅">三军统帅</a>致敬。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103881135" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103881135" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img decoding="async" fetchpriority="high" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id103881135-20240515PHO0015l.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="420" class="size-full wp-image-103881135" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103881135" class="wp-caption-text">520总统副总统就职典礼国军空中全兵力预演15日早晨登场，图为5架IDF经国号战机编队飞越总统府上空。(中央社)</figcaption></figure>
<p>今天的空中全兵力预演分别出动F-16V战隼战机、IDF经国战机、陆军UH-60M所组成的天候观测机，于早晨6时30分、6时40分飞抵目标区。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103881136" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103881136" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img decoding="async" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id103881136-20240515PHO0014l.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="404" class="size-full wp-image-103881136" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103881136" class="wp-caption-text">520总统副总统就职典礼国军空中全兵力预演15日早晨登场，图为5架F-16V（blk20）战机编队飞越总统府上方。 (中央社)</figcaption></figure>
<p>7时9分由国旗梯队登场，陆军出动1架CH-47SD契努克运输直升机吊挂长18公尺、宽12公尺的巨幅国旗，由2架UH-60M黑鹰直升机护卫组成国旗小队，后方依序有3架AH-64E阿帕契攻击直升机、3架AH-1W超级眼镜蛇攻击直升机，以及3架海军S-70C反潜直升机各自编队组成旋翼小队，跟随国旗小队飞越总统府前方上空。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103881138" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103881138" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img decoding="async" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id103881138-20240515PHO0012l.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="444" class="size-full wp-image-103881138" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103881138" class="wp-caption-text">图为3架海军S-70C反潜直升机飞越总统府上方。(中央社)</figcaption></figure>
<p>接着在7时23分，快速梯队由5架F-16V（blk20）战机、5架IDF经国战机、5架幻象2000-5战机与象征国机国造的5架勇鹰新式高教机，依序组成5机编队飞越总统府前方上空。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103881140" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103881140" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id103881140-20240515PHO0018l.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="418" class="size-full wp-image-103881140" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103881140" class="wp-caption-text">图为5架幻象2000战机编队飞行，通过总统府前方上空。(中央社)</figcaption></figure>
<p>7时29分，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/空军雷虎小组">空军雷虎小组</a>的5架AT-3教练机压轴，自总统府后方飞越总统府上方，施放蓝、白、红3色彩烟，场面壮观。</p>
<p>17日上午将再进行就职典礼空中全兵力预演，据了解，规划的操演时序与今天操演相同。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103881139" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103881139" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/05/id103881139-20240515PHO0020l.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="389" class="size-full wp-image-103881139" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103881139" class="wp-caption-text">图为空军雷虎小组5架AT-3教练机飞越总统府上方，施放蓝、白、红3色彩烟。(中央社)</figcaption></figure>
<p>（新唐人亚太台/责任编辑：夏明义）</p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin;">视频显示，</span><span lang="EN-US">LCSO</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin;">特别行动组（</span><span lang="EN-US">SOU</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin;">）的一名<ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/狙击手">狙击手</a>在另一名警官与阿拉瓦奇谈判时，用武器瞄准阿拉瓦奇，他的枪管放在另一名警官的肩上，</span><span lang="EN-US">LCSO</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin;">的发言人奥尔默（</span><span lang="EN-US">Todd Olmer</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin;">）中尉将其描述为“受队员支持的射击位置”。</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin;">特别行动组主要应对超出正常巡逻能力范围的事件，例如设置路障的嫌疑人、武装嫌疑人、自杀者、<ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/人质">人质</a>营救、要人保护和人群控制。</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin;">据</span><span lang="EN-US">LCSO</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin;">发布的音频文件，报告该抢劫事件的第一个</span><span lang="EN-US">911</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin;">电话称阿拉瓦奇跳过银行柜台，声称他有炸弹，并命令所有人离开银行。其中还能听到“把你的头放在地上”和“我不想开枪打死你！”等尖叫声。</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">LCSO</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin;">表示，阿拉瓦奇劫持了一名男子和一名女子人质。根据监控录像，劫匪明显用</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin;">手锁住了女性人质的头，并用一把刀指向她的喉咙。从警察的角度来看，<ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/电脑显示器">电脑显示器</a>遮挡了劫匪和女人质的一部分。</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin;">“在谈判过程中，他一度动手，”</span><span lang="EN-US">LCSO</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin;">警长马塞诺（</span><span lang="EN-US">Carmine Marceno</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin;">）告诉记者。</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin;">视频显示，谈判人员在银行外对阿拉瓦奇喊道，“我想帮助你，好吧？作为同样有孩子的人，我想帮助你。</span><span lang="EN-US">100%</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin;">。你听到我说的话了吗？来吧，阿拉瓦奇，好吧？继续跟我说话。继续跟我说话。让我知道你现在在想什么，你有什么顾虑”。</span></p>

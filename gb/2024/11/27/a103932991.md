<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>冬季吃这些时令菜味道更好 看图识菜名</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id103933011-a43e3688746ae543846b28a1616a4362-800x488.jpeg" />
<h6>时令菜的味道总是最好的，寒冬的冬季也不例外。（Shutterstock)</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【新唐人北京时间2024年11月27日讯】<ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/时令菜">时令菜</a>的味道总是最好的，寒冬的<ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/冬季">冬季</a>也不例外。许多冬季<ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/时令菜">时令菜</a>经过霜冻之后，味道更佳。因为气温下降后，植物将储存的能量分解成糖，增进了蔬菜的甜味。下面这几种常见的冬令蔬菜，是冬季食材的理想选择，看图认识一下它们吧。</p>
<h2>红卷心菜（Red Cabbage）</h2>
<figure id="attachment_103932992" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103932992" style="width: 1200px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img decoding="async" fetchpriority="high" class="wp-image-103932992 size-facebook_1200_630" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id103932992-3d57e81b94817d1efe1de03b2572c7b8-1200x630.jpeg" alt="" width="1200" b="630" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103932992" class="wp-caption-text">红卷心菜富含的抗氧化剂花青素，让它呈深红色。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>红卷心菜富含抗氧化剂花青素，正是这种成分赋予了它深红色的色调。可以生吃，切成细丝，与其他绿叶蔬菜一起拌匀，作出一道色彩缤纷的<ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/冬季">冬季</a>沙拉；还可以用黄油和醋炒熟，做成类似酸菜那样的搭配肉类的配菜。</p>
<h2><ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/羽衣甘蓝">羽衣甘蓝</a>（Kale）</h2>
<figure id="attachment_103932993" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103932993" style="width: 1200px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img decoding="async" class="wp-image-103932993 size-facebook_1200_630" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id103932993-7f8e24f9c8a4616c3f9aa7579f99a37d-1200x630.jpeg" alt="" width="1200" b="630" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103932993" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/羽衣甘蓝">羽衣甘蓝</a>富汗维生素A和K，对眼睛有益。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>羽衣甘蓝被称为超级食物是有理由的，它含有维生素A和K，对眼睛健康有益。</p>
<p>羽衣甘蓝口感微苦，但在冬天会显着变甜。可以做色拉，或放进丰盛的冬季炖菜中。还可以淋上橄榄油，烤至酥脆，也是一道有趣的小吃。</p>
<h2><ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/瑞士甜菜">瑞士甜菜</a>（Swiss chard）</h2>
<figure id="attachment_103932994" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103932994" style="width: 1200px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img decoding="async" class="wp-image-103932994 size-facebook_1200_630" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id103932994-8cedefb943cffa041e4e414cd36a5576-1200x630.jpeg" alt="" width="1200" b="630" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103932994" class="wp-caption-text"><ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/瑞士甜菜">瑞士甜菜</a>可炒也可炖，味道都不错。（ThuTruong / Getty Images Plus)</figcaption></figure>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/甜菜">甜菜</a>是一种十字花科蔬菜，是维生素A和C以及铁的良好来源。 成熟的甜菜叶和茎通常被煮熟或炒，它们的苦味会随着烹饪而消失，留下比煮熟的菠菜更细腻的精致味道。还可以切碎后煎熟，做成煎蛋饼，或者代替菠菜放入汤中。</p>
<h2>欧防风（Parsnips）</h2>
<figure id="attachment_103932995" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103932995" style="width: 756px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="wp-image-103932995 size-full" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id103932995-6a7d5af751165e5c434c4005ad8d18f5.jpeg" alt="" width="756" b="504" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103932995" class="wp-caption-text">欧防风富含维生素C、K和叶酸。（Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p>欧防风味道甘甜，是一种冬季非常受欢迎的菜，它的外形与胡萝卜相似，只是颜色为奶油色。这种看着不起眼的根茎类蔬菜富含维生素C、K和叶酸。</p>
<p>欧防风可以烤、做汤和炖。特别是烤过之后淋上黄油和糖浆，是一道美味的烤菜 。</p>
<h2>孢子甘蓝（Brussels sprouts）</h2>
<figure id="attachment_103932996" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103932996" style="width: 1200px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="wp-image-103932996 size-facebook_1200_630" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id103932996-38bf9129377f335b506ec6ed05209438-1200x630.jpeg" alt="" width="1200" b="630" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103932996" class="wp-caption-text">孢子甘蓝虽然个头小，但营养价值高。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>抱子甘蓝属于十字花科，虽然个头小但营养价值高。它富含维生素C，还含有异硫氰酸酯可对抗癌症，还有多种维生素能促进皮肤胶原蛋白生成，有助于抗氧化、抗发炎、增强免疫力。</p>
<p>孢子甘蓝可以与其他蔬菜一起蒸、炒或烤，虽然带有些微苦味，但如果做法得当会很好吃，特别是煎炒到表面微焦，会带出烟熏甜香味。</p>
<h2>胡萝卜（Carrots）</h2>
<figure id="attachment_103932997" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103932997" style="width: 1000px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="wp-image-103932997 size-facebook_1200_630" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id103932997-629dfd9032fc7dc8de707e007519d226-1000x630.jpeg" alt="" width="1000" b="630" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103932997" class="wp-caption-text">胡萝卜在一年中较冷的月份里最甜。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>胡萝卜富含维生素 A，它喜欢凉爽的天气，因而在一年中较冷的月份里味道最甜。胡萝卜有很多种，大小和颜色各异，但橙色品种在烤过后最香甜。</p>
<h2>芜菁甘蓝 (Rutabaga)</h2>
<figure id="attachment_103932999" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103932999" style="width: 1200px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="wp-image-103932999 size-facebook_1200_630" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id103932999-d85aff1a6a35f9ebf27dd4ba55223d2c-1200x630.jpg" alt="" width="1200" b="630" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103932999" class="wp-caption-text">芜菁富含维生素C。（Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p>这种根茎类蔬菜看起来与萝卜相似，有些人说它的味道有点像卷心菜和萝卜的混合物。可以像土豆一样煮熟后捣碎，与酪乳和黄油混合，像土豆泥一样混合搅拌。也可以用橄榄油烤熟，并撒上新鲜的意大利香草。</p>
<h2><ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/甜菜">甜菜</a> (Beets)</h2>
<figure id="attachment_103933001" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103933001" style="width: 870px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="wp-image-103933001 size-full" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id103933001-a1fa8fc812d242694a5ba4e2c82f7e07.jpeg" alt="" width="870" b="522" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103933001" class="wp-caption-text">新鲜甜菜的味道，与罐装的腌渍甜菜完全不同。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure>
<p>如果你只吃过罐装的腌渍甜菜，那应该试一试新鲜甜菜的味道，与罐装完全不同。</p>
<p>一种简单的烹调法是烤甜菜，用铝箔纸将甜菜根包裹，高温慢烤的方式会使甜菜根焦糖化，能去除土味。也可以切成薄片，放在沙拉上，配上山羊奶酪，味道很搭 。</p>
<h2>苤蓝（Kohlrabi）</h2>
<figure id="attachment_103933002" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103933002" style="width: 1200px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="wp-image-103933002 size-facebook_1200_630" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id103933002-dd12cba56c6163bc15647745c49d2444-1200x630.jpg" alt="" width="1200" b="630" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103933002" class="wp-caption-text">苤蓝可以凉拌生吃，口感爽脆。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>苤蓝（又称芥兰头、大头菜），它在冷天也长得很好，在霜冻出现前几周下种，几周之内就能收获。</p>
<p>大头菜的味道有点像带点辛辣的西兰花，可以像土豆一样煮熟捣成泥吃，也适合切丝凉拌生吃，口感爽脆。</p>
<p>（记者金晶综合报导/编辑：林清）</p>

<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>女儿奴！73岁王石独自带娃 被贴了个大花脸</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id103931154-Unknown-868x488.jpeg" />
<h6>万科创始人王石被拍到带女儿看电影时，被女儿用贴纸贴了一个大花脸，实实在在一个“女儿奴”形象。(视频截图合成)</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【新唐人北京时间2024年11月21日讯】73岁的“商界大佬”<ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/万科">万科</a>创始人<ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/王石">王石</a>今年初自曝有一个3岁女儿，近日更被拍到带女儿看电影时，被女儿用贴纸贴了一个大花脸，实实在在一个“女儿奴”形象。</p>
<p>11月20日，多名网友在微博贴出图文和视频，称在广州偶遇<ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/王石">王石</a>带娃看电影。73岁的王石身穿黑色西装，搭配白色球鞋，显得年轻了不少。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103931157" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103931157" style="width: 399px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" fetchpriority="high" class=" wp-image-103931157" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id103931157-006dAXvGgy1hvsvi45721j32c0340u0y-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="399" b="532" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103931157" class="wp-caption-text">（网络图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_103931156" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103931156" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-103931156" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id103931156-9bc12931gy1hvt0bz14lqj22c0340qv6-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="533" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103931156" class="wp-caption-text">（网络图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_103931158" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103931158" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" class=" wp-image-103931158" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id103931158-006dAXvGgy1hvsvjrs07oj32c0340e82-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="533" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103931158" class="wp-caption-text">（网络图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_103931159" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103931159" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class=" wp-image-103931159" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id103931159-d622d2a7gy1hvsscflt2sj22c03407wj-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="533" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103931159" class="wp-caption-text">（网络图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>画面显示，王石牵着4岁女儿的手现身电影院，脸上一脸贴纸。<ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/田朴珺">田朴珺</a>跟老公女儿会合后，看到王石的狼狈模样，忍不住笑了出来。</p>
<p>早前的画面显示，一开始，女儿在车里要跟爸爸玩贴纸，王石“受宠若惊”，没想到女儿把贴纸一一贴到他的脸上和身上，成了一个“大花脸”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103931155" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103931155" style="width: 406px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class=" wp-image-103931155" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/11/id103931155-007Xgj01gy1hvt4im4o79j30wr1cm13e-600x891.jpg" alt="" width="406" b="603" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103931155" class="wp-caption-text">（网络图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在宝贝女儿面前，王石不再是商界大佬，满满都是爱，说话声音都嗲嗲的。</p>
<p>王石老来得女，但一直很低调。今年1月5日，王石在一个线下活动中公开承认自己有一个3岁的女儿，还特别强调：“不是私生女啊，是婚配的。”该话题一度冲上社媒热搜榜。</p>
<p>随后，王石跟<ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/田朴珺">田朴珺</a>开始高调晒娃。石承认女儿的存在，田朴珺则在朋友圈发布一家三口合影。</p>
<p>最近，王石夫妇的曝光率增高，除了带娃被拍，他们还高调现身了广州车展。</p>

<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>文革重演？传大陆数学试卷满是“习语录”</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id103861247-e9709fd6df95bdbad6b84b2cbef9841c.jpg" />
<h6>2024年3月，网传中国某地小学试卷。（网络图片）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【新唐人北京时间2024年03月13日讯】中共文革时期，课本章节和考试题都要以“毛语录”开头。近日网传大陆某地数学<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/试卷.md#1">试卷</a>，每道题都冠以“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/习语录.md#1">习语录</a>”或中共政策的“大帽子”，宛如文革复辟。</p>
<p>根据网上截图，这份<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/试卷.md#1">试卷</a>疑似小学试卷。其中第7题是关于“平均数”的试题，前边却扣上了和试题毫不相干的所谓中共“二十大精神”、“文化自信”等大帽子，然后让学生计算所谓“新时代好少年·传承经典·筑梦未来”演讲比赛得分的平均数。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103861237" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103861237" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id103861237-GIdz1txa0AA8eR-.jpg"><img decoding="async" fetchpriority="high" class="wp-image-103861237 size-medium" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id103861237-GIdz1txa0AA8eR--600x99.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="99" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103861237" class="wp-caption-text">网传大陆某地<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/小学数学.md#1">小学数学</a>试卷。（网络图片）</figcaption></figure>
<p>第20题是关于数学图标制作，前边扣的“大帽子”是习近平关于所谓“网络空间命运共同体”的讲话。</p>
<p>第22题是<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/小学数学.md#1">小学数学</a>中经典的“鸡兔同笼”问题，也扣上“习近平关心体育”的“帽子”，然后用健身器材的价格来出题。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103861239" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103861239" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id103861239-GIdzr29b0AACbLF.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-103861239" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id103861239-GIdzr29b0AACbLF-600x250.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="250" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103861239" class="wp-caption-text">网传大陆某地小学数学试卷。（网络图片）</figcaption></figure>
<p>众网友嘲讽“真是全面照抄文革”。一些网友纷纷贴出文革时代的数学课本和数学试卷的截图，并指出当时课本和试题也必须和政治挂钩，多数开篇就是“毛语录”。</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id103861240-GIe6tnMbQAAx1iF.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-103861240" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id103861240-GIe6tnMbQAAx1iF.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="533" /></a></p>
<figure id="attachment_103861242" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103861242" style="width: 385px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id103861242-GIea9xOagAAtMzH.jpg"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="wp-image-103861242 size-full" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id103861242-GIea9xOagAAtMzH.jpg" alt="" width="385" b="525" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103861242" class="wp-caption-text">网传文革时代的数学课本。（网络图片）</figcaption></figure>
<p>其实，此前网上就曾传出大陆某高校高等数学的课本，开篇就将马列和“习思想”列为该学科的“指导思想”，课本中的章节也多次引用“<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/习语录.md#1">习语录</a>”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103861243" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103861243" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id103861243-FRYp_Y9XwAAle36.jpg"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="wp-image-103861243" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id103861243-FRYp_Y9XwAAle36.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="472" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103861243" class="wp-caption-text">“习语录”写入大学高等数学课本。（网络图片）</figcaption></figure>
<p>甚至某高校大学英语课本，每篇课文也都是用“习语录”开头，和文革时期的英语课本毫无二致。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103861244" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103861244" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id103861244-F7NAQLIXAAAxudf-600x.jpg"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="wp-image-103861244" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/03/id103861244-F7NAQLIXAAAxudf-600x.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="572" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103861244" class="wp-caption-text">大学英语每篇课文都以“习语录”开头。（网络图片）</figcaption></figure>

<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>传江苏学生密集跳楼 全省高三破天荒放长假</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id103847825-FotoJet-1-868x488.jpg" />
<h6>左图：大陆高中教室的反人性标语。右图：2024年1月，网传江苏学生密集跳楼，换来高三超长年假。（网络图片）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【新唐人北京时间2024年01月31日讯】中共高考制度给学生制造巨大压力。今年“高考大省”江苏罕见下令高三放25天年假。网络热传是因为<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/南通市.md#1">南通市</a>五名高中生接连跳楼，但据说实际跳楼人数更多。</p>
<p>近日，“江苏五连跳换来25天长假”一度成为大陆社交媒体，特别是抖音平台上的热门话题。许多网民感叹这个长假带着“血腥味”。不过，相关消息和视频都已被删除殆尽，只有知乎网站和抖音平台的零星讨论中，还有当地网民透出只言片语。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103847820" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103847820" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id103847820-GFFi4NSaQAA4YKz.jpg"><img decoding="async" fetchpriority="high" class="wp-image-103847820" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id103847820-GFFi4NSaQAA4YKz-600x1375.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="917" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103847820" class="wp-caption-text">网民爆料江苏“五连跳”换来高三超长年假。（网页截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>目前抖音上讨论“江苏25天长假”的视频仍未被屏蔽。但相关视频多不提学生跳楼，还有的试图“洗地”，声称江苏高三破天荒<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/放长假.md#1">放长假</a>是因为“省政府严格执行双减”云云。但许多知情网民在留言中提及“五连跳”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103847830" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103847830" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id103847830-c8fea94fca37783a644c32d3ff28bdd6.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="wp-image-103847830 size-medium" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id103847830-c8fea94fca37783a644c32d3ff28bdd6-600x438.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="438" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103847830" class="wp-caption-text">大陆知乎网站上的零星讨论。（网页截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>根据各路网络消息，今年江苏当局下令包括高三在内，高中都放25天年假。有地方还严令假期“不准补课”。不过，各地执行情况不一。有的地方放假不补课。有的地方只是名义上“放假”，实际上将校园授课改成了学生在家上网课，还命令家长严厉监督孩子上课。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103847832" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103847832" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id103847832-f385a6c0d7d68b3809fa9adc4bdf677b.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="wp-image-103847832" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id103847832-f385a6c0d7d68b3809fa9adc4bdf677b-600x475.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="316" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103847832" class="wp-caption-text">网传江苏某学校通知家长，放假不准上网课。（网页截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_103847834" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103847834" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id103847834-ab86a84cd8e94354976d0aa837305d44tplv-p14lwwcsbr-7.jpg"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="wp-image-103847834" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id103847834-ab86a84cd8e94354976d0aa837305d44tplv-p14lwwcsbr-7-600x1333.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="889" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103847834" class="wp-caption-text">网传江苏某高三教师给家长发出假期网课的时间表。（网页截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>对于这次<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/放长假.md#1">放长假</a>，家长们的反应也大相径庭。有的家长庆幸孩子终于可以“松一口气”。但也有很多家长非常不满，认为耽误孩子高考，还有人试图串联要求学校复课。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103847842" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103847842" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id103847842-image-2.jpg"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-medium wp-image-103847842" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id103847842-image-2-600x683.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="683" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103847842" class="wp-caption-text">网传江苏某高三家长们在家长群中强烈反对放长假，并大骂举报“学校偷偷上课”的人士。（网页截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>关于这次江苏反常放长假的原因，网传是因为江苏<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/南通市.md#1">南通市</a>高三原计划过年只放三天假，导致不堪重负的学生接连绝望地跳楼。大陆抖音平台上虽然删除了相关视频，但该平台保留的“搜索记录”中，仍可见到多个令人触目惊心的热搜话题。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103847846" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103847846" style="width: 300px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id103847846-6c2724b920be4f498f364264eee41ff7.jpg"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="wp-image-103847846 " src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id103847846-6c2724b920be4f498f364264eee41ff7.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="544" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103847846" class="wp-caption-text">抖音网页展示的搜索记录。（网页截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>还有当地网民爆料，最初爆出的消息是“南通五连跳”，但很快就增加到“六连跳”、“七连跳”。还有人称，南通市近日真实的跳楼人数是17人，但相关消息被地方当局封杀了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103847847" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103847847" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id103847847-0f83b3be9bc31feb2ab19f90c2b65bfd.jpg"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="wp-image-103847847" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id103847847-0f83b3be9bc31feb2ab19f90c2b65bfd-600x1011.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="674" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103847847" class="wp-caption-text">网民爆料南通市“17连跳”。（网页截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>还有当地网民爆料，除了南通市，该省其它地区也有学生密集跳楼。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103847849" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103847849" style="width: 356px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id103847849-image.jpg"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="wp-image-103847849" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id103847849-image-600x677.jpg" alt="" width="356" b="401" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103847849" class="wp-caption-text">网传江苏各地学生密集跳楼。（网页截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>中共针对不同的省份实施不公平的高考政策。<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/江苏省.md#1">江苏省</a>因为人口众多，成为高考难度最大的省份之一，历年的江苏高考题都难度偏高。但江苏依然被称为“高考大省”，高考分数名列前茅。当地网民指责，这与该省维持中小学的学习高压有关。有网民称，其它省份都执行“减负”两年了，江苏和浙江仍“拒不执行”，继续让学校“激烈内卷”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103847857" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103847857" style="width: 354px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id103847857-image-1.jpg"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="wp-image-103847857 " src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id103847857-image-1.jpg" alt="" width="354" b="790" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103847857" class="wp-caption-text">中国网民评论。（网页截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>有江苏网民爆料称，这次江苏高三放长假，省教育厅放了“狠话”，用各地教育系统官员的乌纱帽威胁必须放假。不过，有网民批评可能只是“做做样子”。还有网友认为，当前中国经济衰退，人口锐减，当局可能意识到了“人矿”（被压榨的底层民众）的价值已经超过了高考成绩。</p>
<p>近年来，中共当局推动高中生向职业学校“分流”。有分析人士指，在经济衰退的大背景下，高校毕业生大量失业，当局可能试图减少参加高考的人数，培养更多的从事低端工作的劳动力。</p>
<p>（记者周归航综合报导/责任编辑：林清）</p>

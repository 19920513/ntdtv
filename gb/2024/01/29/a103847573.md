<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>传上海松江大爆炸 官方“辟谣”惹猜疑（视频）</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id103847574-GFApiMKawAADTGi-868x488.jpg" />
<h6>北京时间2024年1月29日晚，网络热传上海松江区发生大爆炸。（网络图片）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【新唐人北京时间2024年01月30日讯】中国新年将至，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/上海松江.md#1">上海松江</a>区突然传出巨大<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/爆炸.md#1">爆炸</a>声，当地人纷纷询问实情。有传化工厂爆炸，有传<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/实验室.md#1">实验室</a>出事。当地官方铁口否认，网民猜测是否“出大事了”。</p>
<p>北京时间1月29日晚19点24分左右，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/上海松江.md#1">上海松江</a>区传出巨大的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/爆炸.md#1">爆炸</a>声。随后大量当地市民在网上爆料称。有人说，“窗户差点震碎了”。有人说，“整栋房子都晃了一下”。还有闽行区和其它行政区的许多居民也说听到爆炸声。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id103847590-GFAltPZXgAEvN7z.jpg"><img decoding="async" fetchpriority="high" class="aligncenter wp-image-103847590" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id103847590-GFAltPZXgAEvN7z.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="794" /></a></p>
<figure id="attachment_103847592" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103847592" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id103847592-6f0df7d8ly1hmar2h30caj20u00v6q4i.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="wp-image-103847592" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id103847592-6f0df7d8ly1hmar2h30caj20u00v6q4i-600x623.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="416" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103847592" class="wp-caption-text">当地许多人挺多巨大的爆炸声。（网页截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>有当地网民分享当时的监控视频，显示伴随着巨大的爆炸声，整个监控画面都出现抖动。</p>
</p>
<p><a title="1月29日上海松江大爆炸监控画面" src="https://www.ganjingworld.com/zh-TW/embed/1gg3ek4ts9669fX1jj83m4v8C11r1c" width="560" b="315" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen"></a></p>
<p>
<p>当晚“#上海松江#”一度成为微博热门话题，许多听到爆炸声的当地居民纷纷上网询问发生了什么事，不少外地网民也参与讨论。</p>
<p>网上没有传出近距离的现场影像，但有远距离的疑似火光的照片，显示爆炸可能引发大火。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103847575" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103847575" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id103847575-GFA5kwGbMAAw6Rm.jpg"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="wp-image-103847575" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id103847575-GFA5kwGbMAAw6Rm.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="622" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103847575" class="wp-caption-text">网传<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/上海松江大爆炸.md#1">上海松江大爆炸</a>的远距离照片。（网络图片）</figcaption></figure>
<p>有当地网民称，发生爆炸的可能是松江区玉树路工业园区。有传是当地化工厂爆炸。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103847583" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103847583" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id103847583-25b77270db700d67a62650bbd19c11c8.jpg"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="wp-image-103847583" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id103847583-25b77270db700d67a62650bbd19c11c8-600x839.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="559" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103847583" class="wp-caption-text">当地网民传说是松江区玉树路工业园区发生爆炸。（网页截图）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_103847587" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103847587" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id103847587-007ekUGCgy1hmas6ouovxj30u01sxdlv.jpg"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="wp-image-103847587" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id103847587-007ekUGCgy1hmas6ouovxj30u01sxdlv-600x1298.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="866" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103847587" class="wp-caption-text">有人猜测是否玉树路化工厂爆炸。（网页截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>也有人说是天然气爆炸。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103847584" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103847584" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id103847584-GFAos-QaMAA06nz.jpg"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="wp-image-103847584" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id103847584-GFAos-QaMAA06nz-600x207.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="138" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103847584" class="wp-caption-text">有人说是天然气爆炸。（网页截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>还有人说是“松江李塔汇601兵工厂800所液氮塔做分离实验时爆炸了”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103847588" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103847588" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id103847588-GFA3MxaaUAAJF9x.jpg"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="wp-image-103847588" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id103847588-GFA3MxaaUAAJF9x-600x1400.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="933" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103847588" class="wp-caption-text">有人说是“松江李塔汇601兵工厂800所液氮塔做分离实验时爆炸了”.（网页截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>有人分享了松江区中心医院内部群组发出的“突发事件批量伤员情况”报告。该报告显示，1月29日19时30分左右，玉树路蓝箭航天发射实验爆炸，三名男子受伤。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103847577" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103847577" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id103847577-GFB4weLWgAANRFJ.jpg"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="wp-image-103847577" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id103847577-GFB4weLWgAANRFJ-600x1302.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="868" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103847577" class="wp-caption-text">网传医院工作群的报告。（网络图片）</figcaption></figure>
<p>还有人分享了疑似<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/实验室.md#1">实验室</a>受伤人员在医院就医的照片。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103847576" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103847576" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id103847576-GFA5ixva4AAlsyH.jpg"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="wp-image-103847576" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/01/id103847576-GFA5ixva4AAlsyH-600x1333.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="889" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103847576" class="wp-caption-text">网传受伤人员就医的照片。（网络图片）</figcaption></figure>
<p>面对网上各种传言和市民的重重疑虑，当晚松江区应急管理局只回应一句，“松江区并未发生爆炸，松江区政府会统一解释”。</p>
<p>这种“不问你信不信，就问你服不服”的“辟谣”，引发网民的怒火和各种猜测。有人说，“既然都出来辟谣了，说明肯定发生爆炸了。”</p>
<p>对于官方“辟谣”的原因，有网民认为可能只是“新年前维稳”，也有网民猜测可能“有大事发生”。有人说“是不是发生恐怖袭击了”，有人则怀疑是否当地军方“打响了起义第一枪”。</p>
<p>（记者周归航综合报导/责任编辑：徐耕文）</p>

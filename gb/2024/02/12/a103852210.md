<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>CNN盘点32种必吃中国菜 看看英文名是啥</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id103852211-03012-600x400-1-868x488.jpg" />
<h6>宋代大文豪苏轼为后代留下许多美妙诗词，更留下东坡肉。（维基百科／大纪元合成）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【新唐人北京时间2024年02月12日讯】<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/中华美食.md#1">中华美食</a>历史悠久，名扬海内外，讲究色香味俱全，烹饪技巧丰富多样，酱料搭配丰富，不同地区有不同菜系。随着越来越多的中国人移民西方国家，中华美食也逐渐地被西方人知晓。时值中国新年期间，美国媒体盘点了32道必吃的<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/中国菜.md#1">中国菜</a>。</p>
<p>“民以食为天”，中国自古以来饮食文化博大精深，是中华传统文化的重要组成部分。<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/中国菜.md#1">中国菜</a>的特色是品种繁多，中华大地，从南到北，从东到西，令人难以置信的丰富，无穷无尽，永远不会让你感到厌倦。</p>
<p>由于气候、物产和风俗的差异，各地的饮食习惯和口味爱好迥然不同。源远流长的烹调艺术经过历代的创造和承传，形成了丰富多彩的地方菜系。</p>
<p>那么在外国人眼中，哪些中国菜最富盛名和美味呢？以下是CNN盘点的32道必吃的中国菜，其中一些菜肴对外国人来说，是不可思议和不可置信的。一起来看看它们的英文名字是什么，以及CNN对每道菜的简介。</p>
<p>其中一些中文菜名精简清晰；用英文表述就变成很繁琐，读完还得想一想到底是什么中国菜；有些菜肴甚至无法用英文表述。</p>
<p><strong>1.北京烤鸭（Peking duck）：</strong>?一口酥脆的鸭皮，多汁的鸭肉，萝卜、黄瓜、葱、甜面酱，一起包裹在薄薄的饼里。</p>
<p><figure id="attachment_103852213" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103852213" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img decoding="async" fetchpriority="high" class="wp-image-103852213 size-medium" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id103852213-1007221517541366-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103852213" class="wp-caption-text">招牌北京烤鸭。（潘在殊／大纪元）</figcaption></figure><br />
<strong>2.鸭血粉丝（Vermicelli with duck blood）：</strong>这道街头美食由鸭骨汤、鸭血和鸭肝、鸭肫等鸭内脏制成，充分利用了鸭子的每个部位，带来令人难以置信的浓郁风味。</p>
<p><strong>3.清蒸鱼（Steamed fish）：</strong>蒸鱼看似简单，但其实是一门很难掌握的厨艺。</p>
<p><figure id="attachment_103852214" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103852214" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img decoding="async" class="wp-image-103852214 size-full" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id103852214-9e6eda74dcb9cb8bb96f548a07cc9c3e-600x338-1.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103852214" class="wp-caption-text">清蒸鱼。（新唐人）</figcaption></figure></figure>
<p><strong>4.担担面（Dandan noodles）：</strong>想要不喜欢中国的担担面几乎是不可能的。问题是：要尝试哪个版本？</p>
<p><strong>5.剁椒鱼头（Steamed fish head with chopped salted chili）：</strong>没有哪道菜比“剁椒鱼头”更能代表湖南菜。</p>
<p><figure id="attachment_103852215" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103852215" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img decoding="async" class="size-full wp-image-103852215" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id103852215-140719103731836-600x400-1.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103852215" class="wp-caption-text">剁椒鱼头。（张学慧／大纪元）</figcaption></figure><br />
<strong>6.粤式烧鹅（Roasted goose）：</strong>当烧鹅入嘴时，它会神奇地分解，带来脆皮、融化的脂肪和嫩肉的混合风味。</p>
<p><strong>7.潮州冻蟹／鱼（Chaozhou cold crab/fish）：</strong>最大限度地发挥新鲜食材的原始鲜味。</p>
<p><strong>8.过桥米线（Cross-the-bridge rice noodles）：</strong>相传过桥米线是多年前由一名有爱心的妻子发明的，她每天都要过桥给读书丈夫送午餐。为了让丈夫吃到热腾腾的午餐，这位妻子带上一锅滚烫的鸡汤，外加米线和原料。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103852216" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103852216" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="wp-image-103852216 size-medium" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id103852216-1002152252501983-600x503.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="503" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103852216" class="wp-caption-text">加了蛋、肉与猪肝的“过桥米线”是营养美味的滇菜小吃。（彩霞／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>9.宫保鸡丁（Kungpao chicken）：</strong>宫保鸡丁是国际上最著名的中国菜之一，由鸡肉丁与葱、姜、花椒、辣椒和油炸花生一起炒制而成。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103852217" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103852217" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-full wp-image-103852217" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id103852217-Kung-Bao-YT4-600x338-1.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="338" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103852217" class="wp-caption-text">麻辣宫保鸡丁，超级下饭。（美食天堂提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>10.咕噜肉／荔枝肉（Sweet and sour pork / Lychee pork）：</strong>菜中的凤梨和糖、醋、酱油调成酱汁，为脆皮肉增添几分新鲜感。</p>
<p><figure id="attachment_103852218" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103852218" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-full wp-image-103852218" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id103852218-1006220330531366-600x383-1.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="383" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103852218" class="wp-caption-text">咕噜肉。（邝天明／大纪元）</figcaption></figure><br />
<strong>11.水饺（Dumplings）：</strong>吃了饺子后谁还需要炸薯条？无论你是喜欢蒸、煮或煎，饺子一口就富含碳水化合物、蛋白质和蔬菜。</p>
<p><figure id="attachment_103852219" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103852219" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-full wp-image-103852219" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id103852219-b70654c6f37bf581fef938c620bbc76a-600x401-1.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103852219" class="wp-caption-text">水饺。(Shutterstock)</figcaption></figure><br />
<strong>12.福建肉燕饺子（Rouyan pork dumplings）：</strong>最有趣的饺子风格之一是福建的肉燕饺子——美味到足以在这份名单上占一席之地。</p>
<p><strong>13.文昌鸡饭（Wenchang chicken rice）：</strong>虽然海南鸡饭实际上并非来自海南省（它首先在马来西亚），但这道菜的灵感来自海南及其著名的文昌鸡。</p>
<p><strong>14.麻婆豆腐（Mapo tofu ）：</strong>挑剔的当地美食家坚持认为，最好的麻婆豆腐应该用来自四川郫都地区的汉源花椒和蚕豆辣椒酱制成的。</p>
<p><figure id="attachment_103852220" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103852220" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-full wp-image-103852220" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id103852220-DSC_7452-600x401-1.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="401" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103852220" class="wp-caption-text">麻婆豆腐。（龚安妮／大纪元）</figcaption></figure><br />
<strong>15.梅菜扣肉（Pork belly with dry, pickled Chinese mustard）：</strong>鲜嫩红烧的五花肉自然令人无法抗拒，但这道客家菜的明星实际上是梅菜，为这道丰盛的炖菜增添酸咸的味道。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103852221" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103852221" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-full wp-image-103852221" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id103852221-1601310341152763-600x400-1.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103852221" class="wp-caption-text">梅菜扣肉，客家菜代表，菜、肉和汤汁比例拿捏得恰到好处。（许心如／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>16.炒饭（Fried rice）：</strong>无论是用切丁的鲍鱼和松露制成的升级版炒饭，还是冰箱里剩下的即将变质食材做出的炒饭，每一种经典炒饭都有两种重要成分——干燥但多汁的米饭和镬气（Wok Hei）。</p>
<p><figure id="attachment_103852222" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103852222" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-full wp-image-103852222" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id103852222-shutterstock_1730946862-600x400-1.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103852222" class="wp-caption-text">用剩饭做出味道诱人的蛋炒饭，需要用一些技巧提炼出食材的美味。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure></figure>
<p><strong>17.煎饼果子（无）：</strong>天津街头早餐。</p>
<p><figure id="attachment_103852223" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103852223" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-full wp-image-103852223" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id103852223-1602070402132763-600x451-1.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="451" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103852223" class="wp-caption-text">煎饼果子是天津街头巷尾常见的早餐小吃。（谭安／大纪元）</figcaption></figure><br />
<strong>18.小笼包（无）：</strong>想知道小笼包皮是否会在蒸笼和进嘴之间的漫长旅程中破裂，这是餐桌上最悬念的时刻之一。</p>
<p><figure id="attachment_103852224" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103852224" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-full wp-image-103852224" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id103852224-shutterstock_356894687-600x419-1.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="419" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103852224" class="wp-caption-text">西方人一提到中国的水饺就会联想到小笼包。以皮薄、馅料丰满、充满鲜美的汤汁精华闻名于世。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure><br />
<strong>19.陈村米粉（Chen cun rice noodles）：</strong>如果你是那些认为牛肉炒粉真正的亮点是粉而不是牛肉的人之一，那么应该尝试陈村米粉。</p>
<p><strong>20.东坡肉（Dongpo pork）：</strong>东坡肉以著名诗人、画家、政治家苏东坡的名字命名，由红烧五花肉、冰糖、酱油、黄酒等调味料制成。</p>
<p><strong>21.酸辣汤（Hot and sour soup）：</strong>一碗酸辣汤应该有酸（醋）和辣（辣椒）的平衡，但不是辣椒的辣。</p>
<p><figure id="attachment_103852225" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103852225" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-full wp-image-103852225" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id103852225-20190902_shouhuihsu_Hot_and_Sour-Soup_01-600x400-1.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103852225" class="wp-caption-text">酸辣汤。（图／美食天堂提供）</figcaption></figure><br />
<strong>22.中式点心（Dim sum）：</strong>粤菜文化中的一种膳食——而不是一种特定的菜肴。</p>
<p><figure id="attachment_103852226" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103852226" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-full wp-image-103852226" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id103852226-901231757182181-600x443-1.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="443" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103852226" class="wp-caption-text">中式点心。（郑严／大纪元）</figcaption></figure><br />
<strong>23.水煮鱼／牛肉（Beef/fish poached in chili oil broth）：</strong>?“水”指的是辛辣的辣椒油汤，用来煮切成薄片的牛肉（水煮牛）、猪肉（水煮肉片）或鱼（水煮鱼）。</p>
<p><figure id="attachment_103852227" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103852227" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-full wp-image-103852227" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id103852227-1-3-4TaiwanFood-2-600x337-1.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="337" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103852227" class="wp-caption-text">水煮鱼。（黄剑宇／大纪元）</figcaption></figure><br />
<strong>24.叉烧肉（Barbecue pork）：</strong>最好的叉烧应该是外表微焦，含有恰到好处的麦芽糖、酒和酱油的甜咸味。</p>
<p><figure id="attachment_103852228" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103852228" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-full wp-image-103852228" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id103852228-Char-Siu1-600x400-1.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103852228" class="wp-caption-text">广式叉烧肉。（图／美食天堂提供）</figcaption></figure><br />
<strong>25.包子（Bao）：</strong>包子是一种馒头卷，里面填满了各种配料，包括肉类或蔬菜，有多种形状和大小。</p>
<p><figure id="attachment_103852229" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103852229" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-full wp-image-103852229" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id103852229-IMG_0103-1-1068x892-600x501-1.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="501" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103852229" class="wp-caption-text">包子。（勤宜／大纪元）</figcaption></figure><br />
<strong>26.肉夹馍（无）：</strong>西安街头小吃，包括烤馍和手撕五花肉，这些五花肉在酱油、冰糖和肉桂、八角、丁香和花椒等香料中炖了几个小时。</p>
<p><figure id="attachment_103852230" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103852230" style="width: 582px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-full wp-image-103852230" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id103852230-1553e3a2262800c4_ttl7dayWBS_Screen_Shot_2018-09-11_at_3.49.49_PM.png" alt="" width="582" b="486" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103852230" class="wp-caption-text">肉夹馍。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure><br />
<strong>27.粤式老火靓汤（Cantonese herbal soup）：</strong>这些汤要煮上几个小时，使汤汁具有治疗功效和美味。</p>
<p><strong>28.腊肠（Preserved sausages）：</strong>与欧洲腊肠不同，中国腊肠在食用前应先蒸熟。</p>
<p><figure id="attachment_103852231" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103852231" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-full wp-image-103852231" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id103852231-989d6916a18712b12052ef0ed71d3637.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="335" /><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103852231" class="wp-caption-text">腊肠。(Laurent Fievet/AFP/GettyImages)</figcaption></figure><br />
<strong>29.甜芋泥（Sweet taro paste）：</strong>芋泥的美味秘诀在荔浦芋头，又名槟榔芋。这种芋头的果肉呈白色和紫色，香气浓郁，味道带有泥土、坚果和甜味。</p>
<p><strong>30.粥（Congee）：</strong>瘦肉松皮蛋粥是中国南方的经典食物之一。北部流行以小米代大米、南瓜调味的粥。</p>
<p><strong>31. 潮州牛肉丸（Chaozhou beef balls）：</strong>超级有弹性、味道鲜美的牛肉丸子。</p>
<p><strong>32.甜汤圆（Sweet rice balls）：</strong>柔软的枕头状外皮是用糯米制成的，而馅料是用黑芝麻、糖和猪油制成的。</p>

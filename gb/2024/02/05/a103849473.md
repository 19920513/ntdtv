<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>福建高速多车相撞起火爆炸 现场惨烈至少7死</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id103849478-FotoJet-868x488.jpg" />
<h6>2024年2月4日，福建省沙厦高速公路发生惨烈车祸，至少7人死亡。（网络视频截图）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【新唐人北京时间2024年02月06日讯】临近中国新年，<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/福建省.md#1">福建省</a>沙厦<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/高速公路.md#1">高速公路</a>发生<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/惨烈车祸.md#1">惨烈车祸</a>，一辆牵引车连撞多辆小汽车，导致起火爆炸，至少7人死亡。</p>
<p>福建泉州高速交警支队通报，2024年2月4日9时许，沙厦<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/高速公路.md#1">高速公路</a>德化境内下坡路段发生一起一辆重型半挂牵引车追尾4辆小汽车后起火的交通事故，造成7人死亡。</p>
<p>网传视频显示，现场浓烟滚滚，满地车辆碎片，除了起火爆炸的车辆，还有小轿车被撞击得严重变形。</p>
<p>根据身在现场的网民爆料，车祸造成堵车三个多小时。多名网民表示，自己有亲友死于这场事故。</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id103849474-2ddec9ee7fd3cebd6a680a040dc9f526.jpg"><img decoding="async" fetchpriority="high" class="aligncenter wp-image-103849474" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id103849474-2ddec9ee7fd3cebd6a680a040dc9f526-600x342.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="228" /></a></p>
<p><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id103849475-429b023e1662afee2f1cd2013028a736.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="aligncenter wp-image-103849475" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id103849475-429b023e1662afee2f1cd2013028a736-600x298.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="199" /></a></p>
<figure id="attachment_103849479" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103849479" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id103849479-8bac73e4533e2ef6a3d7937e0069615d.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="wp-image-103849479" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id103849479-8bac73e4533e2ef6a3d7937e0069615d-600x150.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="100" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103849479" class="wp-caption-text">多名知情网民爆料。（网页截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>有目击者称，先是一辆大车冒着火冲过去，将一辆黑色小车撞出十多米远，然后开始起火爆炸。“总共有四辆车烧没了，还有一个小孩”。</p>
<p>“那辆黑色小轿车里面的人一个都没跑出来。”</p>
<p><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id103849476-f92db16de75acb70686a3104928657ae.jpg"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="aligncenter wp-image-103849476" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id103849476-f92db16de75acb70686a3104928657ae-600x277.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="185" /></a></p>
<figure id="attachment_103849477" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103849477" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id103849477-597697f3b62ad59c9f4efe0167e7f521.jpg"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="wp-image-103849477" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/02/id103849477-597697f3b62ad59c9f4efe0167e7f521-600x231.jpg" alt="" width="400" b="154" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103849477" class="wp-caption-text">车祸现场网民爆料。（网页截图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>近期中国气候异常，连南方都大范围降下冻雨和冰雪，因为路滑，交通事故频发。</p>

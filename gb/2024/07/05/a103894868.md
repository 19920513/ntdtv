<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>美洲杯：罚失三粒点球 厄瓜多尔惜败阿根廷</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id103894972-GettyImages-2159973630-868x488.jpg" />
<h6>2024年7月4日，美洲杯首场八强淘汰赛在美国德州休斯顿市NRG体育场进行，卫冕冠军阿根廷队与厄瓜多尔队激战90分钟不分胜负。两队进行点球大战，阿根廷门将达米安·马丁内斯（红衣）发挥出色，扑出对方两个点球，帮助球队4比2击败对手，晋级半决赛。(CHARLY TRIBALLEAU/AFP via Getty Images)</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【新唐人北京时间2024年07月05日讯】7月4日，<ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/美洲杯">美洲杯</a>首场八强淘汰赛在美国德州休斯顿市NRG体育场进行，卫冕冠军<ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/阿根廷">阿根廷</a>队与<ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/厄瓜多尔">厄瓜多尔</a>队激战90分钟各入一球，不分胜负。在<ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/点球大战">点球大战</a>中，阿根廷门将达米安·马丁内斯发挥出色，扑出对方两个点球，帮助球队4比2击败对手，晋级半决赛。</p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/美洲杯">美洲杯</a>历史上，双方此前一共交手16次，<ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/阿根廷">阿根廷</a>11胜5平，未尝败绩。在上一届2021年巴西美洲杯上，两队同样是在1/4决赛中相遇，当时阿根廷3比0完胜对手。</p>
<p>上半时<ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/厄瓜多尔">厄瓜多尔</a>队踢得并不保守，传接球熟练，给卫冕冠军造成很大威胁。第15分钟，边锋萨米恩托门前捅射，被门将用腿挡出，险些进球。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103894973" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103894973" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id103894973-GettyImages-2160651650-scaled.jpg"><img decoding="async" fetchpriority="high" class="wp-image-103894973 size-medium" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id103894973-GettyImages-2160651650-600x400.jpg" alt="HOUSTON, TEXAS - JULY 04: Jeremy Sarmiento of Ecuador battles for possession with Cristian Romero of Argentina during the CONMEBOL Copa America 2024 quarter-final match between Argentina and Ecuador at NRG Stadium on July 04, 2024 in Houston, Texas. " width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103894973" class="wp-caption-text">第15分钟，厄瓜多尔边锋16号萨米恩托门前捅射，险些破门。(Buda Mendes/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>然而卫冕冠军经验老到。尽管场面并不占优，第35分钟阿根廷还是利用角球机会破门得分，将比分改写为1比0。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103894974" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103894974" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id103894974-GettyImages-2159966881-scaled.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="wp-image-103894974 size-medium" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id103894974-GettyImages-2159966881-600x400.jpg" alt="TOPSHOT - Argentina's defender #25 Lisandro Martinez celebrates scoring his team's first goal during the Conmebol 2024 Copa America tournament quarter-final football match between Argentina and Ecuador at NRG Stadium in Houston, Texas, on July 4, 2024. (Photo by CHARLY TRIBALLEAU / AFP) " width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103894974" class="wp-caption-text">第35分钟，阿根廷队后卫25号利桑德罗·马丁内斯后点头球破门，打入自己在国家队的首粒进球。(CHARLY TRIBALLEAU/AFP via Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>下半时第62分钟，阿根廷球员禁区内手球犯规，被主裁判处以极刑。厄瓜多尔队长、前锋瓦伦西亚主罚，虽骗过门将，但过于追求角度，击中门柱弹出，错失绝佳机会。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103894975" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103894975" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id103894975-GettyImages-2160656820-scaled.jpg"><img decoding="async" class="size-medium wp-image-103894975" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id103894975-GettyImages-2160656820-600x400.jpg" alt="HOUSTON, TEXAS - JULY 04: Enner Valencia of Ecuador misses a penalty during the CONMEBOL Copa America 2024 quarter-final match between Argentina and Ecuador at NRG Stadium on July 04, 2024 in Houston, Texas. " width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103894975" class="wp-caption-text">第62分钟，厄瓜多尔13号老将瓦伦西亚主罚点球，太过追求角度，打中立柱弹出，错失得分机会。(Buda Mendes/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>本场比赛，球王<ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/梅西">梅西</a>似乎不在状态，阿根廷也未能利用领先优势，组织起有效的反击。而厄瓜多尔主教练桑切斯则频繁调兵遣将，继续向对手施压。</p>
<p>伤停补时阶段，厄瓜多尔两位替补球员完成配合，约翰·叶博亚右路斜传禁区，凯文·罗德里格斯头球后蹭，攻破了门将达米安·马丁内斯的十指关，将比分扳平。这是阿根廷队本届杯赛的首个失球。</p>
<p>比赛最后一分钟，厄瓜多尔险些绝杀对手，中锋乔迪·凯塞多门前抢点攻门偏出，惊出卫冕冠军一身冷汗。</p>
<p>最终全场比赛结束，两队战成1比1平，阿根廷队国际赛事八连胜就此止步。</p>
<p>根据本届美洲杯赛规定，除决赛以外的其它淘汰赛，如果两队在90分钟不分胜负，将不踢加时赛，直接进入点球决战。</p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/点球大战">点球大战</a>，球王<ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/梅西">梅西</a>首个出场，但其踢出的“勺子”点球打在横梁上弹出界外，出师不利。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103894976" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103894976" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id103894976-GettyImages-2159970367-scaled.jpg"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-medium wp-image-103894976" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id103894976-GettyImages-2159970367-600x400.jpg" alt="Argentina's forward #10 Lionel Messi gestures after missing a goal from the penalty spot during a penalty shoot out during the Conmebol 2024 Copa America tournament quarter-final football match between Argentina and Ecuador at NRG Stadium in Houston, Texas, on July 4, 2024. (Photo by CHARLY TRIBALLEAU / AFP) " width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103894976" class="wp-caption-text">2024年7月4日美洲杯首场1/4决赛，阿根廷对阵厄瓜多尔的比赛进入点球大战，梅西首个主罚，但击中横梁弹出界外。(CHARLY TRIBALLEAU/AFP via Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>不过此后成为阿根廷门将达米安·马丁内斯的个人表演，他连扑对方两个点球，力挽狂澜，将卫冕冠军从死亡线上拉了回来。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103894977" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103894977" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id103894977-GettyImages-2160668400-scaled.jpg"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-medium wp-image-103894977" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id103894977-GettyImages-2160668400-600x420.jpg" alt="HOUSTON, TEXAS - JULY 04: Emiliano Martinez of Argentina saves the first penalty from Angel Mena of Ecuador in the penalty shoot out during the CONMEBOL Copa America 2024 quarter-final match between Argentina and Ecuador at NRG Stadium on July 04, 2024 in Houston, Texas. " width="600" b="420" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103894977" class="wp-caption-text">2024年7月4日，美洲杯首场1/4决赛的点球大战中，阿根廷门将达米安·马丁内斯扑出厄瓜多尔两个点球，帮助球队晋级半决赛。(Buda Mendes/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>最终，阿根廷队5罚4中，而厄瓜多尔队4罚2中，最后一名罚球队员已无需出场，阿根廷4比2击败对手，率先挺进四强。他们将于7月9日，对决委内瑞拉和加拿大之间的胜者。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103894992" aria-describedby="caption-attachment-103894992" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><ahref="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id103894992-1170682694bac612bbf9f57d33c89e56-scaled.jpg"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="size-medium wp-image-103894992" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2024/07/id103894992-1170682694bac612bbf9f57d33c89e56-600x192.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="192" /></a><figcaption id="caption-attachment-103894992" class="wp-caption-text">2024美洲杯赛程赛果。（新唐人电视台制图）</figcaption></figure>
<p>阿根廷队首发（4-4-2）：23-达米安·马丁内斯/26-莫利纳、13-罗梅罗、25-利桑德罗·马丁内斯、3-塔利亚菲科/24-恩佐、20-麦卡利斯特、7-德保罗、15-尼古拉斯·冈萨雷斯/10-梅西、22-劳塔罗·马丁内斯</p>

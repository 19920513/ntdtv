<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【禁闻】青岛公安大地震 迫害法轮功遭恶报</h2>
<a href="https://dp5vkf7bbqo9d.cloudfront.net/O5s7QdPCz"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/19920513/www/master/t/ntdtv/ntdtv600.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/19920513/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【新唐人2011年10月14日讯】近日，山东青岛市公安系统多名高官突然被拘捕，因为涉及青岛“最大涉黑案”聂磊案，充当黑社会的保护伞，包括青岛市原公安局长王永利、市北区公安分局局长于国铭等人纷纷落马，引发青岛公安系统大地震。而这些落马的公安官员都是迫害法轮功的凶首。</p>
<p>广州《新快报》记者爆料：9月29号晚，青岛市李沧区公安分局局长冯越欣、市北区公安分局局长于国铭、市南区刑警大队长、崂山区政法委书记被抓；市南区公安分局局长在逃；崂山区政协主席也被抓捕；青岛市原公安局长王永利被双规。 </p>
<p>山东省公安厅发言人日前证实部分公安官员落马，案件涉及黑社会头目聂磊案。据说，聂磊案在宣判前叫停，中共高层指示此案要重办。 </p>
<p>青岛多家夜总会老板聂磊及130多人去年被逮捕，其中有15名处级官员，他们被控杀人、开赌场、非法持有枪支、贩毒、组织卖淫、敲诈勒索等罪名。 </p>
<p>被称为青岛“打黑英雄”的冯越欣，被曝光白天是公安局长，晚上是黑社会老大，他利用取保候审等机会搜刮巨额钱财，冯越欣在青岛的三处别墅价值500万元，并在澳洲投资矿产，妻儿早已移居澳洲。 </p>
<p>青岛落马的公安官员几乎都参与了对法轮功的迫害。在李沧区公安分局局长冯越欣的管辖区，法轮功学员关君、高铭霞、姜吉星等多人被警察绑架、抄家抢劫、非法关押劳教。 </p>
<p>《追查迫害法轮功国际组织》2009年发布了对青岛市市北区公安分局局长于国铭等人的追查通告。法轮功学员荆奉莉、李涛、陆雪琴等13人，2008年被市北区公安分局以“奥运”的名义绑架，13位学员都遭受酷刑折磨，其中，郑晓强被打得毁容，徐智峰的双眼被殴打几乎失明，刘连军前门的牙齿被打掉了一排。 </p>
<p>陆雪琴遭到警察连续9天9夜的殴打，造成全身肿胀、大面积瘀血和血栓，骨骼变形，昏迷不醒，多次濒临死亡，警方拒绝让她做手术而延误了治疗时机，造成她下半身瘫痪。 </p>
<p>陆雪琴：“就是拳打脚踢，他穿的那个大皮鞋，踢我的腹部、腰部。9天9夜没睡觉，迷糊就打我，提我的头发往地下摔，不让睡。” </p>
<p>《自由亚洲电台》等媒体报导了陆雪琴等青岛学员被迫害的案例。当地法院为了掩盖酷刑，把陆雪琴作为个案，与其他12名学员分开。江天勇、李苏滨、韩智广等15名维权律师为这13位法轮功学员作无罪辩护。 </p>
<p>2009年3月此案开庭审理，15名律师们认为整个案件从始至终都严重违法，15名律师集体拒绝出庭表达抗议，并联名控告青岛市法院违法。 </p>
<p>维权律师江天勇：“我觉得这完全是一个系统的、非常系统的违法，这种情况是非常严重的，我既没有碰到过，我也没有听说过，我觉得这太荒唐了。做为法官要公然去违法，我们就认为他这种行为，说真的，就是一种颠覆国家政权，你把一个国家的法律彻底给颠覆，那这个国家还能运行吗？” </p>
<p>被双规的青岛市公安局原局长、党委书记王永利，是当地迫害法轮功的主要凶手之一，数次被《追查国际》发布通告追查他的迫害罪行。 </p>
<p>自1999以来，青岛地区被迫害致死的法轮功学员至少有39位，被非法劳教、判刑的学员高达数千人，多人被判十年以上重刑，至少60名学员曾被绑架进精神病院，遭强迫注射破坏中枢神经药物以及电针、灌食等摧残，更多的学员遭受酷刑、洗脑、关押、经济敲诈等等。 </p>
<p>青岛其他迫害法轮功的主要凶手，包括青岛市原公安局长万国忠，因涉黄赌黑畏罪自杀﹔青岛莱西市教体局局长张为才被人枪击身亡；青岛市检察长刘命信因酗酒突然死亡；青岛市法院副院长刘青峰涉贪污案被捕；原青岛市委书记杜世成涉“公共情妇门”，被判无期徒刑。 </p>
<p>《明慧网》指出，善恶有报是天理，参与迫害的中共官员，不知悔改，都将受到天理严厉的惩罚。 </p>
<p>新唐人记者李元翰、萧宇综合报导。</p>
<p>Security Officials Caught In Mafia Case</p>
<p>Recently, many Qingdao Public Security officials<br />have been arrested suddenly for their involvement in the largest mafia case ever in Qingdao city.<br />They included former director Wang Yongli,<br />north district branch director Yu Guoming and more.<br />This has caused a major shake<br />in the Qingdao public security system.<br />All of the arrested officials are culprits<br />in the persecution of Falun Gong practitioners.</p>
<p>Guangzhou based New Express reported that on Sept. 29,<br />of the arrests of Qingdao police security officials,<br />like the branch director of Lichang district, Feng Yuexin,<br />north district branch director Yu Guoming,<br />south district detective chief, and Laoshan district<br />party secretary of the Political and Judiciary Committee.<br />The South district branch director is on the run.</p>
<p>Laoshao district political consultation chair was also arrested.<br />Former Qingdao bureau director Wang Yongli was detained.</p>
<p>The spokesperson for Shandong provincial public security<br />authorities has recently confirmed the arrests of the officials,<br />as they are involved in the case of mafia gang leader Nie Lei.<br />Right before the sentencing, the case was called off.<br />The high-level of the Chinese Communist Party (CCP)<br />wanted the case to be re-evaluated.</p>
<p>Last year, Nie Lei, owner of several night clubs and over 130<br />others were arrested, among which 15 were CCP officials.<br />They are charged with homicide, running casino businesses,<br />illegal possession of weapons, trafficking, organized prostitution, blackmailing and extortion.</p>
<p>The so-called “anti-mafia” hero Feng Yuexin was revealed<br />to be a public security director during the day and a gang leader at night.<br />He had amassed a huge fortune taking advantage of paroles.</p>
<p>Feng has three villas worth over RMB5 million in Qingdao.<br />He has invested in the Australian mining industry.<br />His wife and children have long emigrated to Australia.</p>
<p>All the arrested officials in Qingdao are culprits<br />in the persecution of Falun Gong.<br />In the Lichang district where branch director Feng Yuexin<br />was in charge, several Falun Gong practitioners including<br />Guang Jun, Gao Minxia and Jiang Jixing were abducted,<br />home ransacked and illegally sentenced to forced labor camps.</p>
<p>The World Organization to Investigate the Persecution of<br />Falun Gong issued in 2009 an announcement to investigate<br />the north district branch director Yu Guomin and others.<br />Thirteen Falun Gong practitioners including Jing Fengli, Li Tao<br />and Lu Xueqin were abducted by police from this branch<br />in 2008 in the name of Olympic Games. They were all tortured.<br />Among them, Zheng Xiaoqiang was beaten to disfigurement.<br />Xue Zhifeng was beaten to near blindness. Liu Lianjun’s front teeth were all knocked out.</p>
<p>Lu Xueqin was beaten by the police for 9 consecutive days,<br />causing her body to be swollen and bruised all over.<br />She was in deep coma with skeletal deformities.<br />Several times, she was on the brim of death.<br />Police refused her medical treatments, causing her to miss<br />the optimal treatment time and she is paralyzed since.</p>
<p>Lu Xueqin: “They beat me with hands and feet, with boots,<br />against my abdomen and waist.<br />I was deprived of sleep for 9 days. If I was to fall asleep<br />they beat my head against the ground to keep me awake.”</p>
<p>Radio Free Asia and other media have reported on the case<br />of Lu Xueqin and other Falun Gong practitioners in Qingdao.<br />The local court separated Lu’s case from other 12 practitioners<br />in order to cover up the facts on torture.<br />Fifteen human rights lawyers including Jiang Tianyong,<br />Li Subin and Han Zhiguang defended these 13 practitioners.</p>
<p>The court held the first hearing on this case in March 2009.</p>
<p>The 15 lawyers all believe the authorities are seriously<br />illegal in handling this case throughout the entire process.<br />The 15 lawyers lodged a protest by collectively refusing to<br />appear in court. They also sued the court for illegal conduct.</p>
<p>Rights lawyer Jiang Tianyong: “I believe this is a systematic,<br />very systematic breach of the law.<br />This is a serious situation, which I never heard<br />or came across before. It is rather ridiculous.<br />The judge is brazenly violating the law. His actions<br />themselves are, we believe, kind of subversion of state power.<br />When you completely subvert the law of a country,<br />how could this country still be functioning?”</p>
<p>The now detained ex-chief of the Public Security Bureau<br />and a former CCP’ secretary, Wang Yongli,<br />is one of the major culprits who persecute Falun Gong locally.<br />He has been placed in the investigation list several times<br />by the World Organization to Investigate<br />the Persecution of Falun Gong.</p>
<p>Since 1999, there have been at least 39 Falun Gong<br />practitioners who were persecuted to death.<br />Several thousand practitioners were illegally sentenced<br />to forced labor camps or given prison terms,<br />were forcibly injected with psychotropic drugs,<br />and suffered from force feedings.<br />Many more practitioners have been tortured, subjected to<br />brainwashing, detainments and financial extortions.</p>
<p>Other culprits persecuting Falun Gong in Qingdao include<br />ex-chief of public security bureau Wang Guozhong,<br />who committed suicide due to his involvement in mafia gangs;<br />Laixi city education bureau director Zhang Weicai,<br />who was assassinated.<br />Qingdao prosecution director Liu Mingxin,<br />who died a sudden death due to alcoholism.<br />Qingdao court’s associate director Liu Qingfeng,<br />who was arrested due to corruption.<br />ex-CCP secretary of Qingdao Du Shicheng,<br />who received life sentence for the “public mistress incident.”</p>
<p>As Clearwisdom.net points out, good is rewarded with good<br />and evil meets with retribution.<br />The CCP officials involved in the persecution of Falun Gong<br />will meet with punishment should they do not stop and repent.</p>
<p>NTD reporters Li Yuanhan and Xiao Yu</p>

<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【禁闻】迫害法轮功 沈阳康平党官频遭恶报</h2>
<a href="https://d1205lb7lnyr8t.cloudfront.net/M5oTZ8nDg"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/19920513/www/master/t/ntdtv/ntdtv600.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/19920513/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【新唐人2011年10月4日讯】中国辽宁省是迫害<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/法轮功.md#1">法轮功</a>最严重的地区之一，据不完全统计，至今已有4百多名法轮功学员被迫害致死。辽宁省会沈阳市以马三家劳教所、张士劳动教养院最为臭名昭著。然而，善恶有报、天理昭昭，以迫害至今仍然严重的沈阳市康平县为例，主导迫害的党官们频频遭到恶报的下场。</p>
<p>《追查迫害<ahref="https://github.com/19920513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/法轮功.md#1">法轮功</a>国际组织》今年6月发布追查通告，对沈阳市康平县政法委书记刘景山、康平县公安局长王天明、沈阳市公安局长许文有等人立案追查。 </p>
<p>6月1号，在刘景山与沈阳市公安局指挥下，康平县公安局对法轮功学员进行了一系列的疯狂迫害行动，导致七名学员被非法拘捕，四名学员被骚扰，一名学员被迫流离失所，大量的电脑、打印机、法轮功书籍和资料等私人财产被抄家抢劫。 </p>
<p>参与这次行动和涉案的有康平县小城子镇派出所所长赵洪江、指导员姚立军、警员刘兰田、张东明，胜利乡派出所所长戴永胜、教导员宋洪波，镇北派出所所长刘春雨，开发区派出所所长刘慧权等人。 </p>
<p>6月1号当晚，镇北派出所4名警察，拿着电子发射器对法轮功学员李满新家进行了非法搜查。9月21号早晨6点左右，建设街委和镇北派出所警察7、8个人，再次到李满新家翻箱倒柜无证搜查，把墙上贴的“法轮大法好”福字摘走。 </p>
<p>李满新曾被非法劳教于沈阳市张士劳动教养院。为逼迫法轮功学员放弃“真、善、忍”信仰，张士教养院一度盛行使用“十八蒙”酷刑进行所谓的“转化”。 </p>
<p>陈松(沈阳法轮功学员)：“中共政府镇压法轮功，不惜用巨额资金，拉拢腐蚀警察、还有610办公室，他们就是要我们放弃信仰，以挂靠奖金为名，他采取手段非常邪恶。” </p>
<p>2002年4月25号，李效元、王进民、陈松、张振武、李满新等多名法轮功学员，拒穿号服和奴工劳动，同时声明修炼法轮功无罪，要求还大法清白。张士教养院在院长程殿坤的指使下成立了迫害小组。</p>
<p>四大队长杨树和一帮犯人用12万伏电棍电击王进民、李效元，并且围殴、狠踢他们的头部、大腿根部等处。李效元于2003年11月9号在那里被迫害致死。</p>
<p>5月7号，三大队教导员高广臣、大队长王勇带领几名犯人把陈松、张振武的衣服扒光，手戴上背铐，用20多根电棍电了他俩一上午。5月9号又对李满新施行了电击。2002年冬天，陈松、张振武、李满新等学员被关进墙上挂满白霜的“冷冻房”面壁而立。</p>
<p>陈松：“我也遭过他用电棍电，强制转化，其实也是遭受他让我们到那个‘冷冻房’，他所谓的‘冷冻房’，就是冬天比较没有温度的房间让我们在里面壁站着，进行体罚迫害，还有就是用不让睡觉进行迫害，让你精神上崩溃，让你放弃修炼。”</p>
<p>《追查国际》先后对原康平县委书记邓福林、原县长徐凤翔、原政法委书记李兴文、原公安局长牟国明等人，发布了追查通告。</p>
<p>另一方面，辽宁省及康平县迫害法轮功的党官们连连遭到报应。辽宁省政法委副书记、610头子朱锦遭恶报，殃及儿子暴病身亡。去年11月，42岁的康平县长赵连渤突发心梗死亡。</p>
<p>去年1月，沈阳市公安局指挥中心副主任刘效明，从8楼坠下当场身亡。刘效明任康平县、法库县公安局长期间，积极迫害法轮功，众多法轮功学员被绑架、拘留、劳教、判刑。</p>
<p>还有原康平县委副书记田柏祥因迫害法轮功，殃及儿子离奇车祸死亡；原康平县公安局长张军被撤职、儿子车祸；原康平县小城子镇党委副书记李洪涛突发心脏病死亡；原康平县法官范斌在诬判法轮功学员后一个月，自己便锒铛入狱成了阶下囚。等等。</p>
<p>《明慧网》奉劝中共公检法等官员，不要昧着良心干坏事，赶紧悬崖勒马，立即释放被关押的法轮功学员。否则，恶报临头时，后悔已经晚了！</p>
<p>新唐人记者何平、李元翰、李月采访报导。</p>
<p>Officials Face Retributions For Falun Gong?</p>
<p>Liaoning is one of the provinces in China, where<br />persecution of Falun Gong (Falun Dafa) is the most severe.<br />According to statistics, over 400 Falun Gong practitioners<br />have been persecuted to death in Liaoning.<br />Masanjia and Zhangshi Forced Labor Camps<br />in Shenyang City are among the most notorious institutions,<br />known for persecuting Falun Gong practitioners. However,<br />as the saying goes, good is rewarded, evil receives retribution.<br />Many Chinese officials of Kangping County, Shenyang City,<br />involved in the persecution, seem to meet with retributions.</p>
<p>The World Organization to Investigate the Persecution<br />of Falun Gong (WOIPFG) released a report in June,</p>
<p>requesting to investigate Liu Jingshan, the secretary<br />of the Political and Legal Committee of Kangping County,</p>
<p>Wang Tianming, head of the Kangping County police<br />and Xu Wenyou, head of the Shenyang City police.</p>
<p>On June 1, Liu and Shenyang City police illegally arrested<br />seven Falun Gong practitioners. Four others were harassed.</p>
<p>One was forced out on the streets, homeless. Many computers,<br />printers, Falun Gong books and materials were confiscated.</p>
<p>Involved in this arrests are police staff from Xiaochengzi<br />and Shengli Counties, Zhenbei and the Kaifa District.</p>
<p>On the night of June 1, four policemen from Zhenbei police<br />illegally searched Falun Gong practitioner Li Manxin’s home.</p>
<p>At 6am of September 21, the street committee and staff<br />of Zhenbei Police Station again illegally searched Li’s home.</p>
<p>In the process they also ripped off there a banner,<br />displaying the words “Falun Dafa is Good.”</p>
<p>Li had previously been sent to Zhangshi Forced Labor Camp.<br />The forced labor camps use various torture methods</p>
<p>to force Falun Gong practitioners to give up their belief<br />in “Truthfulness, Compassion, Tolerance”.</p>
<p>Chen Song (Falun Gong Practitioner in Shenyang City):<br />“The Chinese Communist Party (CCP) spends huge amounts</p>
<p>of money to persecute Falun Gong practitioners.<br />In order to lure police and Office 610 agents to help them,</p>
<p>the CCP awards them with money if they could force us<br />to give up our belief. The methods used are very evil.”</p>
<p>On April 25, 2002, several Falun Gong practitioners including<br />Li Manxin refused to wear prisoners’ clothes and do slavery.</p>
<p>They declared that practicing Falun Gong is innocent<br />and demanded the good name of Falun Gong to be restored.</p>
<p>Zhangshi Forced Labor Camp’s head, Cheng Diankun<br />ordered to form a special group to persecute Falun Gong.</p>
<p>Yang Shu, the Fourth Squad captain, and a group of inmates<br />used 120,000-volt electric batons</p>
<p>in the torture process of practitioners Wang Jinmin<br />and Li Xiaoyuan. They kicked their heads and bodies.</p>
<p>Li Xiaoyuan was persecuted to death on November 9, 2003.</p>
<p>On May 7, Gao Guangchen, a trainer of the Third Squad<br />and Wang Yong, the captain of the squad,</p>
<p>led several inmates to strip off the clothes of Falun Gong<br />practitioners Chen Song and Zhang Zhenwu.</p>
<p>They handcuffed and applied on them over 20 electric shocks.<br />On May 9, they again used electric shocks on Li Manxin.</p>
<p>In the winter of 2002, practitioners Chen Song, Li Manxin<br />and Zhang Chenwu were locked into a frosty cold room.</p>
<p>Chen Song: “I was tortured with electric batons.<br />He forced us to stand in the ice-cold room.</p>
<p>The so-called ‘cold room’ is a room with freezing temperature.<br />He forced us to stand facing the wall as a form of punishment.</p>
<p>They also did not allow us to sleep, as to force us<br />to mentally collapse and give up the practice.”</p>
<p>WOIPFG previously issued a notice to investigate<br />Deng Fulin, former CCP secretary of Kangping County,</p>
<p>Xu Fengxiang, former Mayor of Kangping,<br />Li Xingwen, former Political and Legal Committee Secretary,</p>
<p>and Mou Guoming, former chief of Police.</p>
<p>Besides, Chinese officials involved in the persecution<br />of Falun Gong seem to be receiving retributions too.</p>
<p>The son of Zhu Jin, Vice Secretary of the Political and Legal<br />Committee of Liaoning Province, and head of the 610 office,</p>
<p>died of a sudden illness. Last November, 42-years old Mayor<br />of Kangping County, Zhao Lianbo died suddenly too.</p>
<p>Last January, Liu Xiaoming, the vice head of Shenyang City<br />Police’ Command Center died after falling from the 8th floor.</p>
<p>When Liu was the acting chief of the County Police Station,<br />he was actively involved in the persecution of Falun Gong.</p>
<p>Many Falun Gong practitioners were kidnapped, detained,<br />sent to forced labor camps and sentenced to jail, as a result.</p>
<p>The son of Tian Boxiang, former deputy secretary of CCP<br />in Kangping County, died in a car accident.</p>
<p>Zhang Jun, former chief of Kangping County Police Station,<br />was fired from his job. His son had a car accident.</p>
<p>Li Hongtao, former deputy secretary of the CCP<br />in Xiaochengzi Town died of a sudden heart attack.</p>
<p>Fan Bin, former Judge of Kangping County was sentenced<br />to prison one month after framing Falun Gong practitioner.</p>
<p>The Clearwisdom website advises the Chinese officials<br />to immediately stop persecuting Falun Gong practitioners</p>
<p>and release the detained Falun Gong adherents by CCP,<br />as to avoid retributions and other regrets in the future.</p>
<p>NTD reporters He Ping, Li Yuanhan and Li Yue</p>

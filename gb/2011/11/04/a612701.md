<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【禁闻】湖南维权村长离奇死亡 再版钱云会</h2>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【新唐人2011年11月4日讯】浙江乐清维权村长钱云会惨案已淡出人们的视线。但最近网络又出现一位湖南维权村长离奇死亡事件。连续担任15年村支书记与主任等职务的<ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/游济安">游济安</a>，到县政府上访后失踪，几天后浮尸资江。家人怀疑他在县委内被活活打死，半夜抛尸，村民联名致信全国人大常委会要求缉凶，家人也拒绝赔偿、拒绝火化尸体，一定要为死者讨回公道。</p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/游济安">游济安</a>的女儿游珍娟对《新唐人》说，她的父亲连续担任了15年的村支书，为村民做了很多实事，今年换届又被选为了村长，谁知刚上任几天就这样不明白不白的惨死了，所以一定要了解真相。</p>
<p>游珍娟：“我爸爸是习武的身体，素质也好，也没生病也没什么病，这么进去就死了？他早晨从家里出去的时候就是个好人，如果在家里断了两根肋骨的话，还有在外面断两根肋骨的话，怎么到你县政府去了？我们说没什么要求，只要把我们这个事实查清楚就可以了。”</p>
<p>游济安的儿子游飞告诉记者，游济安一直以来协助村民维权，反映新化县里的非法征地问题，今年6月8号他再次到县政府找领导反映，结果神秘失踪，直到13号，他的尸体在离佛光村100多公里的资江被发现。</p>
<p>游珍娟表示，镇长与镇书记找他们好几次了，要给他们10万元安葬费，一套廉租房，一个的士指标，条件就是早点火化尸体，否则政府会强制火化！</p>
<p>游珍娟：“他们隔三差五的联系我们，包括我妈妈的亲戚，说我妈妈没有工作，搞一个房子，我弟弟刚刚（劳教）回来可以搞一个的士指标，已经找了好几次了。他说我们告政府是告不羸的，所以我们就说心里不甘心，不可能这样不明不白，把这个事情给我查清楚了。”</p>
<p>6月14号，游游济安家属把他尸体运到殡仪馆冷冻起来。经法医鉴定发现他脑内有瘀血，胸内有两跟肋骨断裂，身上皮肤多处有青紫色瘀血。</p>
<p>新化县公安局在新化县委大院内找到了游济安出事当天乘坐的【湘K61937】车，调阅8号晚县委大楼的监控视频，发现游济安于当晚1点左右被县政府的6名保安从电梯中拖出来，扔到汽车尾箱，然后驾车离开。过程中，游济安没有动过一下。 </p>
<p>家人要求公安局立案调查，4个月过去了，县公安局一直不予立案，声称游济安是溺水死亡，与保安无关。</p>
<p>游珍娟告诉记者，县保安在接受调查时承认把游济安放到汽车车尾箱，但却说游济安当时是活着的，监控视频里中看到的游济安不动是在装死。</p>
<p>游珍娟：“那个视频的话，律师可以去看，如果记者去看的话可以，他可以给你看，但是他不取证给你。我们律师去找的话，他也不给我们，他说我这个要去找领导，找县里的领导去函见，给领导商量了，可不可以给你，领导可以就可以，领导不可以我们也没这个权力。”</p>
<p>新化镇领导也坚持说游济安是不小心溺水身亡，并认定他被送到镇政府时是活人。</p>
<p>游家镇政协主任陈仲斌：“他（游济安）是晚上2点钟以后县访（信访办）把他送到我们镇里是一个活人哎，一个活生生的人哎。”</p>
<p>游家镇人大主席肖锦华：市公安局做了那个尸检，溺水死亡。</p>
<p>游珍娟认为镇领导在做假证。游珍娟表示，父亲很热心，经常帮村民维权，她怀疑父亲因此得罪县领导。</p>
<p>游珍娟：“不管是医疗纠纷还是个人纠纷，在外面基本上人权，维权一般都喊他去的，反正他也不收钱，包括我们还其他的，还有民政的，土地问题啊，或者什么，因为他也能说也能写，反正他帮助别人，实际上他也不是什么律师啊，但是他帮助别人，别人省了不少费用，他喜欢打抱不平。”</p>
<p>游济安出事之后，佛光村村民们曾集结去镇政府静坐示威，并联名写了一封给吴邦国委员长的投诉信，每个人都按了指印，要求将凶手缉拿归案。</p>
<p>去年年底浙江乐清村长钱云会惨死在车轮下，民间普遍认为：钱云会是因为为村民土地维权而遭到杀害，但官方最后认定为普通交通事故处理。而游济安的死因到底有什么内幕，我们将跟踪报导。</p>
<p>新唐人记者李韵、王明宇采访报导。</p>
<p>The Strange Death of a Village Head</p>
<p>The tragic death of Qian Yunhui, a human rights director<br />in Yueqing, Zhejiang Province, has been mostly forgotten.<br />Recently the strange death of You Ji’an,<br />a village party secretary and director,<br />as well as a human rights director, has gripped the village<br />of Foguang in Hunan Province.<br />You Ji’an, who served his village for 15 consecutive years,<br />suddenly disappeared after he petitioned the county government.<br />His body was found several days later, floating in the Zi River.</p>
<p>His family suspected that he was beaten to death<br />then thrown in the river at midnight.<br />Villagers jointly sent a letter to the NPC Standing Committee<br />asking them to punish the murderers.<br />The family not only refused to allow Ji’an’s body<br />to be cremated, but they also refused to accept any form of financial compensation.<br />They simply wanted justice for the dead.</p>
<p>Yu Ji’an&#8217;s daughter, You Zhenjuan, told NTD TV<br />that her father had served as village secretary for 15 years,<br />and did a lot of practical things for the residents, there.</p>
<p>He was elected as village head this year, but mysteriously died<br />a few days later.<br />Her family is anxious to solve the mystery<br />surrounding his death.</p>
<p>Yu Zhenjuan: &#8220;My father practices martial arts, has very good<br />health, and no illnesses at all.<br />How could he die after petitioning the county government?<br />When my father went out this morning, he was physically fit.<br />If he broke two ribs at home or elsewhere, how could he go<br />to the county government?<br />We have no other requirements other than to find the truth.”</p>
<p>Yu Ji’an&#8217;s son, You Fei, told reporters, “You Ji’an has been<br />helping villagers uphold their rights regarding illegal land acquisition in Xinhua County.<br />On June 8, he again visited the county government<br />to tell officials about these problems, but mysteriously disappeared.<br />On June 13, his body was found in the Zi River,<br />100 kilometers (62 miles) away from Foguang Village.</p>
<p>You Zhenjuan said, the director and Party secretary of<br />the town had talked to them several times,<br />offering the family RMB 100,000 (US$15,742)<br />in funeral expenses, low-rent housing, and a taxicab,<br />if the family agreed to quickly cremate the body,<br />otherwise the government would force the cremation!</p>
<p>You Zhenjuan: &#8220;They contact us frequently,<br />including my mother and her relatives.<br />They said that since my mother did not work,<br />they could give her a house, and<br />since my brother just got released from a forced labor camp,<br />they would give him a taxicab.<br />They have been talking to our family members<br />several times, now.<br />They said that our family would lose the fight<br />with the government.<br />How can our hearts be reconciled when the circumstances<br />surrounding my father’s death are so unclear? Please help us find out the truth."</p>
<p>On June 14, You Ji’an’s family members sent his body<br />to a funeral home to be frozen.<br />Through forensic investigation, it was determined that<br />his brain had a hole in it and two of his ribs were broken.<br />Also, he had purple bruises all over his body.</p>
<p>The Xinhua County Police Department found You Ji’an’s car<br />in the courtyard of the county government, the car he used that day (tag #: Xiang, K61937).<br />By checking a security surveillance video on the night of July 8,<br />it was determined that around 1 a.m. that day,<br />You Ji’an was dragged out of an elevator<br />by six security personnel then thrown into the trunk of a car.<br />The car quickly sped off.<br />During the whole process, You had not moved at all.</p>
<p>You’s family members asked the county police department<br />to file a case and investigate the issue.<br />Four months later, the county police department refused<br />to set up a case, claiming You had drowned, thus his death had nothing to do with security.</p>
<p>You Zhenjuan told reporters that county security personnel<br />admitted during the investigation<br />that they put You in the trunk of a car,<br />but he was alive then and just pretending to be dead in the video.</p>
<p>You Zhenjuan: “There’s no problem for lawyers<br />to watch the video.<br />If a reporter wants to see it, they’d show it to them,<br />but they will not allow anyone to take the evidence with them.<br />Even when we went there with our lawyer,<br />they would not give the video to us,<br />but told us instead to go to see the county government leaders,<br />and request it from them.<br />‘If the leaders say yes, we can give you the video,<br />but if they say no, then we can not release the video.’”</p>
<p>Xinhua town leaders insisted that You was drowned,<br />and claimed You was alive when he was escorted to the town government seat.</p>
<p>Chen Zhongbin, director of the Chinese People&#8217;s<br />Political Consultative Conference: “He (You Ji’an) was sent<br />to the town government seat around 2 a.m.<br />from the county petition office.<br />He was alive at that time, a living person.”</p>
<p>Xiao Jinhua, People&#8217;s Congress Chairman in You’s Village:<br />“The Municipal Police Department’s autopsy shows the cause of death was due to drowning.”</p>
<p>You Zhenjuan believed the town leader<br />had given false testimony.<br />She said that her father is very enthusiastic<br />and often helped villagers uphold their rights.<br />She feels that her father’s actions had offended<br />the county leaders.</p>
<p>You Zhenjuan: “Whether it involved medical malpractice<br />or personal disputes, villagers always called upon him to help them uphold human rights.<br />He never asked for financial compensation, in any way.</p>
<p>Due to his good speaking and writing skills,<br />he was also asked to intercede in local civil affairs, land issues, etc.<br />Anyway, he helped a lot of people save a lot of money,<br />even though he was not a lawyer. He just liked to make things right.”</p>
<p>After You’s death, villagers in Foguang Village<br />gathered at the town government seat for a sit-in protest.<br />They wrote a letter to Chairman Wu Bangguo about<br />their complaints,<br />asking him to help them secure the fingerprints of each person,<br />involved in the case so that they can bring the murderers to justice.</p>
<p>Late last year, Qian Yunhui, village head of Yueqing,<br />Zhejiang, died in a car accident.<br />People generally believe that Qian was killed because<br />he called for land rights for villagers.<br />But officially, it was recorded as a regular car accident.<br />What is the truth behind You Ji’an’s death? We will follow up.</p>
<p>NTD reporters Li Yun and Wang Mingyu</p>

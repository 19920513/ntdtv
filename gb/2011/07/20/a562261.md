<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【禁闻】7.20-他们的故事</h2>
<a href="https://dsgpxulwvf05x.cloudfront.net/Z9sQTHNFu"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/19920513/www/master/t/ntdtv/ntdtv600.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://github.com/19920513/www/blob/master/README.md#8">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【新唐人2011年7月20日讯】今年的7·20，就是法轮功团体的第12个7月20号，也是中共镇压法轮功的第12个年头。这个为时不短的12年，对于许多中国大陆的法轮功修炼者来说，是历经一个出生入死，沧桑巨变的过程。他们在面对强权的镇压，有过绝望、痛苦，濒临崩溃和死亡﹔他们在铁腕高压面前坚持信仰，在生死交战之际浩劫重生，最终坦然的在镜头下，讲述他们的故事。</p>
<p>720特别报导，《禁头人物》献给大家——“他们的故事”。</p>
<p>这是来自河南的金昭宇，2008年她嫁到芬兰之后，一直忙碌奔波于营救自己的母亲陈真萍。金昭宇和她的芬兰籍丈夫Tommy这几年来见过了芬兰政要、人权组织、大大小小的芬兰媒体，就是希望修炼法轮功的母亲陈真萍能够获得自由。</p>
<p>十多年来，金昭宇几乎身兼母职，从20岁起独自抚养小她九岁的妹妹。</p>
<p>金昭宇：“记得2000年镇压以后，几乎每个新年，家中只有我和我妹妹。大年初一的时候，我和我妹妹吃着方便面，吃着吃着都会哭起来，因为家里没有妈妈，家里空荡荡，妈妈不是在看守所，就是在劳教所，不是在拘留所，就是在洗脑班。”</p>
<p>现在金昭宇的妈妈被判刑8年，目前被关押在河南省新乡市女子监狱，年仅20岁的妹妹金昭桓也因为当地迫害法轮功组织610和警察不间断的抓捕，迫不得已逃到了泰国，另一位来自吉林长春的兰丽华，也是在这样的花样年华，遭到了警察无理的拘捕和殴打。她回忆起2002年3月5号，《长春电视》成功插播法轮功真相后，她随后被关押7个月，巨大的精神压力使她的外貌全变了样。</p>
<p>兰丽华：“架着我就开始走，出去之后给我塞到一个警车里，几个人同时对我暴打，当时的时候那件事情对我冲击特别大，因为我那个时候才23岁，我从来没有经历过几个男的对一个女的同时打，就是那种冲击我当时就抽了，整个人浑身全部抽搐。”</p>
<p>与兰丽华同是吉林人的房思邑，是一位高级中医美容师，目前和兰丽华都被联合国安置在芬兰，2010年她到日内瓦联合国人权会上作证，讲述她在狱中被药物折磨的经历，令在场很多人都动容落泪。</p>
<p>房思邑：“打得什么针我不知道，从那天打针的时候肚子就开始肿胀，一天比一天大，不到一个星期的时候那肚子就像快生孩子了，就比要临生产的肚子还要大，已经成了透明的球了，我跟那些医生说，我炼法轮功被他们迫害成这样，只要我放回家，我会完完整整健健康康的来见你们，那个医生抿嘴楞一下，什么都没说，当时有一个医生就说，你要是能活着就是奇迹，我说我不但能活着，还能健康的活着。”</p>
<p>房思邑回忆，当时医院有许多民众围观，在这些善良人们的帮助下，狱方迫于压力让她保外就医。今天房思邑以健康红润的样貌接受采访，证实了医生口中的这个奇迹。</p>
<p>在中共残酷镇压法轮功长达12年的时间，有许许多多类似金昭宇、兰丽华和房思邑的故事。共产党使用了各种各样的手段，想把信仰真、善、忍的好人，转化中共洗脑下假、恶、斗的坏人。现在居住在澳洲的曾铮，曾是“北京大学”的高材生，不料想，一心想要报效祖国，不愿意出国留学的她，却在经历四次的关押后，被迫远走他乡。</p>
<p>曾铮：“一进去首先听到的声音就是蹲下，然后就是电棍，如果慢了一点电棍马上上到你的身上，从第2天开始，每天早晨洗脸刷牙上厕所，一共只给2分钟的时间，然后每天把你的生活安排得紧紧的，16 个小时都要你背劳教所的规则，搞得人都要崩溃了，让你一点做人的尊严都没有，每天都只想着，挣扎着不要自己疯掉。”</p>
<p>这种迫害是持续而漫长的，今年盛夏的美国首府，将近2000个烛火点亮了华盛顿的夜空，悼念着那些在血腥暴力下失去生命的法轮功修炼者。还有多少的漫漫长夜需要度过，没有人知道答案，这些法轮功追随者只希望把他们的故事，传递出去，让正义和良知与他们同在。</p>
<p>新唐人记者唐宇、萧宇采访报导。</p>
<p>The Stories of Falun Dafa Practitioners</p>
<p>July 20, 2011 marks the 12th anniversary of <br />the Chinese Communist Party&#8217;s (CCP)<br />persecution towards Falun Dafa practitioners, <br />which started in 1999. <br />In the past 12 years, many Falun Dafa practitioners <br />in China have gone through fire and water. <br />Facing repression, they have been in despair and pain, <br />and at the edge of collapse and death, <br />yet they remained steadfast in their faith <br />and experienced rebirth after excruciating experiences. <br />Now they calmly tell their stories. <br />Here is a July 20th Special Report. </p>
<p>Jin Zhaoyu is originally from Henan Province. </p>
<p>She went to live in Finland in 2008<br />after she got married.<br />She has been busy rescuing her mother since then. </p>
<p>Her mother Chen Zhenping is a Falun Dafa practitioner. </p>
<p>Jin and her husband Tommy have met with Finnish politicians, <br />human rights organizations and the Finnish media <br />in the past few years, striving to rescue her mother.</p>
<p>For over a decade, Jin acted as a mother to her sister, <br />who is 9 years her junior.</p>
<p>Jin Zhaoyu: “Since the persecution started, only my sister <br />and I spent the New Years together almost every year. <br />On the New Year eve, we ate instant noodles and cried. <br />Mom was not at home, our house was empty. <br />Mom was at the detention center, <br />in the labor camp or in a brainwashing class.</p>
<p>Jin&#8217;s sister, who is only 20 years old, <br />was forced to flee to Thailand <br />to escape from the harassment of the police. </p>
<p>Lan Lihua from Changchu, Jilin Province <br />was also tortured by the police at a young age. <br />She talked about being detained for seven months <br />after the program about the truth of Falun Dafa <br />was broadcasted at the Changchu TV station. </p>
<p>Her appearance completely changed<br /> due to enormous amount of mental pressure. </p>
<p>Lan Lihua: “They took me away and <br />put me into a police vehicle. <br />Several people brutally beat me. It shocked me. <br />At that time I was only 23 years old.<br />I&#8217;ve never seen several men beating one woman. <br />My whole body shivered and twitched. </p>
<p>Fang Siyi, also from Jinlin, <br />was a senior Chinese beautician.<br />Both Fang and Lan went to Finland <br />under the arrangement of the UN.<br />In 2010, Fang testified at the <br />UN Human Rights Conference in Geneva, <br />about the torture by drugs she experienced in prison,<br />many people onsite were moved to tears.</p>
<p>Fang Siyi: <br />“I don&#8217;t know what kind of injection it was.<br />My belly was swollen after they started the injection, <br />and got bigger and bigger. <br />Within a week, my belly was like a pregnant woman&#8217;s,<br />or even bigger, it was almost like a transparent ball. <br />I asked the doctor to let me go home, <br />and I would come back in good health. <br />One doctor did not say anything; another one said <br />that it would be a miracle if I could survive. <br />I told them that I would survive and be in good health.</p>
<p>Fang recollected that many people <br />who witnessed this helped her. <br />Under the pressure, the prison put her on bail for treatment.</p>
<p>Today Fang Siyi is in good health <br />and proved the miracle she told the doctors.</p>
<p>In the past 12 years, there were many similar stories. </p>
<p>The CCP wanted to transform good people, who believe in <br />“Truthfulness, Compassion and Tolerance” into bad ones.<br />Zeng Zheng was a top student from Beijing University. </p>
<p>She never thought about going abroad before, <br />and always wanted to serve her country. <br />After a 4-year detention, she was forced to leave China.</p>
<p>Zeng Zheng: “Once I got in, I first need to squat, <br />then they would shock me with electrical batons. <br />Since the 2nd day, we were only given 2 minutes in the morning <br />for washing, brushing teeth and using the restroom. <br />Daily schedule was very intense.<br />You need to recite rules and principles 16 hours a day. <br />I was on the brink of collapse. <br />There was no dignity at all. <br />Each day I struggled not to lose sanity.” </p>
<p>This persecution is continuous and very long. </p>
<p>In the summer of 2011, nearly 2,000 candles <br />lit the night sky of Washington D.C.,<br />mourning the Falun Dafa practitioners who lost their lives. </p>
<p>How many more dark nights are there? <br />No one knows. <br />Falun Dafa followers only hope to spread their stories. <br />May justice and conscience be with them.</p>
<p>NTD reporters Tang Yu and Xiao Yu</p>

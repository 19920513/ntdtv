<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【禁闻】“抗日战争”究竟是谁打的？</h2>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【新唐人2011年9月20日讯】日军侵华的“9.18事变”距离今天已经80年了，当年的“抗日战争”，究竟是谁打的？中共当局企图定调“抗日战争”是由共产党领导。学者认为，“爱国主义”和“民族主义”是中共六四以来，用来宣传中共意识形态的基调，而谎言与暴力是维系专制政权的两个主要手段，中共不可能放弃。</p>
<p>过去多年，中共宣传国民党不抗日，近年来才改称国民党也抗日。</p>
<p>不过，《人民日报》“九一八社论：勿忘国耻 共襄复兴”中写道，中国共产党“号召全民族团结起来抗日救国，反对不抵抗政策。”，企图定调“抗日战争”是由共产党领导。</p>
<p>复旦大学历史系教授钱文忠18号在网上指出:“中国抗战始于 80年前今天，抗战 14年而非8年；中国抗战正面战场，绝对主力是当时国民政府军队!”</p>
<p>80年前的9月18号，日本军以中国军炸毁日本修筑的一段南满铁路为由，发动战争，占领了沈阳，走上全面侵入中国的道路，历史上称为“9.18事变”。</p>
<p>钱文忠表示，历史记载，14年抗战中，国民党军发动大型会战22次，重要战役1,127次，国军206位将军殉国，包括8名上将。</p>
<p>历史学者吴仁华说，1949年中共建政以后，他们想掩盖的历史真相中，“抗日战争”是目前学者突破的最大禁区。</p>
<p>历史学者吴仁华：“在中国抗战的史实方面，中共当局只是在掩盖真相，这种真相掩盖了很多年。抗日战争主要起作用的不是共产党领导的八路军、新四军，而是国民政府领导的国民革命军。所以共产党所看的历史，他是从1937年西安事变以后，有了八路军以后他们开始算。”</p>
<p>9.18当天，中国各地举行了各项活动。东北3省&#8212;-辽宁、吉林及黑龙江，共同举行撞响警钟及鸣警报仪式，与此同时，全中国有100多座城市也在这一刻拉响防空警报。据报导，这次纪念9.18活动规模是历史之最。</p>
<p>吴仁华：“所以说他在每年的9.18，他当然抓住这机会，因为这对中共当局来讲，他认为这是最好的宣扬爱国主义跟民族主义情绪的一个机会。中共当局在9.18的时候，举行很多官方的庆祝纪念活动，他的主要的政治目地就在于这方面。”</p>
<p>中共当局高调纪念 9.18，甚至，有网友发现：“前两天《央视》播《小小飞虎队》，三个十岁小孩，能把日本兵耍死，比新闻联播还假!”</p>
<p>吴仁华：“他们历来就是把这些影视作品作为宣传品来推行的，我想根本都不值得太多的关注跟评论。光是从中共抗日的影视作品统计，被打死的日军总人数，真的超过了日军进入中国，军队的总人数的好多倍。”</p>
<p>但是，真相只有一个，中共为什么把残酷的战争如此游戏化呢？</p>
<p>吴仁华：“因为专制政权它维护自己统治只有两个手段，一个就是欺骗，靠谎言，另外一个手段就是恐惧，用暴力，就是用监狱制造恐惧。”</p>
<p>今年虽然中共当局高调纪念9.18，可是，当天，准备到沈阳举行纪念活动的民间保钓团体却被阻挠，甚至受到警方威胁。很多网友对当局阻挠民间纪念活动大表不满。</p>
<p>新唐人记者常春、周平、张健采访报导 。</p>
<p>Who Led &#8220;The War of Resistance Against Japan"?</p>
<p>The &#8220;9.18 Incident" refers to the Japanese military invasion<br />of China 80 years ago. Who actually fought in this war?<br />The Chinese Communist Party (CCP) is trying <br />to take the credit for &#8220;The War of Resistance Against Japan".<br />Scholars believe that &#8220;patriotism" and &#8220;nationalism" have<br />been part of CCP’s ideology propaganda since June 4th.<br />Lies and violence are two major means in despotic regimes.<br />Is it impossible for CCP to give them up?</p>
<p>Over the past years, CCP spread the propaganda that KMT<br />(Kuomintang) was not anti-Japanese during this war.<br />Until recent years, CCP did not change its propaganda line<br />on &#8220;The War of Resistance Against Japan."</p>
<p>However, the report &#8220;Editorial of September 18: Do not forget<br />national humiliation, to cooperate with the grand revival"<br />published by People&#8217;s Daily, states that CCP “called on all<br />the nations to unite and resist Japan, and save the country, opposing the policy of not resisting Japan.” <br />With that CCP attempts to set the tone that <br />&#8220;The War of Resistance Against Japan" was led by CCP.</p>
<p>Professor Qian Wenzhong, from the Department of History<br />at Fudan University, wrote online on September 18th,<br />that “China’s resistance against Japan began on that day<br />80 years ago. It had lasted 14 years, not 8 years. <br />Chinese absolute main force on the front line against the<br />Japanese at that time was the KMT government’s military.”</p>
<p>On September 18th, 80 years ago, Japanese military forces<br />launched the war against China.<br />They used the excuse that Chinese military bombed a<br />section of the South Manchuria Railway, built by Japan. <br />Japan occupied Shengyang and embarked the path of full<br />invasion of China, known in history as the &#8220;9.18 Incident."</p>
<p>Qian Wenzhong said that according to the historical records, <br />during the 14 years of resistance against Japan, <br />KMT’s military undertook large battles 22 times <br />and important battles 1,127 times.<br />In this war they lost 206 generals,<br />eight of which were high level military generals. </p>
<p>Wu Renhua, a historian, said that since CCP took power<br />in 1949, among the historical truth CCP tries to hide, <br />“The War of Resistance Against Japan” has the strictest <br />restrictions, that scholars try to break through at present.</p>
<p>Wu Renhua: &#8220;In the historical facts during the war <br />of China’s resistance against Japan, <br />Chinese authorities are just covering up the truth. <br />And these truths have been hidden for many years.<br />The main role in “The War of Resistance Against Japan”<br />was not the CCP’s leadership of the Eighth Route Army<br />and the New Fourth Army, but the KMT’s leadership<br />of the National Revolutionary Army.<br />Thus the history from CCP’s perspective started after the<br />Xi&#8217;an Incident in 1937, with the Eighth Route Army formed."</p>
<p>On September 18, various activities were held across China.</p>
<p>Three Northeast Provinces, Liaoning, Jilin and Heilongjiang, <br />held together a ceremony of sounding bells and sirens. <br />Meanwhile, there were more than 100 cities with air sirens<br />sounded at that moment.<br />According to reports, these activities were <br />the largest in history, marking the 9.18 Incident.</p>
<p>Wu Renhua: &#8220;Thus September 18th each year is a chance <br />for CCP to propagate its patriotism and nationalism.<br />During this time CCP authorities hold many official activities<br />for commemoration. It has a main political agenda in this." </p>
<p>With the high-profile of 9.18 commemoration by the CCP,<br />some netizens even mentioned of “The Little Flying Tigers”<br />broadcast by CCTV two days ago, where three 10-year-old<br />children defeat Japanese soldiers,<br />which was more fake than CCTV’s News themselves, <br />wrote the netizents.</p>
<p>Wu Renhua: &#8220;They have taken the film and TV works <br />as promotional means to spread their products recently. <br />I think it doesn’t deserve much attention and comments.</p>
<p>Based on the statistics of the film and TV works <br />about the anti-Japanese war made by CCP,<br />the total number of Japanese soldiers killed is several times<br />higher than the number of Japanese troops entering China."</p>
<p>However, the truth can be only one.<br />Why does the CCP turn a brutal war into a game?</p>
<p>Wu Renhua: &#8220;Because authoritarian regimes use 2 means<br />only. One is to cheat, so they are depending on lies. <br />The other is the use of fear, creating the fear with violence, <br />namely, using means like jails to create such fear."</p>
<p>Although the CCP made a high-profile memorial for the 9.18, <br />the Diaoyu Islands’ civil groups were blocked,<br />and even were threatened by the police while they were<br />on their way to Shengyang for the memorial activities. <br />Many netizens show great dissatisfaction from authorities<br />for obstructing the commemoration from civil organizations. </p>
<p>NTD reporters Changchun, Zhou Ping and Zhang Jian</p>

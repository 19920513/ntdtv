<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【禁闻】地沟油链责在体制 特供滋生毒食品</h2>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【新唐人2011年9月22日讯】曾追踪报导<ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/地沟油">地沟油</a>丑闻的河南记者李翔，身中十几刀遇刺身亡，再度引起国内舆论对地沟油猖獗的关注。专家估计，中国人每10顿饭可能就有1顿吃到地沟油。海内外评论指出，权贵特供体系滋生地沟油等有毒食品，中共的腐败体制加剧社会道德沦丧和民众生存危机。</p>
<p>河南洛阳电视台记者李翔9月18号回家途中，被人连捅13刀身亡，随身携带的电脑包被抢。20号，两名嫌犯被抓获，警方通报说是酒后抢劫杀人。</p>
<p>今年30岁的李翔负责洛阳电视台民生、法制报导。他15号发布生前最后一则微博，与当地炼制<ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/地沟油">地沟油</a>窝点有关。早前外界猜测，他的死因可能是“地沟油商买凶杀人”。 </p>
<p>《无国界记者》组织发表声明，对李翔被刺身亡表示震惊。《郑州日报》报导说，李翔遇害被怀疑与地沟油有关，实质在一定程度上也成了公众对地沟油焦虑舆情的渲泄口。 </p>
<p>所谓地沟油是从下水道中捞取的泔水残渣、餐饮垃圾，经过加工、脱色脱臭后，重新变成清亮的一种油，外观和沙拉油没什么差别。 </p>
<p>医学研究显示，地沟油中所含的黄麦霉素是一种强烈的致癌物质，毒性是砒霜的100倍。 </p>
<p>全国粮油标准化委员会油料和油脂工作组组长何东平教授去年说，目前全中国每年返回餐桌的地沟油有200-300万吨，占全中国食用油市场十分之一。但他随后否认说过这样的话，并表示“压力很大”。 </p>
<p>黄良天(《百姓》杂志前主编)：“应该是受到一些压力，因为他这种言论影响了安定团结的社会局面，造成人心恐慌，以这个借口肯定他那个。他说出来应该是很自然的，后来出来改口应该是不自然的，能够对他造成压力肯定不是老百姓。” </p>
<p>“华农食品科技学院”教授王承明认为，何东平所说的只是保守的数字，2009年全中国地沟油的使用量达400-500万吨。 </p>
<p>经济学家郎咸平指出，暴利造就了中国地沟油产业链，地沟油行业一年的总利润达到15-20亿元。 </p>
<p>近日，浙江、山东、河南等地摧毁了一个跨越14个省的地沟油网络，查获地沟油100多吨、已罐装假冒品牌“食用油”100多箱，抓获主要嫌犯32名。 </p>
<p>“济南格林生物能源公司”以生产销售生物柴油为名，从浙江、四川、贵州等地收购粗炼过的地沟油，加工为成品油，产量达每天数十吨，批发给一些粮油公司流入食品市场，或者罐装零售给宾馆、饭店、夜排档、油条摊。 </p>
<p>16号，乌鲁木齐市也查获了一个地沟油加工厂，把又脏又臭的地沟油加工制成酸化油，卖给化工厂成为生产香皂和油漆的原料。 </p>
<p>《南方都市报》评论指出，地沟油：又一个“谁都在管谁都不管”。《新华网》时评说，在警方侦破的地沟油案件中，淘捞、粗炼、倒卖、深加工、批发、零售等各个环节，都没有看到监管部门的身影。</p>
<p>英国《金融时报》20号的文章指出，权贵“特供”经济，将中共官员食品排除在市场体系之外，每一级官员都会如法炮制，让自己成为有毒食品中的安全孤岛。 </p>
<p>《新华网》16号报导，浙江丽水遂昌县的绿色农产品基地，保障了浙江一些政府部门和单位的内部供应，因而变成“特供农场”。当地农业局进行了反驳，但网络上依然群情汹涌。 </p>
<p>朱健国(深圳独立作家)：“比如说国务院现在已经有很多的资料和文章证明，他们几十年来一贯制的在吃着特供食品﹔各地的政府都有形形色色的特供渠道，富豪执政者他根本就感觉不到食品安全问题。还有个问题就是他们的官商勾结，一旦要真正动手，就要危害他们企业界的朋友，比如说三聚氰胺的都是王牌企业。” </p>
<p>《金融时报》的评论指出，如同以往历次执法，没有人因为大范围的有毒食品泛滥被问责。可怕的食品安全显示出（中国）社会道德无底线、诚信彻底缺失、公平市场规则的沦丧。 </p>
<p>新唐人记者常春、李元翰、黎安安采访报导。</p>
<p>Hogwash Oil: For Public Only</p>
<p>Henan journalist, Li Xiang, was stabbed for his investigation<br />of hogwash oil scandals, spotlighting the scandal once again.<br />It is said that on average, every Chinese has one out of ten<br />meals cooked with hogwash oil.<br />Critics believe that this special provision system<br />leads to tainted food problems.<br />The Chinese Communist Party’s (CCP) corruption system<br />destroys morality and threatens lives.</p>
<p>Journalist Li Xiang from Henan Luoyang TV was stabbed<br />13 times on his way home on September 18.<br />His computer was also taken from his home.<br />On September 20, police announced that two suspects<br />were arrested for robbery.</p>
<p>The 30-years old TV journalist was reporting on legal issues.<br />His last microblog on September 15 was about hogwash oil.<br />Some believe that hogwash oil manufacturers<br />may have paid someone to murder him.</p>
<p>Reporters Without Borders released a statement<br />expressing their shock.<br />Zhenzhou Daily stated that the allegation of the murder<br />being related to Li Xiang’s hogwash oil reports,<br />shows to some extent the public’s concern<br />about the hogwash oil issue.</p>
<p>Hogwash oil is a refined oil from food leftovers,<br />kitchen trash, and sewage water.<br />After special treatment for color and odor,<br />it looks clear, like normal salad oil.</p>
<p>Medical research shows however, that hogwash oil contains<br />a strong carcinogen, 10 times more poisonous than arsenic.</p>
<p>He Dongping, expert from National Food and Oil Standard<br />Committee said last year,<br />that 2-3 million tons of hogwash oil is used annually,<br />making up to 1/10 of China’s total oil consumption.<br />However, later he denied what he said in his speech,<br />explaining that he was under “a lot of pressure” afterwards.</p>
<p>Huang Liangtian (Former editor, Bai Xing magazine):<br />“He must have suffered some pressure,<br />because his speech was viewed as stirring public concerns,<br />and affecting social stability.<br />What he said was the truth, his denying it later was not.<br />Whoever put pressure on him won’t just be a public member.”</p>
<p>Professor Wang Chenming from Huanong Food Technology<br />College believes that the given by He Dongping figure<br />was underestimated. Professor Wang claims that in 2009,<br />hogwash oil consumption was 4-5 million tons.</p>
<p>Economist Lang Xianping explained how China’s hogwash<br />oil industry is highly lucrative, with its ca. RMB2 bill. a year.</p>
<p>Recently, a hogwash oil marketing network<br />in more than 14 provinces was exposed,<br />with over 10 tons of oil and 100 boxes of fake brands<br />cooking oil confiscated, and 32 suspects arrested.</p>
<p>Under the name of producing bio diesel, the Jinan Gelin<br />Bio Energy company collected crude hogwash oil<br />from other provinces, and refined them into oil,<br />with a daily production of more than 10 tons.<br />Then, they wholesaled or retailed them to food markets,<br />hotels, restaurants or street stalls.</p>
<p>On September 16, Wulumuqi City also found<br />a hogwash oil production facility.<br />The dirty and smelly oil was processed<br />and sold to chemical factories to make soaps or paints.</p>
<p>Commentaries from South Metropolis Daily said, hogwash<br />oil is just another issue the government does nothing about.<br />Xinhua News Agency said that when reviewing the cases<br />of hogwash oil, in every stage, from collection, refinement,<br />wholesale to retail, one could never find<br />any government agency monitoring.</p>
<p>Financial Times’ article from Sept. 20 states, the officials’<br />Special Provision System removes their food from the market.<br />Throughout China people are trying to copy this system<br />to stay safe amidst the booming industry of tainted foods.</p>
<p>Xinhua website reported on September 16, that the organic<br />farm at Suichang Jiangsu was transformed into a special farm<br />supplier to provincial governments, which was refuted by<br />local agriculture agency, but criticism flooded the micro blogs.</p>
<p>Zhu Jiangu (Shenzhen Independent Writer):<br />“State Council has many documents<br />showing that they have been enjoying special food supplies,<br />and every local government has their own provision channel.<br />The rich officials never feel the threats of tainted foods.<br />Even worse, officials and businessman collaborate, so they wouldn’t harm each other.<br />The company producing tainted baby formula<br />was labeled as a premier business.”</p>
<p>Financial Times’ commentaries note that just like before,<br />nobody is ever held responsible for tainted foods.<br />The terrible state of food safety shows how China’s society<br />is losing moral grounds on honesty and fairness.</p>
<p>NTD reporters Chang Chun, Li Yuanhan and Li Anan</p>

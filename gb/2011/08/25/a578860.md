<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【禁闻】陆保障房赛高铁 豆腐渣工程露相</h2>
<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【新唐人2011年8月25日讯】中共当局表示，今年10月底前要完成对全国保障性住房项目的巡视，保证1000万套的政治指标，年底前如期完成。对于这样的一个建设速度，外界担忧，各地<ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/保障房">保障房</a>建设“大干快上”，会同高铁建设一样“大跃进”，存在类似高铁的事故隐患。最近全中国已有多处爆出保障房质量问题。请看报导。</p>
<p>中国住建部表示，截至7月底，全国保障性住房的“开工率”已达72%，这个数字在5月底却只有32%。在短短两个月时间内，全国保障性住房已开工400万套。媒体曾发表社论警告说，“片面讲速度，忽视工程品质，会引发混乱，变成‘伤心工程’。”</p>
<p>《美国之音》报导，在此之前，中国大陆媒体纷纷报导各地<ahref="https://github.com/1992513/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/focus/保障房">保障房</a>屋建设存在使用瘦身钢筋、漏水、验收走形式等品质问题。内蒙包头“民馨家园”部分新房的墙面水泥被发现“疏松得像土块一样，一使劲就能捏碎”；北京市“明月湾”专案，六栋保障楼房日前也因品质问题被责令推倒重建。</p>
<p>《北京晨报》8月21号，刊登一则题为《真无奈：苍梧县沙头镇双尚新农村又现豆腐渣工程》的文章。</p>
<p>文章说，广西梧州市部分村民入住新房子不久，发现房屋渗水，墙壁几乎都有不同程度的裂缝，部分屋内地面出现下沉。</p>
<p>王先生（广西工程机械有限公司人员）：“水泥啊、钢筋啊，建材这一块啊，本来他要求用多高级别的，他就给它那偷梁换柱，用小的、用差的嘛。都在牟利，当官的也要牟利，他承包商也要牟利，是吧？”</p>
<p>广西上思县住宅楼工程财务处黄女士表示，老百姓本来搬进新房是件高兴的事情，可是现在却担心起安全问题，有的老百姓已经搬走了。</p>
<p>黄女士：“肯定有房子住是一件很开心的事情，住进去了发现这样的问题，肯定是心里也不高兴。老百姓就觉得政府不可靠。”</p>
<p>经济学家草庵居士表示，中国现在面临着最严重的通货膨胀，民众对当局有很多的不满。他认为，中共当局是被迫的提出兴建保障房，来解决民众的愤怒。</p>
<p>草庵居士（经济学家）：“中共开工这么多保障房，但是它在建筑上是粗制滥造。而且贪污腐败问题里面很严重。所以，我们看到中共现在解决这些问题的时候，它还是没有吸取过去的教训。没有把人民的生命安全放在首位。”</p>
<p>草庵居士认为，表面上看，这是一个社会问题，但实际是反应一个政治制度的问题。他说，只有在得到公正的监督，把人民的整体利益放在首位的时候，才会出现以高质量、高标准来建设的公共福利设施。</p>
<p>另外，广西住房和城乡建设厅督查组，随机抽查20个保障性住房项目，发现，在保障房建设过程中，仍有工程项目使用违规的‘瘦身钢筋’。有的保障房专案，没有办理施工许可就已经开工。</p>
<p>新唐人记者唐睿、孙宁采访报导。</p>
<p>Jerry-built Welfare Housing Projects</p>
<p>The Chinese Communist Party (CCP) ordered the <br />review of a project to construct 10 million welfare <br />housing units, by the end of October 2011.<br />The goal is to finish the construction in late 2011. <br />People criticized that such a high speed construction <br />would follow the tragedy of the high-speed trains. <br />There is still potential for accidents, <br />some of which exist on a national level.</p>
<p>The Chinese housing department said that <br />the national welfare housing project enjoyed <br />an operating rate of 72% by the end of July. <br />This is compared to that of 32% at the end of May. </p>
<p>It means, four million welfare houses were put into <br />construction within two months. <br />The media warned, &#8220;the disproportion with the project speed <br />and quality will finally render the project a heart-breaking one."<br />Voice of America said that Chinese media have quite often <br />disclosed quality problems with the welfare housing,<br />such as thinned steel, water leakage <br />and superficial examining. <br />For example, the Minxin House in Baotou of Inner Mongolia <br />was found to be with wall cement being as soft as soil.<br />It could be easily crumbed. </p>
<p>The Moon Bay House (Yueliangwan) in Beijing <br />was known for its six apartments being reconstructed <br />due to quality problems.</p>
<p>The Beijing Morning Post published an article on Aug. 21 </p>
<p>titled &#8220;Frustration: The Jerry-built Project Return <br />in Shuangshang Village, Cangwu County in Shatou Town.<br />The article says tenants, who just moved in, <br />have discovered water leakages and wall cracks. <br />On the ground level, some rooms appeared to have sunken. </p>
<p>Mr. Wang (staff of Guangxi Engineering Machinery Co. Ltd): </p>
<p>Construction materials such as cement, concrete and steel <br />were required at a certain standard or measure. <br />They are then replaced with smaller, products of poorer quality, <br />for the purpose of gaining profit.<br />Both the contractors and the CCP officials seek profits <br />by any means possible.<br />Ms. Huang from the Finance department of the housing <br />project in Shangsi County of Guangxi said that<br />moving into a new house could be pleasant for people. </p>
<p>But the experience is far from a happy one, <br />since you are driven away by the issues of safety.<br />Ms. Huang: &#8220;It was a pleasant thing to <br />move into a new house. <br />However, people become upset, <br />when they find problems after settling in. <br />They think the government is unreliable." </p>
<p>Economist Cao’an Jushi said that Chinese people were <br />discontent with the authorities, due to the severe inflation. <br />He thinks that the CCP caved in to public pressure <br />by providing the welfare housing project.<br />Cao’an Jushi: Despite plenty of CCP authorized welfare <br />houses, they are generally built poorly. <br />Corruption is also rampant throughout the project procedure. </p>
<p>The CCP does not learn its lesson from the past, <br />nor put public safety and well being as top priorities.<br />He also said that it was more than a social problem, <br />it is in fact a political systemic problem. <br />Only through sound supervision and setting the public <br />wellbeing as top priority, can the communal facilities <br />be completed in high quality and standard.</p>
<p>20 random investigations were conducted <br />in the Guangxi welfare houses. <br />The supervising team found there are a number of <br />construction projects still using the illegal thinned steel. <br />Some projects just started operating <br />without any construction permits.</p>
<p>NTD reporters Tang Rui and Sun Ning.</p>
